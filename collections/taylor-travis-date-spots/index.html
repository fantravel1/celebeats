Where do Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce go on dates?

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's favorite date spots span their two home bases: New York City (Taylor's Tribeca neighborhood) and Kansas City (Travis's home turf). In NYC, they frequent The Waverly Inn in the West Village for cozy dinners, Emilio's Ballato for classic Italian, and Via Carota for intimate evenings. In Kansas City, they've been spotted at Travis's own steakhouse 1587 Prime and celebrating Chiefs victories at Q39. The couple got engaged at Gram & Dun in Kansas City in August 2025.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's favorite date spot?
Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce's favorite date spot is The Waverly Inn in New York's West Village. They've been photographed there on multiple occasions since they started dating in 2023. The intimate setting and excellent American cuisine make it perfect for their cozy date nights away from the spotlight.
Do Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce own a restaurant?
Yes! Travis Kelce is a co-owner of 1587 Prime, an upscale steakhouse in Kansas City. Taylor has been spotted dining there several times, particularly after Chiefs games. The restaurant has become a hotspot for fans hoping to catch a glimpse of the power couple.
Where did Travis Kelce propose to Taylor Swift?
Travis Kelce proposed to Taylor Swift in August 2025 during an intimate dinner at Gram & Dun in Kansas City. The American restaurant known for its romantic ambiance provided the perfect setting for the surprise proposal, which reportedly left Taylor in tears of joy.
Can you get a reservation at Taylor and Travis's favorite restaurants?
Most of Taylor and Travis's favorite spots accept reservations, though they can be difficult to secure. The Waverly Inn requires advance booking, Emilio's Ballato is first-come first-served, and members-only clubs like San Vicente Bungalows require membership. For 1587 Prime in Kansas City, reservations are recommended especially on game days.
What do Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce typically order on dates?
Taylor Swift is known to favor pasta dishes and tends to order the chicken parmesan at The Waverly Inn. Travis Kelce, being a Kansas City native, loves BBQ and steaks. At their own restaurant 1587 Prime, he's been known to order the signature ribeye. The couple often shares appetizers and reportedly has a sweet tooth for dessert.
Which Kansas City restaurants do Taylor and Travis visit after Chiefs games?
After Chiefs games, Taylor and Travis are often spotted at 1587 Prime (Travis's own restaurant) or Q39 for celebratory BBQ. They typically dine with friends and family including Patrick and Brittany Mahomes. The post-game celebrations have become a tradition during football season.
