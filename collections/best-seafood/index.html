The most popular seafood restaurants among celebrities include Nobu Malibu for its legendary Black Cod Miso and oceanfront setting, Catch LA for its buzzy rooftop scene, and Giorgio Baldi for Italian seafood that has made it Rihanna's favorite. In NYC, Balthazar and Le Bernardin lead the pack, while Miami's Komodo and Joe's Stone Crab draw the A-list crowd.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular seafood restaurant among celebrities?
Nobu Malibu is the most popular seafood restaurant among celebrities. Located directly on the Pacific, it combines world-famous Japanese-Peruvian cuisine (especially the Black Cod Miso) with stunning ocean views. The Kardashians, Rihanna, Leonardo DiCaprio, and countless other A-listers are regulars.
Where do celebrities eat seafood in Los Angeles?
In Los Angeles, celebrities eat seafood at Nobu Malibu for Japanese, Giorgio Baldi for Italian seafood, Catch LA for the rooftop scene, Providence for fine dining, and The Lobster at Santa Monica Pier for casual ocean views. Each offers a different seafood experience.
What are the best celebrity seafood dishes?
The most famous celebrity seafood dishes include Nobu's Black Cod Miso (beloved by countless A-listers), Giorgio Baldi's Branzino (Rihanna's go-to), Catch LA's Truffle Sashimi, and Balthazar's raw bar platters. These dishes have become synonymous with celebrity dining culture.
