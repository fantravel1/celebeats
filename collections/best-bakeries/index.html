What bakeries do celebrities love?

Celebrity favorite bakeries include Levain Bakery (Oprah's "Favorite Things" pick), Tartine in San Francisco, Republique in LA, and Dominique Ansel in NYC. Many celebrities also love Porto's Bakery for Cuban pastries and Magnolia Bakery for nostalgic American treats.

Bakery FAQs

Top celebrity bakeries include Levain Bakery in NYC for cookies, Tartine in SF for bread and pastries, Republique in LA for French pastries, Dominique Ansel for Cronuts, and Porto's Bakery for Cuban treats. Sweet Lady Jane is popular for celebrity cakes.

Oprah's favorite cookie is the chocolate chip walnut cookie from Levain Bakery in New York. She called them "life-changing" on her Favorite Things list, and the 6-ounce cookies have become a tourist destination.

Celebrity birthday cakes often come from Sweet Lady Jane in LA (famous triple berry cake), Milk Bar in NYC, Hansen's Cakes in LA, and high-end custom bakers. Some celebrities hire personal pastry chefs for major events.

Porto's Bakery is arguably LA's most beloved bakery, famous for Cuban pastries and cheese rolls. For upscale pastries, Republique is a celebrity and industry favorite. Sweet Lady Jane is the go-to for celebration cakes.
