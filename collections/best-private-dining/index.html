Private Dining FAQs

Carbone in NYC and Miami has the most coveted private rooms. Craig's "The Vault" in West Hollywood offers true A-list privacy. Nobu Malibu's private deck provides ocean views with exclusivity.

Expect minimum spends of $5,000-$25,000 depending on venue and room size. Carbone's private room requires around $10,000 minimum, while smaller spaces may start at $5,000.

Most require direct contact with the restaurant's events team. Having a relationship with the restaurant helps - regulars get priority. Some venues like Rao's are invitation-only with no public reservations.

Craig's "The Vault" is considered the most exclusive, offering complete privacy with a separate entrance. Spago's private room is preferred for industry events and Oscar-related celebrations.
