Where do celebrities eat breakfast?

Top celebrity breakfast spots include The Polo Lounge at The Beverly Hills Hotel, Nate 'n Al's in Beverly Hills, The Carlyle in NYC, and Russ & Daughters for classic bagels. Many celebrities also frequent hotel restaurants for privacy and upscale service.

Breakfast FAQs

Top LA breakfast spots include The Polo Lounge at The Beverly Hills Hotel for power breakfasts, Nate 'n Al's in Beverly Hills for classic deli fare, Republique for pastries, and Cecconi's at Soho House for industry meetings.

Most celebrities order healthy options: egg white omelets, avocado toast, smoked salmon, fresh fruit, acai bowls, and green juices. Athletes and fitness-focused celebs prioritize protein-rich breakfasts with lean meats and eggs.

NYC favorites include The Carlyle Restaurant for elegant hotel breakfast, Russ & Daughters Cafe for legendary bagels and lox, Barney Greengrass on the Upper West Side, and Sant Ambroeus for Italian-style breakfast in the West Village.

It varies widely. Fitness-focused celebrities like Mark Wahlberg eat very early (around 3-4 AM). Most Hollywood executives have power breakfasts at 8-9 AM. Models and late-night entertainers often skip traditional breakfast hours.
