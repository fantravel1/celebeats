Which celebrity chefs have the best restaurants?

Celebrity Chef FAQs

Major celebrity chefs with restaurants include Gordon Ramsay, Wolfgang Puck, Nobu Matsuhisa, Bobby Flay, Guy Fieri, David Chang, Emeril Lagasse, and Giada De Laurentiis. Each has built empires ranging from fine dining to casual concepts.

Gordon Ramsay Steak at Paris Las Vegas is considered his best US fine dining option. Hell's Kitchen at Caesars Palace is the most popular and immersive experience. For casual dining, Gordon Ramsay Fish & Chips in Las Vegas is beloved.

Nobu is co-owned by Chef Nobu Matsuhisa, actor Robert De Niro, and film producer Meir Teper. They opened the first Nobu in Tribeca in 1994 and have expanded to 50+ locations worldwide. The celebrity partnership helped make it a star magnet.

Gordon Ramsay has the largest global empire with 50+ restaurants. Nobu Matsuhisa has 50+ Nobu locations (with partners). Wolfgang Puck operates 20+ restaurants and has extensive airport and catering operations. Guy Fieri has expanded rapidly with casual concepts.
