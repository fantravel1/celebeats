Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most popular Asian restaurant among celebrities?
Nobu (particularly Nobu Malibu and Nobu Downtown NYC) is the most popular Asian restaurant among celebrities. Founded by Nobu Matsuhisa with Robert De Niro, the empire spans the globe and has hosted virtually every A-lister from Rihanna to the Kardashians. Mr Chow in Beverly Hills is the second most iconic, serving celebrities since 1974.
Where do celebrities eat Chinese food?
Celebrities favor Mr Chow in Beverly Hills and NYC for upscale Chinese dining with signature Beijing duck. Hakkasan in Las Vegas and Miami offers Michelin-starred Cantonese cuisine. For more casual options, celebrities frequent Din Tai Fung for dumplings and Philippe Chow for family-style Chinese.
What Korean BBQ restaurants do celebrities visit?
In Los Angeles, celebrities visit KBBQ spots like Quarters Korean BBQ in Koreatown and Cote Korean Steakhouse for upscale KBBQ. BTS has popularized Korean restaurants globally, with members spotted at traditional spots in LA's Koreatown. Kang Ho-dong Baekjeong is another celebrity favorite.
