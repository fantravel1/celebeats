What is the most popular Italian restaurant among celebrities?

Where do celebrities eat Italian food in New York?
In New York, celebrities favor Carbone for its retro Italian-American cuisine and theatrical service, Via Carota in the West Village for its rustic charm, and Locanda Verde for Robert De Niro's TriBeCa spot. Each offers a different Italian dining experience popular with the A-list crowd.
Which celebrity pasta dishes are famous?
