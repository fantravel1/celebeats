Where do vegan celebrities eat?

Vegan celebrities frequent Crossroads Kitchen in West Hollywood (Joaquin Phoenix's favorite), Gracias Madre for vegan Mexican, Cafe Gratitude in Venice, and Eleven Madison Park in NYC. These restaurants offer upscale plant-based cuisine that attracts A-list vegans like Billie Eilish, Ariana Grande, and Woody Harrelson.

Why Celebrities Go Vegan

The Crossroads Effect

Chef Tal Ronnen founded Crossroads Kitchen after catering Ellen DeGeneres and Portia de Rossi's wedding. The restaurant proved vegan food could be luxurious, attracting A-listers who never considered plant-based dining before.

Tier A

Eleven Madison Park's Revolution

When the three-Michelin-star restaurant went fully plant-based in 2021, it changed fine dining forever. Celebrities who dined there for the wagyu now return for the sunflower butter and vegetable-forward tasting menu.
