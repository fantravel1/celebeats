Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most famous celebrity outdoor dining spot?
The Ivy in Los Angeles is the most iconic celebrity outdoor dining destination. Its white picket fence patio on Robertson Boulevard has been photographed with virtually every A-lister for decades. The paparazzi presence is legendary, making it the go-to spot for celebrities who want to be seen dining al fresco.
Where do celebrities eat outdoors in NYC?
In New York, celebrities favor Via Carota's charming sidewalk tables in the West Village, Sant Ambroeus's European-style terrace, and Balthazar's iconic SoHo sidewalk seating. For rooftop dining, Le Bain at The Standard and The Roof at The Edition are celebrity favorites.
Which LA restaurants have the best celebrity patios?
LA's best celebrity patios include The Ivy's famous white picket fence setting, Chateau Marmont's secluded garden terrace, Nobu Malibu's oceanfront deck, and Soho House West Hollywood's rooftop pool area. Craig's and The Nice Guy also have popular outdoor sections.
