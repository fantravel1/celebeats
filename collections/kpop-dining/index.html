Frequently Asked Questions

Where do BTS members eat in Seoul?
BTS members have been spotted at various Seoul restaurants including Maple Tree House for Korean BBQ, Yoojung Sikdang for traditional Korean food, and various cafes in Gangnam and Itaewon. Jin has mentioned loving Korean BBQ spots, while RM frequents art district cafes.
What are BLACKPINK's favorite restaurants?
BLACKPINK members have different dining preferences. Jennie and Rose have been spotted at trendy cafes and fine dining spots in Seoul and LA. Lisa enjoys Thai restaurants when traveling, while Jisoo favors traditional Korean cuisine. They've been seen at Cote Korean Steakhouse in NYC.
Can fans visit K-Pop idol restaurants in Seoul?
Yes, most restaurants frequented by K-Pop idols are open to the public. Popular spots include Maple Tree House, various Gangnam cafes, and Hongdae restaurants. However, some upscale venues require reservations. Fans should be respectful if they encounter idols dining.
