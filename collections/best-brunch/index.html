Where do celebrities go for brunch?

Top celebrity brunch spots include Sadelle's in NYC and LA, Cecconi's in West Hollywood, Balthazar in SoHo, The Ivy in LA, and Soho House locations. Models and influencers favor avocado toast spots like Cafe Gratitude.

Brunch FAQs

Top spots include Sadelle's (NYC/LA), Cecconi's (WeHo/Miami), Balthazar (NYC), The Ivy (LA), and health-focused spots like Cafe Gratitude for models and fitness enthusiasts.

Popular celebrity brunch orders include: avocado toast, egg white omelets, smoked salmon bagels, acai bowls, fresh-pressed juices, and clean eating options. Many celebrities avoid heavy carbs and alcohol at brunch.
