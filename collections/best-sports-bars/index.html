Famous Athlete Sightings

LeBron at Prime 112

During his Miami Heat years, LeBron was a Prime 112 regular. He'd order multiple entrees including the bone-in ribeye, truffle mac & cheese, and the famous house salad. He continues to visit when in town.

Tom Brady's Strict Diet

Tom Brady famously follows the TB12 Method diet. When dining out, he's been spotted at clean-eating spots and has mentioned enjoying avocado ice cream. He avoids nightshades and processed foods even at restaurants.

Tier A

UFC Fighters at LAVO Vegas

After UFC events, fighters often head to LAVO in Las Vegas for post-fight celebrations. Dana White has been known to host after-parties there, making it a hub for MMA athletes.

Athlete Dining FAQs

NBA players frequently dine at Prime 112 in Miami, 40/40 Club in NYC, Catch LA in Los Angeles, and TAO in various cities. These spots offer late-night service, private areas, and high-protein menu options.

LeBron has been spotted frequently at Prime 112 in Miami, Catch LA in Los Angeles, and various high-end steakhouses. He's also an investor in Blaze Pizza and visits those locations regularly.

Many athletes follow strict diets. Tom Brady avoids nightshades and processed foods. Many NBA players focus on high-protein, clean eating. Restaurants frequented by athletes often accommodate these needs with custom preparations.

NFL players celebrating in Vegas head to LAVO, Marquee, and XS nightclubs. For dining, SW Steakhouse at Wynn and Cut by Wolfgang Puck are popular. After Super Bowls, entire teams have been known to take over LAVO.
