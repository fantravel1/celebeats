Frequently Asked Questions

What wine bars do celebrities frequent?
Celebrities frequent intimate wine bars like Bar Pisellino in NYC's West Village, Domaine LA for natural wines in Los Angeles, and Augustine Wine Bar in Sherman Oaks. These spots offer excellent wine selections, small plates, and the low-key atmosphere celebrities prefer for casual evenings out.
Which restaurants have the best celebrity wine programs?
Restaurants with legendary wine programs popular among celebrities include Eleven Madison Park in NYC, The French Laundry in Napa Valley, and Spago in Beverly Hills. These establishments have award-winning sommeliers and extensive cellars with rare vintages that attract wine-loving celebrities.
Do celebrities prefer natural wine?
Many celebrities have embraced natural wine, frequenting spots like Domaine LA, Bar Bandini, and Helen's Wine Shop in LA, or Ten Bells and Anfora in NYC. Natural wine's health-conscious appeal and connection to sustainable farming attracts wellness-focused celebrities.
