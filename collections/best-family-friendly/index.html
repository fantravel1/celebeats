Family Dining FAQs

Popular LA spots include Malibu Farm for beachside healthy eating, The Ivy for classic lunches, Cecconi's for Italian, Sugar Fish for sushi, and Joan's on Third for casual brunch. These spots offer privacy, healthy options, and welcoming atmospheres for kids.

The Kardashian-Jenner family has been spotted with their kids at Sugar Fish, The Ivy, and Cafe Gratitude. Kourtney Kardashian particularly favors organic and health-conscious restaurants for her children, including plant-based spots.

Many celebrity-frequented restaurants are excellent for families. Spots like Malibu Farm, Serendipity 3, and Joan's on Third specifically cater to families with kid-friendly menus, casual atmospheres, and accommodating staff.

Serendipity 3 is famous for its Frrrozen Hot Chocolate - a blended, creamy chocolate drink that's been a celebrity and tourist favorite since 1954. Many famous families bring their kids specifically for this iconic dessert.
