Tier A

Fashion Week Sightings

Anna Wintour at The Odeon

Anna Wintour has maintained a regular table at The Odeon for decades. She typically orders the same dish and uses the space for pre-NYFW planning meetings and post-show dinners.

Tier A

Supermodels at Indochine

Indochine has been the unofficial after-party spot for models since the 80s. Naomi Campbell, Christy Turlington, and the 90s supers made it legendary. Today's models continue the tradition.

Tier A

Donatella Versace at Paper Moon

Paper Moon in Milan is a fashion week staple. Donatella Versace, Miuccia Prada, and other Italian designers have been spotted during Milan Fashion Week, often with international editors.
