Robert De Niro's Nobu Empire

De Niro helped launch Nobu Matsuhisa's first restaurant in 1994. Today, Nobu has over 50 locations worldwide and has expanded into hotels. The empire is valued at hundreds of millions.

Ryan Gosling's Secret Tagine
