What is the best celebrity steakhouse?

Prime 112 in Miami and Mastro's in Beverly Hills are considered the top celebrity steakhouses. Prime 112 is famous for its truffle mac & cheese and late-night celebrity crowd, while Mastro's is known for its butter cake and power lunches.

Celebrity Steak Orders

LeBron James at Prime 112

LeBron is known for ordering multiple entrees during his Miami Heat era visits. His go-to includes the bone-in ribeye, truffle mac & cheese, and sides for the table.

The Rock's Steaks
