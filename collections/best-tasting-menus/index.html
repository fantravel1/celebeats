Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best tasting menu restaurant celebrities visit?
The French Laundry in Napa Valley is considered the ultimate celebrity tasting menu destination in America. Thomas Keller's three-Michelin-star restaurant offers a 9-course experience at $350+ per person. Eleven Madison Park in NYC and Alinea in Chicago are equally prestigious for multi-course fine dining.
How much do celebrity tasting menus cost?
Celebrity tasting menus range from $200 to $500+ per person before wine pairings. The French Laundry and Eleven Madison Park cost $350+, Alinea ranges from $295-395, and Atelier Crenn is $365. Wine pairings add $150-400. A complete experience for two can exceed $2,000.
How do you get reservations at celebrity tasting menu restaurants?
Most elite tasting menu restaurants release reservations exactly 30-60 days in advance. The French Laundry opens at 10am PT 60 days out. Eleven Madison Park uses Tock. Alinea sells tickets like a show. Many celebrities use concierge services or restaurant connections for access to otherwise impossible reservations.
