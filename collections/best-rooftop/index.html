Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best celebrity rooftop restaurant?
Catch LA in West Hollywood is widely considered the top celebrity rooftop restaurant. With stunning views, excellent seafood, and a strict no-photos policy, it's the go-to for A-listers seeking privacy with their sunset views.
How do I get a reservation at Catch LA?
Reservations at Catch LA are available on Resy. Book 2-4 weeks in advance for weekend dinners. Sunset tables (around 6-7pm) are most coveted. Walk-ins are possible at the bar.
What should I wear to a rooftop restaurant?
Most celebrity rooftop restaurants expect upscale casual to dressy attire. Think fashionable and put-together. Avoid athletic wear, flip-flops, and overly casual clothing.
