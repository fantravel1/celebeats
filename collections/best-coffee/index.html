Where do celebrities get their coffee?

The most popular celebrity coffee spot is Alfred Coffee in LA, where stars like Hailey Bieber, Selena Gomez, and the Kardashians are regulars. In NYC, Sant Ambroeus and Cha Cha Matcha draw the fashion crowd. Blue Bottle and Verve Coffee are favorites among tech and entertainment executives.

Famous Celebrity Coffee Orders

Hailey Bieber - "The Bieber"

Hailey's order at Alfred Coffee became so popular they named it after her: an iced latte with oat milk, vanilla, and a brown sugar espresso. She's credited with making oat milk mainstream in LA.

Coffee FAQs

Alfred Coffee is the undisputed celebrity favorite with locations in Melrose Place, Brentwood, and Studio City. Verve Coffee, Blue Bottle, and Intelligentsia are also popular among A-listers who prefer artisan roasts.

Oat milk lattes dominate celebrity orders, especially iced versions. Matcha lattes are popular with health-conscious stars. Many celebrities avoid sugary drinks and prefer simple espresso-based orders with alternative milks.

The unofficial "Hailey Bieber" drink is an iced latte with oat milk, vanilla syrup, and brown sugar espresso. Her frequent orders of this combination made it so popular that many consider it the signature celebrity coffee order of the 2020s.

Sant Ambroeus serves the fashion elite with Italian espresso. Cha Cha Matcha is popular with wellness influencers. La Colombe's Tribeca location attracts creative industry professionals, while Cafe Gitane in Nolita remains a model favorite.
