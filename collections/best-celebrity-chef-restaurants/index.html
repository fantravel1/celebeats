The best celebrity chef restaurants include Gordon Ramsay's Hell's Kitchen in Las Vegas, Nobu's flagship locations in Malibu and NYC, Wolfgang Puck's Spago in Beverly Hills, and Guy Fieri's Vegas Kitchen. Celebrity Sightings at Chef Restaurants

Nobu Malibu Celebrity Scene

Nobu Malibu is the ultimate celebrity chef restaurant for star sightings. Rihanna practically lives there, ordering the Black Cod Miso. The Kardashians celebrate birthdays here. Beyonce and Jay-Z have been spotted on date nights. The oceanfront deck is paparazzi heaven.

Spago's Oscar Tradition

Wolfgang Puck has catered the Governors Ball after the Oscars for over 25 years. Spago Beverly Hills hosts the official Oscar viewing party and pre-ceremony dinners. The smoked salmon pizza on brioche has been served to every major movie star.

Tier A

Gordon Ramsay's Vegas Kingdom

Gordon Ramsay dominates the Las Vegas dining scene with 7 restaurants. Hell's Kitchen draws celebrity crowds during fight nights - Floyd Mayweather, Conor McGregor, and countless UFC stars dine here. The Beef Wellington is legendary.
