These spots combine world-class cuisine with breathtaking water vistas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best waterfront celebrity restaurant?
Nobu Malibu is widely considered the best waterfront celebrity restaurant. Located directly on Carbon Beach, it offers stunning Pacific Ocean views, world-class Japanese cuisine, and regular A-list sightings including the Kardashians, Leonardo DiCaprio, and Cindy Crawford.
Where do celebrities eat by the ocean?
Celebrities frequent oceanside restaurants like Nobu Malibu, Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, The Surf Club in Miami, and Soho House Malibu. These spots offer stunning water views, excellent cuisine, and the privacy that A-listers require for a relaxing seaside meal.
Which Miami waterfront restaurants do celebrities visit?
Top Miami waterfront celebrity spots include The Surf Club (Thomas Keller's beachfront restaurant), Cipriani Downtown on the Miami River, Nikki Beach for ocean views, and Casa Tua for Mediterranean vibes. During Art Basel and boat shows, these spots are packed with A-listers.
What's the best time to visit waterfront celebrity restaurants?
Sunset is prime time at most waterfront spots for both views and celebrity sightings. For Nobu Malibu, request a 6-7pm reservation for golden hour. Brunch on weekends is also popular at places like Malibu Farm. Book well in advance, especially for summer months.
Do I need a reservation for waterfront celebrity restaurants?
Yes, reservations are essential at most waterfront celebrity restaurants, especially Nobu Malibu and The Surf Club. Book 2-4 weeks ahead for weekend dinners. Some spots like Malibu Farm accept walk-ins for off-peak times. Soho House requires membership.
