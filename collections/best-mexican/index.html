These spots range from casual taco joints to elevated fine dining.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best celebrity Mexican restaurant?
Cosme in NYC (by Enrique Olvera) is widely considered the best high-end Mexican restaurant among celebrities, famous for its duck carnitas and corn husk meringue. In LA, Gracias Madre and Casa Vega are favorites.
Where do celebrities eat Mexican food in Los Angeles?
In LA, celebrities eat Mexican at Gracias Madre for vegan Mexican in West Hollywood, Casa Vega in Sherman Oaks for old-school vibes, Tocaya Organica for healthy options, and Broken Spanish for elevated dining.
