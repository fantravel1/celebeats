Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best omakase restaurant where celebrities eat?
Masa in NYC is considered the ultimate celebrity omakase destination, with prices starting at $600+ per person. Chef Masa Takayama serves the most exclusive sushi experience in America. In LA, n/naka offers a stunning kaiseki experience, while Sushi Ginza Onodera brings Tokyo-level fish quality.
How much does celebrity omakase cost?
Celebrity omakase ranges widely: Masa costs $600-800+ per person, n/naka runs $285+, Sushi Nakazawa is around $180, and Sushi Park offers relative value at $150-200. These prices typically exclude beverages, sake pairings, and gratuity. The most exclusive experiences can exceed $1,000 per person.
What is omakase and why do celebrities prefer it?
Omakase means "I leave it up to you" in Japanese - the chef creates a personalized multi-course sushi experience. Celebrities prefer omakase for its exclusivity, intimate counter seating (8-12 guests), privacy, and the prestige of experiencing the chef's finest seasonal ingredients. It's dining as performance art.
