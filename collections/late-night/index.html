Late Night Celebrity Habits

The Nice Guy Speakeasy

LA's ultimate late-night celebrity spot. The unmarked entrance, private rooms, and 2AM closing time make it perfect for post-awards show dinners and after-party gatherings. Leo DiCaprio, Rihanna, and Drake are regulars.

Miami's Midnight Steaks

Prime 112's kitchen runs until 1AM on weekends, making it the go-to for post-club celebrity dining. After LIV or Story, A-listers head here for truffle mac & cheese and wagyu sliders.

Komodo's Dinner-to-Party Transition

What starts as dinner becomes a party at Komodo. The three-story venue transitions from restaurant to club atmosphere, with celebrities staying from 10PM until close.

Late Night FAQs

Top spots include The Nice Guy (LA), Prime 112 (Miami), Komodo (Miami), Delilah (LA), and Carbone (NYC). These restaurants serve until midnight or later.

Celebrities typically eat late - 9PM to midnight for dinner. Post-event dining often happens between 11PM and 1AM. Early dinners (6-7PM) are rare except for family occasions.
