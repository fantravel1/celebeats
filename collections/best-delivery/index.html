Delivery FAQs

Top celebrity delivery restaurants include Jon & Vinny's for Italian, Sugarfish for sushi, Craig's and Craig's Vegan, Pizzana for pizza, Erewhon for healthy options, and Jitlada for Thai. These restaurants maintain quality during delivery.

Popular Jon & Vinny's orders include the Spicy Fusilli (vodka sauce with calabrian chili), Margherita Pizza, Meatballs, Caesar Salad, and their famous garlic bread. The menu of Italian-American comfort food travels well for delivery.

Sugarfish is the go-to celebrity sushi delivery in LA - their "Trust Me" omakase sets are designed to travel well. Kazunori (same owners) for hand rolls. Nobu offers delivery for special occasions. NYC celebrities order from Blue Ribbon Sushi.

Many celebrities use regular delivery apps like Postmates, DoorDash, and Uber Eats, often under aliases. Some use assistants to order. High-end restaurants often have direct delivery for VIP clients. Privacy-focused services also exist for celebrity clientele.
