Where does the fashion industry eat during Fashion Week?

During Fashion Week, the fashion industry gravitates to specific restaurants in each city. In NYC, Balthazar and Sant Ambroeus are power spots. Paris sees editors at Cafe de Flore and Caviar Kaspia. Milan's Paper Moon is essential, while London's Scott's and The Wolseley host the fashion elite. Models often choose health-conscious spots, while designers celebrate at exclusive venues.

Fashion Week Dining by Occasion

Different occasions call for different restaurants during Fashion Week:

Model Quick Bites

Health-conscious spots like Smile (NYC), Wild & The Moon (Paris) for clean eating between castings and shows.

Editor Power Lunch

Sant Ambroeus, Cafe de Flore, Paper Moon - where deals are made and trends are discussed over espresso.

Designer Celebrations

Post-show dinners at Carbone (NYC), Caviar Kaspia (Paris), Ceresio 7 (Milan) - exclusive and celebratory.

Industry Networking

Hotel bars and restaurant lounges where buyers, press, and PRs mingle: Mercer Hotel, Le Bristol, Bulgari.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do models eat during Fashion Week?
Models during Fashion Week often choose health-conscious restaurants with clean eating options. In NYC, they favor Smile, Cafe Cluny, and Jack's Wife Freda. Many opt for salads, smoothie bowls, and light proteins. However, after shows, celebratory dinners at Carbone or Balthazar are common.
What restaurants do fashion editors prefer?
Fashion editors and Vogue staff frequent classic power lunch spots. In NYC, Sant Ambroeus and Cafe Select are favorites. In Paris, Cafe de Flore and Caviar Kaspia attract the fashion elite. These spots offer the right atmosphere for networking and being seen.
Where do designers celebrate after shows?
After successful shows, designers often host private dinners at exclusive restaurants. In NYC, Carbone and The Polo Bar are popular. In Paris, Caviar Kaspia and L'Avenue see post-show celebrations. Milan's Paper Moon and London's Scott's are designer favorites.
