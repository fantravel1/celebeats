Celebrity Bar Sightings

The Nice Guy Regulars

The Nice Guy is one of the most paparazzi'd spots in LA. Regulars include Leonardo DiCaprio, Rihanna, Drake, and the Kardashians. The Italian supper club vibe and private booths make it perfect for celebrity outings.

Tier A

Delilah's Star Power

Delilah attracts A-listers with its supper club format and live entertainment. Drake, Justin Bieber, and Kendall Jenner have been spotted here. The same owners as The Nice Guy know how to draw celebrities.

Tier A

NYC Cocktail Scene

NYC celebrities favor more low-key spots. Employees Only attracts food and beverage industry celebrities. Death & Co draws craft cocktail enthusiasts. Bemelmans Bar at The Carlyle is old-money elegant.

Tier B

Cocktail Bar FAQs

The Nice Guy and Delilah in West Hollywood are the most popular celebrity bars. No Vacancy in Hollywood offers a speakeasy experience. Tower Bar at Sunset Tower is classic Hollywood. Private clubs like San Vicente Bungalows offer complete privacy.

Employees Only in the West Village is legendary among industry insiders. Death & Co pioneered the craft cocktail revival. Attaboy offers bespoke drinks with no menu. Bemelmans Bar at The Carlyle attracts old Hollywood glamour seekers.

San Vicente Bungalows is the most exclusive with strict membership and no-photo policies. The Nice Guy is highly exclusive for non-members. Soho House locations require membership. Delilah's private rooms are reserved for VIPs.

Many celebrities drink tequila or mezcal cocktails - George Clooney's Casamigos made tequila trendy. Vodka sodas remain popular for low-calorie options. Champagne and wine are common. Some celebrities own their own spirits brands.
