What makes Alo Restaurant special?

Alo holds 2 Michelin stars, making it Toronto's highest-rated fine dining destination. Chef Patrick Kriss crafts refined French cuisine with Canadian ingredients in an intimate 45-seat dining room. The multi-course tasting menu changes seasonally and showcases modern French technique at its finest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Alo holds 2 Michelin stars, making it Toronto's highest-rated restaurant. It received its stars when Michelin launched in Toronto in 2022 and has maintained them since.

Reservations open on Tock exactly 28 days in advance at 10am ET. They sell out within minutes. Set alerts and be ready to book immediately when they open. Credit card required to hold.

The tasting menu is approximately $250-300 CAD per person. With wine pairings, expect $400-500+ per person before tax and gratuity. It's a splurge-worthy special occasion destination.

Smart casual to business casual is appropriate. Most guests dress up for the occasion - it's a 2 Michelin star restaurant after all. No shorts or athletic wear.
