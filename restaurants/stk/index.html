What is STK Steakhouse known for?

STK is the steakhouse-meets-nightclub concept that revolutionized dining. Famous for its in-house DJ, chic lounge atmosphere, and prime cuts of beef, STK attracts celebrities, models, and a fashion-forward crowd. It's positioned as a female-friendly steakhouse alternative to the traditional boys' club steakhouse vibe.

Frequently Asked Questions

STK combines a high-end steakhouse with nightclub atmosphere. It features an in-house DJ, chic decor, and scene-y vibe that attracts celebrities and a younger, fashion-forward crowd.

STK has 15+ locations worldwide including NYC (Meatpacking and Midtown), Miami, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, Atlanta, London, Dubai, and Ibiza. The flagship is in NYC's Meatpacking District.

Upscale casual to dressy. Think nightclub-meets-steakhouse. No athletic wear, flip flops, or overly casual attire. People dress up, especially on weekends.

Expect $100-200+ per person for dinner. Steaks range from $50-150+. The experience is premium pricing for the steakhouse-lounge atmosphere.
