What celebrities go to Catch LA?

Catch LA is frequented by the Kardashians, Gigi and Bella Hadid, David Beckham, Jennifer Lopez, and many Instagram influencers. The stunning rooftop and photogenic food make it a social media favorite.

The Experience

Catch LA is designed for Instagram. The flower wall entrance, hanging plant installations, and panoramic LA views create endless photo opportunities. The seafood-focused menu features dishes like truffle sashimi and whole fish presentations that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

Catch LA FAQs

The Kardashians, Gigi and Bella Hadid, David Beckham, Jennifer Lopez, and many Instagram influencers frequent Catch LA.

Catch LA accepts reservations through Resy. Book 2-3 weeks in advance for weekend dinners, especially for rooftop seating. Request a rooftop table specifically for the best experience.

Catch LA has an upscale casual dress code. Think stylish LA - designer casual, fashionable outfits. Avoid athletic wear. Many guests dress up, especially for weekend dinners.
