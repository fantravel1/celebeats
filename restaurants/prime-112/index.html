The Vibe

Prime 112 occupies a historic 1915 Art Deco building on Ocean Drive. The interior features dark wood, white tablecloths, and a buzzing energy. The late-night hours attract the post-club crowd, and the outdoor patio offers prime people-watching.

Prime 112 FAQs

LeBron James, Drake, DJ Khaled, Bad Bunny, Jennifer Lopez, Dwyane Wade, and countless other A-listers. It's been Miami's celebrity steakhouse since 2004.
