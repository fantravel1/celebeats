Stubborn Seed FAQs

Chrissy Teigen, John Legend, Gordon Ramsay, Guy Fieri, David Chang, and numerous Top Chef alumni have dined at Stubborn Seed. The restaurant attracts culinary-focused celebrities who appreciate chef-driven creativity.

Chef Jeremy Ford, winner of Top Chef Season 13, is the chef and owner. He opened Stubborn Seed in 2017 to showcase his progressive American cooking style with creative techniques and artistic presentations.

The tasting menu is highly recommended for the full Stubborn Seed experience. It showcases Chef Ford's creativity and technique across multiple courses. Allow 2-3 hours for the experience.

Book via Resy or call (786) 322-5211. Reservations are essential, especially for weekend dinner. Book 1-2 weeks ahead for prime times, further during Wine & Food Festival.
