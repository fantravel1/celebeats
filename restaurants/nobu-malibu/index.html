What is Nobu Malibu's signature dish?

Nobu's signature dish is the Black Cod with Miso - a sweet miso-marinated black cod that's become iconic worldwide. Other celebrity favorites include Yellowtail Jalapeno, Rock Shrimp Tempura, and the New Style Sashimi.
