The Experience

Petit Trois is a tiny 26-seat counter-only bistro that transports you to Paris. No tables, no reservations - just belly up to the bar and order classic French comfort food. The intimate space means you might find yourself sitting next to anyone from a celebrity to a line cook on their day off.

Petit Trois FAQs

Petit Trois is owned by renowned French chef Ludo Lefebvre, who opened the original location in 2014. Known for his appearances on Top Chef and his innovative French cuisine, Lefebvre created Petit Trois as a love letter to classic French bistro cooking.

Petit Trois is famous for its Double Cheeseburger with Burgundy truffle and caramelized onion, the perfect Omelette made with boursin and chives, and authentic French onion soup. The tiny counter-service restaurant embodies Parisian bistro culture.

No, the original Petit Trois on Highland Ave does not take reservations. It operates on a first-come, first-served basis with counter seating only. Petit Trois Le Valley in Sherman Oaks does accept reservations for a full-service dining experience.

First-timers must try the Double Cheeseburger and the Omelette - both are legendary. Add the Escargot and French Onion Soup for a complete experience. Finish with a Chocolate Mousse or the Creme Caramel.
