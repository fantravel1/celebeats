What is Estiatorio Milos Las Vegas?

Estiatorio Milos at The Venetian is a premier Greek seafood restaurant where guests select their fish from an impressive ice display featuring Mediterranean catches flown in daily from Greece. The cooking philosophy is simple: pristine ingredients prepared with Greek olive oil, fresh lemon, and herbs. It's the Strip's answer to Mykonos waterfront dining.

The Fish Display Experience

At Milos, you choose your dinner from a stunning display of whole fish on ice. From lavraki (sea bass) to fagri (red snapper), each is priced by weight and grilled to perfection. It's interactive, theatrical, and guarantees the freshest seafood in Vegas.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Estiatorio Milos special?
Estiatorio Milos flies fresh fish daily from Greece and the Mediterranean. Guests select their fish from a stunning ice display, and it's prepared simply - grilled with Greek olive oil and lemon. The quality and freshness are unmatched on the Strip.
How does pricing work at Milos?
Most fish at Milos is priced by the pound, typically ranging from $50-120 per pound depending on the species. The server will weigh your selection and confirm the price before cooking. A whole fish typically serves 2-3 people.
What is the Milos Special?
The Milos Special is the restaurant's signature appetizer featuring paper-thin slices of zucchini and eggplant, layered with creamy feta cheese and drizzled with honey. It's light, crispy, and the perfect start to any meal.
