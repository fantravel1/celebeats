The Vibe

Cecconi's occupies a prime oceanfront position at Soho Beach House. The elegant interior features Venetian-inspired decor with green and white striped awnings, while the outdoor terrace offers direct beach views. The crowd is creative, fashionable, and deliberately low-key despite its A-list composition.
