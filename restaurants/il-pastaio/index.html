The Paparazzi Scene

Il Pastaio's Canon Drive patio is Beverly Hills' most reliable celebrity photo location. Paparazzi stake out the restaurant daily, making it both a blessing (guaranteed celebrity sightings) and a choice (celebrities who dine here know they'll be photographed). The outdoor seating, natural lighting, and high foot traffic create the perfect conditions.
