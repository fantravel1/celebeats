The Experience

Crossroads Kitchen features a warm, sophisticated dining room with exposed brick and an open kitchen. The atmosphere is upscale but welcoming, proving that vegan dining can be elegant. Perfect for business dinners, date nights, or anyone curious about high-end plant-based cuisine.

Crossroads Kitchen FAQs

Crossroads Kitchen is the top celebrity vegan restaurant in LA. Regular diners include Beyonce, Oprah Winfrey, Ellen DeGeneres, Portia de Rossi, Ariana Grande, Miley Cyrus, Natalie Portman, and many plant-based celebrities and health-conscious stars.

Yes, Crossroads Kitchen is 100% vegan. Every dish on the menu is plant-based, including their famous artichoke oysters, Impossible meatballs, and house-made pasta. They use no animal products in any dishes, making it safe for strict vegans.

Crossroads Kitchen was founded by celebrity chef Tal Ronnen, who has cooked for Oprah Winfrey, Ellen DeGeneres, and catered events for major celebrities. He's known as one of America's top vegan chefs and authored "The Conscious Cook" cookbook.

Absolutely! Crossroads Kitchen is designed to satisfy everyone, not just vegans. The Mediterranean-inspired dishes are so flavorful that many meat-eaters don't even notice they're eating plant-based. The Impossible Bolognese and pizzas are particularly convincing.
