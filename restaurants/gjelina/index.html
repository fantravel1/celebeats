The Experience

Gjelina offers an industrial-chic atmosphere with exposed brick, reclaimed wood, and an open kitchen. The restaurant has a famous no-substitutions policy and features communal seating alongside intimate tables. Next door, GTA (Gjelina Take Away) offers the same menu for takeout.

Gjelina FAQs

Gjelina is frequented by celebrities including Bradley Cooper, Julia Roberts, Leonardo DiCaprio, Jessica Alba, Chris Hemsworth, and Gwyneth Paltrow. Its location on Abbot Kinney Boulevard makes it a favorite for Venice-based celebrities and visitors alike.

Gjelina is famous for its wood-fired pizzas, seasonal vegetable dishes, and farm-to-table California cuisine. The restaurant is known for its no-substitutions policy, communal seating, and the legendary Gjelina pizza with burrata, bacon, and arugula.

Gjelina accepts reservations via Resy. Book 1-2 weeks in advance for dinner, especially on weekends. Walk-ins are welcome at the bar and communal tables. The restaurant also has a takeaway window called GTA (Gjelina Take Away) next door.

Gjelina has a relaxed, California-cool vibe with an industrial-chic interior featuring exposed brick, reclaimed wood, and an open kitchen. The atmosphere is casual yet sophisticated, perfect for both date nights and group dinners.
