What is Hell's Kitchen Las Vegas famous for?

Hell's Kitchen Las Vegas is famous for Beef Wellington (Gordon Ramsay's signature dish), Pan-Seared Scallops, and Sticky Toffee Pudding. The restaurant recreates the iconic TV show atmosphere with red and blue team kitchens visible from the dining room.

Frequently Asked Questions

Gordon Ramsay visits Hell's Kitchen Las Vegas occasionally but is not there daily. The restaurant is designed to recreate the TV show experience with the same recipes and high-energy atmosphere.

Must-orders include the Beef Wellington (signature dish), Pan-Seared Scallops, Crispy Skin Salmon, and Sticky Toffee Pudding for dessert. These are all dishes featured on the TV show.

Expect to spend $80-150 per person. The Beef Wellington is around $65. Prix fixe menus are available and offer good value for experiencing multiple signature dishes.
