Frequently Asked Questions

Sexy Fish features stunning interiors with two bronze mermaid sculptures by Damien Hirst, crocodile lamps by Frank Gehry, and an underwater ceiling installation by Michael Roberts. It's as much an art gallery as a restaurant, open until 2am.

Japanese-Asian fusion cuisine with a focus on sushi, sashimi, and robata grill items. Signature dishes include the miso black cod, wagyu beef, and extensive raw bar. The cocktails are equally impressive.

The glamorous setting, late-night hours until 2am, stunning art, and "see and be seen" atmosphere make it a celebrity favorite. The Berkeley Square location is prime Mayfair, attracting a well-heeled, fashionable crowd.

Glamorous smart casual. Think designer fashion, heels, and statement pieces. It's a "dress to impress" venue. No sportswear. The crowd gets more dressed up as the night progresses.
