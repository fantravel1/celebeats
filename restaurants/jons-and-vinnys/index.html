The Experience

Jon & Vinny's has a bright, casual atmosphere with white tile walls and an open kitchen. The vibe is trendy but unpretentious - you might wait next to a Kardashian for a table. All-day dining means breakfast pizza is totally acceptable, and late-night pasta runs are encouraged.

Jon & Vinny's FAQs

Jon and Vinny's is one of LA's most celebrity-packed restaurants. Regular diners include Drake, The Weeknd, Hailey Bieber, Justin Bieber, Kendall Jenner, Kylie Jenner, Emily Ratajkowski, Post Malone, and many more A-list celebrities and influencers.

Jon and Vinny's is famous for its Spicy Vodka Rigatoni, Fairfax Pizza (pepperoni with hot honey), and Cacio e Pepe. The restaurant is also known for its trendy atmosphere, all-day dining from breakfast through dinner, and being a constant celebrity hotspot on Fairfax.

Jon and Vinny's is owned by chefs Jon Shook and Vinny Dotolo, who also own Animal, Son of a Gun, and several other popular LA restaurants. They've built a mini-empire of trendy LA dining destinations loved by celebrities and food enthusiasts alike.

Reservations through Resy are highly recommended, especially for dinner. Walk-ins are accepted but expect a wait, particularly on weekends. Breakfast and early lunch are easier to get in without a reservation. The Brentwood location tends to be slightly less crowded.
