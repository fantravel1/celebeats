The Vibe

KYU occupies a converted warehouse in Wynwood with high ceilings, exposed brick, and an open kitchen centered around a wood-burning grill. The space feels industrial yet warm, with communal energy and smoke-scented air. The patio offers prime people-watching in the heart of the arts district.
