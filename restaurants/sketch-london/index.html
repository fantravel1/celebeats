What is Sketch London famous for?

Sketch is famous for its Pink Gallery Room - one of the most Instagrammed restaurants in the world. The venue features egg-shaped toilet pods, whimsical David Shrigley art, and serves London's most coveted afternoon tea. The Lecture Room & Library holds 2 Michelin stars for fine French cuisine.
