What is Joe's Stone Crab famous for?

Joe's Stone Crab is a 110+ year-old Miami Beach institution famous for its jumbo stone crab claws served chilled with mustard sauce, the best key lime pie in Florida, crispy hash browns, and its legendary no-reservations policy. Presidents, athletes, and celebrities have waited in line like everyone else since 1913.
