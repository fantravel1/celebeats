What is Catch Steak?

Catch Steak is the steakhouse spinoff from EMM Group (creators of Catch LA and Catch NYC). Located in the Meatpacking District, it offers premium dry-aged steaks, an extensive wine list, and the same celebrity magnetism as its seafood sibling. The see-and-be-seen atmosphere attracts A-listers nightly.

Tier A: Restaurant Verified

EMM Group Empire

Catch Steak is part of the EMM Group hospitality empire that includes Catch (LA, NYC, Las Vegas), Catch Steak, and other celebrity hotspots. The group has mastered the art of creating restaurants that attract both A-list celebrities and their fans.

Frequently Asked Questions

What celebrities go to Catch Steak?
Catch Steak attracts A-list celebrities including the Kardashians, Drake, Justin Bieber, Gigi Hadid, and many sports stars. As part of the EMM Group, it shares the celebrity clientele of Catch LA and NYC.
How do I get a reservation at Catch Steak?
Reservations are available on Resy. For weekend dinners, book 2-3 weeks in advance. Weekday lunches are easier to secure. Walk-ins may be possible at the bar on slower nights.
What should I order at Catch Steak?
The dry-aged ribeye is the signature, and the truffle mac & cheese is a must-order side. For a splurge, the Wagyu tomahawk for sharing is unforgettable.
