Why is Nobu Tokyo a celebrity magnet?

Nobu Tokyo offers the trusted Nobu experience that celebrities love worldwide - consistent quality, discrete service, and familiar menu. Co-owned by Robert De Niro, the restaurant attracts visiting Hollywood stars who know they'll find their favorite dishes from LA, NY, or London.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Black Cod with Miso is Nobu's most famous dish - sweet miso-marinated black cod that's been copied worldwide. Other signatures include Yellowtail Jalapeno and Rock Shrimp Tempura with spicy creamy sauce.

Nobu Tokyo attracts major celebrities visiting Japan including Robert De Niro (co-owner), Leonardo DiCaprio, Madonna, Tom Cruise, and numerous international stars who seek the familiar Nobu experience while in Tokyo.

Expect to spend 15,000-40,000 yen ($120-300 USD) per person depending on your order. The omakase menus range from 18,000-35,000 yen. A la carte allows more budget flexibility.

Nobu Tokyo is part of the Nobu global chain co-owned by Chef Nobu Matsuhisa and Robert De Niro. Matsuhisa restaurants (like the original in Beverly Hills) are Chef Nobu's personal restaurants with a similar but distinct menu.
