Why is Chiltern Firehouse so popular with celebrities?

Chiltern Firehouse offers discrete private dining areas, a stunning historic setting, exceptional food by Nuno Mendes, and impeccable service. The attached hotel allows celebrities to arrive privately, and the restaurant has a strict no-photography policy that protects VIP guests.
