The Vibe

Boia De is a converted storefront in a strip mall, intentionally humble and focused entirely on the food. The 30-seat space features an open kitchen where husband-wife team Luciana Giangrandi and Alex Meyer work their magic. No pretense, no velvet ropes - just exceptional Italian cooking.

Boia De FAQs

Martha Stewart, Stanley Tucci, Questlove, and numerous celebrity chefs have sought out Boia De. The restaurant attracts those who prioritize culinary excellence over scene and celebrity treatment.

Boia De has only 30 seats and releases reservations via Resy. Multiple James Beard nominations, critical acclaim from every major food publication, and word-of-mouth have created extraordinary demand for very limited supply.

Reservations release on Resy typically 2-4 weeks in advance. Set a notification and be ready exactly when they drop. Alternatively, try for bar seating which is sometimes available for walk-ins.

Order everything. Start with the burrata toast, try at least two pasta dishes, and get whatever protein catches your eye. The menu is small and seasonal - trust whatever they're making that night.
