What celebrities eat at Mr. Chow?

Mr. Chow has been the celebrity Chinese restaurant since 1968. Regular guests include the Kardashian family, Beyonce, Jay-Z, Rihanna, Leonardo DiCaprio, and countless A-listers. It's where Hollywood royalty has celebrated for over 50 years.

The Legacy

Michael Chow opened the first Mr. Chow in London in 1968, revolutionizing how Chinese food was served in the West. He brought Beijing cuisine to an elevated, artistic setting and attracted the era's biggest celebrities from day one.

Historical Celebrity Guests

Over 56 years, Mr. Chow has hosted nearly every major celebrity: Andy Warhol, Mick Jagger, Jack Nicholson, Elizabeth Taylor, David Bowie, Madonna, Michael Jackson, and generations of Hollywood royalty. The walls feature artwork from the celebrity art collection.
