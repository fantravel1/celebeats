Frequently Asked Questions

Craig's is frequented by numerous A-list celebrities including Leonardo DiCaprio, Kim Kardashian, Rihanna, Jennifer Lopez, Justin Bieber, and many other Hollywood stars. It's known as one of LA's most consistent celebrity hotspots.

Reservations at Craig's can be made by calling the restaurant at (310) 276-1900. Due to high demand, reservations should be made well in advance, especially for weekend evenings. Walk-ins are occasionally accommodated at the bar.

Craig's Famous Chicken (brick-pressed half chicken) is the restaurant's signature dish and a celebrity favorite. Other popular items include the Crispy Artichoke Hearts, Vegan Bolognese, and the Filet Mignon.

Craig's is considered upscale casual with entrees ranging from $28-$65. The average dinner for two without alcohol runs approximately $150-200. The price range is comparable to other high-end LA restaurants.
