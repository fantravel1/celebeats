What is The Polo Lounge known for?

The Polo Lounge is Hollywood's most legendary restaurant, operating since 1941 at the iconic Beverly Hills Hotel (the "Pink Palace"). Famous for Booth #1 - the most coveted seat in Hollywood - classic American cuisine, and being where entertainment deals are made. Green and white banana leaf decor is iconic.
