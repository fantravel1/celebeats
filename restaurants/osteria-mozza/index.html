The Experience

Osteria Mozza features a warm, rustic Italian atmosphere with an open kitchen. The famous mozzarella bar anchors the dining room where Nancy Silverton personally crafts seasonal mozzarella preparations. The space feels like a sophisticated Italian farmhouse with exposed brick and warm lighting.

Osteria Mozza FAQs

Osteria Mozza was founded by acclaimed chef Nancy Silverton in partnership with Mario Batali and Joe Bastianich in 2007. Nancy Silverton continues to run the restaurant and is known for her innovative mozzarella bar concept and James Beard Award-winning cuisine.

Osteria Mozza is famous for its mozzarella bar featuring fresh burrata and handmade mozzarella with seasonal accompaniments. The restaurant is also celebrated for its handmade pasta, wood-fired dishes, and the legendary butterscotch budino dessert.

Osteria Mozza attracts food-loving celebrities including Gwyneth Paltrow, Chris Martin, Jessica Alba, Jimmy Kimmel, Chrissy Teigen, and John Legend. It's a favorite among chefs and serious food enthusiasts in LA.

Osteria Mozza accepts reservations through OpenTable and by phone at (323) 297-0100. Book 1-2 weeks in advance for weekend dinners. The mozzarella bar accepts walk-ins on a first-come, first-served basis.
