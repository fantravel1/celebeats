What is The Wolseley famous for?

The Wolseley is famous for being London's premier power breakfast destination, set in a stunning 1920s Art Deco former Wolseley car showroom. It's known for its Viennese cafe atmosphere, celebrity clientele, and classic European dishes like Eggs Benedict and Wiener Schnitzel. From Chris Corbin and Jeremy King, it's where London's elite start their day.
