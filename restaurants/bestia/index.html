The Experience

Housed in a 6,500-square-foot converted warehouse, Bestia features soaring ceilings, an open kitchen, and industrial-chic decor. The energy is electric with a see-and-be-seen vibe. Chef Ori Menashe runs the savory kitchen while pastry chef Genevieve Gergis creates stunning desserts.

Bestia FAQs

Bestia attracts celebrities including Chrissy Teigen, John Legend, Emma Stone, Ryan Gosling, Mindy Kaling, and Kobe Bryant (was a regular). The restaurant's energetic atmosphere and exceptional food make it a favorite for celebrity date nights and celebrations.

Bestia is famous for its house-made pasta (especially the spaghetti rustichella), in-house charcuterie program, whole-animal butchery, and wood-fired dishes. The industrial warehouse setting with an open kitchen creates an electric dining atmosphere.

Bestia reservations are notoriously competitive. Book via Resy exactly 30 days in advance when reservations open at 10am PT. Weekend reservations sell out within minutes. Weeknight dining is easier to secure with 2-3 weeks notice.

Bestia consistently ranks among LA's best restaurants since opening in 2012. The combination of exceptional house-made pasta, creative Italian dishes, impressive charcuterie, and vibrant atmosphere justifies its reputation and makes it worth the reservation effort.
