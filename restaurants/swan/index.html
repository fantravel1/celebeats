Does Pharrell Williams own Swan Miami?

Yes, Pharrell Williams co-owns Swan in partnership with David Grutman, the nightlife mogul behind LIV, Story, and Komodo. The restaurant opened in 2018 in Miami's Design District and has become the epicenter of celebrity dining in the art and fashion neighborhood.

The Vibe

Ken Fulk's jaw-dropping design features pink interiors, tropical plants, and a massive swan sculpture. The energy shifts from elegant dinner to late-night party on weekends. The adjacent Bar Bevy offers a more intimate cocktail experience. Dress stylishly - it's the Design District after all.

Swan Miami FAQs

Yes, Pharrell co-owns Swan with David Grutman (the nightlife mogul behind LIV and Komodo). It opened in 2018 and has become the Design District's top celebrity spot.

Swan is known for its stunning Ken Fulk interiors (pink walls, tropical plants, swan sculpture), Mediterranean cuisine, late-night scene, and constant celebrity sightings.

Stylish casual to dressed up. It's the Design District, so people dress fashionably. No athletic wear or flip flops. Think "going out" attire, especially on weekends.

Expect $75-150 per person for dinner with drinks. It's more accessible than some Miami celebrity spots, but late-night bottle service can run much higher.
