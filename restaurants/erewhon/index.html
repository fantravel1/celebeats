Why is Erewhon the celebrity grocery store?

Erewhon Market has become LA's unofficial celebrity headquarters for wellness and organic shopping. The luxury market is famous for its celebrity smoothie collaborations - including the viral Hailey Bieber Strawberry Glaze - and being where A-listers like Kendall Jenner, Bella Hadid, and Gwyneth Paltrow regularly shop. It's the ultimate "see and be seen" spot for health-conscious celebrities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which celebrities shop at Erewhon?
Erewhon is essentially the celebrity grocery store of Los Angeles. Regular shoppers include Hailey Bieber (who has her own smoothie), Kourtney Kardashian (Poosh collab smoothie), Kendall Jenner, Bella Hadid, Emma Chamberlain, Jake Gyllenhaal, Gwyneth Paltrow, and virtually every health-conscious celebrity in LA.
What is the Hailey Bieber Erewhon smoothie?
The Hailey Bieber 'Strawberry Glaze Skin' smoothie became a viral sensation in 2023. It contains strawberries, dates, avocado, maple syrup, coconut cream, vanilla, and Hailey's skincare brand Rhode's Peptide Glazing Fluid collagen. It costs around $18-20 and regularly sells out.
Why is Erewhon so expensive?
Erewhon focuses on premium organic, biodynamic, and locally-sourced products. They have extremely strict quality standards, refusing to carry products with certain ingredients. The smoothie bar uses high-quality supplements and superfoods. The premium pricing reflects their curated selection and the 'see and be seen' factor.
Which Erewhon location is best for celebrity sightings?
The Calabasas location is popular with the Kardashian-Jenner family, while the Beverly Hills and West Hollywood stores see frequent celebrity traffic. The Silver Lake location attracts a more indie celebrity crowd. Weekday mornings tend to have the most sightings as celebrities grab smoothies during their daily routines.
