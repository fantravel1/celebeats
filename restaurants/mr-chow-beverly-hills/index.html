What is Mr Chow Beverly Hills famous for?

Mr Chow Beverly Hills is the original celebrity Chinese restaurant, open since 1974. Famous for Beijing Duck carved tableside, hand-pulled noodles made to order in the dining room, and a Champagne-fueled atmosphere that has attracted every major celebrity for five decades. It's where deals are made and Hollywood royalty gathers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Mr Chow Beverly Hills is famous for its Beijing Duck carved tableside, hand-pulled noodles made to order, Champagne lifestyle, and being the original celebrity Chinese restaurant since 1974.

Michael Chow (born Zhou Yinghua), a British-Chinese restaurateur and artist, founded Mr Chow. He opened London in 1968, Beverly Hills in 1974, and New York in 1979.

Expect to spend $100-175+ per person. Beijing Duck is around $98, and sharing dishes family-style is recommended. It's a special occasion destination.

Must-orders include Beijing Duck, hand-pulled noodles (for the show), Green Prawns, and Chicken Satay. Many regulars let the staff order for them.
