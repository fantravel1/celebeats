The Vibe

Michael Mina occupies a refined space within the iconic Fontainebleau, Miami's most legendary hotel. The dining room features warm woods, soft lighting, and an intimate atmosphere suited for special occasions. Floor-to-ceiling windows and impeccable service complete the experience.
