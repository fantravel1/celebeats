Giorgio Baldi FAQs

Giorgio Baldi is frequented by Adele, Beyonce, Jay-Z, Kim Kardashian, Jennifer Lawrence, Taylor Swift, Rihanna, and many other A-list celebrities. It's considered the top celebrity restaurant in Los Angeles.

Giorgio Baldi is famous for its handmade pasta, particularly the spaghetti with sea urchin (ricci di mare). The intimate 12-table restaurant is also known for its strict no-photo policy and discreet celebrity service.

Giorgio Baldi accepts reservations by phone only at (310) 573-1660. Book 2-3 weeks in advance for weekend dinners. Weeknight reservations are easier to secure. The restaurant has only 12 tables, so availability is limited.

Giorgio Baldi has an upscale casual dress code. Smart casual to elegant attire is appropriate. Avoid athletic wear, shorts, and flip-flops. Many guests dress up for the experience.
