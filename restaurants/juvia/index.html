The Vibe

Perched on the 9th floor of a Herzog & de Meuron-designed parking garage, Juvia offers breathtaking 360-degree views of Miami Beach. The sleek, modern space features indoor and outdoor seating, a lively bar scene, and impeccable people-watching. Sunset is the prime time to arrive.

Juvia FAQs

Kim Kardashian, David Beckham, Victoria Beckham, Bad Bunny, A-Rod, Pharrell Williams, and Gigi Hadid are among the many celebrities who frequent Juvia. The rooftop setting makes it a top choice during Art Basel and Miami events.

Juvia is famous for its unique Japanese-French-Peruvian fusion cuisine, stunning rooftop views, and celebrity clientele. Signature dishes include crispy yellowtail, truffle pizza, and Wagyu beef tataki.

Book via OpenTable or Resy, or call (305) 763-8272. Reservations are essential, especially for weekend dinners and during Art Basel. Request outdoor seating for the best experience.

Juvia has an upscale casual dress code. Think Miami chic - stylish but not overly formal. No swimwear or flip-flops. Most guests dress to impress, especially on weekend evenings.
