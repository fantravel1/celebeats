What is Beauty & Essex's hidden entrance?

Beauty & Essex is hidden behind a functioning pawn shop. Walk through the pawn shop (which actually sells items - jewelry, watches, guitars), and you'll find a door that leads to a massive, glamorous restaurant. The concept pays homage to Prohibition-era speakeasies while the pawn shop serves as a legitimate business and clever disguise. It's the perfect Instagram moment before dinner.

The Experience

What to Expect

The Entrance

You'll walk into what looks like an ordinary pawn shop. Browse the jewelry, guitars, and random items (they're real and for sale). Then head to the back where the host will welcome you into the secret restaurant. It never gets old.

The Atmosphere

Once inside, prepare for dramatic glamour. Crystal chandeliers, pearl curtains, red velvet, and gold accents create an opulent speakeasy vibe. Multiple rooms offer different moods - from intimate booths to the energetic bar area. NYC has a more eclectic Lower East Side edge; LA leans Hollywood Art Deco.

The Dining Style

This is social dining at its best. Order 3-4 dishes per person and share everything family-style. Start with the grilled cheese dumplings (mandatory), add some tacos and salads, share a protein, and finish with the cookie skillet. Great for groups and conversation.

Late Night

Like its TAO Group siblings, Beauty & Essex gets more energetic as the night goes on. DJs spin on weekends, the bar gets lively, and it transitions from restaurant to lounge scene. Stay for the full experience.
