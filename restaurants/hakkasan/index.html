What is Hakkasan known for?

Hakkasan is the world's most acclaimed luxury Chinese restaurant group, known for Michelin-starred modern Cantonese cuisine in stunning, nightclub-integrated venues. Famous for Peking Duck, signature Crispy Duck Salad, and world-class dim sum. The Las Vegas venue at MGM Grand is legendary for combining fine dining with one of the world's top nightclubs featuring superstar DJs.
