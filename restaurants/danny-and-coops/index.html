What is Danny & Coop's?

Danny & Coop's is a Philly cheesesteak shop in New York City co-owned by Oscar-nominated actor Bradley Cooper (the "Coop") and his partner Danny DiGiampietro. Cooper, a proud Philadelphia native, brought his hometown's iconic sandwich to NYC in 2024. The shop went mega-viral on TikTok with every food influencer in the city covering the celebrity cheesesteak phenomenon. Authentic Philly-style, no frills, just perfect sandwiches.

Celebrity Owner: Bradley Cooper

The Oscar-nominated actor and Philly native is the "Coop" in Danny & Coop's. He's been spotted working behind the counter and serving customers - yes, really. A true passion project bringing his hometown flavors to NYC.

The Classic Cheesesteak

Thinly sliced ribeye, melted American cheese (or whiz), onions optional ("wit or witout"). The authentic Philly way - just like Bradley Cooper grew up eating in Philadelphia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bradley Cooper really involved with Danny & Coop's?
Yes! Bradley Cooper, a proud Philadelphia native, partnered with Danny DiGiampietro to open Danny & Coop's. The "Coop" in the name refers to Cooper himself. He's been spotted at the shop multiple times and is actively involved in the business, bringing his hometown cheesesteak passion to NYC.
Why did Danny & Coop's go viral on TikTok?
Danny & Coop's exploded on TikTok in Summer 2024 when food influencers discovered Bradley Cooper's cheesesteak shop. Videos of the A-list actor serving customers, the authentic Philly-style sandwiches, and the celebrity buzz created a perfect viral storm. Every NYC food TikToker had to cover it.
What does "wit" and "witout" mean?
Classic Philly lingo! "Wit" means with fried onions, "witout" means without. When ordering in Philly (or at Danny & Coop's), you'd say "cheesesteak whiz wit" for a cheesesteak with Cheez Whiz and onions. It's part of the authentic experience Bradley Cooper grew up with.
