What makes Nobu Downtown special?

Nobu Downtown is Robert De Niro's flagship Nobu location in TriBeCa, featuring Japanese-Peruvian cuisine in a stunning David Rockwell-designed space. As the largest Nobu in the world, it's a power dining destination where celebrities, finance moguls, and cultural icons gather for the legendary Black Cod Miso and innovative omakase experiences.

The De Niro Connection

Robert De Niro partnered with Chef Nobu Matsuhisa and Drew Nieporent to open the original Nobu in 1994. The TriBeCa location—De Niro's home neighborhood—has become his unofficial dining room. It's not uncommon to spot the legendary actor at his regular table, sometimes greeting famous friends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who owns Nobu Downtown?
Nobu Downtown is co-owned by Chef Nobu Matsuhisa, actor Robert De Niro, and restaurateur Drew Nieporent. De Niro has been instrumental in the Nobu empire since 1994.
What celebrities eat at Nobu Downtown?
Nobu Downtown regularly hosts Robert De Niro, Beyoncé and Jay-Z, Leonardo DiCaprio, Rihanna, Taylor Swift, and countless entertainment industry figures.
How do I get a reservation at Nobu Downtown?
Book via Resy 2-4 weeks in advance for dinner. Lunch is easier to secure. The bar area accepts walk-ins for the full menu.
