What is the Lake of Dreams show at SW Steakhouse?

SW Steakhouse features lakeside seating overlooking Wynn's Lake of Dreams, a spectacular water show with puppetry, holograms, and music that occurs every 30 minutes in the evening. Patio tables offer the best views of this unique Vegas experience.
