Why is Bar Pitti famous for celebrity sightings?

Bar Pitti is famous because it's the great equalizer - everyone waits in line, from A-listers to tourists. The sidewalk tables on Sixth Avenue create prime people-watching, and the no-reservations, cash-only policy keeps it authentic. Celebrities love that it's unpretentious yet glamorous.
