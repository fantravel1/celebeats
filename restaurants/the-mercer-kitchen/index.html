Why is The Mercer Kitchen a celebrity hotspot?

The Mercer Kitchen is a celebrity magnet thanks to its location inside The Mercer Hotel, where A-listers frequently stay. The underground setting provides privacy, while Jean-Georges Vongerichten's sophisticated American cuisine delivers quality. It's the perfect intersection of hotel convenience and fine dining.
