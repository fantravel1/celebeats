What is Carbone famous for?

Carbone is a retro Italian-American restaurant in Greenwich Village known for its theatrical tableside service, vintage ambiance, and celebrity clientele. The Spicy Rigatoni Vodka is the signature dish that launched a thousand Instagram posts, while the red-jacketed servers create an old-school fine dining experience that has made it NYC's most coveted reservation.

Spicy Rigatoni Vodka

The dish that defines Carbone. Perfectly al dente rigatoni in a creamy, spicy vodka sauce, finished tableside. Bella Hadid's go-to order.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a reservation at Carbone?
Carbone reservations are notoriously difficult. They open on Resy exactly 30 days in advance at midnight. Set an alarm, have your payment ready, and refresh constantly. Walk-ins are occasionally possible for bar seating on weeknights.
What should I order at Carbone?
The must-orders include: Spicy Rigatoni Vodka (the signature dish), Veal Parmesan (massive and shareable), Caesar Salad (prepared tableside), Meatballs, and the Cheesecake. The theatrical tableside preparations are part of the experience.
What celebrities eat at Carbone?
Carbone's celebrity guest list includes Drake, Bella Hadid, Anna Wintour, Rihanna, Kim Kardashian, A$AP Rocky, and many fashion industry insiders. It's especially popular during Fashion Week.
