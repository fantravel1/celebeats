Is Carbone Miami the same as Carbone NYC?

Yes, Carbone Miami is the official Miami outpost of the legendary NYC Carbone by Major Food Group. It serves the same famous dishes including the viral Spicy Rigatoni Vodka, tableside Caesar Salad, and Veal Parmesan. The Miami location adds a glamorous beachside setting at the historic Four Seasons Surf Club.

The Vibe

The same vintage 1950s Italian-American glamour as NYC, but with ocean breezes and palm trees. Tuxedoed waiters, red leather banquettes, and theatrical tableside preparations. The terrace offers al fresco dining with Surf Club elegance.

NYC vs Miami: What's Different?

  • Same Menu: All the NYC classics including Spicy Rigatoni and Veal Parm
  • Beachside Setting: Ocean views and al fresco dining on the terrace
  • Surf Club History: Set in the legendary 1930s Surf Club building
  • Same Difficulty: Reservations equally impossible to score
  • Resort Access: Four Seasons guests may have priority booking
  • Miami Crowd: More Latin American celebrities and Art Basel clientele
