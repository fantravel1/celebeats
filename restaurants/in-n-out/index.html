Frequently Asked Questions

Which celebrities love In-N-Out Burger?
In-N-Out is beloved by celebrities across all industries. Famous fans include Kylie Jenner, Gordon Ramsay, Adele, Paris Hilton, Selena Gomez, Julia Roberts, Chrissy Teigen, and Miley Cyrus. The chain is particularly popular at award show after-parties.
What is Animal Style at In-N-Out?
Animal Style is In-N-Out's most famous secret menu item. For burgers, it means a mustard-grilled patty with extra pickles, extra spread, and grilled onions. For fries, Animal Style adds cheese, spread, and grilled onions on top.
What is In-N-Out's secret menu?
In-N-Out's famous secret menu includes: Animal Style (burgers and fries), Protein Style (lettuce wrap instead of bun), 3x3 or 4x4 (triple or quadruple meat/cheese), Flying Dutchman (just meat and cheese), Neapolitan Shake, and Well-Done Fries.
Why is In-N-Out only in certain states?
In-N-Out refuses to franchise and owns all locations to maintain quality control. They also never freeze their ingredients, so all restaurants must be within a day's drive of their distribution centers. This commitment to freshness is why celebrities and locals alike trust the quality.
