What is Nobu Las Vegas known for?

Nobu Las Vegas is known for its signature Black Cod with Miso, Yellowtail Jalapeno, and world-class omakase experience. The restaurant is part of the exclusive Nobu Hotel at Caesars Palace, making it a full luxury destination for celebrity guests.
