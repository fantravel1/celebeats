What makes Canoe Restaurant special?

Canoe occupies the 54th floor of TD Tower, offering panoramic views of Toronto's skyline and Lake Ontario. Chef John Chicken showcases regional Canadian cuisine - from Atlantic lobster to Alberta beef, Quebec foie gras to BC salmon. It's Toronto's premier destination for power lunches, celebrations, and celebrity dining with a view.
