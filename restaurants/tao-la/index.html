What is TAO Los Angeles?

TAO Los Angeles is Hollywood's premier Asian restaurant-nightclub, featuring a towering 20-foot Buddha statue as its centerpiece. The West Coast sibling of NYC's legendary TAO, it combines upscale pan-Asian cuisine (Japanese, Chinese, Thai) with one of LA's hottest nightlife scenes. Dinner transitions into dancing, making it a full evening destination for celebrities and party-goers.
