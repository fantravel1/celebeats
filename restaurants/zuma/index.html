Is Zuma Miami the same as Zuma London and Dubai?

Yes, Zuma is a global restaurant group founded by chef Rainer Becker. The Miami location is part of the same group that operates Zuma London, Dubai, Hong Kong, New York, and 18+ other international cities. All locations feature the same izakaya-style Japanese concept.

The Vibe

Sleek, minimalist Japanese design with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking Biscayne Bay. The open kitchen features robata grills, sushi counter, and theatrical preparation. The energy is buzzy and sophisticated. Dress code is smart casual to upscale.

Zuma FAQs

Yes, all Zuma locations are part of the same global restaurant group founded by chef Rainer Becker. Same izakaya concept, same signature dishes, same A-list clientele worldwide.

Zuma is known for modern Japanese izakaya-style dining, robata grill dishes, miso black cod, premium sushi, and its sleek design. It's a global celebrity favorite with 18+ locations.

Expect $100-200+ per person for dinner with drinks. The omakase and robata dishes add up quickly. Lunch is slightly more accessible. It's a splurge experience.

Smart casual to upscale. No athletic wear or flip flops. The crowd tends to dress fashionably, especially for dinner. Think sophisticated Miami style.
