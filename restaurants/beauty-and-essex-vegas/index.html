What is the Beauty and Essex pawn shop entrance?

Beauty and Essex features a hidden entrance through a functioning pawn shop. Guests walk through the shop and a secret door leads to the glamorous restaurant inside. It's one of Vegas's most unique and Instagram-worthy dining experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Beauty and Essex has a hidden entrance through a functioning pawn shop. You walk through the shop and a secret door leads to the glamorous restaurant inside. The pawn shop is real and sells jewelry and vintage items.

Beauty and Essex has a glamorous, speakeasy-style atmosphere with chandeliers, pearl curtains, and lounge seating. It's upscale but fun, perfect for groups and celebrations.

Must-orders include the Grilled Cheese Tomato Soup Dumplings, Tuna Poke Wonton Tacos, Fried Chicken Sliders, and the Jewel Heist cocktail which comes with a piece of jewelry.
