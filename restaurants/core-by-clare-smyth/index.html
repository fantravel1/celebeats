Who is Clare Smyth?

Clare Smyth is the first and only British female chef to hold 3 Michelin stars. She trained under Gordon Ramsay and Alain Ducasse before opening Core in 2017. She cooked at Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's wedding reception and was named World's Best Female Chef in 2018. Her restaurant celebrates the best of British produce with refined, modern techniques.

Clare Smyth is the first and only British female chef to hold 3 Michelin stars. She trained under Gordon Ramsay and Alain Ducasse, cooked at the Royal Wedding, and was named World's Best Female Chef 2018.

Core is famous for exceptional modern British cuisine celebrating UK produce, the signature 'Potato and Roe' dish, and being one of only a handful of 3 Michelin star restaurants in the UK.

The Core tasting menu costs approximately 185 GBP per person. With wine pairings, expect 300-350+ GBP per person. There's also a lunch menu available at a lower price point.
