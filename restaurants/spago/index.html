What makes Spago famous for celebrity dining?

Spago Beverly Hills is Wolfgang Puck's flagship restaurant that revolutionized California cuisine and has been Hollywood's premier dining destination since 1982. The restaurant has hosted virtually every major celebrity, serves as the caterer for the Academy Awards Governors Ball, and is known for its iconic smoked salmon pizza, impeccable service, and prime Beverly Hills location that continues to attract A-list clientele four decades later.

Restaurant History

1982

Wolfgang Puck opens the original Spago on Sunset Boulevard, quickly becoming the hottest table in town.

1984

Spago hosts its first post-Oscar party, beginning a Hollywood tradition.

1994

Wolfgang Puck begins catering the Academy Awards Governors Ball, a partnership that continues today.

1997

Spago relocates to its current Beverly Hills location on Canon Drive.

2012

Celebrates 30 years with complete renovation, receiving AAA Five Diamond rating.

2022

40th anniversary celebration, still one of the most sought-after reservations in LA.

Frequently Asked Questions

What celebrities eat at Spago Beverly Hills?
Spago has hosted virtually every major Hollywood celebrity over its 40+ year history. Regular guests have included Tom Hanks, Oprah Winfrey, Steven Spielberg, George Clooney, Jennifer Aniston, and countless Oscar winners. The Academy Awards Governors Ball has been catered by Wolfgang Puck for decades.
What is Spago's most famous dish?
Spago's smoked salmon pizza is legendary and helped revolutionize California cuisine. Other signature dishes include the agnolotti with braised short ribs, dry-aged steaks, and Wolfgang Puck's Austrian-inspired desserts like the apple strudel.
How far in advance should I book Spago?
Reservations at Spago should be made 3-4 weeks in advance for weekend dining. Weeknight reservations may be available with 1-2 weeks notice. For special occasions or large parties, book 4-6 weeks ahead.
What is the dress code at Spago Beverly Hills?
Spago maintains an upscale casual dress code. Business casual to elegant attire is appropriate. Jackets are not required but many guests dress up. Avoid athletic wear, shorts, and flip-flops.
