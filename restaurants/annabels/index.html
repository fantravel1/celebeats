What is Annabel's known for?

Annabel's is London's most prestigious private members club since 1963. The venue spans multiple floors of a stunning Georgian townhouse with themed rooms, gardens, restaurants, bars, and a nightclub. Famous for lavish interiors, celebrity members, and legendary parties.

Frequently Asked Questions

Membership is by invitation only or requires nomination by existing members. There is a substantial waiting list and membership fees start at several thousand pounds annually. The club maintains strict exclusivity and vetting processes.

Non-members can only visit as guests of members. Members may bring a limited number of guests. Some special events may have different guest policies. The club is very strict about this to maintain its exclusive atmosphere.

Smart and elegant. No jeans, trainers, or casual attire. Men typically wear suits or smart jackets. Women dress glamorously. The nightclub has slightly relaxed but still smart dress code. Think Mayfair elegance at all times.
