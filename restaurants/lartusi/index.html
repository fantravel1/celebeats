What makes L'Artusi special?

L'Artusi stands out for its exceptional house-made pastas, particularly the simple yet perfect spaghetti with garlic and olive oil. The olive oil cake has achieved legendary status as one of NYC's best desserts. Combined with a romantic West Village setting and excellent wine program, it's become a go-to for celebrity date nights.

Frequently Asked Questions

L'Artusi is known for exceptional house-made pastas like the spaghetti aglio e olio, the legendary olive oil cake dessert, and its romantic West Village atmosphere. It's consistently ranked among NYC's best Italian restaurants.

L'Artusi reservations can be competitive, especially for weekend dinners. Book via Resy about 2-3 weeks in advance. The wine bar area accepts walk-ins and is a great alternative for pasta and a glass of wine.

L'Artusi is one of NYC's best date night restaurants. The romantic West Village setting, excellent wine list, shareable pastas, and the famous olive oil cake make it perfect for special occasions.
