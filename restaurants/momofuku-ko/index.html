What is Momofuku Ko?

Momofuku Ko is David Chang's flagship fine dining restaurant, featuring an intimate 12-seat counter where diners watch chefs prepare a multi-course tasting menu. The cuisine blends Korean, Japanese, and French techniques into innovative American dishes. It was revolutionary for being the first NYC restaurant to take online-only reservations when it opened in 2008.

Frequently Asked Questions

Reservations are on Tock and open 2-4 weeks in advance at 10am ET. Prepayment is required. The restaurant has a strict no-modifications policy and cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable.

Momofuku Ko has two Michelin stars, which it has maintained since 2009. It's one of the most consistently acclaimed restaurants in NYC.

The tasting menu is $195 per person, excluding beverages, tax, and gratuity. Wine pairings and optional courses add to the total. Expect $300-400+ per person all-in.

Smart casual is appropriate. The atmosphere is relaxed but refined - think nice jeans and a blazer. No formal dress code is enforced.
