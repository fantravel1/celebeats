What is Wakuda Las Vegas?

Wakuda is celebrity chef Tetsuya Wakuda's first American restaurant, located at The Venetian Las Vegas. The legendary Japanese-Australian chef, whose Sydney restaurant spent decades on the World's 50 Best list, brings his refined cooking techniques and signature Confit of Ocean Trout to the Strip. The elegant space features a dramatic open kitchen and exclusive omakase counter.

Confit of Ocean Trout

The dish that made Tetsuya famous worldwide. Delicately cooked ocean trout with konbu, daikon, and wasabi - a perfect balance of Japanese technique and Australian ingredients that has been served at Tetsuya's for over 30 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Chef Tetsuya Wakuda?
Tetsuya Wakuda is a legendary Japanese-Australian chef whose Sydney restaurant Tetsuya's was consistently ranked among the world's best for over two decades. Wakuda Las Vegas marks his first U.S. restaurant, bringing his refined Japanese cooking techniques to the Strip.
What is Wakuda Las Vegas known for?
Wakuda is known for its exceptional omakase experience, signature Confit of Ocean Trout (a dish that made Tetsuya famous), premium wagyu selections, and sushi crafted by master chefs. The elegant dining room features a dramatic open kitchen and private omakase counter.
How much is the omakase at Wakuda?
The omakase experience at Wakuda starts at $350 per person for a multi-course chef's tasting menu at the exclusive counter. The experience includes seasonal dishes, premium ingredients, and the signature Confit of Ocean Trout.
