Does Hakkasan London have a Michelin star?

Yes, Hakkasan London holds one Michelin star for its modern Cantonese cuisine. It was one of the first Chinese restaurants in London to receive this recognition, helping revolutionize perceptions of Chinese fine dining in the West. The restaurant is known for its innovative dim sum, signature dishes, and dramatic dark wood lattice interiors.
