What is Carbone Riviera?

Carbone Riviera is Major Food Group's latest masterpiece, bringing the legendary NYC Carbone experience to the Bellagio in Las Vegas with a Lake Como-inspired twist. The restaurant combines the theatrical Italian-American dining that made Carbone famous with Mediterranean coastal elegance, featuring seafood-forward dishes alongside the iconic Spicy Rigatoni. It's where Vegas high-rollers and A-list celebrities converge for the most glamorous Italian dining on the Strip.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Carbone Riviera different from NYC Carbone?
Carbone Riviera takes the classic NYC experience and adds Lake Como-inspired coastal glamour. The menu features more seafood-forward dishes like the Seafood Tower and Branzino, while still offering the iconic Spicy Rigatoni. The design evokes Italian Riviera elegance with Mediterranean blues alongside the signature Carbone red.
How do I get a reservation at Carbone Riviera?
Reservations can be made through Resy or by contacting the Bellagio concierge. Hotel guests at Bellagio and MGM Resorts properties may have access to priority reservations. The restaurant books up quickly, especially on weekends and fight nights, so plan at least 2-3 weeks in advance.
What celebrities have been spotted at Carbone Riviera?
Carbone Riviera attracts the same A-list crowd as the NYC location, including Drake, the Kardashian-Jenner family, Vegas residency performers like Adele and Usher, and fashion industry insiders. It's become a pre-show dining destination for headliners at nearby venues.
