Komodo is frequented by Bad Bunny, Drake, DJ Khaled, Jennifer Lopez, Post Malone, The Weeknd, and countless other A-listers. David Grutman's three-story venue is Miami's hottest celebrity dining destination.

The Three-Story Experience

Komodo spans three floors, each with a distinct vibe:

  • Ground Floor: Main dining room with open kitchen, Southeast Asian decor
  • Second Floor: Lounge seating, mezzanine views, private dining rooms
  • Rooftop: Open-air bar, DJ booth, bottle service, Miami skyline views

Komodo FAQs

Bad Bunny, Drake, DJ Khaled, Jennifer Lopez, Post Malone, The Weeknd, and many other A-listers. It's Miami's hottest celebrity restaurant, owned by David Grutman.

Three-story venue, rooftop lounge with DJ, Southeast Asian fusion, Peking duck (Komodo Dragon), truffle dumplings, and the dinner-to-party atmosphere unique to Grutman properties.

David Grutman, Miami's hospitality king and owner of Groot Hospitality. He also owns LIV nightclub, Swan, Strawberry Moon, and is known as the unofficial mayor of Miami nightlife.

Book via Resy or call (305) 534-2211. Reserve 1-2 weeks ahead for weekends. Rooftop requires bottle service on busy nights. Walk-ins possible early weeknights or for bar seating.

Upscale casual to dressy. Miami glamour is expected, especially on weekends and for rooftop access. No athletic wear, flip-flops, or overly casual attire. Think stylish and nightclub-ready.
