What is San Vicente Bungalows?

San Vicente Bungalows is LA's most exclusive members-only private club, founded by Jeff Klein. Famous for its strict no-phone policy, lush garden setting with bungalow-style seating, and being the ultimate celebrity sanctuary. Membership is invitation-only with extensive waiting lists.

Frequently Asked Questions

San Vicente Bungalows is LA's most exclusive members-only private club, founded by Jeff Klein. Known for its strict no-phone policy, lush garden setting, and being the ultimate celebrity sanctuary.

Membership is by invitation only and requires sponsorship from existing members. The waiting list is extensive, and the club is highly selective. Annual fees are approximately $4,500.

SVB has a strict no-phone policy. All phones are locked in pouches upon entry to ensure complete privacy for members. This policy is the main reason celebrities love the club.

SVB counts A-list celebrities as members including Adele, Drake, Jennifer Lawrence, Leonardo DiCaprio, Margot Robbie, and many top entertainment executives. The privacy policy makes it a favorite retreat.
