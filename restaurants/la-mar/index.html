The Vibe

La Mar occupies a stunning waterfront terrace at the Mandarin Oriental with panoramic views of Biscayne Bay and the Miami skyline. The open-air dining room features bright Peruvian colors, a lively ceviche bar, and the constant spectacle of boats passing by. Sunset is magical.

La Mar FAQs

Sofia Vergara, Gloria Estefan, Marc Anthony, Shakira, Daddy Yankee, and Inter Miami soccer players are among the celebrities who dine at La Mar. The Peruvian cuisine attracts Latin American stars and global food enthusiasts.

Gaston Acurio is Peru's most celebrated chef and a global ambassador for Peruvian cuisine. He's credited with putting Peru on the world culinary map and operates acclaimed restaurants worldwide, including La Mar locations in multiple cities.

Start with multiple ceviches and tiraditos - they're exceptional. Try the anticuchos (beef heart skewers) for authentic Peruvian street food elevated. The lomo saltado is a classic. Don't skip the pisco sours.

Book via OpenTable or call (305) 913-8358. Request outdoor terrace seating for bay views. Weekend brunch and sunset dinner are most popular - book a week ahead for prime times.
