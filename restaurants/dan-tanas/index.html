Dan Tana's has hosted Hollywood legends for 60 years. Past regulars included Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, and the Rat Pack. Today's celebrities include Al Pacino, George Clooney, Leonardo DiCaprio, Ryan Gosling, and countless industry power players.

The Dan Tana's Experience

Dan Tana's is a time capsule. Red-and-white checkered tablecloths, celebrity photos on wood-paneled walls, intimate booths, and waiters who've worked there for decades. It's deliberately unchanged - a feature, not a bug. Next door to the legendary Troubadour music venue, it's where musicians dine before and after shows.

The Rat Pack Era

Dan Tana's was a Rat Pack hangout. Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, Sammy Davis Jr., and their crew made this their spot. The tradition of Hollywood royalty dining here started in 1964 and never stopped. Photos on the walls tell the story of six decades of stardom.

The Dan Tana's Rules

  • Call for Reservations - No apps. No online booking. Call (310) 275-9444.
  • Be a Regular - Loyalty is rewarded. Return visits get better tables.
  • Don't Ask to Change Anything - The menu hasn't changed. Neither should your order.
  • Embrace the Chaos - It's loud, crowded, and perfect that way.
  • Tip Well - The waiters have been there forever. They remember everything.
  • Stay Late - The real scene is after 10 PM until close at 1 AM.
