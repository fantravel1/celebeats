What was The Spotted Pig famous for?

The Spotted Pig was NYC's most influential gastropub, famous for Chef April Bloomfield's chargrilled burger with Roquefort and shoestring fries, deviled eggs, and ricotta gnudi. Its legendary third floor was a private celebrity hangout where Jay-Z, Beyonce, Bono, and A-listers gathered away from paparazzi. It held a Michelin star for 10 consecutive years.
