What is The Tower Bar known for?

The Tower Bar is Hollywood's most legendary power dining destination, set in the glamorous 1930s Art Deco Sunset Tower Hotel. Famous for the McCarthy Salad, Prime Rib, and being where major industry deals are made. Old Hollywood elegance meets modern celebrity culture.
