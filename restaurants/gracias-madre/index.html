Gracias Madre FAQs

Gracias Madre is a celebrity hotspot for plant-based dining. Regular visitors include Beyonce, Adele, Kourtney Kardashian, Kim Kardashian, Chrissy Teigen, John Legend, Lady Gaga, and many health-conscious celebrities and influencers.

Yes, Gracias Madre is 100% plant-based and vegan. All dishes are made without animal products, using organic and locally-sourced ingredients. They're also certified organic and committed to sustainable practices throughout their operations.

Gracias Madre is famous for its vegan tacos (especially the coconut carnitas and cauliflower), cashew queso nachos, and amazing margaritas made with fresh juices. The beautiful outdoor patio with string lights creates an Instagram-perfect atmosphere.

Start with the Nachos con Queso - the cashew queso is legendary. Try the Coconut Carnitas Tacos and Coliflor (cauliflower) Tacos. Don't skip the Watermelon Margarita or Spicy Jalapeno Margarita. For brunch, the chilaquiles are a must.
