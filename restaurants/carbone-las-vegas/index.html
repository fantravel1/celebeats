What is Carbone Las Vegas famous for?

Carbone Las Vegas is famous for its Spicy Rigatoni Vodka (the TikTok viral sensation), tableside Caesar Salad, and Veal Parmesan. The experience includes tuxedoed waiters, a 1950s Italian-American atmosphere, and the same menu that made the NYC original impossible to book.
