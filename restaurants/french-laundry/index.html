What is The French Laundry's signature dish?

The French Laundry's most iconic dish is Oysters and Pearls - a sabayon of pearl tapioca with Island Creek oysters and white sturgeon caviar. On the menu since 1996, it exemplifies Thomas Keller's philosophy of "the law of diminishing returns" - perfect simplicity at its finest.
