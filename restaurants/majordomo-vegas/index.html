Who is the chef at Majordomo Las Vegas?

Majordomo Las Vegas is a David Chang restaurant, part of his Momofuku empire. David Chang is a James Beard Award-winning chef, Netflix star (Ugly Delicious), and culinary innovator known for revolutionizing modern Asian-American cuisine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Majordomo Las Vegas is a David Chang restaurant, part of his Momofuku empire. Chang is a James Beard Award winner and host of Netflix's Ugly Delicious.

The Bo Ssam (whole pork shoulder) is legendary - order it for groups of 6-8. Also try the Beef Short Rib, Bing Bread, and any of the whole animal preparations. Come hungry.

Reservations are strongly recommended, especially for weekends and large groups ordering the Bo Ssam. Book through Resy or OpenTable in advance.
