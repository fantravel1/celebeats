The Experience

Felix features a stunning glass-enclosed pasta lab at its entrance where guests can watch sfogline (pasta makers) hand-roll pasta using traditional techniques. The bright, airy space includes a marble bar, open kitchen, and sun-drenched patio. Chef Evan Funke trained in Bologna and brings obsessive dedication to authentic Italian pasta.

Felix FAQs

Felix attracts pasta-loving celebrities including Jonah Hill, Timothee Chalamet, Emily Ratajkowski, Hailey Bieber, Kendall Jenner, and Dakota Johnson. The restaurant's Venice location and exceptional pasta make it a favorite among young Hollywood.

Evan Funke is an acclaimed chef who trained in Bologna, Italy and became famous for his handmade pasta. He previously led Bucato in Culver City before opening Felix in 2017. He's known for his obsessive dedication to traditional Italian pasta-making techniques and appears on Chef's Table: Pizza.

Felix is famous for its glass-enclosed pasta lab where guests watch fresh pasta being made by hand. Signature dishes include the pici cacio e pepe, pappardelle with pork sugo, and seasonal pasta preparations. The restaurant showcases regional Italian cooking with California ingredients.

Felix accepts reservations via Resy. Book 2-3 weeks in advance for prime weekend times. The restaurant opens at 5:30pm and early reservations are often easier to secure. Walk-ins can try their luck at the bar or patio.
