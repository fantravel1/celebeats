Who is the chef at Four Seasons Surf Club?

The Surf Club Restaurant is helmed by legendary chef Thomas Keller, the mastermind behind The French Laundry and Per Se. This is his East Coast flagship outside of New York, bringing his refined French-American cuisine to Miami's most discerning diners and celebrities.

The Vibe

Set in the historic 1930s Surf Club, the restaurant exudes old-world glamour with Art Deco details, ocean views, and impeccable service. The terrace offers al fresco dining with Atlantic breezes. Dress code is resort elegant - no shorts or flip flops.
