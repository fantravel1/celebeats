The Keith McNally Empire

Balthazar is the crown jewel of restaurateur Keith McNally's empire. His ability to create timeless spaces that attract celebrities is legendary. The red leather banquettes, antique mirrors, and Parisian atmosphere have remained virtually unchanged since 1997.

Balthazar FAQs

Virtually every A-lister in NYC has dined at Balthazar. Regulars include Anna Wintour, Gigi Hadid, Taylor Swift, and countless fashion, film, and music celebrities.

Balthazar is known for its authentic Parisian atmosphere, celebrity clientele, legendary bread basket (from their own bakery), weekend brunch, steak frites, and late-night dining until midnight.

Book via Resy or call (212) 965-1414. Weekend brunch is hardest to get - book 2+ weeks ahead. Walk-ins are possible at the bar and for late-night dining after 10PM.
