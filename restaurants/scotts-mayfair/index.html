Frequently Asked Questions

Scott's is London's most prestigious seafood restaurant since 1851. Famous for its raw bar with the finest oysters, Dover sole, caviar service, and elegant Art Deco interiors. A favorite of royalty, celebrities, and London's elite.

Scott's regularly hosts A-list celebrities including George Clooney, Michael Caine (a regular), Kate Middleton, Hugh Grant, Madonna, and members of the Royal Family. Paparazzi often wait outside on Mount Street.

Book well in advance, especially for dinner and weekends. Call the restaurant directly as they don't use third-party booking apps. Lunch is slightly easier to secure. The oyster bar sometimes has walk-in availability.

Smart casual to smart. Mayfair elegant - think well-dressed without being overly formal. No sportswear or trainers. Gentlemen often wear jackets at dinner.
