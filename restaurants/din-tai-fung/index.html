Why is Din Tai Fung so famous?

Din Tai Fung is a Taiwanese restaurant chain that became a global phenomenon for their perfectly crafted Xiao Long Bao (soup dumplings), each made with exactly 18 folds. Founded in Taiwan in 1958, it exploded on TikTok with viral videos of their Cucumber Salad towers and mesmerizing dumpling-making process. Celebrities, influencers, and foodies alike line up for what the New York Times once called one of the top 10 restaurants in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why did Din Tai Fung go viral on TikTok?
Din Tai Fung exploded on TikTok thanks to videos of their signature Cucumber Salad being stacked into impressive towers, the hypnotic precision of their dumpling-making process, and food influencers documenting the perfect soup dumpling bite technique. The hashtag #dintaifung has billions of views.
What should I order at Din Tai Fung?
Must-orders include: Xiao Long Bao (original soup dumplings), Truffle & Pork Dumplings, the viral Cucumber Salad, Sauteed String Beans, Shrimp Fried Rice, and Chocolate Xiao Long Bao for dessert. The Spicy Wontons are also a crowd favorite.
How long is the wait at Din Tai Fung?
Wait times vary by location and time. Peak hours (weekend lunch/dinner) can see waits of 1-2 hours. Use the Yelp Waitlist feature to join the queue remotely before arriving. Weekday lunch is typically the shortest wait.
