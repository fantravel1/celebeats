What is Nobu London famous for?

Nobu London is famous for its signature Black Cod with Miso, innovative Japanese-Peruvian fusion cuisine, and being a longtime celebrity hotspot. Located in the Metropolitan Hotel on Park Lane, it's been the UK's premier Japanese destination since 1997, attracting everyone from Madonna to David Beckham.
