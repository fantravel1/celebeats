Is Eleven Madison Park fully vegan?

Yes, since June 2021, Eleven Madison Park has served an entirely plant-based menu. Chef Daniel Humm made the revolutionary decision to eliminate all animal products while maintaining 3 Michelin stars, proving that plant-based cuisine can be the pinnacle of fine dining.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, since June 2021, EMP serves an entirely plant-based menu. Chef Daniel Humm eliminated all animal products while maintaining 3 Michelin stars, revolutionizing fine dining.

EMP is known for being named World's Best Restaurant in 2017, its groundbreaking plant-based menu, Art Deco setting overlooking Madison Square Park, and legendary hospitality.

The tasting menu is approximately $365 per person, excluding beverages, tax, and gratuity. Wine pairings and additional options increase the total.

EMP attracts environmentally-conscious celebrities including Beyonce, Jay-Z, Leonardo DiCaprio, Natalie Portman, Paul McCartney, and many others drawn to its plant-based innovation.
