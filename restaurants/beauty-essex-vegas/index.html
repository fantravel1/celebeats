What is Beauty & Essex Las Vegas?

Beauty & Essex at The Cosmopolitan is TAO Group's glamorous hidden gem, concealed behind a working pawn shop entrance. This speakeasy-style restaurant features vintage Art Deco design, shareable New American plates, and a devoted celebrity following. The photogenic interior - with its crystal chandeliers and rose gold accents - makes it an Instagram favorite among A-listers.

The Pawn Shop Entrance Experience

Walk through a real pawn shop filled with vintage jewelry and curiosities before a hidden door swings open to reveal the stunning restaurant. It's a signature TAO Group concept that creates anticipation and exclusivity from the moment you arrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Beauty & Essex hidden behind a pawn shop?
The pawn shop entrance is a signature TAO Group concept that creates a speakeasy-style experience. Guests walk through a working pawn shop filled with vintage items before a hidden door reveals the glamorous restaurant. The original NYC location popularized this unique entrance.
What is the food like at Beauty & Essex?
Beauty & Essex specializes in shareable New American plates with global influences. Signature dishes include the Grilled Cheese & Tomato Soup Dumplings, Jewel Salad, and the famous Tuna Poke Wonton Tacos. The menu is designed for groups to share multiple plates.
Is it good for Instagram photos?
Absolutely. Beauty & Essex is one of the most photogenic restaurants in Vegas. From the pawn shop entrance to the crystal chandeliers, rose gold accents, and 24k Gold Chocolate Cake dessert, every corner is Instagram-worthy.
