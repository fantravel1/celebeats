Why is The Ivy famous?

The Ivy has been London's celebrity dining institution since 1917. Originally the hangout of West End theatrical stars, it became the place to spot celebrities. Famous for its iconic stained glass windows, shepherd's pie, and impeccable service. It remains one of London's hardest reservations.
