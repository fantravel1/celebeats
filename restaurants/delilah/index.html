The h.wood Group

Delilah is part of the h.wood Group hospitality empire, which also includes The Nice Guy, Bootsy Bellows, and other celebrity hotspots. The group is known for creating venues that attract Hollywood's elite with exceptional service and discretion.

The Supper Club Experience

Delilah transports guests to 1920s glamour with red velvet booths, live jazz and cabaret performances, and an atmosphere that blends dinner and nightlife. Perfect for post-awards show celebrations.
