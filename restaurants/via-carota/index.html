The Via Carota Story

Chefs Jody Williams and Rita Sodi opened Via Carota in 2014 and created instant magic. Their commitment to seasonal, vegetable-forward Tuscan cooking earned a James Beard Award for Outstanding Restaurant in 2022. The no-reservation policy keeps it democratic - celebrities wait alongside everyone else.
