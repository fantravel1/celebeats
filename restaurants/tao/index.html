Frequently Asked Questions

Which celebrities go to Tao?
Tao attracts A-list celebrities including Leonardo DiCaprio, Rihanna, Drake, the Kardashians, Justin Bieber, and countless others. It's a favorite for birthday parties, album release celebrations, and after-parties.
What is Tao famous for?
Tao is famous for its 16-foot Buddha statue, theatrical Asian-fusion dining experience, and seamless transition from upscale restaurant to nightclub. The satay, crispy rice, and Peking duck are signature dishes.

Reservations can be made through Resy or the Tao website. For VIP/bottle service and private events, contact the venue directly. Weekend reservations should be made 2-3 weeks in advance.
