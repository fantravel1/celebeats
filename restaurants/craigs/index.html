Frequently Asked Questions

Which celebrities eat at Craig's?
Craig's is one of Hollywood's most popular celebrity restaurants. Regular guests include Justin and Hailey Bieber, the Kardashian-Jenner family, LeBron James, Leonardo DiCaprio, Jennifer Lopez, and virtually every A-list celebrity in LA.
Does Craig's have vegan options?
Yes, Craig's is famous for its extensive vegan menu. Owner Craig Susser developed the vegan offerings to cater to LA's health-conscious celebrity clientele. Popular vegan dishes include the Impossible Burger, vegan chicken parmesan, and various plant-based pasta dishes.
How do I get a reservation at Craig's?
Reservations at Craig's can be made by phone at (310) 276-1900. Due to its popularity, booking 1-2 weeks in advance is recommended, especially for weekend dinners. Walk-ins are sometimes accommodated at the bar.
