The Speakeasy Experience

The Nice Guy operates like an old-school speakeasy. The entrance is unmarked, the lighting is dim, and the atmosphere is designed for privacy. Red leather booths, candlelight, and a strictly enforced no-photo policy create a haven for celebrities seeking discretion.

h.wood Group Empire

The Nice Guy is owned by the h.wood Group, who also run Delilah, The Peppermint Club, and Bootsy Bellows. They've perfected the art of celebrity hospitality in LA.
