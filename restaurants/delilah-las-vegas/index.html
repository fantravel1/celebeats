What is Delilah Las Vegas?

Delilah Las Vegas is h.wood Group's glamorous supper club at Wynn Las Vegas that transports guests to the golden age of Hollywood. With Jazz Age Art Deco decor, live entertainment nightly, and classic American cuisine, Delilah has become the Strip's ultimate celebrity destination. From Drake to the Kardashians, A-listers make this their post-show and late-night hotspot.

The Experience: Dining Meets Entertainment

Delilah isn't just dinner - it's a full sensory experience. Live jazz, burlesque performances, and rotating entertainment transform the dining room into a modern speakeasy where celebrities and guests become part of the show.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Delilah Las Vegas?
Delilah Las Vegas is a glamorous supper club from h.wood Group at Wynn Las Vegas. The venue combines Jazz Age decor, live entertainment, and classic American cuisine in an Art Deco setting that attracts A-list celebrities and high rollers nightly.
What should I wear to Delilah Las Vegas?
Delilah enforces an upscale dress code befitting its glamorous atmosphere. Think cocktail attire: dresses, heels, and suits for men. No athletic wear, sneakers, or casual clothing. The vibe is old Hollywood glamour meets Vegas sophistication.
Is there live entertainment at Delilah?
Yes, Delilah features live entertainment nightly including jazz performances, burlesque shows, and rotating acts. The entertainment is integrated into the dining experience, making every night feel like an event.
