The Emilio Legend

Emilio Vitolo Sr. has run this Houston Street institution since 1992. His warmth, authenticity, and old-world hospitality have made this tiny restaurant a celebrity magnet. He treats everyone like family - whether you're a president or a first-time visitor. The walls are covered with photos of famous guests.

Emilio's Ballato FAQs

The celebrity list is legendary: Barack Obama, Justin Bieber, Rihanna, Bella Hadid, Bradley Cooper, Lady Gaga, Katie Holmes, and countless fashion and music industry insiders. The unpretentious atmosphere is exactly what draws them.

Authentic old-school Italian cooking, red-sauce classics done perfectly, the legendary Emilio himself, zero pretense, and the fact that A-listers eat here precisely because it's not a "scene." The chicken parm and simple pastas are famous.

Traditionally, no reservations - you wait like everyone else. That said, celebrities and connected regulars often get accommodated. For regular guests, arrive early (before 6 PM or after 9 PM) to minimize wait time.

Start with the burrata. Get any pasta - the spaghetti pomodoro is perfection. The chicken parmigiana is legendary. Don't overthink it - this is old-school Italian done right. Ask Emilio what's good that day.
