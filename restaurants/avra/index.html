What is Avra known for?

Avra is renowned for its fresh whole fish flown in daily from the Mediterranean, displayed on ice for guests to select. The Grilled Branzino and Mediterranean Langoustines are celebrity favorites, prepared simply with premium olive oil, fresh lemon, and Greek herbs.
