The Building

Mother Wolf occupies the ground floor of the historic 1924 Hollywood Athletic Club, a stunning Art Deco building that once hosted Charlie Chaplin, Rudolph Valentino, and John Wayne. The 9,000-square-foot space features soaring ceilings, original architectural details, multiple dining rooms, and a vibrant bar scene.

Mother Wolf FAQs

Mother Wolf attracts Hollywood's elite including Drake, Chris Pine, Florence Pugh, Andrew Garfield, Margot Robbie, and Pete Davidson. The stunning location in the historic Hollywood Athletic Club makes it perfect for industry dinners and celebrity celebrations.

Mother Wolf is famous for authentic Roman cuisine including the four classic Roman pastas: cacio e pepe, carbonara, amatriciana, and gricia. The restaurant features handmade pasta, an extensive Italian wine list, and stunning Art Deco interiors in the historic 1924 Hollywood Athletic Club.

Both restaurants are by chef Evan Funke. Felix in Venice focuses on regional Italian cooking from various parts of Italy, while Mother Wolf specifically celebrates Roman cuisine and traditions. Mother Wolf is larger, more formal, and housed in a historic Hollywood building.

Mother Wolf accepts reservations via Resy. Book 3-4 weeks in advance for weekend dining. The restaurant is large with multiple dining rooms, so availability is better than many LA hot spots. Early evening and weeknight reservations are easier to secure.
