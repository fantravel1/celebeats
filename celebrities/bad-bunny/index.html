Puerto Rican Food Culture

Bad Bunny is proud of his Puerto Rican heritage and frequently showcases traditional cuisine:

  • Mofongo - Fried plantain mash, his comfort food
  • Arroz con Gandules - Rice with pigeon peas, holiday staple
  • Lechon - Roasted pork, Puerto Rican BBQ
  • Tostones - Fried plantains, side dish favorite
  • Medalla Beer - Puerto Rico's beloved lager

He's been spotted at authentic Puerto Rican restaurants in Orlando and NYC's Spanish Harlem, supporting Boricua businesses.

Bad Bunny FAQs

Bad Bunny is frequently spotted at Komodo, Prime 112, Swan, and LIV in Miami. Komodo is his go-to for late-night dining and bottle service.

Bad Bunny loves authentic Puerto Rican cuisine, especially mofongo, arroz con gandules, and lechon. He's also enjoys Asian fusion at Komodo and steaks at Prime 112.

As of 2025, Bad Bunny hasn't opened his own restaurant, though he's invested in various Puerto Rican businesses. He's been rumored to be exploring hospitality ventures.
