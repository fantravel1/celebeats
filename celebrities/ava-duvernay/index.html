Frequently Asked Questions

What are Ava DuVernay's favorite restaurants?
Ava DuVernay strongly supports Black-owned restaurants and community-focused LA establishments. She's highlighted spots like Post & Beam, Roscoe's House of Chicken and Waffles, Dulan's, and various soul food establishments. Her dining choices reflect her commitment to community economic empowerment.
Where is Ava DuVernay from?
Ava DuVernay was born in Long Beach, California and raised in Compton and Lynwood. Her deep LA roots inform her commitment to supporting local businesses and her appreciation for authentic community restaurants.
Does Ava DuVernay support Black-owned restaurants?
Yes, Ava DuVernay is a vocal advocate for Black-owned businesses, including restaurants. She regularly uses her social media platform to highlight and support Black-owned establishments, viewing economic empowerment as part of her broader mission.
What is ARRAY, Ava DuVernay's company?
ARRAY is Ava DuVernay's film distribution and production company, which focuses on films by people of color and women. Based in LA, her company and its campus reflect her commitment to community, which extends to supporting local restaurants and businesses.
