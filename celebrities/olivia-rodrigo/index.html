Filipino Food Heritage

Family Heritage

Olivia Rodrigo is proud of her Filipino heritage on her father's side. She has spoken about growing up with Filipino food:

  • Lumpia - Filipino spring rolls, a family favorite
  • Pancit - Stir-fried noodles often made at family gatherings
  • Adobo - The iconic Filipino braised dish
  • Family Recipes - Passed down through generations

She's mentioned in interviews that Filipino food represents comfort and connection to her roots.
