What is Shah Rukh Khan's favorite food?

Shah Rukh Khan is a non-vegetarian who famously loves biryani, particularly Hyderabadi and Lucknowi styles. His annual Eid parties at Mannat are legendary for their elaborate spreads of biryani, kebabs, and traditional Mughlai delicacies. SRK also enjoys home-cooked meals prepared by his wife Gauri and their private chef.

SRK's Legendary Eid Parties at Mannat

The Most Famous Party in Bollywood

Every Eid, Shah Rukh Khan hosts an extravagant celebration at his Bandra mansion Mannat that has become a Bollywood institution. The entire film industry attends, and the feast includes:

  • Biryani - Multiple varieties including Hyderabadi and Lucknowi
  • Seekh Kebabs - Signature Mughlai preparation
  • Sheer Khurma - Traditional Eid vermicelli dessert
  • Sewaiyan - Sweet vermicelli pudding
  • Korma & Nihari - Rich meat curries

SRK personally greets thousands of fans gathered outside Mannat from his balcony after Eid prayers.

SRK's Diet Philosophy

The King's Eating Habits

Despite his age (approaching 60), SRK maintains incredible fitness for action films like Pathaan and Jawan. His approach:

  • Non-vegetarian diet with focus on lean proteins during filming
  • Loves biryani and Mughlai food but moderates during shoots
  • Famous for barely sleeping - survives on 4-5 hours and coffee
  • Works with trainer Prashant Sawant for action roles
  • Enjoys lavish feasts at Eid but maintains discipline otherwise

"I eat what I like but I also work out enough to balance it."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shah Rukh Khan's favorite food?
Shah Rukh Khan's favorite food is biryani, particularly Hyderabadi and Lucknowi styles. He also loves Mughlai cuisine including kebabs, korma, and nihari. His Eid parties at Mannat are famous for their elaborate biryani spreads.
What happens at SRK's Eid parties at Mannat?
Shah Rukh Khan hosts legendary Eid celebrations at his Bandra mansion Mannat every year. The entire Bollywood industry attends these parties featuring elaborate feasts of biryani, kebabs, sheer khurma, and traditional Eid delicacies. SRK personally greets fans from his balcony.
Is Shah Rukh Khan vegetarian or non-vegetarian?
Shah Rukh Khan is non-vegetarian. He enjoys meat dishes, particularly biryani, kebabs, and Mughlai cuisine. His North Indian and Delhi background reflects in his preference for rich, meat-based preparations.
Where does Shah Rukh Khan eat in Mumbai?
Shah Rukh Khan prefers private dining at his mansion Mannat or at five-star hotels like the Taj Mahal Palace and Trident. He rarely dines at public restaurants, preferring the privacy of exclusive venues and home-cooked meals by his private chef.
How does Shah Rukh Khan stay fit despite loving rich food?
Despite loving biryani and Mughlai food, SRK maintains incredible fitness through rigorous training with his trainer Prashant Sawant. He moderates his diet during film shoots, especially for action roles like Pathaan and Jawan, while enjoying lavish feasts during celebrations.
