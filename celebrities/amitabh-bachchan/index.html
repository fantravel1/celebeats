What happens at Amitabh Bachchan's legendary parties at Jalsa?

Amitabh Bachchan hosts legendary Holi and Diwali celebrations at his Juhu mansion Jalsa. The entire Bollywood industry attends, from the biggest stars to directors and producers. These parties feature elaborate vegetarian feasts, traditional sweets, colors for Holi, and fireworks for Diwali.

Big B's Legendary Holi and Diwali Parties at Jalsa

The Most Famous Parties in Indian Cinema

For decades, Amitabh Bachchan has hosted India's most anticipated celebrity gatherings at his Juhu mansion Jalsa:

  • Holi at Jalsa - Colors, music, and Bollywood's biggest names playing Holi together
  • Diwali Celebrations - Lights, fireworks, traditional puja, and feast
  • Vegetarian Feast - Elaborate spread of traditional sweets and savories
  • Guest List - From Shah Rukh to Aamir, everyone attends
  • Media Tradition - Photographers gather outside Jalsa annually
  • Family Affair - Jaya, Abhishek, Aishwarya, Shweta all participate

These gatherings have been a Bollywood tradition for over 30 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Amitabh Bachchan vegetarian?
Yes, Amitabh Bachchan is a strict vegetarian. He has maintained this throughout his life and career. His discipline with food is one aspect of his overall disciplined lifestyle.
What happens at Amitabh Bachchan's Holi party?
Big B's Holi party at Jalsa is Bollywood's most famous celebration. The entire industry attends, playing with colors, enjoying music, and feasting on vegetarian delicacies. It's been a tradition for over 30 years.
What is Jalsa?
Jalsa is Amitabh Bachchan's iconic bungalow in Juhu, Mumbai. It's where he hosts his legendary Holi and Diwali parties. The name means "celebration" or "gathering" in Hindi, fitting for a home known for its parties.
How does Amitabh Bachchan stay fit at 80+?
Big B maintains his health through disciplined eating (vegetarian, moderate portions), early rising, continuous work that keeps him mentally active, no alcohol, and addressing health issues promptly with medical care.
Does Amitabh Bachchan drink alcohol?
No, Amitabh Bachchan famously doesn't drink alcohol. Despite playing roles involving alcohol and parties, he has maintained sobriety throughout his life. This discipline extends to his diet and lifestyle.
