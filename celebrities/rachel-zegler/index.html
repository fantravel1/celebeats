Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Rachel Zegler eat in NYC?
Rachel Zegler frequents NYC Theater District spots like Joe Allen, Sardi's, and Bar Centrale. These are classic Broadway haunts where theater performers dine before and after shows. She also enjoys downtown spots like Via Carota in the West Village.
Where does Rachel Zegler eat in London?
During her Evita run on the West End, Rachel explored London's theater dining scene including The Ivy, J Sheekey, and Covent Garden spots like Dishoom. These are classic establishments beloved by the theatrical community.
Where is Rachel Zegler from?
Rachel Zegler was born on May 3, 2001, in Hackensack, New Jersey. She's of Colombian descent through her mother and has spoken proudly about her Latin heritage.
What is Rachel Zegler known for?
Rachel Zegler rose to fame as Maria in Steven Spielberg's West Side Story (2021). She stars as Snow White in Disney's 2025 live-action remake and received acclaim for her role as Eva Peron in Evita on London's West End, including a viral balcony scene.
What kind of food does Rachel Zegler like?
Rachel has spoken about her Colombian heritage and New Jersey upbringing, suggesting appreciation for both Latin cuisine and classic Jersey diner fare. As a working singer, she also follows vocal-health-friendly eating practices.
