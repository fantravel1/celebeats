Frequently Asked Questions

Does King Felipe VI promote Spanish cuisine?
Yes, King Felipe VI is an ambassador for Spanish cuisine and culture. The royal family hosts state dinners featuring Spanish gastronomy and supports the country's culinary reputation internationally.
What is King Felipe VI's connection to Basque Country cuisine?
The Spanish royal family appreciates Basque Country cuisine, home to restaurants like Arzak and Mugaritz. State dinners may feature Basque chefs, and the region's innovative gastronomy is a point of Spanish national pride.
Where does King Felipe VI host state dinners?
King Felipe VI hosts state dinners at the Royal Palace of Madrid, showcasing Spanish hospitality and cuisine to world leaders. These events highlight Spain's culinary excellence from various regions.
Was King Felipe VI an Olympic athlete?
Yes, King Felipe VI competed in sailing at the 1992 Barcelona Olympics. He represented Spain in the Soling class and has maintained his passion for sailing throughout his life.
Who is Queen Letizia?
Queen Letizia was a journalist and news anchor before marrying King Felipe VI in 2004. She's known for her style and has brought a modern sensibility to the Spanish royal family.
