What does Dua Lipa eat?

Albanian Food Heritage

Dua Lipa's parents are from Kosovo, and Albanian cuisine plays a big role in her life:

  • Burek - Savory phyllo pastry filled with meat, cheese, or spinach
  • Flija - Traditional layered crepe dish baked over open fire
  • Qebapa - Grilled minced meat sausages, a Balkan specialty
  • Baklava - Sweet layered pastry with nuts and honey
  • Family gatherings centered around shared meals and traditional recipes
