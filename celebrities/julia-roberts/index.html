Where does Julia Roberts eat in Malibu?

Julia Roberts enjoys Malibu's coastal dining scene, frequently visiting Nobu Malibu for Japanese cuisine, Malibu Farm for organic farm-to-table fare, and The Sunset Restaurant for ocean views. However, she's best known for cooking at home for her family most nights of the week.

Julia's Home Cooking Philosophy

Family Dinner Is Sacred

Julia Roberts has spoken extensively about her commitment to family dinners:

  • Cooks dinner for her three children most nights
  • Maintains a large vegetable garden at home
  • Shops at local farmers markets religiously
  • No phones allowed at the dinner table

"I cook every night. Cooking for my family is my way of showing love."

Julia follows a healthy, balanced diet focused on whole foods, organic produce from her garden and farmers markets, lean proteins including fresh fish, and minimal processed foods. She doesn't follow extreme diets.
