What is Meghan Markle's food and lifestyle background?

Meghan is a major foodie with deep culinary roots. She ran The Tig, a popular lifestyle blog (2014-2017) featuring food, wine, and travel. In 2024, she launched American Riviera Orchard, a California-based lifestyle brand. She's known for her love of cooking, pasta, wine, and hosting elaborate dinner parties.

Frequently Asked Questions

What was Meghan Markle's blog The Tig?
The Tig was Meghan's lifestyle blog that she ran from 2014-2017 before her relationship with Prince Harry became public. Named after her favorite wine (Tignanello), it featured food, travel, fashion, and wellness content including recipes and restaurant recommendations.
What is American Riviera Orchard?
American Riviera Orchard is Meghan's lifestyle brand launched in 2024. Based in California, it focuses on home, food, and lifestyle products including homemade jams and preserves made from local ingredients. It reflects her love of cooking and entertaining.
What is Meghan Markle's favorite food?
Meghan is a major foodie who loves pasta, wine, and California cuisine. She's passionate about fresh pasta, good wine (especially Italian), roast chicken, and healthy California cooking with fresh vegetables.
Does Meghan Markle cook?
Yes, Meghan is known as an excellent cook. She's famous for hosting dinner parties, making fresh pasta, and cooking the roast chicken Harry proposed over. She's passionate about home cooking and entertaining.
Where does Meghan Markle eat in LA?
Meghan has been spotted at Craig's in West Hollywood, Cafe Habana in Malibu, and Tre Lune in Montecito. She's also known for preferring home cooking and entertaining at their Montecito estate.
