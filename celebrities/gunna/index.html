How did Gunna lose so much weight?

Gunna (Sergio Giavanni Kitchens) underwent one of hip-hop's most dramatic physical transformations, losing over 100 pounds. The College Park, Georgia native shifted from Atlanta's indulgent trap cuisine to a disciplined, health-focused diet emphasizing lean proteins, vegetables, and portion control. The transformation became as talked about as his music comeback.

Frequently Asked Questions

How did Gunna lose weight?
Gunna lost over 100 pounds through a combination of dietary changes and lifestyle shifts. He moved away from Atlanta's heavy Southern and fast food cuisine toward lean proteins, vegetables, and portion-controlled meals. The transformation was visually dramatic and became one of hip-hop's most discussed stories.
Where does Gunna eat in Atlanta?
Gunna frequents upscale Atlanta restaurants like STK, Nobu Atlanta, and American Cut. Post-weight loss, he favors spots with healthier options while still enjoying Atlanta's dining scene. His College Park roots connect him to traditional Atlanta soul food and wing spots like J.R. Crickets.
What does Gunna eat now?
After his dramatic weight loss, Gunna focuses on lean proteins like grilled chicken and fish, vegetables, salads, and controlled portions. This is a major shift from the fried wings, waffles, and heavy Southern food that characterized his earlier eating habits.
What is lemon pepper wet and why is it connected to Atlanta rap?
Lemon pepper wet wings are an Atlanta food icon - wings tossed in lemon pepper seasoning and then coated in a buttery sauce. J.R. Crickets is credited with creating the style. Atlanta rappers including Gunna, Future, and others have made it a cultural symbol of the city's trap music scene.
How much weight did Gunna lose?
Gunna is reported to have lost over 100 pounds. The transformation was evident in his music video appearances and public sightings, particularly noticeable during his "Fukumean" comeback era. It remains one of the most talked-about celebrity weight loss stories in hip-hop.
