Is Miley Cyrus vegan?

Miley Cyrus has been a dedicated vegan and animal rights advocate for years. She became vegan in 2014 after the death of her beloved dog Floyd. Her commitment to plant-based eating stems from her deep love for animals, and she's been vocal about the ethical and environmental reasons for choosing veganism.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Miley Cyrus vegan?
Miley Cyrus became vegan in 2014 after the death of her dog Floyd. She's been a passionate advocate for veganism and animal rights, using her platform to promote plant-based living and ethical eating.
Where does Miley Cyrus eat in Los Angeles?
Miley frequents vegan restaurants in LA including Real Food Daily, Crossroads Kitchen, Cafe Gratitude, and Gracias Madre. She's also spotted at health-conscious spots in Malibu where she has property.
What are Miley Cyrus's Nashville food memories?
Growing up in Franklin, Tennessee as Billy Ray Cyrus's daughter, Miley has fond memories of Southern comfort food. While she later became vegan, she grew up with traditional Nashville cuisine and country cooking.
What is Miley Cyrus's favorite vegan food?
Miley has mentioned loving vegan tacos, smoothie bowls, avocado-based dishes, and vegan pizza. She enjoys plant-based comfort food that satisfies without animal products.
Why did Miley Cyrus become vegan?
Miley became vegan primarily for ethical reasons related to animal welfare, especially after losing her beloved dog Floyd in 2014. She's vocal about her love for animals being the driving force behind her dietary choices.
