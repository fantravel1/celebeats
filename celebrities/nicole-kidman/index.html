Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Nicole Kidman eat in Nashville?
Nicole and Keith Urban frequent Nashville's best restaurants including Husk for Southern farm-to-table cuisine, The Catbird Seat for tasting menus, and Rolf and Daughters for Italian-inspired dishes. They prefer intimate settings with exceptional, locally-sourced food.
What is Nicole Kidman's diet?
Nicole follows a balanced, Mediterranean-influenced diet emphasizing whole foods, lean proteins, fresh vegetables, and minimal processed foods. Her Australian upbringing instilled a love for fresh seafood and quality ingredients that continues today.
Does Nicole Kidman cook at home?
Yes, Nicole enjoys cooking for her family and has spoken about preparing healthy meals with fresh ingredients. She incorporates both Australian and Southern influences into her home cooking in Nashville.

Nicole has mentioned loving fresh seafood, particularly from her Australian roots. She also enjoys farm-to-table cuisine and healthy Mediterranean dishes with lots of vegetables and olive oil.
Do Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban go on dinner dates?
