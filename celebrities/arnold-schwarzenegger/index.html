Is Arnold Schwarzenegger vegan?

Arnold is approximately 80% plant-based, though not strictly vegan. After multiple heart surgeries, he dramatically reduced meat consumption for health and environmental reasons. He's an executive producer of The Game Changers documentary promoting plant-based diets for athletes - a major shift from his famous steak-heavy bodybuilding days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Arnold Schwarzenegger vegan?
Arnold is about 80% plant-based but not strictly vegan. He dramatically reduced meat consumption for health and environmental reasons after heart surgeries, and is an executive producer of The Game Changers documentary.
What did Arnold Schwarzenegger eat as a bodybuilder?
During his bodybuilding career, Arnold ate a high-protein diet heavy on steak, eggs, whole milk, and protein shakes. He consumed around 5,000 calories daily with emphasis on protein for muscle building.
Why did Arnold change his diet?
Arnold changed his diet after multiple heart surgeries (1997 and 2018) and learning about health and environmental benefits of plant-based eating. He credits the change with improved energy and heart health.
Did Arnold Schwarzenegger own a restaurant?
Yes, Arnold owned Schatzi on Main in Santa Monica, an Austrian restaurant that operated from the 1990s until the mid-2000s. It served traditional Austrian cuisine and was a popular celebrity spot.
What does Arnold Schwarzenegger eat now?
Arnold now eats primarily plant-based meals including vegetables, oatmeal, and plant-based protein shakes. He occasionally eats meat but stays away from it most of the week, crediting this approach with helping him feel fantastic.
