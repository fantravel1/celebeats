Frequently Asked Questions

What are Taika Waititi's favorite restaurants?
Taika Waititi has eclectic tastes spanning his New Zealand roots to LA's diverse dining scene. In LA, he's been spotted at Nobu Malibu, Chateau Marmont, and various trendy spots. In New Zealand, he appreciates local cuisine and Pacific Rim flavors.
Where is Taika Waititi from?
Taika Waititi is from New Zealand, born in Wellington. He's of Maori (Te Whanau-a-Apanui) and Jewish descent. His New Zealand roots deeply influence his work and his appreciation for New Zealand cuisine.
Is Taika Waititi married?
Yes, Taika Waititi is married to singer Rita Ora. They married in 2022 and are often spotted dining together at restaurants in LA, London, and around the world.
What is Taika Waititi's heritage?
Taika Waititi is of mixed Maori (Te Whanau-a-Apanui iwi) and Jewish (Russian) descent. His Maori heritage is an important part of his identity and influences both his filmmaking and his connection to New Zealand food culture.
