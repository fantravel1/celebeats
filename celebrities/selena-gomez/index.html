What does Selena Gomez like to eat?

Selena Gomez loves Tex-Mex comfort food from her Texas upbringing, including her mom's traditional Mexican recipes. She's admitted to loving pickles, hot Cheetos, and sushi. Through her HBO Max show "Selena + Chef," she's explored cuisines from around the world while being refreshingly honest about being a beginner cook.

Selena + Chef: Learning to Cook on Camera

During the pandemic, Selena launched "Selena + Chef" on HBO Max, where celebrity chefs teach her to cook via video call. Now in its fourth season, the show has featured chefs like Gordon Ramsay, Ludo Lefebvre, and Roy Choi. Selena's genuine reactions and kitchen mishaps have made it a fan favorite.
