Where does Jeff Bezos eat?

Jeff Bezos now primarily dines in Miami since his 2023 relocation. He's been spotted at Carbone Miami, Casa Tua, and various waterfront spots. In Seattle, he was a regular at Canlis, The Walrus and the Carpenter, and El Gaucho. He's known for enjoying Mediterranean cuisine and adventurous dishes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Jeff Bezos is famously known for eating octopus for breakfast during an important Amazon meeting. He enjoys Mediterranean cuisine, fine dining, and has been spotted at high-end restaurants in Miami and Seattle.

Since moving to Miami, Jeff Bezos has been spotted at upscale establishments including Carbone Miami, Casa Tua, Swan, and various waterfront restaurants in the Miami Beach area.

In a famous anecdote, Jeff Bezos ordered Mediterranean octopus for breakfast during a meeting, saying "You're the octopus I'm having for breakfast" to a potential business partner, demonstrating his unconventional approach to business and food.

Jeff Bezos announced his move to Miami in 2023, citing proximity to his parents, Blue Origin operations at Cape Canaveral, and a desire for a change. Florida's tax benefits were also noted as a factor.
