William's Favorite Comfort Foods

Chocolate Biscuit Cake

This no-bake cake made with dark chocolate and Rich Tea biscuits was Queen Elizabeth II's favorite. Prince William loved it so much that he requested it as his groom's cake at his 2011 Royal Wedding. The recipe has been a palace staple for generations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prince William's favorite food?
Prince William has stated that shepherd's pie is one of his favorite comfort foods. He also loves roast chicken, pizza, and the famous chocolate biscuit cake - a Royal Family tradition recipe that was served at his wedding.
Where does Prince William eat in London?
Prince William frequents private members clubs and discreet restaurants near Kensington Palace. The Royal Family values privacy when dining out, often choosing establishments with private rooms or a reputation for discretion.
What is the chocolate biscuit cake Prince William requested at his wedding?
The chocolate biscuit cake is a no-bake Royal Family recipe made with dark chocolate, butter, and Rich Tea biscuits. It was Queen Elizabeth II's favorite, and William requested it as his groom's cake at his 2011 wedding to Catherine.
Does Prince William cook?
While Catherine is known as the cook in the family, William has mentioned making breakfast for the children and enjoying family cooking time. He's been open about preferring simple, homemade meals.
What do Prince William and Kate feed their children?
The Wales family reportedly enjoys home-cooked meals including pasta, roast chicken, and pizza. Catherine is known for cooking for the family and ensuring balanced, healthy meals with occasional treats.
