How many Michelin stars does Gordon Ramsay have?

Gordon Ramsay currently holds 7 Michelin stars across his restaurant empire. Throughout his career, his restaurants have earned a total of 16 Michelin stars, including 3 stars at Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea, London, which has maintained its top rating since 2001.

Frequently Asked Questions

Gordon Ramsay currently holds 7 Michelin stars across his restaurant empire. His career total is 16 Michelin stars, including 3 stars at Restaurant Gordon Ramsay in Chelsea, London.

Gordon Ramsay owns and operates over 35 restaurants worldwide through Gordon Ramsay Restaurants. His empire spans London, Las Vegas, Dubai, Singapore, and more.

Gordon Ramsay's most famous signature dish is his Beef Wellington, featuring perfectly cooked beef fillet wrapped in mushroom duxelles and puff pastry. His scrambled eggs and pan-seared scallops are also iconic.

Gordon Ramsay trained under some of the world's greatest chefs including Marco Pierre White in London, Guy Savoy in France, and Albert Roux at Le Gavroche. This classical French training forms the foundation of his cooking.
