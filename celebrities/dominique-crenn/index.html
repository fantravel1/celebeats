Who is the first female chef with 3 Michelin stars in the United States?

Dominique Crenn became the first female chef to receive three Michelin stars in the United States in 2018 for her San Francisco restaurant Atelier Crenn. Born in Versailles, France, she pioneered "poetic culinaria" - a tasting menu where each course represents a line of poetry.

Breaking Barriers in Fine Dining

A Trailblazer's Journey

Dominique Crenn's path to becoming the first female 3-Michelin-star chef in the US was marked by determination and vision:

  • Adopted in France - Raised in Versailles by a family that valued art, food, and culture
  • 2011 - Opens Atelier Crenn in San Francisco
  • 2012 - First Michelin star
  • 2014 - Second Michelin star, World's Best Female Chef
  • 2018 - Third Michelin star - first female chef in US to achieve this
  • 2019 - Diagnosed with breast cancer, continued cooking through treatment
  • 2020 - Made Atelier Crenn entirely meat-free

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the first female chef with 3 Michelin stars in the US?
Dominique Crenn became the first female chef to receive three Michelin stars in the United States in 2018 for her San Francisco restaurant Atelier Crenn. This historic achievement cemented her status as one of the most important chefs in American culinary history.
What is poetic culinaria?
Poetic culinaria is Dominique Crenn's signature culinary philosophy where each course is inspired by a line of poetry. Her tasting menus at Atelier Crenn are presented as poems, with each dish representing a verse that tells a story about her life, memories, and emotions. Guests receive the poem but discover the dishes as they're served.
What restaurants does Dominique Crenn own?
Dominique Crenn owns Atelier Crenn (3 Michelin stars), Bar Crenn (1 Michelin star), and Boutique Crenn, all in San Francisco's Pacific Heights neighborhood. She previously operated Petit Crenn in Hayes Valley, which closed during the pandemic.
Is Atelier Crenn vegetarian?
Since 2020, Atelier Crenn has been entirely meat-free. Chef Crenn made the decision for sustainability and ethical reasons, though the restaurant still serves seafood. The menu focuses on vegetables, grains, and fish prepared with the same artistic precision that earned three Michelin stars.
Is Dominique Crenn married?
Dominique Crenn is engaged to actress Maria Bello. The couple announced their engagement in Paris in December 2019. Crenn has been open about their relationship and her identity, becoming an important LGBTQ+ figure in the culinary world.
