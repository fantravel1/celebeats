Is Jared Leto vegan?

Yes, Jared Leto is a strict vegan and has been for many years. His plant-based diet is part of his famously disciplined lifestyle that includes rock climbing, meditation, and regular exercise. This lifestyle is often credited for his remarkably youthful appearance well into his 50s.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jared Leto vegan?
Yes, Jared Leto is a strict vegan and has been for many years. He avoids all animal products as part of his disciplined health-focused lifestyle that also includes rock climbing and meditation.
Where does Jared Leto eat in LA?
Jared frequents vegan restaurants in Los Angeles including Crossroads Kitchen (LA's premier plant-based fine dining), Cafe Gratitude, and Gracias Madre for vegan Mexican cuisine.
Why does Jared Leto look so young?
Jared's youthful appearance is often attributed to his disciplined lifestyle: strict vegan diet, regular rock climbing and exercise, meditation, no alcohol, and general commitment to health and wellness.
What is Jared Leto known for?
Jared Leto is known as the frontman of 30 Seconds to Mars, his Oscar-winning performance in Dallas Buyers Club, roles in films like Requiem for a Dream and House of Gucci, his rock climbing, and his ageless appearance.
Does Jared Leto drink alcohol?
Jared Leto is known for not drinking alcohol as part of his clean, disciplined lifestyle. This abstinence, combined with his vegan diet and exercise regimen, contributes to his famously youthful appearance.
