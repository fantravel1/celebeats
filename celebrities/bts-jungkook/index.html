What does BTS Jungkook eat?

Jungkook's favorite foods include samgyeopsal (Korean pork belly BBQ), pizza, lamb skewers, and instant ramen. He's famous among ARMY for loving banana milk and plain white bread. During LA visits, he enjoys In-N-Out Burger, Korean BBQ in Koreatown, and American comfort food. His mukbang livestreams often feature late-night ramen and Korean fried chicken.

Korean Food Culture

Jungkook represents the food culture of Korea, often sharing meals on camera:

  • Mukbang Culture - Eating broadcasts where he enjoys meals with fans watching
  • Chimaek - Korean fried chicken with beer, a social dining tradition
  • Ramyeon - Late-night instant noodles, a Korean college student staple
  • Ssam - Wrapping BBQ meat in lettuce with garlic and ssamjang
  • Convenience Store Food - Triangle kimbap and banana milk runs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jungkook's favorite food?
Jungkook has mentioned his favorite foods include samgyeopsal (Korean pork belly BBQ), pizza, lamb skewers, and instant ramen. He's also famous for loving banana milk and plain white bread.
What Korean foods does Jungkook eat?
Jungkook enjoys traditional Korean foods including samgyeopsal, galbi (Korean BBQ ribs), jjigae (stews), bibimbap, and Korean fried chicken. He often shows these foods during VLive streams and BTS content.
Where does Jungkook eat in Los Angeles?
When in LA, Jungkook has been spotted at Koreatown BBQ restaurants, In-N-Out Burger (a BTS favorite), and various food spots. He's explored LA's diverse food scene during tours and his recent solo activities.
