Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Taylor Swift eat in New York?
Taylor frequents The Waverly Inn in the West Village and several Tribeca spots near her apartment including Locanda Verde, Bubby's, and The Odeon. She's known for intimate dinners at The Waverly Inn's cozy, candlelit setting.
What is Taylor Swift's favorite food?
Taylor has mentioned chicken tenders as one of her all-time favorite foods. She also loves Shake Shack burgers, cinnamon rolls, and anything she bakes herself - especially her famous chai chocolate chip cookies.
Does Taylor Swift bake?
Yes! Taylor is famous for baking cookies. Her chai chocolate chip cookies have been given to Ed Sheeran, the Kansas City Chiefs, SNL cast members, and countless other friends and collaborators. She's documented her baking on social media multiple times.
What does Taylor Swift order at The Waverly Inn?
While specific orders aren't always confirmed, The Waverly Inn is known for American comfort food including their famous chicken pot pie, burgers, and classic cocktails. Taylor has been spotted there on numerous date nights and friend dinners.
Does Taylor Swift follow a strict diet?
No, Taylor doesn't follow a strict diet. She's spoken about enjoying a balanced approach - eating healthy during tours while still indulging in comfort food favorites like chicken tenders and Shake Shack when she wants to.
