Frequently Asked Questions

What are Steven Spielberg's favorite restaurants?
Steven Spielberg frequents LA's top industry power dining spots. He's a longtime regular at Spago, The Grill on the Alley, and Giorgio Baldi. He's been going to Spago since it opened and uses these restaurants for deal-making, celebrations, and family gatherings.
Does Steven Spielberg keep kosher?
Yes, Steven Spielberg keeps kosher and observes Jewish dietary laws. This influences his restaurant choices, and many high-end establishments accommodate his requirements. His Jewish heritage is deeply important to him, as reflected in films like Schindler's List.
Where does Steven Spielberg live?
Steven Spielberg has multiple properties but is primarily based in Los Angeles, specifically in the Pacific Palisades area. This puts him close to many of his favorite Beverly Hills and Santa Monica dining spots.
Is Steven Spielberg friends with other directors?
Yes, Spielberg is famously close friends with George Lucas and other filmmakers. They often dine together at industry spots. He's also known for hosting gatherings and has relationships with chefs like Wolfgang Puck.
