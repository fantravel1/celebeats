Lady Gaga embraces her Italian-American heritage through food. Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lady Gaga's favorite food?
Lady Gaga loves authentic Italian food reflecting her heritage. Her favorites include her mother's pasta, classic NYC pizza, osso buco, and Italian desserts like tiramisu. Food from her family's restaurant Joanne Trattoria holds a special place.
Does Lady Gaga own a restaurant?
Yes, her family owns Joanne Trattoria on the Upper West Side of Manhattan. The restaurant is named after her late aunt Joanne Germanotta and serves traditional Italian-American cuisine. Her father Joe Germanotta runs the restaurant.
Where does Lady Gaga eat in New York?
Lady Gaga frequents classic NYC Italian spots including her family's Joanne Trattoria, Carbone in Greenwich Village, Rao's in East Harlem, and various neighborhood pizzerias. She's also been spotted at The Waverly Inn and Balthazar.
Where did Lady Gaga eat during her Vegas residency?
During her Enigma and Jazz & Piano residencies at Park MGM (2018-2022), Lady Gaga was spotted at various Vegas restaurants including Italian spots on the Strip, upscale steakhouses, and the diverse dining options at Park MGM and nearby hotels.
