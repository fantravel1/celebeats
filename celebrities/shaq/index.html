What restaurants does Shaq own?

Shaq has built an impressive food empire including Big Chicken (his fast-casual fried chicken concept), multiple Papa John's franchise locations, Krispy Kreme franchises, Auntie Anne's locations, and various other food investments. He joined the Papa John's board of directors in 2019 and has become one of the most successful athlete-restaurateurs in history.

Big Chicken Spotlight

Big Chicken is Shaq's signature restaurant concept, launched in Las Vegas in 2018. The menu features fried chicken sandwiches, tenders, and sides inspired by recipes from Shaq's family, particularly his mother Lucille. Signature items include the "Big Aristotle" and "Shaq Attack" sandwiches. The restaurants feature his larger-than-life personality with a fun atmosphere and generous portions. Big Chicken has expanded to locations across the US including airports, stadiums, and standalone restaurants, with plans for continued growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Shaquille O'Neal owns Big Chicken, a fast-casual fried chicken restaurant chain with locations across the US. He also owns multiple Papa John's franchise locations, Krispy Kreme franchises, Auntie Anne's locations, and has invested in various other food ventures.

Big Chicken is Shaq's fast-casual restaurant concept featuring fried chicken sandwiches, tenders, and sides. The menu is inspired by recipes from Shaq's family, and the restaurants feature his larger-than-life personality with massive portions and fun atmosphere.

Shaq owns multiple Papa John's franchise locations, primarily in the Atlanta area. He joined the Papa John's board of directors in 2019 and became one of the brand's most visible ambassadors while expanding his franchise ownership.

Shaq has a legendary appetite matching his 7'1" frame. He's known to love fried chicken (hence Big Chicken), pizza, BBQ, and comfort food in general. While he maintained strict diets during his playing career, he's more relaxed now and enjoys indulging in his favorite foods.
