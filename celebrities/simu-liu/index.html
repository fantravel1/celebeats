What does Simu Liu eat?

Simu Liu loves authentic Chinese cuisine including dim sum, hand-pulled noodles, and dumplings. Born in Harbin, China and raised in Canada, he celebrates his Chinese-Canadian heritage through food. He's a champion of Toronto's diverse food scene and vocal advocate for authentic Asian food representation beyond stereotypes.
