Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Celine Song eat in New York?
Based in Brooklyn, Celine Song is connected to NYC's vibrant Korean restaurant scene including acclaimed spots like Atomix and Her. As part of the theater and film community, she's also connected to downtown Manhattan's dining scene.
What is Celine Song's food background?
Born in Seoul, South Korea and raised in Toronto, Canada, Song has a bicultural food background. Her Korean heritage is central to her identity, reflected beautifully in Past Lives which features authentic Korean food and cultural scenes.
Does Celine Song enjoy Korean food?
As a Korean-Canadian filmmaker whose work centers on Korean identity, Song has deep connections to Korean cuisine. NYC's world-class Korean restaurants from Koreatown to Michelin-starred spots represent an important part of her cultural heritage.
Where is Celine Song based?
Celine Song is based in Brooklyn, New York. Before film, she was an established playwright in NYC's theater scene. Brooklyn's diverse creative community and food scene align with her artistic sensibility.
What is Past Lives about?
Past Lives is Celine Song's Oscar-nominated feature debut about two childhood friends from Seoul who reconnect as adults in New York City. The film explores themes of fate, immigration, and the roads not taken, with Korean culture and food playing meaningful roles.
