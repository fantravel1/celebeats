Hyderabadi Heritage

The City of Biryani

Hyderabad is renowned for its unique culinary traditions:

  • Hyderabadi Biryani - The city's crown jewel, world-famous for its layered rice and meat
  • Haleem - Slow-cooked meat and wheat dish, especially during Ramadan
  • Mughlai Cuisine - Rich, aromatic dishes from Nizam era
  • Irani Chai - Unique tea culture from Persian influences
