Frequently Asked Questions

What is Usher's favorite food?
Usher loves Southern soul food from his Atlanta upbringing. His favorites include fried chicken, mac and cheese, collard greens, and cornbread. He's known for appreciating authentic soul food restaurants in Atlanta.
Where does Usher eat in Atlanta?
Usher frequents classic Atlanta soul food spots including Busy Bee Cafe (a historic spot since 1947), South City Kitchen in Midtown, and various restaurants in Buckhead. He's also been spotted at newer spots like Slutty Vegan.
Where does Usher eat during his Vegas residency?
During his "My Way" residency at Park MGM, Usher enjoys the diverse dining options on the Vegas Strip. He's been spotted at upscale steakhouses, Asian restaurants, and various celebrity hotspots in Las Vegas.
Does Usher follow a specific diet?
While Usher maintains his famously fit physique with a disciplined fitness regimen, he's not known for following restrictive diets. He balances healthy eating with enjoying his Southern comfort food favorites in moderation.
