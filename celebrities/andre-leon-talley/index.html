What was Andre Leon Talley's favorite food?

Andre Leon Talley loved Southern soul food, deeply influenced by his grandmother Bennie Frances Davis who raised him in Durham, North Carolina. Her cooking - including fried chicken, collard greens, and sweet potato pie - shaped his lifelong appreciation for comfort food and Southern cuisine.

A Life in Fashion

Remembering Andre Leon Talley

1948 - 2022

A groundbreaking figure who brought Southern grace to the highest echelons of fashion. From his grandmother's kitchen in Durham to the front row at Chanel, Andre Leon Talley never forgot where he came from.

Andre's Legacy

Fashion's Southern Gentleman

Andre Leon Talley's impact on fashion and culture:

  • First African-American male creative director at Vogue
  • Editor-at-large at Vogue for decades
  • Close friend and confidant of designers like Karl Lagerfeld
  • Subject of documentary "The Gospel According to Andre"
  • Author of "The Chiffon Trenches" memoir
  • Iconic caftans became his signature style

"My grandmother taught me everything about grace and dignity. She was the most elegant woman I've ever known."

Frequently Asked Questions

What was Andre Leon Talley's favorite food?
Andre Leon Talley loved Southern soul food, deeply influenced by his grandmother Bennie Frances Davis who raised him in Durham, North Carolina. Her cooking including fried chicken, collard greens, and sweet potato pie shaped his lifelong appreciation for Southern cuisine.
Where did Andre Leon Talley grow up?
Andre Leon Talley was born in Washington D.C. but was raised by his grandmother in Durham, North Carolina. His Southern upbringing profoundly influenced his character, values, and love for soul food throughout his life.
What was Andre Leon Talley known for?
Andre Leon Talley was a legendary fashion journalist who served as creative director and editor-at-large at Vogue. He was known for his signature caftans, larger-than-life personality, and being one of the most influential Black figures in fashion history.
Who was Andre Leon Talley's grandmother?
Bennie Frances Davis raised Andre in Durham, North Carolina after his parents couldn't care for him. He credited her with teaching him about elegance, grace, and dignity. Her cooking and influence shaped his entire life.
When did Andre Leon Talley pass away?
Andre Leon Talley passed away on January 18, 2022, at age 73. He left behind an incredible legacy in fashion journalism and broke barriers as one of the most prominent Black figures in the fashion industry.
