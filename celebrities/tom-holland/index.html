Frequently Asked Questions

What are Tom Holland's favorite restaurants?
Tom Holland frequents health-focused restaurants in both London and LA. In London, he enjoys The Ivy Chelsea Garden and Chiltern Firehouse. In LA, he's a regular at Erewhon Market, Cafe Gratitude, and Craig's. He prioritizes places with high-protein, clean eating options.
What does Tom Holland eat for his Spider-Man physique?
Tom follows a high-protein diet when training for Spider-Man. This includes lean proteins (chicken, fish, eggs), complex carbohydrates (sweet potatoes, brown rice), and plenty of vegetables. He works with nutritionists and trainers to optimize his diet for the demanding stunt work.
Does Tom Holland follow a strict diet?
Tom follows a disciplined diet during filming but allows himself treats during off-periods. He's mentioned loving a full English breakfast and isn't overly restrictive. His approach is about balance - clean eating most of the time with occasional indulgences.
Is Tom Holland vegan or vegetarian?
No, Tom Holland is not vegan or vegetarian. He eats a balanced diet that includes lean meats, fish, and eggs for protein. However, he does enjoy plant-based restaurants and incorporates plenty of vegetables into his meals.
