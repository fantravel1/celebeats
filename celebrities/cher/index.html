Where does Cher eat in Malibu?

Cher is a regular at Malibu's finest restaurants, including Nobu Malibu for Japanese cuisine, Giorgio Baldi for Italian, and Malibu Farm for health-conscious fare. She also relies on a private chef for meals at her iconic Malibu mansion, maintaining her famously strict diet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Cher eat in Malibu?
Cher frequents Nobu Malibu for Japanese cuisine, Giorgio Baldi on PCH for Italian, and Malibu Farm for health-conscious fare. She also relies heavily on her private chef at home for most meals.
What is Cher's diet?
Cher has followed a strict, health-conscious diet for decades. She focuses on lean proteins, fresh vegetables, and avoids processed foods. This disciplined approach is credited as part of her ageless appearance.
Does Cher have a private chef?
Yes, Cher works with private chefs at her Malibu home to prepare meals that meet her specific dietary requirements. This ensures she maintains her strict eating habits consistently.
What does Cher eat on tour?
During tours and residencies, Cher maintains her clean eating habits with the help of tour chefs. She prefers simple, fresh preparations and has been known to request specific healthy options at venues.
Is Cher vegetarian?
Cher is not strictly vegetarian, but she eats a primarily plant-heavy diet with lean proteins. She has explored various dietary approaches over the decades, including macrobiotic eating, while maintaining a focus on fresh, clean ingredients.
