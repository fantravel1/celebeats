Frequently Asked Questions

Does Felix really bake brownies?
Yes! Felix is famous for his brownie baking. His "Felix's brownies" became viral among STAY (Stray Kids fans), and he regularly shares baking content on vlives. He bakes treats for all the Stray Kids members.
What is Felix's favorite food?
Felix loves sweets and baking, particularly brownies. His Australian background also gives him a love for brunch culture, flat whites, and cafe fare. He enjoys both Korean and Western food.
Where does Felix eat in Seoul?
Felix frequents cafes and brunch spots that remind him of Australian cafe culture. He also enjoys Korean BBQ with Stray Kids members and trendy Gangnam restaurants and bakeries.
Does Felix have a sweet tooth?
Yes! Felix is known for loving sweet foods and desserts. His baking hobby and love for treats match his sunny "sunshine" personality that fans adore.
What does Felix bake besides brownies?
Beyond his famous brownies, Felix bakes cookies, cakes for member birthdays, and various seasonal treats. He often shares his baking adventures with fans through vlives and social media.
