The Tim Hortons Collaboration

In 2021, Justin Bieber partnered with Tim Hortons to launch "Timbiebs" - a line of Timbits flavors and merchandise. The Canadian connection runs deep: Bieber grew up in Stratford, Ontario and has been a lifelong Tim Hortons fan. The collab includes Chocolate White Fudge, Sour Cream Chocolate Chip, and Birthday Cake Waffle Timbits.

What is Justin Bieber's favorite restaurant?
Justin Bieber has several favorite restaurants. He's famously loyal to Tim Hortons (he even has a collaboration with them), loves Nobu for sushi dates with Hailey, and frequents Craig's and The Nice Guy in West Hollywood. He's also been spotted at Il Pastaio in Beverly Hills.
