Who is the Barefoot Contessa and does she own a restaurant?

Ina Garten, known as the Barefoot Contessa, is a beloved Food Network host and cookbook author. She does not currently own a restaurant, but she owned a specialty food store called Barefoot Contessa in East Hampton from 1978-1996. Today, she's famous for her approachable French-inspired recipes and Hamptons entertaining style.

The Hamptons Entertaining Lifestyle

From White House to Barefoot Contessa

Ina Garten's journey from government policy to culinary icon is uniquely American:

  • 1974 - Works on nuclear policy at the White House Office of Management and Budget
  • 1978 - Buys a small specialty food store in the Hamptons called Barefoot Contessa
  • 1996 - Sells the store after building it into a beloved local institution
  • 1999 - Publishes first cookbook, "The Barefoot Contessa Cookbook"
  • 2002 - Debuts on Food Network with "Barefoot Contessa"
  • Present - 13 cookbooks, 5 Emmys, and an empire built on "good ingredients, simply prepared"

The Barefoot Contessa Philosophy

"How Easy Is That?"

Ina Garten's cooking philosophy has resonated with millions:

  • "Store-bought is fine" - Permission to take shortcuts with quality ingredients
  • French technique, American accessibility - Julia Child meets weeknight cooking
  • Quality over complexity - Good ingredients, simply prepared
  • Cooking with love - Jeffrey is the ultimate test audience
  • Entertaining, not impressing - Make your guests comfortable, not intimidated

"You can be a really good home cook without being a chef. Chefs have to worry about a million things. Home cooks can just focus on making delicious food."

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ina Garten own a restaurant?
No, Ina Garten does not currently own a restaurant. She previously owned the Barefoot Contessa specialty food store in East Hampton from 1978 to 1996. After selling it, she focused on writing cookbooks and hosting her Food Network show. She is primarily known as a home cook and entertainer, not a restaurant chef.
What is Ina Garten's most famous recipe?
Ina Garten's most famous recipe is her Perfect Roast Chicken, which she frequently makes for her husband Jeffrey. The simple recipe of chicken seasoned with salt, pepper, and butter, roasted at high heat, has become synonymous with her cooking philosophy: good ingredients, simply prepared.
Where does Ina Garten live?
Ina Garten lives in East Hampton in the Hamptons on Long Island, New York. Her beautiful barn-style home with its famous garden has been featured extensively on Barefoot Contessa. She and Jeffrey also maintain an apartment in New York City and spend time in Paris annually.
What does "store-bought is fine" mean?
"Store-bought is fine" is one of Ina Garten's famous catchphrases. It means it's okay to use high-quality pre-made ingredients rather than making everything from scratch. She emphasizes that great cooking is about knowing when to take shortcuts and when to put in extra effort - using good store-bought chicken stock or puff pastry is perfectly acceptable.
Who is Jeffrey Garten?
Jeffrey Garten is Ina Garten's husband of over 55 years. He was Dean of the Yale School of Management and served as Undersecretary of Commerce in the Clinton administration. On Barefoot Contessa, he's famous as Ina's devoted taste-tester and the inspiration for many recipes. Their loving relationship is central to the show's appeal.
