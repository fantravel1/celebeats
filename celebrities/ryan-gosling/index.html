Does Ryan Gosling own a restaurant?

Yes! Ryan Gosling co-owns Tagine, a Moroccan restaurant in Beverly Hills. He opened it with business partner Ben Beallieu and has been involved since the early 2000s. The intimate restaurant serves authentic Moroccan cuisine including tagines, couscous, and traditional appetizers. He also frequents classic LA spots like Musso & Frank and Little Dom's.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ryan Gosling own a restaurant?
Yes, Ryan Gosling co-owns Tagine, a Moroccan restaurant in Beverly Hills. He opened it with business partner Ben Beallieu in the early 2000s. The restaurant serves authentic Moroccan cuisine in an intimate setting.
What are Ryan Gosling's favorite restaurants?
Besides his own restaurant Tagine, Ryan frequents classic LA establishments like Musso & Frank Grill, Dan Tana's, and Little Dom's in Los Feliz. He appreciates old Hollywood spots and neighborhood Italian restaurants.
Where is Tagine restaurant located?
Tagine is located in Beverly Hills, California. It's an intimate Moroccan restaurant serving traditional dishes like tagines, couscous, and Moroccan appetizers.
Is Ryan Gosling Canadian?
Yes, Ryan Gosling was born in London, Ontario, Canada. While he now lives in Los Angeles, he maintains pride in his Canadian roots.
