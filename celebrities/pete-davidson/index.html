What does Pete Davidson eat?

Pete Davidson loves New York comfort food, especially Staten Island favorites. He's known for his love of pizza, diner food, and late-night eats. His laid-back approach to food matches his comedy style.

Staten Island Food Culture

Borough Pride

Pete's Staten Island roots run deep in his food preferences:

  • Staten Island Pizza - Local pizzerias over Manhattan spots
  • Italian-American Food - Classic borough Italian joints
  • Diners - Late-night diner culture
  • Bagels & Deli - NYC deli tradition

The Ferry Lifestyle

Growing up taking the Staten Island Ferry shaped Pete's connection to both his borough and Manhattan. The commute means familiarity with food spots on both sides of the harbor.

SNL & Late Night Eating

30 Rock Life

Years at SNL meant mastering Midtown late-night dining:

  • Post-Show Eats - 1-2 AM dining after live shows
  • Writer Room Snacks - Week-long production fuel
  • Midtown Spots - Quick bites near 30 Rock
  • Cast Dinners - Group outings with SNL crew
  • Green Room Food - Backstage hospitality

Comedian Schedule

Comedy club schedules mean late dinners and after-midnight meals. Pete's eating patterns reflect the nocturnal lifestyle of a working comedian.
