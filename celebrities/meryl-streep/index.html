Where does Meryl Streep eat in New York City?

Meryl Streep favors New York's classic, established restaurants over trendy newcomers. She's been spotted at Il Cantinori in Greenwich Village for Italian cuisine, The River Cafe in Brooklyn for special occasions, and Cafe Luxembourg on the Upper West Side. Her choices reflect a preference for intimate, well-established venues with exceptional food.

The Julie & Julia Connection

Learning to Cook Like Julia Child

For her role as Julia Child in Julie & Julia (2009), Meryl immersed herself in French cooking:

  • Studied cooking at Julia Child's alma mater, Le Cordon Bleu
  • Learned to make beef bourguignon, sole meuniere, and other French classics
  • The role deepened her appreciation for home cooking and food culture
  • Earned an Oscar nomination for her portrayal
