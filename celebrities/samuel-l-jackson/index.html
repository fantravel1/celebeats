Where does Samuel L. Jackson like to eat?

Samuel L. Jackson combines his love of fine dining with his golf obsession. He frequents upscale golf club dining rooms at prestigious courses worldwide, plus high-end steakhouses and celebrity hotspots in LA and NYC. His golf clause in movie contracts means he's always near a great clubhouse kitchen.

Golf Club Dining

19th Hole Favorites

As one of Hollywood's most dedicated golfers, Sam Jackson has dined at some of the world's most exclusive golf club restaurants:

  • Riviera Country Club - Pacific Palisades, CA
  • St Andrews Links - Scotland's legendary courses
  • Pebble Beach - The Bench restaurant
  • TPC Sawgrass - Home of The Players Championship

Jackson reportedly has a clause in his contracts allowing him to golf twice a week during filming, which means plenty of clubhouse dining.

The Golf-Food Connection

Samuel L. Jackson plays golf nearly every single day. He's played on sets, between takes, and at courses around the world. His love of the game naturally extends to the culinary experience that comes with it.

Post-round meals at the clubhouse have become a ritual. Whether it's a classic club sandwich after 18 holes or a full steak dinner to celebrate a good round, Jackson appreciates the tradition of golf club dining.

He's also known for his love of good whiskey, often enjoying a single malt after a round - a tradition he's carried from Scottish links to American country clubs.

Southern Roots

Born in Washington D.C. and raised in Atlanta, Samuel L. Jackson maintains a deep appreciation for Southern cuisine. He's spoken about his love for:

  • Fried chicken - A childhood comfort food
  • Collard greens - Classic Southern side
  • Mac and cheese - The ultimate comfort dish
  • Peach cobbler - Georgia dessert favorite
