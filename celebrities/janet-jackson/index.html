Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Janet Jackson eat in Los Angeles?
Janet Jackson prefers private dining in LA, frequenting upscale spots like Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, Nobu Malibu, and Mastro's in Beverly Hills. She also relies heavily on private chefs for meals at home.
What is Janet Jackson's diet like?
Janet follows a wellness-focused diet that emphasizes balance. After years of public weight fluctuations, she now focuses on lean proteins, vegetables, and hydration. She works with nutritionists and personal chefs.
Does Janet Jackson live in London?
Janet splits her time between Los Angeles and London. She lived in London during her marriage and continues to spend significant time there, enjoying the city's dining scene and privacy.
What kind of food does Janet Jackson like?
Janet enjoys Japanese cuisine, particularly sushi at Nobu locations worldwide. She also has soul food roots from her Gary, Indiana upbringing and appreciates Italian dining at spots like Giorgio Baldi.
Has Janet Jackson spoken about her relationship with food?
Yes, Janet has been open about her weight journey throughout her career. She partnered with Nutrisystem and has spoken in interviews about finding a healthy, balanced approach to eating after years of yo-yo dieting.
