How did Crown Princess Victoria overcome her eating disorder?

Crown Princess Victoria publicly discussed her struggle with an eating disorder in the late 1990s. She took time away from public duties to focus on recovery and has since become an inspiring figure for her openness about mental health. Today, she champions healthy relationships with food and an active outdoor lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Did Crown Princess Victoria have an eating disorder?
Yes, Crown Princess Victoria publicly discussed her struggle with an eating disorder in the late 1990s. She took time away from public duties to recover and has since become an inspiring figure for her openness about mental health.
What Swedish foods does Crown Princess Victoria enjoy?
Crown Princess Victoria embraces traditional Swedish cuisine and food traditions. She participates in Swedish celebrations including Midsummer, Christmas traditions, and crayfish parties. The royal family supports Swedish food culture.
Does Crown Princess Victoria promote healthy eating?
Yes, having overcome an eating disorder, Victoria now promotes healthy, balanced relationships with food. She's an outdoor enthusiast with an active lifestyle and has become an inspiring figure for recovery.
Is Crown Princess Victoria the future Queen of Sweden?
Yes, Victoria is the heir apparent to the Swedish throne and will become Queen of Sweden. She's the eldest child of King Carl XVI Gustaf and Queen Silvia.
What is Victoria's approach to wellness?
Victoria takes a holistic approach to wellness that includes an active outdoor lifestyle with hiking and nature activities, healthy balanced eating without restriction, and openness about mental health and recovery.
