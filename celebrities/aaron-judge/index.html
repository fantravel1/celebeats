Fueling the Power

At 6'7" and 282 pounds, Aaron Judge requires an enormous amount of calories and protein to maintain his powerful frame. His diet is carefully managed by the Yankees' nutritional staff, but when dining out, Judge gravitates toward protein-rich meals at NYC's finest steakhouses. A man his size needs serious fuel.

Athletic Diet

Season Nutrition

During the grueling 162-game MLB season, Judge follows a strict nutritional program. The Yankees employ top nutritionists who work with players to optimize performance, recovery, and injury prevention.

Protein Requirements

Given his size, Judge likely requires 200+ grams of protein daily. This makes steakhouses a natural fit, but his diet also includes lean proteins like chicken and fish, complex carbohydrates, and plenty of vegetables.

Game Day Meals

Pre-game meals focus on providing sustained energy without weighing him down. Post-game nutrition emphasizes recovery with protein and carbohydrates to replenish glycogen stores.

Hydration

Playing in New York summers requires exceptional hydration. Judge maintains strict fluid intake protocols, especially during day games and the dog days of summer.
