What does Adele eat?

Adele is known for her love of British comfort food - a proper English breakfast, fish and chips, and endless cups of tea. Despite her wellness journey, she's been open about still enjoying McDonald's chicken nuggets and not being overly restrictive. Living in LA, she's embraced the healthy California lifestyle while staying true to her British roots.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Adele's favorite food?
Adele loves British comfort food, especially a proper English breakfast, fish and chips, and her beloved cup of tea. She's also famous for loving McDonald's chicken nuggets - she once had it delivered to the Brit Awards.
Does Adele follow a specific diet?
Adele has tried the Sirtfood Diet and made healthier choices as part of her wellness journey, but she's been clear that she's not restrictive. She enjoys a balanced approach, still indulging in her favorite comfort foods while incorporating healthier options.
Where does Adele eat in Los Angeles?
Adele frequents upscale LA restaurants including Nobu Malibu, Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, Craig's in West Hollywood, and The Nice Guy. She's often spotted dining with celebrity friends like Nicole Richie.
Where does Adele eat during her Vegas residency?
During her "Weekends with Adele" residency at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace, Adele has access to world-class dining at Caesars including Gordon Ramsay's Hell's Kitchen, Nobu, and the various fine dining options on the Las Vegas Strip.
