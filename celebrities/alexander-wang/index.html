Where does Alexander Wang like to eat?

Alexander Wang is known for his love of downtown NYC's dining scene, particularly late-night spots in the Lower East Side and Chinatown. His Taiwanese heritage influences his appreciation for authentic Chinese cuisine, and he's frequently spotted at neighborhood favorites rather than flashy celebrity hotspots.
