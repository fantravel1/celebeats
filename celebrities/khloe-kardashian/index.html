What is Khloe Kardashian's diet?

Khloe Kardashian follows a high-protein, low-carb diet with 7 small meals per day. She focuses on lean proteins like chicken and fish, plenty of vegetables, and healthy fats. She avoids dairy, processed foods, and refined carbs. Her disciplined approach helped her achieve her famous transformation.

Khloe's Fitness-Focused Diet

The eating plan behind her incredible transformation.

Core Principle

High Protein, Low Carb

Khloe's diet centers on lean proteins like chicken, fish, and egg whites. She limits carbohydrates to complex carbs like brown rice and sweet potatoes, mostly around workouts.

Tier A
Meal Structure

7 Small Meals Daily

Rather than 3 large meals, Khloe eats 7 smaller portions throughout the day to keep her metabolism running and blood sugar stable. Each meal includes protein.

Tier A
Restriction

Dairy-Free Living

Like her sisters, Khloe avoids dairy products. She uses almond milk, coconut milk, and other alternatives. She's found this helps with inflammation and skin.

Tier A
Hydration

Water & Protein Shakes

Khloe drinks plenty of water throughout the day and incorporates protein shakes, especially post-workout. She avoids sugary drinks and alcohol.
