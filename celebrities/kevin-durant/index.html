Frequently Asked Questions

What food companies has Kevin Durant invested in?
KD's Thirty Five Ventures has invested in Postmates (before Uber acquisition), Pieology pizza chain, and various food tech startups. His food investment portfolio is one of the most diverse among NBA players.
Where does Kevin Durant eat in Phoenix?
Since joining the Suns, Durant has been spotted at Phoenix and Scottsdale steakhouses like Steak 44 and Mastro's. He's also connected to Pieology through his investment in the chain.
What is Kevin Durant's favorite food?
Growing up in the D.C. area, Durant developed a love for Maryland crab and Chesapeake Bay seafood. He's also invested in pizza (Pieology) and enjoys steakhouse dining like many NBA players.
Did Kevin Durant invest in Postmates?
Yes, Durant was an early investor in Postmates through Thirty Five Ventures. When Uber acquired Postmates for $2.65 billion in 2020, Durant's investment paid off significantly.
Does Kevin Durant follow a strict diet?
Despite his slim frame (earning the nickname "Slim Reaper"), Durant maintains elite conditioning through careful nutrition. He works with team nutritionists while balancing his genuine interest in food through investments and dining.
