What is the story of Prince Harry's roast chicken proposal?

Prince Harry proposed to Meghan Markle while she was making roast chicken at their Nottingham Cottage home at Kensington Palace. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the story of Prince Harry's roast chicken proposal?
Prince Harry proposed to Meghan Markle while she was making roast chicken at their Nottingham Cottage home. Harry got down on one knee unexpectedly, and Meghan said "Can I say yes?" before he even finished asking. The story was shared in their BBC engagement interview.
Where does Prince Harry eat in Montecito?
Harry and Meghan frequent several Montecito establishments including Tre Lune for Italian cuisine and Lucky's steakhouse. They're known for enjoying the laid-back California dining scene near their home.
What is Prince Harry's favorite food?
Harry loves roast chicken (famously proposed over it), BBQ, and has developed a taste for African cuisine through his charity work. Since moving to California, he's also embraced healthier eating options.
Where did Harry and Meghan have their first dates?
Harry and Meghan had early dates at Soho House in London, where they could meet discreetly. They also spent time at Dean Street Townhouse and other private members clubs during their courtship.
Does Prince Harry cook?
While Meghan is known as the cook in the family, Harry has mentioned enjoying BBQ and grilling, especially since moving to California where outdoor cooking is popular. The couple enjoys cooking together at home.
