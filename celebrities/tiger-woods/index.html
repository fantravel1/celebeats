Where does Tiger Woods like to eat?

Tiger Woods is a dedicated steakhouse enthusiast who frequents upscale establishments across the country. He owns The Woods Jupiter, his own restaurant and sports bar in Florida, and has been spotted at premium steakhouses including STK, Morton's, and Ruth's Chris. His preference for high-quality beef and classic American cuisine reflects his straightforward approach to dining.

The Woods Jupiter

Tiger Woods opened The Woods Jupiter in 2015, bringing his vision of a modern sports bar and restaurant to his hometown area. The 5,800-square-foot venue features 100+ TVs, craft cocktails, and a menu of elevated American comfort food. Popular dishes include signature burgers, wings, and shareable appetizers. The restaurant has become a local favorite and attracts golf fans from around the world hoping to catch a glimpse of the owner.
