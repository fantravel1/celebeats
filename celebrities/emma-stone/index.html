Frequently Asked Questions

What is Emma Stone's favorite food?
Emma Stone loves carbs - pizza, pasta, French fries, and bread are all favorites she's mentioned in interviews. She's said she could never give up carbohydrates and doesn't believe in restrictive dieting.
Where does Emma Stone eat in Los Angeles?
Emma frequents contemporary LA spots including Gjelina in Venice, Jon & Vinny's for Italian, Petit Trois for French bistro fare, and Osteria Mozza. She appreciates restaurants with great food in relaxed settings.
Does Emma Stone follow a special diet?
No, Emma Stone doesn't follow a restrictive diet. She takes a balanced approach, enjoying her favorite foods (especially carbs) while being generally mindful of nutrition. She's spoken against deprivation diets.
Where is Emma Stone from?
Emma Stone was born and raised in Scottsdale, Arizona. She moved to Los Angeles as a teenager to pursue acting. Her Arizona upbringing gave her an appreciation for Southwestern and Mexican cuisine.
What type of cuisine does Emma Stone prefer?
Emma Stone loves Italian cuisine (pasta, pizza), contemporary California cooking (like Gjelina's farm-to-table fare), and French bistro food. She appreciates quality cooking but isn't pretentious - comfort food is always welcome.
