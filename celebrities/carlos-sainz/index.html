Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Carlos Sainz eat in Madrid?
Carlos frequents Ten Con Ten in Salamanca district, a celebrity-favorite restaurant. He also enjoys traditional Spanish tapas bars and fine dining establishments. As the son of rally legend Carlos Sainz Sr., he's well-known in Madrid's restaurant scene.
What is Carlos Sainz's favorite Spanish food?
Carlos enjoys traditional Spanish cuisine including tapas, jamón ibérico, tortilla española, and Madrid's famous cocido. His Spanish heritage means a deep appreciation for authentic Spanish culinary traditions.
Does Carlos Sainz eat Italian food?
Yes, as a Ferrari driver, Carlos spends significant time in Italy. He's developed an appreciation for Italian cuisine, especially in Maranello and Milan where Ferrari events are held. Pasta and Italian classics complement his Spanish diet.
Where do Ferrari drivers eat in Maranello?
Ferrari drivers enjoy Maranello's local trattorias and Emilia-Romagna's famous cuisine. The region is known for prosciutto, Parmigiano-Reggiano, balsamic vinegar, and rich pasta dishes. Ferrari hospitality also provides world-class dining.
Is Carlos Sainz's father also into food?
Carlos Sainz Sr., the two-time World Rally Champion, shares his son's appreciation for Spanish cuisine. The Sainz family maintains strong Spanish traditions, including gathering around good food when together in Madrid.
