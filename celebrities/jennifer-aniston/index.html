What is the Jennifer Aniston Friends salad?

The viral "Jennifer Aniston salad" became famous when Courteney Cox revealed she and Jen ate the same salad every day for 10 years on the Friends set. The recipe includes bulgur wheat, cucumbers, parsley, mint, red onion, chickpeas, pistachios, and feta with lemon dressing. However, Aniston later clarified on social media that she actually ate a Cobb salad - "The salad that I ate on Friends was a Cobb salad!"

The Famous Friends Salad

The "Jennifer Aniston Salad" (Viral Version)

The recipe that went viral, shared by co-star Courteney Cox:

  • Bulgur wheat (cooked and cooled)
  • Diced cucumbers
  • Fresh parsley and mint, chopped
  • Diced red onion
  • Chickpeas
  • Crumbled feta cheese
  • Pistachios
  • Lemon juice and olive oil dressing

Note: Jen clarified she actually ate a Cobb salad on set, but this healthy grain bowl became famous as "her" salad.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Jennifer Aniston Friends salad?
The viral salad recipe includes bulgur wheat, cucumbers, parsley, mint, red onion, chickpeas, pistachios, and feta with lemon dressing. However, Aniston clarified that she actually ate a Cobb salad on the Friends set - the viral recipe was shared by Courteney Cox.
What is Jennifer Aniston's diet?
Jennifer follows intermittent fasting (16:8 method), starts with celery juice, eats lean proteins and lots of vegetables. She focuses on clean eating but allows herself indulgences like Mexican food and wine in moderation.
Where does Jennifer Aniston eat in Los Angeles?
Jennifer frequents Sunset Tower Hotel, Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, Soho House, and The Ivy. She prefers upscale spots with healthy options and good privacy.
What does Jennifer Aniston eat for breakfast?
Jennifer typically practices intermittent fasting and doesn't eat breakfast in the traditional sense. She starts with celery juice, followed by coffee with collagen peptides. Her first real meal is usually around noon.
What is Jennifer Aniston's guilty pleasure food?
Jennifer has said Mexican food is her guilty pleasure - chips and guacamole, tacos, and margaritas. She enjoys pasta and wine as well when she wants to indulge.
