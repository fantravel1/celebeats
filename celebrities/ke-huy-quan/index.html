What is Ke Huy Quan's background?

Ke Huy Quan was born in Saigon, Vietnam and came to the United States as a refugee. His Vietnamese heritage deeply influences his appreciation for Vietnamese cuisine. Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Ke Huy Quan from?
Ke Huy Quan was born on August 20, 1971, in Saigon, Vietnam. He came to the United States as a refugee and settled in Los Angeles, where his family became part of the Vietnamese-American community.
What did Ke Huy Quan win an Oscar for?
Ke Huy Quan won the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for Everything Everywhere All at Once (2022). It was an incredible comeback after he stepped away from acting for nearly 20 years to work behind the scenes.
What was Ke Huy Quan's first movie?
Ke Huy Quan's first major role was Short Round in Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom (1984). He followed that with Data in The Goonies (1985), making him a beloved child star of the 1980s.
Where does Ke Huy Quan eat in LA?
Based in Los Angeles, Ke Huy Quan enjoys the city's Vietnamese food scene, particularly in Little Saigon (Orange County). As an Oscar winner, he also frequents Hollywood industry spots during awards season.
What is Ke Huy Quan's heritage?
Ke Huy Quan is Vietnamese-American. Born in Saigon, he came to America as a refugee. His Vietnamese heritage deeply influences his appreciation for Vietnamese cuisine and connection to the Vietnamese-American community in LA.
