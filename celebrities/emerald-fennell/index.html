Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Emerald Fennell eat in London?
Fennell is connected to London's exclusive private club scene including Annabel's in Mayfair and creative industry spots like The Groucho Club. Born into London's cultural elite, she's deeply familiar with high-society dining.
Does Emerald Fennell host dinner parties?
Yes, Fennell is known for being an enthusiastic dinner party host. Her elaborate entertaining style mirrors the theatrical dining scenes in her films like Saltburn, which features iconic dinner party sequences.
What is Emerald Fennell's background?
Fennell comes from a prominent London family - her father is jewelry designer Theo Fennell. She was educated at Oxford University and has deep connections to British high society, which she satirizes brilliantly in films like Saltburn.
What has Emerald Fennell directed?
Fennell directed Saltburn (2023) and Promising Young Woman (2020), which won the Oscar for Best Original Screenplay. She also served as showrunner on Killing Eve season 2 and acted as Camilla in The Crown.
What makes Saltburn's dining scenes memorable?
Saltburn features elaborate formal dinners in an aristocratic English manor, capturing the theatrical nature of upper-class British entertaining. The dining scenes serve as a stage for class dynamics and dark comedy.
