Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tom Brady's TB12 diet?
The TB12 diet is an anti-inflammatory eating plan that's approximately 80% plant-based. Tom Brady avoids nightshades (tomatoes, peppers, eggplant), processed foods, sugar, white flour, and most dairy. He focuses on organic vegetables, lean proteins, and drinks at least half his body weight in ounces of water daily.
What foods does Tom Brady not eat?
Tom Brady avoids nightshade vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, eggplant, potatoes), white sugar, white flour, MSG, most dairy, coffee, and highly processed foods. He also limits his fruit intake and avoids strawberries entirely. He hasn't eaten a strawberry in his entire life by his own admission.
Where does Tom Brady eat in Miami?
Tom Brady has been spotted at several Miami restaurants during his time with the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and post-retirement. His preferences lean toward health-focused establishments, though he's been seen at Carbone Miami and Prime 112 for occasional dinners.
Does Tom Brady ever have cheat meals?
Tom Brady does occasionally indulge. He's admitted to having pizza and beer after Super Bowl wins, and allows himself more flexibility during the off-season. However, his definition of 'cheat' is stricter than most‚Äîhe might add avocado ice cream or a little extra dark chocolate.
