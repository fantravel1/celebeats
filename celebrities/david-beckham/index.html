Where does David Beckham eat?

David Beckham frequents upscale restaurants in both London and Miami. In London, he's regularly spotted at Scott's in Mayfair, Chiltern Firehouse, and The River Cafe. In Miami, as Inter Miami co-owner, he dines at Zuma, Swan, and Carbone. His diet focuses on healthy, lean proteins and Mediterranean-style cuisine.
