Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quinta Brunson's favorite Philadelphia food?
Quinta is a proud Philadelphian who loves cheesesteaks, soft pretzels, water ice, and hoagies. She's spoken about her hometown food pride in numerous interviews and incorporates authentic Philly food culture into Abbott Elementary.
Where is Quinta Brunson from?
Quinta Brunson was born and raised in West Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Her experiences growing up there directly inspired Abbott Elementary, which is set in a Philadelphia public school.
Does Abbott Elementary feature real Philadelphia restaurants?
Yes! Abbott Elementary includes authentic references to Philadelphia food culture including soft pretzels, cheesesteaks, and local spots. The show's attention to Philly detail is part of what makes it feel authentic.
Is Quinta Brunson an Eagles fan?
Absolutely! Quinta is a devoted Philadelphia Eagles fan. She's been seen repping the Eagles and discussing her fandom, which naturally includes Eagles game day food traditions.
Where does Quinta Brunson live now?
Quinta Brunson is based in Los Angeles where she works as showrunner of Abbott Elementary. However, she maintains strong ties to Philadelphia and returns frequently.
