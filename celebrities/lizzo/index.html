What does Lizzo eat?

Lizzo primarily follows a plant-based diet featuring green smoothies, vegan tacos, salads, and plant-based proteins. She's documented her food journey extensively on TikTok. While she eats mostly vegan, she rejects strict labels and diet culture, advocating for a healthy relationship with food that honors both her health goals and her soul food heritage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lizzo vegan?
Lizzo eats primarily plant-based and has documented her vegan meals extensively on TikTok. However, she doesn't strictly identify as vegan and rejects diet culture labels. She focuses on eating intuitively and making choices that feel good for her body.
What does Lizzo eat in a typical day?
Based on her TikTok content, Lizzo typically starts with green smoothies, has plant-based meals like vegan tacos or salads for lunch, and cooks healthy dinners at home including vegan mac and cheese and plant-based proteins.
What is Lizzo's approach to diet and body image?
Lizzo advocates for body positivity and a healthy relationship with food that isn't about restriction or weight loss. She focuses on nourishment, enjoyment, and how food makes her feel rather than following strict diet rules.
