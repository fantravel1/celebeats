Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Law Roach eat in Los Angeles?
Law Roach is based in Los Angeles where he styles A-list clients. The city's industry dining scene includes celebrity hotspots in West Hollywood and Beverly Hills where Hollywood's creative community gathers for power dinners and industry events.
What is Law Roach's background?
Law Roach grew up in Chicago and started his career there in vintage fashion before becoming one of fashion's most influential stylists. He's known for his work with Zendaya, whom he helped establish as a fashion icon, as well as Celine Dion and Ariana Grande.
Does Law Roach attend Fashion Week?
Yes, Law Roach is a fixture at fashion weeks in Paris, Milan, and New York. He accompanies clients to shows and is often photographed at the most exclusive fashion events, front rows, and after-parties.
Who does Law Roach style?
Law Roach has styled some of the biggest names in entertainment including Zendaya (his most famous collaboration), Celine Dion, Ariana Grande, Megan Thee Stallion, and many others. He's known for creating iconic red carpet moments.
Why is Law Roach called the Image Architect?
Law Roach coined the term "Image Architect" to describe his approach to styling. Rather than just choosing clothes, he builds complete visual identities for his clients, crafting their public image through fashion choices and presentation.
