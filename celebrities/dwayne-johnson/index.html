What does The Rock eat in a day?

The Rock eats 5,000-6,000 calories daily across 5-7 meals. His diet includes cod fish, chicken, steak, rice, vegetables, egg whites, and oatmeal. He's famous for eating over 800 pounds of cod per year during movie prep.

Typical Day of Eating

  • Meal 1: 10oz cod, 2 cups oatmeal, 2 whole eggs
  • Meal 2: 8oz cod, 2 cups rice, 1 cup vegetables
  • Meal 3: 8oz chicken, 2 cups rice, 1 cup vegetables
  • Meal 4: 8oz cod, 2 cups rice, 1 cup vegetables
  • Meal 5: 8oz steak, baked potato, spinach salad

Why So Much Cod?

The Rock consumes massive amounts of cod fish because it's lean protein that supports his muscle mass without excess fat. During filming for Hercules, he reportedly ate 821 pounds of cod in a year - about 2.3 pounds daily.

Teremana Tequila

He often pairs his cheat meals with Teremana, creating the perfect Sunday ritual that he shares with millions of followers.
