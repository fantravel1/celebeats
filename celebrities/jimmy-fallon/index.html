What does Jimmy Fallon eat?

Jimmy Fallon is a self-proclaimed Italian food lover, enjoying pasta, pizza, and classic Italian-American dishes. Growing up in an Irish-Italian neighborhood in New York shaped his appreciation for comfort food and family-style dining.

  • Pizza - NYC-style, of course

Jimmy frequents classic New York Italian restaurants, from old-school red sauce joints to trendy Manhattan trattorias. His enthusiasm for food often makes it into Tonight Show segments where he dines with guests.

Brooklyn & Saugerties

Jimmy's New York upbringing shaped his tastes:

  • Brooklyn Born - Bay Ridge neighborhood
