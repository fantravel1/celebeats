Frequently Asked Questions

What does Coco Gauff eat?
Coco follows a health-conscious diet with an interest in plant-based eating. She focuses on lean proteins, fresh vegetables, smoothies, and proper hydration. Growing up in Florida gives her access to fresh produce and seafood year-round. She balances performance nutrition with enjoying treats occasionally.
Is Coco Gauff vegan?
Coco has expressed interest in plant-based eating and is vegan-curious, though she hasn't confirmed being fully vegan. She incorporates many plant-based meals into her diet and is conscious about her food choices, representing Gen-Z's interest in sustainable eating.
Where does Coco Gauff eat in Florida?
Coco is based in Delray Beach, Florida, where she enjoys local restaurants along Atlantic Avenue. She frequents juice bars for smoothies and acai bowls, health-conscious cafes, and fresh seafood restaurants that the coastal location offers.
What is Coco Gauff's favorite food?
While Coco focuses on healthy eating, she's mentioned enjoying smoothie bowls, fresh seafood, and plant-based options. Like any 20-year-old, she also enjoys treats like pizza and ice cream on occasion. Her favorites reflect both her Florida roots and health-conscious approach.
Does Coco Gauff follow a strict diet?
Coco maintains a balanced approach to nutrition. While she's mindful of her food choices and leans toward healthy options, she doesn't follow an extremely restrictive diet. She believes in balance and still enjoys treating herself while maintaining her athletic performance.
