The Kapoor family Christmas brunch is Bollywood's most famous annual gathering. Started by Raj Kapoor, it brings together the entire clan - from Randhir to Kareena to Ranbir - for an elaborate feast featuring Punjabi delicacies, roast turkey, and family recipes passed down through generations.

The Legendary Kapoor Family Christmas Brunch

Bollywood's Most Famous Family Gathering

The Kapoor Christmas brunch is an institution in Indian cinema. Started by the legendary showman Raj Kapoor, it continues to this day:

  • Started by Raj Kapoor - The tradition began decades ago at RK Cottage
  • Extended Kapoor Clan - All branches of the family gather annually
  • Elaborate Spread - Punjabi food, roast turkey, plum cake, traditional sweets
  • Bollywood Attendance - Spouses and close friends of family join
  • Now at Randhir's Home - After RK Cottage sale, tradition continues

The brunch is so legendary that paparazzi gather outside annually to photograph arriving guests.

The Kapoor Food Legacy

The Kapoors are Bollywood's most food-loving family. From Prithviraj Kapoor to Ranbir, food has been central to family identity.

Four Generations of Food Love

  • Prithviraj Kapoor - Started the Punjabi food traditions
  • Raj Kapoor - Legendary host, started Christmas brunch tradition
  • RK Studio Kitchen - Was famous for feeding entire film crews
  • Randhir & Rishi - Continued food-centric family gatherings
  • Neetu Kapoor - Renowned for her home cooking and recipes
  • Kareena & Ranbir - Carry forward the family food traditions
  • Raha - Fourth generation Kapoor growing up with food heritage

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Kapoor family Christmas brunch?
The Kapoor Christmas brunch is Bollywood's most legendary annual family gathering, started by Raj Kapoor. The entire extended Kapoor family gathers for an elaborate feast featuring Punjabi delicacies, roast turkey, plum cake, and family recipes. It's been a tradition for decades.
What is Ranbir Kapoor's favorite food?
Ranbir's favorite food is his mother Neetu Kapoor's home cooking. He loves Punjabi food like butter chicken, biryani, and the rich dishes the Kapoor family is famous for. He also enjoys pizza as a casual favorite.
Is Ranbir Kapoor vegetarian?
No, Ranbir Kapoor is not vegetarian. The Kapoor family is known for their love of non-vegetarian Punjabi food. He enjoys meat dishes, though he has followed specific diets for film roles when required.
Where does Ranbir Kapoor eat in Mumbai?
Ranbir primarily enjoys family meals at Kapoor homes. When dining out, he frequents Bastian (often with Alia), Soho House Mumbai, and other celebrity hotspots. But family gatherings and mother's cooking remain his favorites.
Why are the Kapoors famous for food?
The Kapoor family has been associated with food-loving traditions for four generations. From Raj Kapoor's legendary hospitality and Christmas brunches to the famous RK Studio kitchen that fed entire film crews, food has always been central to their family identity.
