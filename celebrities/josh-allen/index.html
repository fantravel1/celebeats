Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Josh Allen eat in Buffalo?
Josh Allen frequents Buffalo's famous wing joints like Anchor Bar and Duff's, as well as local steakhouses and upscale restaurants. He's fully embraced the city's food culture since becoming the Bills' franchise quarterback.
Does Josh Allen like Buffalo wings?
Absolutely! As the face of the Buffalo Bills, Allen has become an ambassador for Buffalo wing culture. He's participated in wing-related events and promotions, fully embracing the city's most famous culinary export.
What is Josh Allen's diet like?
As an elite NFL quarterback, Allen follows a strict nutrition program focused on performance. He works with team nutritionists to maintain his powerful build while still enjoying Buffalo's food scene in moderation.
Where is Josh Allen from originally?
Allen grew up on a cotton farm in Firebaugh, California, in the San Joaquin Valley. His rural, farming background shaped his work ethic and gave him an appreciation for simple, quality food before moving to Buffalo.
Where does Josh Allen eat during the offseason?
During the offseason, Allen spends time in California near his hometown and has been seen in various locations. His relationship with Hailee Steinfeld also means time in Los Angeles and other cities.
