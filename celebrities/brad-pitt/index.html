Frequently Asked Questions

Does Brad Pitt own a winery?
Yes, Brad Pitt owns Chateau Miraval in Provence, France. The estate produces acclaimed rose wine and has become one of the world's most celebrated rose producers. He's deeply involved in the winemaking process.
What are Brad Pitt's favorite restaurants?
Brad Pitt frequents design-forward LA restaurants like Felix Trattoria, Gjelina, and Osteria Mozza. He's drawn to architecturally interesting spaces and appreciates Japanese cuisine at spots like n/naka. In France, he dines at his Miraval estate and Riviera establishments.
Where is Brad Pitt from originally?
Brad Pitt was born in Shawnee, Oklahoma but grew up in Springfield, Missouri. Despite his sophisticated lifestyle today, he maintains appreciation for Midwest comfort food alongside his refined palate.
Is Brad Pitt interested in architecture?
Yes, Brad Pitt is a well-known architecture enthusiast. This passion extends to his restaurant choices, where he gravitates toward design-forward spaces. He's been involved in various architecture and design projects throughout his career.
