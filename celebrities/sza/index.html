SZA Food & Diet FAQs

SZA has followed a vegetarian diet and spoken about plant-based eating. Her approach is flexible and focused on wellness rather than strict labels. She prioritizes whole foods and listens to her body's needs.

SZA focuses on plant-based foods including vegetables, fruits, whole grains, and plant proteins. She's known for loving smoothies, fresh juices, salads, and wellness-focused meals. Her diet supports her demanding performance schedule.

SZA frequents LA wellness spots like Cafe Gratitude, Gracias Madre, Crossroads Kitchen, and various juice bars. She's drawn to restaurants that offer organic, plant-based options aligned with her lifestyle.

SZA has spoken about how her wellness practices, including diet, support her creativity and emotional health. Eating clean helps her maintain energy during tours and recording sessions. Her holistic approach to self-care influences her authentic, introspective music.
