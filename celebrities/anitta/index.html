What does Anitta eat?

Anitta loves authentic Brazilian cuisine from her Rio de Janeiro roots - feijoada, acai bowls, churrasco, and coxinha. She maintains her connection to carioca food culture while embracing Miami's Latin scene. She enjoys beach lifestyle foods, Brazilian steakhouses, and shares her love of home cooking on social media.

Anitta's Food Favorites

Feijoada

Brazil's national dish - black bean stew with pork, served with rice, collard greens, farofa, and orange slices. A Saturday tradition.

Tier A

Acai Bowls

The superfood from the Amazon, served frozen with granola, banana, and guarana. A Rio beach staple and Anitta's go-to healthy choice.

Tier A

Churrasco (Brazilian BBQ)

All-you-can-eat grilled meats served rodizio style. Picanha (top sirloin cap) is the star cut at any churrascaria.

Tier A

Coxinha & Salgados

Brazilian fried snacks including coxinha (chicken croquettes), pao de queijo (cheese bread), and pastel. Perfect with cold beer.

Brazilian Food Culture

Anitta embodies Rio de Janeiro's carioca food traditions:

  • Feijoada Saturdays - The traditional day for Brazil's national dish
  • Boteco Culture - Casual bars serving cold beer and petiscos (snacks)
  • Beach Lifestyle - Acai, agua de coco (coconut water), and fresh seafood
  • Churrasco - Rodizio-style BBQ for celebrations and gatherings
  • Comfort Snacks - Pao de queijo, coxinha, and empadinhas as daily treats

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Anitta's favorite Brazilian food?
Anitta loves traditional Brazilian dishes from her Rio de Janeiro roots including feijoada (black bean stew), acai bowls, churrasco (Brazilian BBQ), and coxinha (fried chicken croquettes). She's proud of Brazilian food culture and often shares meals from home on social media.
What does Anitta eat in Miami?
In Miami, Anitta enjoys the Latin American food scene, Brazilian steakhouses like Fogo de Chao, and healthy beach lifestyle options. She frequents South Beach restaurants, Brazilian spots, and embraces the city's diverse Latin flavors.
Where does Anitta eat in Rio de Janeiro?
In her hometown Rio de Janeiro, Anitta enjoys boteco bars (Brazilian pubs), beach kiosks serving acai and fresh coconut water, traditional churrascarias, and local neighborhood restaurants. She stays connected to her Zona Norte roots through authentic carioca food spots.
