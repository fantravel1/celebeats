Where does A$AP Rocky eat?

A$AP Rocky frequents upscale restaurants in NYC and LA that match his fashion-forward lifestyle. In New York, he's spotted at Carbone, The Polo Bar, and Lucien. He stays connected to Harlem through spots like Red Rooster and Sylvia's. With Rihanna, he's been seen at Giorgio Baldi in LA.

Tier A Evidence High Confidence

Harlem Roots & Soul Food

Growing up in Harlem shaped Rocky's appreciation for authentic soul food and community dining. Despite his high-fashion lifestyle, he maintains connections to neighborhood institutions:

  • Sylvia's Restaurant - The Queen of Soul Food, a Harlem landmark since 1962
  • Red Rooster Harlem - Marcus Samuelsson's upscale take on comfort food
  • Amy Ruth's - Famous for chicken and waffles
  • Melba's - Classic Southern comfort food

Rocky has spoken about how Harlem's food culture influenced his identity and remains an important part of who he is, even as he dines at the world's finest restaurants.

A$AP Rocky Food FAQs

A$AP Rocky frequents upscale NYC restaurants including Carbone, Lucien, The Polo Bar, and Balthazar. He's also been spotted at Harlem favorites like Sylvia's and Red Rooster, staying connected to his hometown roots.

A$AP Rocky has expressed love for soul food reflecting his Harlem upbringing, Italian cuisine, and Japanese food. He's known to appreciate fine dining experiences that match his fashion-forward lifestyle.

A$AP Rocky and Rihanna have been spotted at exclusive LA and NYC restaurants including Giorgio Baldi, Carbone, and various spots in Barbados where Rihanna has roots. They prefer private dining experiences.

While A$AP Rocky is known more for fashion collaborations (Dior, Puma, Mercedes-Benz), he hasn't had major fast-food partnerships like some peers. His dining choices lean toward exclusive, fashion-industry-adjacent restaurants.
