What does Kevin Hart eat?

Kevin Hart follows a disciplined high-protein diet with lean meats, fish, vegetables, and complex carbs. He eats 5-6 small meals daily and limits sugar and processed foods. Despite his strict routine, he enjoys Philly cheesesteaks as an occasional treat.

Kevin's Daily Diet

Typical Day of Eating

  • Meal 1: Egg whites, oatmeal, fruit
  • Meal 2: Protein shake, almonds
  • Meal 3: Grilled chicken, brown rice, vegetables
  • Meal 4: Fish (salmon or tilapia), sweet potato
  • Meal 5: Lean steak, salad, vegetables

Hydration & Supplements

Kevin emphasizes staying hydrated, drinking at least a gallon of water daily. He supplements with protein powder, BCAAs, and multivitamins to support his intense workout schedule and recovery.

Philly Cheesesteak Love

Hometown Pride

Kevin never misses a chance to celebrate Philly food culture:

  • Philly Cheesesteaks - His ultimate comfort food
  • Jim's Steaks - South Street favorite spot
  • Pat's vs Geno's - Weighs in on the classic debate
  • Soft Pretzels - Authentic Philly pretzels with mustard
  • Water Ice - Summer nostalgia from childhood

He's known for bringing Philly food to his movie sets and comedy tours.

Post-Accident Recovery Diet

After his serious car accident in 2019, Kevin became even more dedicated to nutrition for recovery:

  • Anti-Inflammatory Foods - Emphasis on reducing inflammation
  • Lean Proteins - For muscle recovery and rebuilding
  • Omega-3 Rich Foods - Salmon, fish oil supplements
  • Whole Foods Focus - Eliminated processed foods
  • Consistent Schedule - Eating at regular intervals

His recovery journey reinforced his commitment to health and fitness.
