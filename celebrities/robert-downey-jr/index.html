Iron Man Meal Prep

Superhero Nutrition

To get in shape for Iron Man and Avengers films, RDJ followed strict nutrition:

  • Five to six small meals throughout the day
  • High protein: chicken, fish, egg whites
  • Complex carbohydrates for energy
  • Plenty of vegetables at every meal
  • Minimal sugar and processed foods
  • Adequate hydration and recovery nutrition

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Robert Downey Jr. eat in Malibu?
RDJ and wife Susan frequent Nobu Malibu for sushi, Malibu Farm for organic fare, and Soho House Malibu for private dining. He prefers restaurants with fresh, clean ingredients that align with his healthy lifestyle.
What is Robert Downey Jr.'s diet?
RDJ follows a clean eating approach focused on organic foods, lean proteins, fresh vegetables, and minimal processed foods. His diet supports his active lifestyle and wellness practices including martial arts and yoga.
Is Robert Downey Jr. sober?
Yes, Robert Downey Jr. has been sober since 2003. His remarkable recovery included adopting healthy eating, martial arts (Wing Chun), yoga, and other wellness practices.
How did Robert Downey Jr. get in shape for Iron Man?
RDJ followed a strict regimen of Wing Chun martial arts, weight training, and a high-protein diet with five to six small meals daily, focusing on lean proteins and vegetables.
Does Robert Downey Jr. practice martial arts?
Yes, RDJ has practiced Wing Chun kung fu for years. He credits the discipline with helping his recovery and overall wellness. He trained with Sifu Eric Oram.
