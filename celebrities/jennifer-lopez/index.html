JLo's Diet & Eating Habits

The No-Sugar Diet

Jennifer Lopez is famous for eliminating sugar and caffeine from her diet. Her trainer Tracy Anderson has confirmed JLo hasn't had sugar or caffeine in years. This discipline extends to her restaurant choices - she typically orders:

  • Grilled fish with vegetables
  • Lean proteins (chicken, turkey)
  • Complex carbs (quinoa, sweet potato)
  • Fresh salads with olive oil
  • Plenty of water

Typical Day of Eating

Breakfast: Protein shake with berries, egg whites
Lunch: Salmon salad with vegetables
Dinner: Grilled chicken or fish with quinoa
Snacks: Vegetables, nuts, fruits
