What is Jennifer Lopez's diet?

Jennifer Lopez follows a strict no-sugar, no-caffeine diet. She focuses on lean proteins like fish and chicken, vegetables, complex carbs like quinoa and sweet potatoes, and healthy fats. Her trainer Tracy Anderson has confirmed JLo hasn't had sugar or caffeine in years - a discipline that contributes to her legendary physique.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Jennifer Lopez's diet?
JLo follows a strict no-sugar, no-caffeine diet. She focuses on lean proteins like fish and chicken, vegetables, complex carbs like quinoa and sweet potatoes, and healthy fats. Her trainer has confirmed she hasn't had sugar or caffeine in years.
Where does Jennifer Lopez eat in Miami?
JLo frequents Prime 112 for celebrations, The Surf Club Restaurant by Thomas Keller for special occasions, Komodo in Brickell, Swan in the Design District, and Makoto in Bal Harbour for fresh sushi.
Where does Jennifer Lopez eat in the Hamptons?
In the Hamptons, JLo has been spotted at Nick & Toni's in East Hampton, Tutto Il Giorno in Sag Harbor, and Sant Ambroeus in Southampton. She favors Mediterranean-style restaurants with fresh, healthy options.
Does Jennifer Lopez drink alcohol?
JLo rarely drinks alcohol and has spoken about avoiding it as part of her healthy lifestyle. She primarily drinks water and avoids caffeine, soda, and most alcoholic beverages.
Does Jennifer Lopez eat carbs?
Yes, JLo eats complex carbohydrates like quinoa, sweet potatoes, brown rice, and yams. She avoids simple carbs and refined sugars but includes healthy carbs as part of her balanced diet for energy and workouts.
