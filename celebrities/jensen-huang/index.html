Did Jensen Huang start NVIDIA at a Denny's?

Yes! In 1993, Jensen Huang and co-founders Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem famously founded NVIDIA at a Denny's restaurant in San Jose, California. This humble chain restaurant became the birthplace of what would grow into a $2 trillion company that powers the AI revolution.

The Legendary Denny's Story

Where NVIDIA Was Born

The founding of NVIDIA at a Denny's has become one of Silicon Valley's most legendary origin stories:

  • 1993 - Three engineers meet at a Denny's in San Jose
  • The Booth - Plans for a graphics chip company drawn on napkins
  • $40,000 - Initial funding to start the company
  • The Vision - Accelerated computing that would change the world
  • Today - NVIDIA powers AI, gaming, and data centers globally

"We were three guys who believed in accelerated computing when nobody else did." - Jensen Huang

Frequently Asked Questions

Did Jensen Huang really start NVIDIA at a Denny's?
Yes! In 1993, Jensen Huang and co-founders Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem founded NVIDIA at a Denny's restaurant in San Jose. The chain restaurant became the birthplace of what is now a $2 trillion company.
What is Jensen Huang's favorite food?
Jensen Huang has Taiwanese heritage and has expressed appreciation for Taiwanese cuisine. He grew up in Tainan, Taiwan before moving to the US, and maintains connections to his culinary roots through family traditions and visits home.
Where does Jensen Huang eat in Silicon Valley?
Jensen frequents Silicon Valley restaurants for business and personal dining. He's known to appreciate both upscale dining for corporate events and casual spots. The original Denny's in San Jose holds special significance for NVIDIA history.
Which Denny's did Jensen Huang start NVIDIA at?
NVIDIA was founded at a Denny's in San Jose, California in 1993. The exact location on Berryessa Road has become a pilgrimage site for tech enthusiasts and NVIDIA fans.
Does Jensen Huang still eat at Denny's?
Jensen Huang has spoken fondly of NVIDIA's Denny's origins in numerous interviews and company events. The humble beginning story is a core part of NVIDIA's corporate culture and Jensen's personal narrative about building the company.
