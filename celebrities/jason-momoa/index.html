What does Jason Momoa like to eat?

Jason Momoa is all about Hawaiian BBQ, poke, and island cuisine. Raised in Hawaii, he has a deep connection to outdoor cooking and grilling. He eats hearty, protein-rich meals to fuel his physique, and famously pairs everything with his beloved Guinness.

Hawaiian Food Culture

Island Favorites

Growing up in Honolulu, Jason Momoa developed a love for Hawaiian cuisine:

  • Poke - Fresh raw fish with rice, a Hawaiian staple
  • Kalua Pig - Slow-cooked in an underground imu
  • Loco Moco - Rice, hamburger patty, fried egg, gravy
  • Plate Lunch - Two scoops rice, mac salad, protein
  • Spam Musubi - Hawaii's beloved snack
  • Haupia - Coconut pudding dessert

Hawaiian BBQ Spots

  • Helena's Hawaiian Food - Honolulu institution
  • Rainbow Drive-In - Classic plate lunch
  • Ono Hawaiian Foods - Traditional favorites
  • Highway Inn - Modern Hawaiian cuisine

The Guinness Legend

A True Guinness Devotee

Jason Momoa's love of Guinness is legendary in Hollywood. He's been photographed drinking the Irish stout countless times and has made it part of his public persona:

  • On set - Known to enjoy Guinness between takes
  • Workout jokes - Has joked about Guinness being his workout fuel
  • Ireland visits - Has made pilgrimages to the Guinness Storehouse in Dublin
  • Press tours - Frequently brings up his love for the stout

His genuine enthusiasm for Guinness has made him an unofficial ambassador for the brand, though he's simply a passionate fan.

BBQ & Grilling

Outdoor Cooking Enthusiast

Jason Momoa loves cooking outdoors and grilling. His approach matches his larger-than-life personality:

  • Big cuts of meat - Steaks, ribs, whole fish
  • Open flame cooking - Primal, direct-heat methods
  • Family gatherings - Cooking for large groups
  • Fresh fish - Grilling fresh catches from the ocean

He's shared videos of himself grilling massive steaks and fish, often with a Guinness nearby.

Training Diet

Aquaman Nutrition

For his role as Aquaman, Jason Momoa worked with trainers on his nutrition, though his approach is notably relaxed compared to other action stars:

  • High protein - Lots of meat and fish
  • Carbs for fuel - Rice, potatoes, bread
  • No strict restrictions - Enjoys food and doesn't obsess
  • Guinness included - Doesn't cut out his favorite beverage

His approach is about enjoying life while staying active, rather than strict dieting. He's spoken about how his Hawaiian upbringing shaped his relationship with food - it's meant to be enjoyed with family and friends.
