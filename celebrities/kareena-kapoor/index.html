What was Kareena Kapoor's size zero diet?

For Tashan (2008), Kareena achieved size zero by working with nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar. She didn't starve but followed a strict routine: 6 small meals daily, no processed foods, lots of vegetables, and regular yoga. She has since evolved to advocate balanced eating over extreme diets.

Kareena's Size Zero to Balanced Eating Journey

The Evolution of Bebo's Diet Philosophy

Kareena's diet journey is one of Bollywood's most influential transformations. Here's how it evolved:

  • Size Zero Era (2008) - Strict 1200 calorie diet, 6 small meals, no junk
  • Post-Marriage - Embraced Pataudi royal kitchen and nawabi cuisine
  • Pregnancy Phase - Focused on nutrition over weight, ate for baby
  • Current Philosophy - Balance, enjoyment, no deprivation
  • Rujuta Collaboration - Co-authored "Pregnancy Notes" sharing her journey

"I don't believe in size zero anymore. I believe in being healthy and happy."

Frequently Asked Questions

What was Kareena Kapoor's size zero diet?
Kareena achieved size zero for Tashan by working with Rujuta Diwekar. She ate 6 small meals daily, avoided processed foods, ate lots of vegetables, and did regular yoga. She didn't starve but followed a strict, nutritionist-supervised plan.
What does Kareena Kapoor eat now?
Kareena now follows a balanced approach. She enjoys parathas, dal, rice, Punjabi food with family, and doesn't deprive herself. She believes in moderation and sustainable eating rather than extreme diets. She still works with Rujuta Diwekar.
What is Kareena Kapoor's favorite food?
Kareena loves Punjabi food - parathas with white butter, dal makhani, and family-style Indian meals. She's also a coffee addict and enjoys biryani from her Pataudi family traditions. The Kapoors are famous for their love of food.
Who is Kareena Kapoor's nutritionist?
Kareena has worked with Rujuta Diwekar since her size zero days. They've co-authored books including "Pregnancy Notes." Rujuta is known for her approach of eating local, seasonal foods and never eliminating food groups.
Does Kareena Kapoor still follow size zero diet?
No, Kareena no longer follows size zero and has spoken against extreme diets. She now advocates balanced eating, enjoying food, and not depriving oneself. She says being healthy and happy is more important than being a specific size.
