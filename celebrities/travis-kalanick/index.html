CloudKitchens is Travis Kalanick's ghost kitchen company that provides commercial kitchen space to restaurants for delivery-only operations. The company operates facilities worldwide and is valued at billions of dollars.

Travis Kalanick is known for frequenting upscale LA establishments including Nobu Malibu, Craig's in West Hollywood, and various trendy spots in the LA dining scene. He's also been spotted at cutting-edge restaurants exploring the latest food trends.

While not a traditional restaurant owner, Kalanick's CloudKitchens company operates hundreds of ghost kitchen facilities worldwide, essentially providing infrastructure for thousands of restaurant brands to operate delivery-only kitchens.

Travis Kalanick co-founded Uber and served as CEO until 2017, when he resigned amid controversies. He left the board in 2019 and has since focused on CloudKitchens, applying lessons learned from Uber to the food delivery infrastructure space.
