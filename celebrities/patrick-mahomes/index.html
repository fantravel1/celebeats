Texas Roots

Born in Tyler, Texas, Patrick Mahomes grew up with Texas BBQ traditions. The brisket-centric style of Texas BBQ is different from Kansas City's sauce-heavy approach, giving Mahomes appreciation for both regional styles.

His father, Pat Mahomes Sr., played MLB, so young Patrick grew up around stadiums and the food culture of professional sports. Texas shaped his food foundation before KC BBQ captured his heart.

Athletic Diet

Season Nutrition

During the NFL season, Mahomes follows a structured diet focused on performance. The Chiefs' nutritional staff ensures he gets the right balance of proteins, carbohydrates, and healthy fats to maintain his elite performance level.

Game Day Fuel

On game days, Mahomes sticks to familiar foods that won't upset his system. Lean proteins and complex carbs provide sustained energy for four quarters of high-level quarterback play.

Off-Season Indulgence

The off-season allows Mahomes to enjoy more BBQ and comfort food. He's been open about loving cheat meals and not being too restrictive outside of training periods.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Patrick Mahomes' favorite restaurant in Kansas City?
Patrick Mahomes has expressed love for Kansas City BBQ, with Q39 and Joe's Kansas City being among the city's most celebrated spots. He's also been seen at various KC restaurants and has invested in local food businesses, showing his commitment to his adopted hometown.

Yes, Patrick Mahomes is famously known for his love of ketchup. He puts it on a variety of foods and even signed a deal with Hunt's Ketchup. His unusual ketchup pairings include steak and macaroni and cheese, which has become part of his public persona.
What does Patrick Mahomes eat on game day?
Patrick Mahomes follows a structured meal plan during the season. His game day nutrition focuses on lean proteins and complex carbohydrates for sustained energy. He works with the Chiefs' nutritional staff to optimize his diet for peak performance.
Does Patrick Mahomes own any restaurants?
Patrick Mahomes has invested in several Kansas City food and beverage businesses as part of his commitment to the KC community. He's also a part-owner of the Kansas City Royals and KC Current, demonstrating his long-term commitment to the city.
