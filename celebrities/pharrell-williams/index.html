Does Pharrell Williams own a restaurant?

Yes, Pharrell Williams is co-owner of Swan, an upscale Mediterranean restaurant in Miami's Design District. Opened in 2018 with hospitality mogul David Grutman, Swan has become one of Miami's most celebrated dining destinations, combining Pharrell's aesthetic vision with world-class cuisine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, Pharrell Williams is a co-owner of Swan, an upscale Mediterranean restaurant located in Miami's Design District. He opened the restaurant with hospitality entrepreneur David Grutman in 2018.

Swan is located in Miami's Design District at 90 NE 39th Street. The restaurant features Mediterranean cuisine in a stunning Art Deco-inspired space and has become a celebrity hotspot. Bar Bevy, an adjacent cocktail lounge, is also part of the venue.

Pharrell has spoken about his love for healthy eating and Mediterranean cuisine, which influenced Swan's menu. He's also known for appreciating Japanese food and has mentioned sushi as a favorite. He frequently dines at Nobu and other Japanese restaurants.

Pharrell Williams is from Virginia Beach, Virginia. He grew up there and later moved to Miami, where he opened Swan. His Southern roots occasionally influence his food preferences, though he's become known for his health-conscious Mediterranean and Japanese dining choices.
