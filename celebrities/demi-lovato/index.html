What is Demi Lovato's approach to food in recovery?

Demi Lovato has been courageously open about her recovery journey. She now practices intuitive eating, focusing on listening to her body rather than following restrictive rules. She's removed labels like "good" and "bad" from food and emphasizes balance, enjoyment, and freedom in her relationship with eating.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Demi Lovato's approach to food in recovery?
Demi practices intuitive eating, focusing on listening to her body rather than following rules. She's removed labels of "good" and "bad" from food and emphasizes balance, enjoyment, and freedom in eating.
Where does Demi Lovato eat in Los Angeles?
Demi frequents Craig's in West Hollywood, La Scala for Italian, Catch LA for special occasions, and various brunch spots like Joan's on Third. She doesn't restrict herself to any particular type of cuisine.
Does Demi Lovato follow a specific diet?
Demi has moved away from restrictive dieting as part of her recovery. She practices intuitive eating and focuses on balance rather than rules. She no longer counts calories or labels foods as off-limits.
What is Demi Lovato's favorite food?
Demi enjoys a variety of foods without restriction, including Italian pasta, tacos, brunch dishes, and frozen yogurt. She's mentioned that allowing herself to enjoy all foods has been key to her recovery.
How has Demi Lovato's relationship with food changed?
Demi has publicly shared her evolution from restriction to food freedom. She no longer follows strict diets, counts calories, or views any food as forbidden. Her focus is on mental health and enjoying meals without guilt.
