Kim's Diet & Eating Habits

Plant-Based Focus

Kim has spoken about eating mostly plant-based. Her typical diet includes:

  • Smoothies with plant protein
  • Salads with grilled vegetables
  • Tacos (her admitted weakness)
  • Sea moss smoothies for skin health
  • Minimal dairy and red meat

Health Nut Obsession

Kim orders from Health Nut in Calabasas almost daily. Her go-to order reportedly includes their Chinese Chicken Salad and fresh juices. The Kardashian family are such regulars that certain items are known as "Kardashian orders."

Notable Dining Moments
