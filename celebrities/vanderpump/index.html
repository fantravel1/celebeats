What restaurants does Lisa Vanderpump own?

Lisa Vanderpump owns a restaurant empire in West Hollywood including SUR (Sexy Unique Restaurant), PUMP Lounge, and TomTom (co-owned with Tom Sandoval and Tom Schwartz). Her restaurants have become famous through the hit Bravo series Vanderpump Rules.
