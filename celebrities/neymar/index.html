What is Neymar's diet?

Neymar's diet blends professional athlete nutrition with his beloved Brazilian cuisine. He enjoys churrasco (Brazilian BBQ), traditional rice and beans, and his mother Nadine's home cooking. During his career, he's worked with nutritionists to balance indulgences with performance needs.
