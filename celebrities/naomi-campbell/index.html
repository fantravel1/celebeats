Wellness & Diet Philosophy

Naomi's legendary discipline and wellness secrets.

One Meal a Day

Naomi has openly discussed eating one main meal per day, typically dinner. She practices intermittent fasting as part of her wellness routine that has kept her runway-ready for decades.
