What is The Charli at Dunkin'?

The Charli is Charli D'Amelio's signature Dunkin' drink - a Dunkin' Cold Brew with whole milk and three pumps of caramel swirl. Launched in September 2020, it was so popular it increased Dunkin' app downloads by 57% and became one of the most successful celebrity beverage collaborations ever.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Charli at Dunkin'?
The Charli is a Dunkin' Cold Brew with whole milk and three pumps of caramel swirl. It was created in collaboration with Charli D'Amelio and launched in September 2020, boosting app downloads by 57%.
What is Charli D'Amelio's favorite food?
Charli loves pasta, pizza, and chicken nuggets. She's described herself as not particularly adventurous with food, preferring familiar comfort foods. Her Italian heritage influences her love of pasta dishes.
Did Charli D'Amelio win Dancing with the Stars?
Yes! Charli won Season 31 of Dancing with the Stars in 2022, partnered with professional dancer Mark Ballas. Her mother Heidi also competed that same season.
How many TikTok followers does Charli D'Amelio have?
Charli D'Amelio has over 150 million TikTok followers. She was the first person to reach 100 million followers on the platform in November 2020.
Who is Charli D'Amelio dating?
Charli D'Amelio has been in a relationship with Landon Barker, son of Blink-182's Travis Barker, since 2022. They're often spotted together at LA restaurants and events.
