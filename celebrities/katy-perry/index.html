Frequently Asked Questions

What is Katy Perry's diet like?
Katy Perry follows a balanced, health-conscious diet with emphasis on vegetables, lean proteins, and whole foods. She incorporates many plant-based meals, influenced by Orlando Bloom, while allowing occasional indulgences like truffle pasta.
Where does Katy Perry eat in Los Angeles?
Katy frequents health-conscious LA spots including Gracias Madre for vegan Mexican, Craig's in West Hollywood, Crossroads Kitchen for upscale plant-based dining, and Nobu Malibu for sushi with ocean views.
Is Katy Perry vegan?
Katy is not fully vegan but incorporates many plant-based meals into her diet. Orlando Bloom's mostly plant-based lifestyle has influenced her eating habits, and she often chooses vegan restaurants for date nights.
What is Katy Perry's favorite food?
Katy has mentioned truffle pasta as one of her favorite indulgences. For everyday eating, she enjoys green juices, avocado toast, sushi, and healthy Mediterranean-style dishes.
Does Katy Perry cook at home?
Yes, Katy has shared that she enjoys cooking at home, especially since becoming a mother. She focuses on preparing healthy, balanced meals for her family and has mentioned meal prepping for busy schedules.
