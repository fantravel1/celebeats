What does Will Smith eat?

Will Smith follows a balanced diet focused on lean proteins, vegetables, and complex carbs. He's experimented with plant-based eating and intermittent fasting. Despite his healthy lifestyle, he still enjoys Philly cheesesteaks and comfort food from his hometown.

Philly Comfort Food Roots

Philadelphia Favorites

Growing up in West Philadelphia, Will developed a deep love for classic Philly food:

  • Philly Cheesesteaks - Pat's and Geno's debates
  • Soft Pretzels - With mustard, the Philly way
  • Water Ice - Rita's Italian Ice nostalgia
  • Hoagies - Classic Italian from local delis
  • Tastykakes - The iconic Philly snack cakes

Mom's Home Cooking

Will has spoken fondly about his mother Caroline's cooking, including soul food classics like fried chicken, mac and cheese, collard greens, and sweet potato pie that shaped his early food memories.

Healthy Transformation Journey

Best Shape of My Life (2021)

Will documented his dramatic transformation on YouTube, losing over 20 pounds through:

  • Intermittent Fasting - 16:8 eating window
  • High Protein - Lean meats, fish, eggs
  • Low Carb - Reduced sugar and processed foods
  • Hydration Focus - 1 gallon of water daily
  • No Alcohol - Complete elimination during transformation

Plant-Based Experiments

Will has experimented with plant-based eating, trying vegan and vegetarian phases. He's invested in plant-based food companies and spoken about reducing meat consumption for health and environmental reasons.

LA & Miami Restaurant Scene

Will splits time between Los Angeles and Miami, frequenting upscale restaurants:

  • Nobu - Japanese cuisine for special occasions
  • Mastro's - Steakhouse for celebrations
  • Cafe Gratitude - Plant-based LA favorite
  • Komodo - Asian fusion in Miami
  • Swan - David Grutman's Miami hotspot

He's known for hosting elaborate dinner parties at his Calabasas mansion.
