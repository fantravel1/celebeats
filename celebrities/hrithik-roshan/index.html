What is Hrithik Roshan's diet?

Hrithik Roshan follows a strict high-protein, moderate-carb diet combined with intermittent fasting (16:8). Frequently Asked Questions

What is Hrithik Roshan's diet plan?
Hrithik follows a high-protein diet with intermittent fasting (16:8 pattern). He eats 6-7 small meals within his eating window, focusing on lean proteins like chicken and fish, complex carbs like brown rice and oatmeal, and plenty of vegetables. He avoids sugar and processed foods completely during film prep.
Does Hrithik Roshan do intermittent fasting?
Yes, Hrithik practices 16:8 intermittent fasting, eating within an 8-hour window (typically 10 AM to 6 PM). He often does fasted workouts in the morning with just black coffee for energy.
How does Hrithik Roshan stay fit despite scoliosis?
Hrithik works with specialized trainers who understand his condition. He focuses on exercises that don't aggravate his spine, emphasizes proper form, and combines diet with targeted training. His discipline is what allows him to maintain his physique despite physical limitations.
Who is Hrithik Roshan's trainer?
Hrithik has worked with celebrity trainer Kris Gethin for several film transformations. He also works with other fitness experts depending on the role requirements. His trainers help him calculate precise macros and design meal plans.
Does Hrithik Roshan have cheat days?
During intense film preparations, Hrithik typically avoids cheat meals entirely for months. However, between films, he allows himself occasional treats. His philosophy is discipline during work and moderation during rest periods.
