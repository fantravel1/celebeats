Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Bella Hadid eat in New York?
Bella frequents Carbone in Greenwich Village for Italian classics, Via Carota for vegetable-forward dishes, and various health-conscious restaurants. She also enjoys Middle Eastern cuisine that connects to her Palestinian heritage.
What is Bella Hadid's diet like?
Bella follows a health-focused, clean eating lifestyle to manage her Lyme disease. She emphasizes anti-inflammatory foods, organic produce, lean proteins, and avoids processed foods. Her Palestinian heritage also influences her love of Middle Eastern cuisine.
Does Bella Hadid have Lyme disease?
Yes, Bella has been open about her Lyme disease diagnosis, which she shares with her mother Yolanda and brother Anwar. Her health journey has significantly influenced her approach to diet and wellness.
What is Bella Hadid's heritage?
Bella is of Palestinian descent through her father Mohamed Hadid and Dutch descent through her mother Yolanda. She's proud of her Palestinian heritage and often incorporates Middle Eastern cuisine into her diet.
Where does Bella Hadid eat in LA?
In Los Angeles, Bella has been spotted at Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, a favorite among celebrities for its Italian seafood and privacy. She also frequents health-focused restaurants and juice bars in the LA area.
