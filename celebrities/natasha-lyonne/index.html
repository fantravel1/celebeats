Where does Natasha Lyonne eat in NYC?

Where is Natasha Lyonne from?
Natasha Lyonne was born on April 4, 1979, in New York City. She's a true native New Yorker, and her NYC roots are central to her identity and work, especially evident in Russian Doll's NYC setting.

Natasha is known for creating and starring in Russian Doll (Netflix), playing Nicky Nichols in Orange Is the New Black, But I'm a Cheerleader, and Poker Face. She's also a respected writer and director.

Natasha is Jewish-American, born and raised in New York City. Her heritage deeply influences her appreciation for Jewish delis, traditional foods, and NYC's vibrant Jewish food culture.
