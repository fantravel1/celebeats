Frequently Asked Questions

What does Anna Wintour always order at restaurants?
Anna Wintour is famous for ordering the same meal everywhere: steak, cooked medium-rare, with no sauce. This consistent, unchanging order has become legendary in the fashion and restaurant world.
Where is Anna Wintour's favorite restaurant?
Anna Wintour is a well-known regular at The Waverly Inn in New York's West Village. The exclusive, intimate restaurant has been her go-to spot for years, where she maintains a regular table.
Does Anna Wintour host the Met Gala dinner?
Yes, Anna Wintour has been the chair of the Met Gala since 1995. The exclusive dinner following the red carpet is one of fashion's most coveted invitations, with carefully curated seating and menus.
Why does Anna Wintour order the same thing everywhere?
Anna Wintour's consistent order reflects her decisive, efficient approach to life. She doesn't waste time on menu decisions - she knows what she wants and orders it. This efficiency extends to her famously short, purposeful lunches.
How long has Anna Wintour been at Vogue?
Anna Wintour has been Editor-in-Chief of American Vogue since 1988, making her one of the longest-serving editors in fashion magazine history. She's also the Global Chief Content Officer of Conde Nast.
