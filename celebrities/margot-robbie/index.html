What are Margot Robbie's favorite restaurants?
Margot Robbie frequents Venice Beach spots including Gjelina, The Butcher's Daughter, Gjusta, and Felix Trattoria on Abbot Kinney. She also enjoys Nobu Malibu and Craig's in West Hollywood. Her Australian background influences her love for fresh, coastal cuisine.
What does Margot Robbie eat?
Margot follows a balanced diet with emphasis on whole foods. She loves avocado toast (very Australian), fresh seafood, and plant-forward dishes. She's mentioned her love for Vegemite, staying true to her Queensland roots.
Is Margot Robbie vegan or vegetarian?
Margot Robbie is not strictly vegan or vegetarian, but she does enjoy plant-based meals and frequents vegetarian-friendly restaurants. She eats a varied diet with plenty of vegetables and seafood.
Where does Margot Robbie live?
Margot Robbie lives in the Venice Beach area of Los Angeles with her husband Tom Ackerley. She's frequently spotted at local restaurants along Abbot Kinney Boulevard and the surrounding neighborhoods.
