Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Victoria Monet eat in LA?
Victoria Monet frequents upscale LA spots including Nobu Malibu for Japanese cuisine, Catch LA for rooftop seafood, Delilah in West Hollywood for its supper club atmosphere, and various Beverly Hills restaurants for industry events and celebrations.
What is Victoria Monet's favorite food?
Victoria has shown a love for Japanese cuisine, particularly at Nobu restaurants, as well as upscale seafood. Growing up in Sacramento, she also appreciates California's farm-fresh produce and diverse food scene.
Where did Victoria Monet celebrate her Grammy wins?
After her historic 2024 Grammy wins (including Best New Artist), Victoria celebrated at various LA after-party venues in West Hollywood and Beverly Hills, marking the milestone with her team, family, and daughter Hazel.
Does Victoria Monet follow a specific diet?
Victoria Monet has not publicly endorsed a specific diet plan. As a new mother and active performer, she appears to take a balanced approach focusing on nutrition and energy, while enjoying celebratory dining for special occasions.
What songs has Victoria Monet written for other artists?
Victoria Monet is a prolific songwriter who has co-written multiple hits for Ariana Grande including "thank u, next," "7 rings," and "Monopoly." She also wrote for Fifth Harmony, Nas, and many others before her own breakout with "On My Mama."
