What is Ranveer Singh's favorite food?

Ranveer Singh is a passionate street food lover. His favorites include Mumbai's iconic vada pav, pav bhaji, bhel puri, and chaat. He's also proud of his Sindhi heritage food and loves biryani. Unlike many stars, Ranveer openly celebrates street food culture and is often spotted at local eateries.

Ranveer's Legendary Street Food Adventures

The Ultimate Mumbai Foodie

Ranveer Singh is famous for his unbridled love of Mumbai street food. Unlike celebrities who stick to five-star restaurants, he regularly dives into the city's food scene:

  • Vada Pav - Mumbai's burger, his ultimate comfort food
  • Pav Bhaji - Late-night favorite, especially from Juhu Beach
  • Bhel Puri - Chowpatty Beach special, loves the tangy crunch
  • Chaat - All varieties - sev puri, dahi puri, ragda pattice
  • Pani Puri - Has been spotted at street stalls eating like a local

"I'm a Mumbai boy at heart. Nothing beats our street food!"

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ranveer Singh's favorite food?
Ranveer's favorites are Mumbai street foods - vada pav, pav bhaji, bhel puri, and chaat. He's also proud of his Sindhi heritage food and loves biryani. Unlike many celebrities, he openly celebrates street food culture.
Where does Ranveer Singh eat in Mumbai?
Ranveer is often spotted at Mumbai street food stalls in Juhu, Chowpatty, and Bandra. For fine dining, he frequents Bastian (especially with Deepika), Soho House, and various Bandra restaurants.
How did Ranveer Singh prepare for 83?
For 83, Ranveer followed a strict diet and cricket training regime to look like Kapil Dev. He worked with trainers, followed a high-protein diet with carb-cycling, and trained in cricket for months to perfect the legendary cricketer's mannerisms.
What do Ranveer and Deepika like to eat together?
Ranveer and Deepika are a foodie power couple. They're regulars at Bastian for seafood, explore local cuisines when traveling, and mix their Sindhi and Konkani food traditions at home. His love for street food complements her refined tastes.
Is Ranveer Singh vegetarian?
No, Ranveer Singh is not vegetarian. He enjoys both vegetarian street food and non-vegetarian dishes. He's particularly fond of biryani, kebabs, and meat dishes, especially during role preparations requiring muscle building.
