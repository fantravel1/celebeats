Frequently Asked Questions

Is Shahid Kapoor vegetarian?
Yes, Shahid Kapoor is a committed vegetarian. He doesn't eat meat, fish, or eggs. He gets his protein from paneer, lentils, dairy, nuts, and plant sources. His physique proves you can be very fit on a vegetarian diet.
How does Shahid Kapoor get protein as a vegetarian?
Shahid gets his protein from paneer (cottage cheese), lentils and dal, dairy products like milk and curd, nuts, seeds, and vegetarian protein supplements. Indian cuisine is rich in vegetarian protein options.
Does Shahid Kapoor practice yoga?
Yes, Shahid is a dedicated yoga practitioner. He practices regularly and credits yoga for his flexibility, mental clarity, and overall well-being. He often shares his yoga sessions on social media.
What is Shahid Kapoor's diet?
Shahid follows a clean vegetarian diet with multiple small meals, focus on protein from paneer and lentils, lots of vegetables, dairy, and minimal processed foods. He avoids junk food and maintains disciplined eating habits.
How did Shahid transform for his roles while being vegetarian?
Shahid achieved his transformations for Kabir Singh, Jersey, and Padmaavat through careful nutrition planning, vegetarian protein sources, proper supplementation, and intense training. Being vegetarian didn't limit his physical transformations.
