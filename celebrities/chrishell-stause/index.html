Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Chrishell Stause eat in LA?
Chrishell frequents West Hollywood and Beverly Hills restaurants including Craig's, Catch LA, BOA Steakhouse, and The Nice Guy. Her real estate career means she often dines at upscale spots for client entertainment.
What is Selling Sunset about?
Selling Sunset is a Netflix reality series following luxury real estate agents at The Oppenheim Group in Los Angeles. The show features high-end property listings and the personal drama between the agents, including Chrishell.
Who is Chrishell Stause married to?
Chrishell is married to G Flip, an Australian musician and drummer. They got married in 2023 after meeting through Dancing with the Stars connections. Previously, she was married to This Is Us actor Justin Hartley.
Was Chrishell Stause on Dancing with the Stars?
Yes, Chrishell competed on Season 29 of Dancing with the Stars in 2020, partnered with professional dancer Gleb Savchenko. She later began a relationship with her dance partner before meeting G Flip.
What is The Oppenheim Group?
The Oppenheim Group is a luxury real estate brokerage in Los Angeles founded by twins Jason and Brett Oppenheim. Chrishell works there as an agent, and the company is the focus of Netflix's Selling Sunset.
