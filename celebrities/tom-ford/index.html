The Tom Ford Approach

Philosophy

Tom Ford brings the same meticulous attention to detail to dining that he does to fashion and film. His approach reflects his personal brand:

  • Quality Over Quantity - Prefers fewer, exceptional dining experiences
  • Classic Establishments - Chooses proven institutions over trendy newcomers
  • Discretion - Values privacy and tends to frequent restaurants that protect celebrity patrons
  • Atmosphere - Appreciates sophisticated, well-designed spaces

His restaurant choices often become influential, as the fashion and entertainment industries follow his lead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Tom Ford frequents upscale London establishments including Scott's in Mayfair, The Wolseley, and various private members' clubs. He's known for preferring classic, elegant dining environments that match his sophisticated aesthetic.

In Los Angeles, Tom Ford has been spotted at upscale establishments like Spago, Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, and Craig's in West Hollywood. He tends to favor classic, refined restaurants with impeccable service and celebrity-friendly atmospheres.

Tom Ford is known for his disciplined approach to health and fitness. He's spoken about eating clean, avoiding processed foods, and maintaining a consistent routine. He reportedly prefers simple, high-quality ingredients over elaborate dishes and has mentioned the importance of moderation.

Tom Ford splits his time between London and Los Angeles. He has residences in both cities and has historically been based in London while maintaining strong ties to the LA entertainment industry, particularly following his success as a film director.
