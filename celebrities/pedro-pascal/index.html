Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pedro Pascal's favorite food?
Pedro Pascal has deep connections to Chilean cuisine, particularly empanadas and traditional comfort dishes. He also enjoys diverse international cuisines including Italian, Japanese, and Latin American food from his time living in NYC and LA.
Where does Pedro Pascal eat in New York?
Pedro Pascal has been spotted at various NYC restaurants including Carbone, Balthazar, and neighborhood Italian spots in the West Village. He spent years in NYC during his theater career and knows the restaurant scene well.
Does Pedro Pascal cook Chilean food?
Pedro has mentioned his connection to Chilean cuisine from childhood. While he often dines out, he maintains strong ties to the flavors of his heritage and seeks out authentic Latin American restaurants.
What is Pedro Pascal's connection to Chile?
Pedro Pascal was born in Santiago, Chile, and his family fled during Pinochet's regime when he was young. He maintains strong cultural connections to Chile, including through food and traditions.
Does Pedro Pascal follow a special diet?
Pedro Pascal doesn't follow any publicly known special diet. He seems to enjoy a diverse range of cuisines and appreciates food as both cultural connection and social experience.
