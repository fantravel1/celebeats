Frequently Asked Questions

What are Mariah Carey's famous food demands?
Mariah is legendary for specific requirements including bendy straws only, Norwegian salmon and capers, room-temperature water of specific brands, and late-night dining reservations. Her backstage riders are among the most detailed in the music industry.
Where does Mariah Carey eat in Las Vegas?
During her Caesars Palace residency, Mariah frequented Nobu at Caesars Palace for late-night sushi, SW Steakhouse at Wynn for special occasions, and Rao's at Caesars for Italian comfort food.
What is Mariah Carey's favorite food?
Mariah has famously claimed that Norwegian salmon and capers are her primary diet. However, she also enjoys pizza, Italian food reflecting her heritage, and champagne is her signature beverage of choice.
Does Mariah Carey follow a strict diet?
Mariah's diet is unique to her. While she claims to eat primarily salmon and capers, she has been seen enjoying pizza and other indulgences. She focuses on what makes her feel good rather than following a strict plan.
Where does Mariah Carey spend the holidays?
The Queen of Christmas often spends holidays in Aspen, Colorado, where she enjoys mountain dining and festive celebrations. She has been spotted at Aspen's upscale restaurants and is known for lavish holiday feasts with her twins.
