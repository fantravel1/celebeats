Frequently Asked Questions

What are Greta Gerwig's favorite restaurants?
Greta Gerwig loves NYC's indie scene restaurants and Brooklyn neighborhood spots. She appreciates cozy cafes, farm-to-table dining, and unpretentious Italian restaurants. Via Carota in the West Village and various Brooklyn spots are favorites.
Where does Greta Gerwig live?
Greta Gerwig is primarily based in New York City, where she lives with partner Noah Baumbach and their children. Though originally from Sacramento, California, NYC has been her home base throughout her indie film career.
Is Greta Gerwig married to Noah Baumbach?
Greta Gerwig and Noah Baumbach are longtime partners and collaborators (Marriage Story, White Noise). They have children together and are frequently seen dining at NYC restaurants together.
Where is Greta Gerwig from originally?
Greta Gerwig was born and raised in Sacramento, California, which inspired her semi-autobiographical film Lady Bird. She moved to New York to pursue acting and became a central figure in the mumblecore indie film movement before transitioning to directing.
