Frequently Asked Questions

What does Canelo Alvarez eat?
During fight camp, Canelo follows a strict diet of lean proteins (chicken, fish, beef), vegetables, and controlled carbohydrates to make his 168-pound weight limit. Outside of camp, he enjoys traditional Mexican cuisine from his Guadalajara roots including tacos, carne asada, birria, and family recipes.
Where does Canelo Alvarez train?
Canelo trains in San Diego with his trainer Eddy Reynoso at his dedicated gym. He also maintains a home and gym in Guadalajara, Mexico. The San Diego location allows proximity to both his Mexican roots and world-class training facilities.
Why is he called Canelo?
Canelo means "cinnamon" in Spanish, a nickname given to Saul Alvarez because of his distinctive red hair, which is rare in Mexico. The name stuck from childhood and became his famous ring name that's now known worldwide.
Where does Canelo eat in Las Vegas?
During fight weeks in Las Vegas, Canelo has been spotted at upscale Mexican restaurants like Javier's at Aria. Post-fight celebrations often include steakhouses and high-end restaurants on the Strip with his team and family.
What is Canelo Alvarez's favorite food?
Canelo's favorite foods reflect his Guadalajara roots - carne asada, tacos, and traditional Jalisco dishes like birria and tortas ahogadas. He's spoken about missing his mother's cooking when training away from home and celebrating victories with family food.
