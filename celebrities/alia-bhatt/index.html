How did Alia Bhatt lose weight?

Alia Bhatt transformed her body through portion control, regular exercise, and guidance from nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar. She didn't eliminate any food group but focused on eating local, seasonal foods and practicing mindful eating. Her approach emphasizes sustainable habits over crash diets.

Alia's Food and Fitness Transformation

From Student of the Year to Gangubai: A Journey

Alia's weight loss journey became one of Bollywood's most talked-about transformations. Working with celebrity nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar, she learned sustainable eating:

  • No Food Elimination - Didn't cut out carbs or any food group entirely
  • Portion Control - Learned to eat smaller, more frequent meals
  • Local & Seasonal - Emphasis on traditional Indian foods
  • Never Skip Meals - Breakfast is non-negotiable
  • Mindful Eating - Focus on eating slowly and enjoying food
  • Treats Allowed - Room for favorites like pasta and chocolate

"I never starved myself. I just learned to eat right."

Rujuta Diwekar's Guidelines

Frequently Asked Questions

How did Alia Bhatt lose weight?
Alia lost weight by working with nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar, focusing on portion control, eating local and seasonal foods, never skipping meals, and allowing herself treats in moderation. She didn't follow any crash diet but learned sustainable eating habits.
What is Alia Bhatt's favorite food?
Alia's favorite food is pasta - she's a self-confessed carb lover. She also loves dal-chawal, her mother Soni Razdan's cooking, ice cream, and chocolate.
Who is Alia Bhatt's nutritionist?
Alia works with celebrity nutritionist Rujuta Diwekar, who is known for her approach of eating local, seasonal foods and never eliminating entire food groups. Rujuta also works with other Bollywood stars like Kareena Kapoor.
What does Alia Bhatt eat for breakfast?
Alia never skips breakfast. Her typical breakfast includes traditional Indian options like poha (flattened rice), upma (semolina dish), or eggs with toast. She believes breakfast is the most important meal.
Is Alia Bhatt vegetarian?
No, Alia Bhatt is not strictly vegetarian. She eats both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. Her diet includes eggs, fish, and chicken along with plenty of vegetarian Indian dishes.
