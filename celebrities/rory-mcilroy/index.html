Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Rory McIlroy live?
Rory McIlroy is based in Jupiter, Florida, a popular area for professional golfers including Tiger Woods. He maintains connections to his hometown of Holywood in Northern Ireland and frequently visits during the European Tour season and for personal visits.
Where does Rory McIlroy eat in Jupiter Florida?
Rory enjoys the Jupiter and Palm Beach dining scene, including waterfront restaurants like Guanabanas and 1000 North, upscale establishments in West Palm Beach, and casual spots frequented by the golf community in the area.
What is Rory McIlroy's diet?
Rory has worked with nutritionists to optimize his diet for golf. He focuses on lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, and fresh produce while maintaining balance. He enjoys Irish comfort food and doesn't follow an overly restrictive approach.
Does Rory McIlroy drink Guinness?
As a proud Northern Irishman, Rory has been photographed enjoying Guinness and has celebrated victories with the iconic Irish stout. It's a connection to his Irish roots and heritage.
Where does Rory McIlroy eat during the Masters?
During Masters week in Augusta, Georgia, Rory likely visits popular golfer spots like Frog Hollow Tavern and other farm-to-table restaurants. Augusta has a vibrant dining scene during tournament week with restaurants catering to players and patrons alike.
