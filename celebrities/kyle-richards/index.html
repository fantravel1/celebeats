Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Kyle Richards eat in Beverly Hills?
Kyle frequents The Polo Lounge at The Beverly Hills Hotel, Spago, The Ivy, and Mastro's. She's also regularly spotted at Craig's in West Hollywood and Lisa Vanderpump's restaurants.
What restaurants appear on Real Housewives of Beverly Hills?
RHOBH films at numerous high-end restaurants including The Polo Lounge, Nobu Malibu, Craig's, PUMP, and various private dining rooms in Beverly Hills hotels. Kyle has dined at most of these locations on camera.
Is Kyle Richards still on Real Housewives of Beverly Hills?
Yes, Kyle Richards is the only original cast member who has appeared in every season of RHOBH since its premiere in 2010. She's known as the OG Beverly Hills housewife.
Is Kyle Richards related to Paris Hilton?
Yes, Kyle Richards is Paris Hilton's aunt. Kyle's half-sister Kathy Hilton is Paris's mother. Kathy also appeared on RHOBH as a "friend of" the housewives.
What is The Agency Kyle Richards is associated with?
The Agency is the luxury real estate brokerage founded by Kyle's husband Mauricio Umansky. The company has been featured on RHOBH and now has its own Netflix show, Buying Beverly Hills.
