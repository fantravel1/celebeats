What is Jason Sudeikis' favorite food?

Jason Sudeikis is famously devoted to Kansas City BBQ. He's a proud KC native and has spoken extensively about his love for Joe's KC (formerly Oklahoma Joe's), particularly their burnt ends and the legendary Z-Man sandwich. BBQ is central to his Midwest identity and he's brought national attention to KC's BBQ scene.

Jason's Signature Order

Joe's KC Z-Man Sandwich

Jason Sudeikis has repeatedly named Joe's KC (formerly Oklahoma Joe's) as his favorite restaurant. The Z-Man sandwich - slow-smoked brisket with smoked provolone, onion rings, and BBQ sauce - is his go-to order along with burnt ends.

  • The Order: Z-Man Sandwich, burnt ends, fries
  • Favorite Spot: Joe's KC, Kansas City
  • BBQ Style: Kansas City-style (sweet, tomato-based)
