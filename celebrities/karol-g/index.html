Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Karol G eat in Miami?
Karol G frequents Miami's Latin dining scene including Komodo in Brickell, Swan in the Design District, and Colombian restaurants across the city. She enjoys Miami's vibrant nightlife dining culture that blends food with entertainment.
What is Karol G's favorite food?
Karol G is proud of her Colombian roots and loves traditional foods from Medellin including bandeja paisa, arepas, empanadas, and Colombian street food. She has spoken about missing her hometown's food when touring internationally.
Does Karol G eat in Medellin?
Yes, Karol G returns to Medellin regularly and enjoys both traditional spots like Mondongos for bandeja paisa and modern restaurants like El Cielo for contemporary Colombian cuisine.
What is bandeja paisa?
Bandeja paisa is Colombia's national dish from the Antioquia region (Medellin). It features beans, rice, chicharron (fried pork belly), sweet plantain, avocado, arepa, fried egg, and ground beef on one massive platter. It is central to Karol G's food heritage.
Does Karol G follow a strict diet?
Karol G maintains her fitness for demanding performances and tours but does not appear to follow an extremely restrictive diet. She balances her Colombian comfort food heritage with healthier choices and stays active through her intense performance schedule.
