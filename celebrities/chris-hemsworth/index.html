What does Chris Hemsworth eat for Thor?

Chris Hemsworth eats 4,500+ calories daily when preparing for Thor. His diet includes 8 meals per day with lean proteins (chicken, fish, steak), complex carbs (rice, sweet potato), vegetables, and protein shakes. He works with trainer Luke Zocchi.

Intermittent Fasting

When not in intense training for a role, Chris practices intermittent fasting with an 8-hour eating window. He's spoken about the mental clarity benefits and uses this approach for maintenance between filming.
