What does Chris Pratt like to eat?

Chris Pratt embraces farm-to-table eating, using lamb and other meats from his own Washington farm. He loves casual American dining, hearty comfort food, and cooking with family. For Marvel films, he follows high-protein, low-carb diets but normally enjoys a relaxed approach to food.

Farm Life

San Juan Islands Farm

Chris Pratt owns a farm in the San Juan Islands, Washington, where he raises animals and grows food:

  • Sheep - Raises lambs for meat, shared openly on social media
  • Pigs - Heritage breed pigs
  • Chickens - Fresh eggs daily
  • Garden vegetables - Seasonal produce

He's passionate about sustainable farming and knowing where his food comes from. He's shared the entire process from raising animals to processing meat.

Farm-to-Table Philosophy

Chris Pratt has been vocal about his farm-to-table approach:

  • Transparency - Shares the reality of farm life
  • Sustainability - Focus on responsible animal husbandry
  • Family involvement - Involves his kids in farm activities
  • Self-sufficiency - Growing and raising their own food

Marvel Transformation

Guardians of the Galaxy Diet

Chris Pratt famously transformed his physique for the first Guardians of the Galaxy, losing 60 pounds in 6 months. His approach included:

  • High protein - Lots of lean meats and fish
  • Low carb - Cutting out beer and processed carbs
  • Meal timing - Multiple smaller meals throughout the day
  • Water - Massive hydration focus
  • No alcohol - Cut out beer entirely during training

He's spoken about the difficulty of this transformation, especially cutting out beer which he's been honest about loving. Between projects, he returns to a more relaxed approach.

Minnesota Roots

Born in Virginia, Minnesota, Chris Pratt grew up in Lake Stevens, Washington. His Midwest and Pacific Northwest upbringing shaped his food preferences:

  • Hunting and fishing - Grew up outdoorsy
  • Comfort food - Hearty, unpretentious meals
  • Grilling - Weekend BBQ traditions
  • Local and seasonal - Pacific Northwest approach

He's spoken about fishing trips and outdoor activities that continue to influence his relationship with food and nature.

Home Cooking

Family Meals

With wife Katherine Schwarzenegger, Chris Pratt focuses on family meals and home cooking:

  • Farm-raised lamb - From their own sheep
  • Grilled meats - Regular weekend BBQs
  • Fresh eggs - From their chickens
  • Garden vegetables - Seasonal from the garden

Family meals are a priority, and they try to eat together as much as possible despite his busy filming schedule.
