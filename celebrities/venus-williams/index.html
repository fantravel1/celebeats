Is Venus Williams vegan?

Yes, Venus Williams follows a raw vegan diet. She adopted this lifestyle in 2011 after being diagnosed with Sjogren's syndrome, an autoimmune disease that causes fatigue and joint pain. The plant-based diet has helped her manage symptoms and remarkably continue her professional tennis career well into her 40s. Venus has become an advocate for plant-based eating in athletics.
