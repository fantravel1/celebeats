What is Rihanna's favorite food?

Rihanna is passionate about Caribbean cuisine, especially from her native Barbados. Her favorites include macaroni pie (a Bajan staple), fried fish, cou-cou, and anything with Bajan pepper sauce. In LA, she frequents Giorgio Baldi for Italian and Nobu for Japanese. In NYC, she loves Carbone and Emilio's Ballato. She's famous for her love of spicy food and late-night dining.

What is Rihanna's favorite food?
Rihanna loves Caribbean food from her native Barbados, including macaroni pie, fried flying fish, cou-cou, and anything with Bajan pepper sauce. She's also fond of Italian food, especially late-night pasta at Giorgio Baldi.
Where does Rihanna eat in Los Angeles?
Rihanna frequents Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica for Italian, Nobu Malibu for Japanese, Craig's in West Hollywood, and The Nice Guy for late-night dining. She prefers upscale spots with good privacy.

Rihanna doesn't follow strict dietary restrictions. She embraces comfort food, Caribbean flavors, and indulgent meals. She's known for her love of spicy food and doesn't shy away from carbs or late-night eating.

Born and raised in Barbados, Rihanna maintains strong ties to her Caribbean culinary heritage. She's spoken in interviews about craving Bajan dishes like macaroni pie, flying fish, and cou-cou, and keeps Bajan seasoning in her kitchen.
