Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tan France's favorite food?
Tan France has spoken about his love for curry, reflecting his British-Pakistani heritage. Growing up in Doncaster with Pakistani parents, South Asian cuisine was central to his upbringing and remains his comfort food.
Where does Tan France live?
Tan France lives in Salt Lake City, Utah with his husband Rob France. Despite being from England, he's made SLC his home base while filming Queer Eye and pursuing other projects.
Is Tan France British or American?
Tan France is British, born in Doncaster, England to Pakistani parents. He later became an American citizen. His British-Pakistani heritage strongly influences his food preferences and cultural identity.
What is the French Tuck?
The French Tuck is Tan France's signature styling technique where you tuck just the front of your shirt into your pants. He popularized this look on Queer Eye and it's become synonymous with his fashion advice.
Does Tan France miss British food?
Yes! Tan has spoken about missing British curry houses and the UK's South Asian food culture. While he's adapted to life in Salt Lake City, he still seeks out good curry and enjoys visiting the UK for the food.
