Where does Cindy Crawford eat?

Cindy Crawford and husband Rande Gerber embrace the Malibu lifestyle. Their go-to spots include Nobu Malibu for beachfront Japanese, Soho House Malibu for private dinners, Malibu Farm for healthy California fare, and the legendary Cafe Habana for Cuban cuisine.
