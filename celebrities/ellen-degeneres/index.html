What does Ellen DeGeneres eat?

Ellen DeGeneres follows a vegan diet, avoiding all animal products. She adopted veganism for ethical reasons, particularly her love of animals. Her diet includes plant-based foods, vegetables, fruits, and vegan alternatives.
