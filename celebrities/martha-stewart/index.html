Frequently Asked Questions

Martha Stewart is known as America's original lifestyle icon. She built a media empire around cooking, entertaining, gardening, and home decorating through Martha Stewart Living magazine, numerous cookbooks, and television shows.

Martha Stewart has written over 90 books, with many focused on cooking and entertaining. Her notable cookbooks include "Entertaining" (1982), "Martha Stewart's Cooking School", and her annual holiday guides.

Martha Stewart frequents upscale New York restaurants including The Polo Bar, Balthazar, Le Bernardin, and Jean-Georges. She's also known for hosting elaborate dinner parties at her homes in Bedford, Maine, and the Hamptons.

Martha Stewart and Snoop Dogg have an unlikely but genuine friendship that began on her show. They co-hosted "Martha & Snoop's Potluck Dinner Party" on VH1 and continue to collaborate, proving that great food brings everyone together.
