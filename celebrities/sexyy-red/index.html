Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Sexyy Red eat in St. Louis?
Sexyy Red is proudly from St. Louis and connected to the city's unique food scene. Iconic STL spots like Imo's Pizza (provel cheese), Pappy's Smokehouse (BBQ), and Sweetie Pie's (soul food) represent the food culture she grew up with. She embraces St. Louis cuisine as part of her identity.
What is provel cheese and why is it a St. Louis thing?
Provel is a processed cheese blend of cheddar, Swiss, and provolone that was invented in St. Louis and is virtually unknown outside the city. It's the signature topping on Imo's-style St. Louis pizza - thin crust, square-cut, with gooey provel. It's a love-it-or-hate-it regional delicacy that Sexyy Red's hometown is famous for.
What is toasted ravioli?
Toasted ravioli (T-ravs) are a St. Louis original - ravioli that are breaded and deep-fried instead of boiled, served with marinara sauce. Invented on The Hill (St. Louis's Italian neighborhood), they're a must-have appetizer at every STL restaurant and sports event. They're as St. Louis as the Gateway Arch.
Does Sexyy Red follow a diet?
No, Sexyy Red is unapologetically open about eating what she wants. She doesn't project a curated health image. Fast food, BBQ, soul food, and St. Louis specialties are all part of her lifestyle. Her authentic, no-pretense personality extends to everything including food.
Where does Sexyy Red eat when touring?
When touring, Sexyy Red frequents Atlanta's late-night dining scene (wings, waffles, Waffle House), Miami's nightlife restaurants, and LA spots. She embraces the late-night eating culture that comes with performing and the hip-hop touring lifestyle.
