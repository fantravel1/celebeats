Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tom Hanks' favorite restaurant?
Tom Hanks is famously devoted to In-N-Out Burger, calling it his favorite fast food. He typically orders a Double-Double with grilled onions. He also enjoys classic LA spots like The Apple Pan, Dan Tana's, and Musso & Frank Grill.
Where does Tom Hanks eat in Los Angeles?
Tom frequents In-N-Out Burger locations across LA, The Apple Pan in West LA for burgers, Dan Tana's in West Hollywood for Italian, and Musso & Frank Grill in Hollywood for classic American fare and martinis.
What does Tom Hanks like to eat?
Tom Hanks enjoys classic American comfort food - burgers, steaks, Italian-American cuisine, and traditional diner fare. He prefers simple, well-made food over trendy restaurants. His go-to is a Double-Double from In-N-Out.
What is Tom Hanks' In-N-Out order?
Tom Hanks orders a Double-Double (two beef patties, two slices of cheese) with grilled onions at In-N-Out Burger. He's been photographed and interviewed about his love for this order many times.
Does Tom Hanks follow a special diet?
Tom has Type 2 diabetes and has spoken about managing it through diet awareness, but he doesn't follow a strict or trendy diet. He enjoys his favorite foods in moderation and focuses on classic American comfort food.
