Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Max Verstappen eat in Monaco?
Max Verstappen frequents Monaco's upscale dining scene including Zuma for Japanese cuisine, Nobu Monte Carlo for Japanese-Peruvian fusion, and various Mediterranean restaurants. As a Monaco resident, he enjoys the principality's world-class food scene.
What is Max Verstappen's diet like?
As an elite F1 driver, Max follows a strict nutrition program during race season. This includes lean proteins, complex carbs, and careful hydration. Red Bull's performance team manages his nutrition to optimize weight and physical performance for racing.
Does Max Verstappen eat Dutch food?
While Max represents the Netherlands and was born in Belgium, his Monaco lifestyle means more Mediterranean cuisine. However, he's mentioned enjoying Dutch treats like stroopwafels and bitterballen when visiting home or during Dutch GP weekends.
Where do F1 drivers eat during race weekends?
F1 drivers primarily eat at team hospitality units in the paddock, which feature world-class chefs and nutrition-focused menus. Red Bull is known for having exceptional hospitality. Drivers also explore local restaurants during race weekends.
Is Max Verstappen a foodie?
Max enjoys good food but is known more for his gaming and racing interests. His girlfriend Kelly Piquet often shares food content, and they're spotted at upscale Monaco restaurants together. During race season, food is more functional than indulgent.
