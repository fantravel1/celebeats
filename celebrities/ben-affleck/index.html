Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Ben Affleck always at Dunkin' Donuts?
Ben's Dunkin' devotion stems from his Boston roots. It's become his signature, with hundreds of paparazzi photos capturing him with iced coffee. In 2024, he starred in a Super Bowl commercial for Dunkin', fully embracing his meme status.
What is Ben Affleck's Dunkin order?
Ben typically orders a large iced coffee with cream and sugar from Dunkin'. It's perhaps the most documented celebrity beverage order in history.
Where do Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez eat?
Ben and JLo frequent LA hotspots like Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica (their go-to), Nobu Malibu, and Avra Beverly Hills. In NYC, they've been spotted at Carbone and Scalinatella.
Is Ben Affleck from Boston?
Ben grew up in Cambridge, Massachusetts, right next to Boston. He met best friend Matt Damon there as a kid, and both maintain strong Boston roots and accents.
Did Ben Affleck really work at Dunkin'?
For the 2024 Super Bowl commercial, Ben "worked" the Dunkin' drive-thru, surprising customers. While he doesn't actually work there, his passion for the brand is completely genuine.
