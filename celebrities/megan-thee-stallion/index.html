Megan Thee Stallion Food FAQs

Megan Thee Stallion loves Texas BBQ, especially brisket and ribs from Houston spots. She also enjoys Southern soul food, Tex-Mex, and has developed a taste for healthy, protein-rich meals as part of her fitness journey.

Megan follows a balanced diet focused on lean proteins and vegetables. She practices portion control and works with nutritionists to fuel her performances. She believes in balance - eating healthy most of the time while still enjoying her Texas favorites.

Megan frequents Houston BBQ legends like Truth BBQ, Killen's BBQ, and Gatlin's BBQ. She's also been spotted at local Tex-Mex restaurants and supports her hometown's diverse food scene.

Megan combines intense workout routines with a balanced diet. She works with trainers and nutritionists, focuses on strength training and cardio, and maintains discipline while still allowing herself to enjoy food. Her "Hot Girl" philosophy is about confidence and health.
