What does Anderson Cooper eat?

Anderson Cooper enjoys refined dining with a global palate developed from extensive travel. He appreciates high-quality restaurants in NYC, international cuisine, and maintains a sophisticated approach to food befitting his Vanderbilt heritage.

Vanderbilt Heritage

Old Money Upbringing

Anderson's Vanderbilt heritage shaped his palate:

  • Gloria Vanderbilt - Mother's artistic, cultured lifestyle
  • NYC Society - Exposure to fine dining from childhood
  • European Travel - Early exposure to continental cuisine
  • Refined Tastes - Appreciation for quality and presentation
  • Classic American - Traditional upscale American dishes

Sophisticated Yet Grounded

Despite his privileged background, Anderson has spoken about living below his means and not inheriting his mother's fortune. His food choices balance refinement with practicality, shaped by years of reporting from challenging locations worldwide.

Greenwich Village Life

Anderson's historic firehouse home in Greenwich Village puts him near some of NYC's best restaurants. The neighborhood's mix of classic establishments and innovative newcomers suits his sophisticated but unpretentious style.
