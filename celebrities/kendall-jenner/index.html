Frequently Asked Questions

Kendall frequents Carbone for Italian-American classics, Bar Pitti in Greenwich Village, Cipriani Downtown in SoHo, and The Mercer Kitchen. These spots are especially popular during Fashion Week.

Kendall follows an intuitive eating approach rather than strict dieting. She enjoys avocado toast, smoothies, lean proteins, and doesn't deprive herself of pizza or pasta. Before fashion shows, she eats cleaner but doesn't believe in crash dieting.

818 Tequila is Kendall's award-winning tequila brand, named after the Calabasas area code. Launched in 2021, it offers Blanco, Reposado, and Anejo varieties, produced sustainably in Jalisco, Mexico.

In LA, Kendall is regularly spotted at Craig's in West Hollywood, Nobu Malibu, and The Nice Guy. Many of these restaurants now serve her 818 Tequila in their cocktail menus.
