Is Anne Hathaway vegan?

Anne Hathaway was strictly vegan for several years but transitioned away from it during filming of Interstellar in Iceland. She's spoken about how eating fish made her feel "like a computer rebooting" and more connected to her body. She now follows a balanced, flexible approach that includes some fish and lean proteins while still emphasizing vegetables and whole foods.

Anne's Food Philosophy Evolution

Anne Hathaway's relationship with food has been public and thoughtful. She was vegan for years, partly for ethical reasons and health. During filming in Iceland for Interstellar, she ate salmon and felt an immediate physical change. She's since advocated for listening to your body rather than following strict dietary rules. "I just didn't feel good or healthy... not strong," she said of her vegan period.
