Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blackpink Jennie's favorite food?
Jennie loves Korean food including samgyetang (ginseng chicken soup), jjajangmyeon (black bean noodles), and Korean BBQ. She also enjoys French cuisine during Paris visits and has a well-known love for avocados and healthy foods.
Where does Jennie eat in Paris?
As a Chanel ambassador, Jennie frequents Paris for fashion weeks. She's been spotted at chic Parisian cafes, fine dining restaurants, and trendy spots in the Marais and Saint-Germain neighborhoods. She enjoys French pastries and upscale dining experiences.
What restaurants does Jennie visit in LA?
In Los Angeles, Jennie has been seen at celebrity hotspots like Craig's, Nobu Malibu, and various healthy eateries in West Hollywood. She also frequents Korean restaurants in LA's Koreatown when craving home flavors.
