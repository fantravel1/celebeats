What food brands does MrBeast own?

MrBeast owns two major food brands: MrBeast Burger, a virtual restaurant chain with over 1,700 locations delivering burgers and chicken sandwiches, and Feastables, a chocolate bar brand featuring simple, quality ingredients. Both brands leverage his massive YouTube audience and have become industry disruptors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MrBeast Burger?
MrBeast Burger is a virtual restaurant brand created by Jimmy Donaldson in 2020. It operates as a ghost kitchen through existing restaurants, offering burgers, chicken sandwiches, and sides via delivery apps. The brand expanded to over 1,700 locations worldwide.
What is Feastables chocolate?
Feastables is MrBeast's chocolate bar brand launched in 2022. It features simple, quality ingredients without artificial colors or flavors. Available in milk chocolate, dark chocolate, and peanut butter flavors at major retailers nationwide.
How many subscribers does MrBeast have?
MrBeast is the most-subscribed individual YouTuber with over 300 million subscribers on his main channel. He also runs MrBeast Gaming, Beast Reacts, and Beast Philanthropy channels.
Where is MrBeast from?
MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) was born in Wichita, Kansas on May 7, 1998, but was raised in Greenville, North Carolina, where he still operates his business and creates content.
How can I order MrBeast Burger?
MrBeast Burger is available through delivery apps like DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub. Search for "MrBeast Burger" in your delivery app to see if it's available in your area. Some locations also offer pickup.
