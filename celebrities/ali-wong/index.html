Ali Wong

San Francisco's comedy queen with an unapologetic love of food. From pho to dim sum, Ali brings the same passion to eating as she does to stand-up.

What does Ali Wong eat?

Ali Wong is a passionate foodie who loves Asian cuisine, especially Vietnamese and Chinese dishes from her heritage. She grew up in San Francisco's diverse food scene and frequently discusses food in her comedy, with favorites including pho, dim sum, and authentic Asian restaurants.

Vietnamese-Chinese Heritage

Family Food Traditions

Ali's multicultural Asian background shaped her food identity:

  • Pho - Vietnamese comfort food staple
  • Banh Mi - Vietnamese sandwiches
  • Dim Sum - Chinese brunch tradition
  • Chinese BBQ - Roast duck, char siu
  • Rice Dishes - Foundation of Asian meals

Mom's Cooking

Ali has spoken about her mother's Vietnamese cooking and how it connected her to her heritage. Home-cooked Asian food remains her ultimate comfort food, representing family and cultural identity.

San Francisco Food Scene

Bay Area Favorites

Growing up in San Francisco gave Ali access to legendary food:

  • Chinatown - SF's historic Chinese restaurants
  • Tenderloin - Vietnamese food corridor
  • Mission District - Burritos and tacos
  • Japantown - Japanese cuisine
  • Clement Street - Richmond District Asian spots

Diverse City Influence

San Francisco's multicultural food scene taught Ali to appreciate diverse cuisines. The city's Asian-American population means authentic options abound, setting a high bar for Asian food anywhere else.

Food in Comedy

Ali's comedy frequently features food:

  • Baby Cobra - Food cravings during pregnancy
  • Hard Knock Wife - Mom life and eating
  • Don Wong - Food and family themes
  • Asian Food Pride - Celebrating cultural cuisine
  • Unapologetic Eating - Rejecting diet culture

Her honest discussions of food, body image, and eating resonate with audiences.

LA Life & Restaurants

Living in Los Angeles, Ali has access to diverse Asian cuisine:

  • San Gabriel Valley - LA's authentic Chinese food hub
  • Koreatown - Korean BBQ and more
  • Little Tokyo - Japanese restaurants
  • Thai Town - Authentic Thai cuisine
  • Westminster/Little Saigon - Vietnamese food paradise

LA's diverse Asian communities offer the authentic flavors Ali craves.

Family & Food

Ali's approach to food with her family:

  • Raising Kids - Sharing Asian food culture with daughters
  • Family Meals - Emphasis on eating together
  • Cultural Continuity - Passing on food traditions
  • Restaurant Adventures - Taking family to favorite spots
  • Home Cooking - Asian dishes at home

Food serves as a way to connect her daughters to their Asian-American heritage.

Favorite Foods

Ali's go-to favorites:

  • Pho - Ultimate comfort food
  • Dim Sum - Weekend family tradition

  • Banh Mi - Perfect sandwich
