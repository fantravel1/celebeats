Frequently Asked Questions

Donatella Versace frequents elegant Milan establishments including Paper Moon, Giacomo Bistrot, and various upscale Italian restaurants in the fashion district. She's known for her appreciation of authentic Italian cuisine and glamorous dining settings that match her bold aesthetic.

Donatella Versace loves Italian cuisine, particularly dishes from her native southern Italy. She has mentioned her appreciation for pasta, fresh seafood, and traditional Calabrian recipes from her childhood in Reggio Calabria. The spicy, bold flavors of the region reflect her personality.

The Versace brand operates Palazzo Versace hotels which include fine dining restaurants. The Dubai and Gold Coast properties feature restaurants serving Italian cuisine in the signature Versace luxury style, with opulent decor and menus celebrating Italian culinary traditions.

Donatella Versace was born in Reggio Calabria, in the southern tip of Italy. She later moved to Milan where she worked alongside her brother Gianni at Versace. Her Southern Italian heritage continues to influence her appreciation for bold flavors and authentic Italian cuisine.
