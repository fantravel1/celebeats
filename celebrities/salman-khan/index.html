What is Salman Khan's diet?

Salman Khan follows a strict protein-rich diet to maintain his famous physique. He eats 6-7 small meals throughout the day, focusing on egg whites, grilled chicken, fish, brown rice, and vegetables. The Khan Family Food Tradition

The Khan family is famous for their Sunday brunches and Eid celebrations. Father Salim Khan is a legendary screenwriter, and mother Salma Khan is known as one of the best cooks in Bollywood circles.

Khan Family Gatherings

  • Sunday Brunch - Entire family gathers at Galaxy Apartments weekly
  • Eid Celebrations - Elaborate feasts with the entire Bollywood family
  • Panvel Weekends - Farm-to-table organic meals at the farmhouse
  • Sisters' Cooking - Alvira and Arpita also known for their culinary skills
  • Being Human Events - Charity dinners and community meals

Salman's Daily Diet Plan

The Tiger's Fitness Fuel

Salman's typical day of eating during film shoots:

  • Early Morning: Black coffee or green tea
  • Breakfast: Egg whites (6-8), oatmeal, fruits
  • Mid-Morning: Protein shake with banana
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken/fish, brown rice, vegetables
  • Evening: Sprouts, nuts, or another protein shake
  • Dinner: Light meal - soup, salad, grilled protein
  • Cheat Day: Mother's biryani, kebabs, and family favorites

"I work out to eat what I love on weekends."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Salman Khan's daily diet?
Salman Khan follows a strict protein-rich diet with 6-7 small meals daily. He focuses on egg whites, grilled chicken, fish, brown rice, and vegetables. He avoids sugar and processed foods but enjoys cheat meals with his mother's home cooking on weekends.
What is Salman Khan's favorite food?
Salman Khan's favorite food is his mother Salma Khan's home cooking, especially her biryani and kebabs. Despite his strict fitness diet, he cherishes family meals and considers his mother's cooking the best cheat meal.
Is Salman Khan vegetarian?
No, Salman Khan is non-vegetarian. His fitness diet heavily relies on lean proteins like chicken breast, fish, and egg whites. He also enjoys traditional Pathan cuisine featuring meat dishes from his family heritage.
What happens at Salman Khan's Panvel farmhouse?
Salman's Panvel farmhouse is his weekend retreat where family and close Bollywood friends gather. The farmhouse features organic farming, and gatherings include elaborate home-cooked meals, outdoor activities, and celebrations away from Mumbai's crowds.
How does Salman Khan stay fit at his age?
Salman Khan maintains his physique through disciplined eating, regular workouts, and cycling. He follows a high-protein, low-carb diet during shoots, trains with weights, and is famous for his shirtless scenes even in his late 50s.
