Frequently Asked Questions

What are Austin Butler's favorite restaurants?
Austin Butler frequents upscale LA restaurants including Craig's, Catch LA, and San Vicente Bungalows. As an LA native, he also enjoys local health-focused spots like Erewhon and various cafes around West Hollywood and the surrounding areas.
What did Austin Butler eat for Elvis?
For his role as Elvis Presley, Austin worked with nutritionists and trainers to transform his physique throughout filming. His diet evolved based on the period of Elvis's life being portrayed - eating more for younger Elvis and adjusting for later periods. The transformation was integral to his Oscar-nominated performance.
Does Austin Butler follow a special diet?
Austin Butler maintains a health-conscious diet, particularly when preparing for physically demanding roles. He focuses on lean proteins, vegetables, and quality ingredients. Like many LA-based actors, he prioritizes nutrition but isn't overly restrictive in his approach.
Is Austin Butler vegan or vegetarian?
No, Austin Butler is not vegan or vegetarian. He follows a balanced diet that includes lean proteins. However, growing up in health-conscious Southern California, he appreciates plant-based options and maintains a varied, nutritious diet.
