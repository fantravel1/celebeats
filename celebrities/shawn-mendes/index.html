Is Shawn Mendes plant-based?

Where does Shawn Mendes eat in Toronto?
As a Toronto native, Shawn enjoys the city's diverse food scene including plant-based spots like Planta, Thai restaurants like Pai, and various King West area establishments.
What is Shawn Mendes's diet like?
Shawn focuses on clean eating with whole foods, lots of vegetables, and plant-forward meals. He prioritizes foods that give him energy for performing while being conscious of environmental impact.

In LA, Shawn frequents health-conscious spots like Cafe Gratitude, Gracias Madre, and Erewhon. He's also been spotted at Nobu Malibu and Craig's for special occasions.

Shawn has mentioned enjoying simple, healthy cooking at home. He focuses on quick, nutritious meals that support his active lifestyle and touring schedule.
