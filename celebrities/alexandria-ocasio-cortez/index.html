Where did AOC work as a bartender?

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez worked as a bartender and server at Flats Fix, a taqueria in Union Square, Manhattan. She worked there while organizing her community and before her successful run for Congress, becoming one of the most famous former service industry workers in politics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where did AOC work as a bartender?
AOC worked as a bartender and server at Flats Fix, a taqueria in Union Square, Manhattan. She worked there while organizing and before running for Congress in 2018.
What is AOC's favorite food?
AOC has spoken fondly of Puerto Rican cuisine from her heritage, including mofongo, arroz con gandules, and pernil. She also loves Bronx taco trucks and champions local food businesses in her district.
Does AOC cook?
Yes! AOC has shared cooking videos on Instagram where she makes dinner while discussing various topics. She often connects her cooking to her Puerto Rican heritage and family traditions.
What Puerto Rican dishes does AOC like?
AOC has celebrated traditional Puerto Rican dishes including mofongo (mashed plantains), arroz con gandules (rice with pigeon peas), and pernil (roasted pork shoulder), connecting these to her family heritage.
Where does AOC eat in the Bronx?
AOC champions local Bronx businesses including taco trucks, small restaurants, and bodegas in her district. She advocates for the diverse food culture of the Bronx and Queens communities she represents.
