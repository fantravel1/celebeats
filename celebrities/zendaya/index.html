Is Zendaya vegetarian?

Yes, Zendaya has been vegetarian since childhood. Growing up in Oakland, she never developed a taste for meat and has maintained a plant-forward diet throughout her career. She's not fully vegan - she still enjoys dairy and eggs - but prioritizes vegetables, pasta, and plant-based proteins at restaurants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zendaya vegetarian or vegan?
Zendaya is vegetarian, not vegan. She has been vegetarian since childhood and avoids all meat, but she still consumes dairy products and eggs. She's spoken about simply never liking the taste of meat as a child.
Where does Zendaya eat in LA?
Zendaya prefers low-key restaurants with good vegetarian options. Her LA favorites include Crossroads Kitchen, Gracias Madre, Cafe Gratitude, and Italian spots like Felix where she can order pasta dishes.
What is Zendaya's favorite food?
Zendaya loves pasta and Italian food - it's her reliable go-to since there are always vegetarian options. She's also a fan of Mexican food (veggie tacos, burritos) and appreciates home-cooked meals.
Is Zendaya from Oakland?
Yes, Zendaya was born and raised in Oakland, California. Her Bay Area roots influenced her diverse food appreciation and her vegetarian diet started there as a young child.
Does Zendaya follow a strict diet?
Zendaya maintains a vegetarian diet but isn't overly restrictive. She enjoys comfort foods like pasta and tacos, values home cooking, and doesn't seem to follow trendy diets beyond her lifelong vegetarianism.
