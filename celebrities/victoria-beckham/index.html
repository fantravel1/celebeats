What does Victoria Beckham eat?

Victoria Beckham is famous for her extremely disciplined diet. According to husband David Beckham, she has eaten the same meal - grilled fish and steamed vegetables - for 25 years. She avoids oils, butter, sauces, desserts, and rarely eats carbohydrates. At restaurants, she orders simply prepared fish and plain vegetables.

The Victoria Beckham Diet

One of the most talked-about diets in celebrity culture - extreme consistency over 25+ years.

Daily Staple

Grilled Fish

Victoria's primary protein source. Simply grilled with no oil, butter, or sauce. Often sea bass, salmon, or Dover sole prepared as plainly as possible.

Daily Staple

Steamed Vegetables

The constant companion to her fish. Plain steamed vegetables with no dressing, oils, or seasonings beyond salt. Often spinach, broccoli, or green beans.

Victoria starts each day with apple cider vinegar shots, which she credits as part of her wellness routine. She's mentioned this practice in multiple interviews.
