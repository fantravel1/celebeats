Where does Bill Gates eat?

Bill Gates is famous for his simple food preferences. In Seattle, he's a longtime patron of Dick's Drive-In for burgers and has dined at Canlis for special occasions. He's also been spotted at The Walrus and the Carpenter and various local spots. Despite being one of the world's wealthiest people, he maintains remarkably unpretentious tastes.
