What are Elton John's legendary dinner parties like?

Sir Elton John is arguably the greatest dinner party host in entertainment history. His annual Oscar viewing party is Hollywood's most coveted invitation, raising millions for his AIDS Foundation. At his homes in Windsor, London, and LA, he employs top private chefs, curates elaborate multi-course menus, and his guest lists feature the biggest names in music, film, and fashion.

Elton's Legendary Dinner Parties

The Ultimate Host

Elton John's reputation as a host is as legendary as his music career. His dining events include:

  • Annual Oscar Party - The Elton John AIDS Foundation gala, raising $100M+ over the years
  • Windsor Estate Dinners - Intimate gatherings at his English country home with royalty and rock stars
  • White Tie & Tiara Ball - His famous summer fundraiser with elaborate themed menus
  • Impromptu Suppers - Known for calling friends at 4 PM to host dinner at 8 PM

Frequently Asked Questions

What are Elton John's dinner parties like?
Elton John is considered one of the greatest hosts in entertainment. His annual Oscar viewing party raises millions for his AIDS Foundation, and his home dinners at his Windsor estate and LA home feature top chefs, elaborate menus, and A-list guests from music, film, and fashion.
Where does Elton John eat in London?
Elton is a decades-long regular at The Ivy in the West End and Scott's in Mayfair. He also frequents The Wolseley on Piccadilly and various high-end establishments across London.
Where does Elton John eat in Los Angeles?
In LA, Elton frequents Spago by Wolfgang Puck in Beverly Hills, Craig's in West Hollywood, and Mr Chow. His West Hollywood home is also where he hosts his famous Oscar viewing party.
What is Elton John's favorite food?
Despite his glamorous lifestyle, Elton loves traditional British comfort food including shepherd's pie, roast dinners, and fish and chips. He also has a deep appreciation for Italian cuisine and fine dining.
Has Elton John spoken about his relationship with food?
Yes, Elton has been remarkably open in his autobiography and interviews about past struggles with eating disorders and food issues. He now maintains a balanced, health-focused approach to eating with his family.
