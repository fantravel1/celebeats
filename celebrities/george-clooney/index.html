Frequently Asked Questions

Does George Clooney own Casamigos tequila?
George Clooney co-founded Casamigos tequila with Rande Gerber and Mike Meldman in 2013. They sold the company to Diageo in 2017 for up to $1 billion, but Clooney remains a brand ambassador and continues to enjoy the tequila he helped create.
Where does George Clooney live?
George Clooney divides his time between Villa Oleandra on Lake Como, Italy, and Los Angeles. His Lake Como estate in Laglio has become legendary for the celebrity dinner parties he hosts there.
What are George Clooney's favorite restaurants?
Around Lake Como, George frequents local trattorias like Trattoria del Vapore in Laglio and Il Gatto Nero in Cernobbio. In LA, he's been spotted at Craig's, Giorgio Baldi, and Nobu Malibu. He's also known to prefer hosting dinners at home.
Is George Clooney from Kentucky?
Yes, George Clooney was born in Lexington, Kentucky. Despite his jet-setting lifestyle, he maintains strong ties to his Southern roots, including a love for bourbon and Kentucky comfort food.
