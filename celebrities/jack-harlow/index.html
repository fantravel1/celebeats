What does Jack Harlow eat?

Jack Harlow is proud of his Louisville, Kentucky roots and loves hometown comfort food. He partnered with KFC (also from Louisville) for the Jack Harlow Meal. He enjoys classic Southern fare: fried chicken, the famous Hot Brown sandwich, bourbon-infused dishes, and BBQ. He regularly returns home and supports local Louisville restaurants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Jack Harlow have a deal with KFC?
Yes, Jack Harlow partnered with KFC in 2022 for the Jack Harlow Meal. It was a natural fit since both Jack Harlow and KFC are from Louisville, Kentucky. The collaboration featured his favorite menu items.
What is Jack Harlow's favorite food?
Jack Harlow loves Kentucky comfort food - fried chicken (hence the KFC partnership), the Hot Brown sandwich, BBQ, and bourbon-infused dishes. He's proud of Louisville's food culture and often returns home to enjoy local spots.
Where does Jack Harlow eat in Louisville?
Jack Harlow frequents Louisville restaurants including Ramsi's Cafe on the World, local BBQ joints, and classic Louisville eateries. He's known for returning home regularly and supporting local businesses.
