Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Nicholas Hoult from?
Nicholas Hoult was born on December 7, 1989, in Wokingham, England. He began acting as a child and rose to fame in About a Boy (2002) before transitioning to Hollywood blockbusters.
Where does Nicholas Hoult eat in London?
When in London, Nicholas likely frequents industry hotspots like Chiltern Firehouse, The Wolseley for classic European fare, and Soho House members' clubs popular with the British film community.
What movies is Nicholas Hoult known for?
Nicholas is known for About a Boy, the X-Men prequel series (as Beast), Mad Max: Fury Road, The Great (opposite Elle Fanning), and most recently Nosferatu (2024). He successfully transitioned from child star to A-list actor.
Where does Nicholas Hoult eat in LA?
In Los Angeles, Nicholas frequents industry power spots like Chateau Marmont, Giorgio Baldi for Italian, and Craig's in West Hollywood - all popular with the Hollywood A-list crowd.
Did Nicholas Hoult date Jennifer Lawrence?
Yes, Nicholas Hoult and Jennifer Lawrence dated from 2010 to 2014 after meeting on the set of X-Men: First Class. They remained professional while working together on subsequent X-Men films.
