Victoria's Secret Angel Diet

The legendary preparation behind the wings.

Pre-Show Prep

The Famous Liquid Diet

Adriana famously revealed her pre-VS show routine: protein shakes and liquids only for 9 days before the show, no solid food for 12 hours before, and no water 12 hours before. This extreme prep is not for everyday life.

Regular Life

High Protein Focus

In daily life, Adriana follows a high-protein, low-carb approach. She enjoys lean meats, fish, eggs, and lots of vegetables. This fuels her intense boxing workouts without extreme restriction.

Brazilian Food Heritage

How Adriana's Bahian roots influence her palate.

Home Flavors

Bahian Cuisine

Adriana grew up in Salvador, Bahia - known for its rich Afro-Brazilian cuisine. She's shared her love for moqueca (coconut fish stew), acaraje (black-eyed pea fritters), and the bold flavors of her home region. She often seeks out authentic Brazilian restaurants when traveling.

Tier A High Confidence
Indulgence

Churrasco Love

When indulging, Adriana enjoys Brazilian churrasco - grilled meats served rodizio-style. This protein-rich tradition aligns with her high-protein approach while connecting her to Brazilian culture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Adriana frequents Zuma Miami for Japanese, Juvia for rooftop dining, The Surf Club Restaurant by Thomas Keller, and Cecconi's at Soho Beach House. She also enjoys Brazilian restaurants and acai spots throughout Miami.

Adriana follows a high-protein, low-carb diet in daily life, with lean meats, fish, vegetables, and Brazilian superfoods like acai. Before VS shows, she followed an extreme liquid diet - but that's not her everyday approach.

Adriana loves Bahian cuisine including moqueca (fish stew), acai bowls, tropical fruits, pao de queijo (cheese bread), and churrasco (Brazilian BBQ). Her Salvador roots heavily influence her food preferences.

Adriana is famous for her boxing workouts - often training two hours a day. She combines this with a high-protein diet, staying hydrated, and eating to fuel performance rather than restricting. Her fitness approach is about strength, not just appearance.
