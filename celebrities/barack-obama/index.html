Where does Barack Obama eat in New York City?

Barack Obama is a devoted regular at Emilio's Ballato in SoHo, the classic Italian restaurant where he's been photographed numerous times enjoying pasta and wine. He's also spotted at upscale NYC establishments and enjoys exploring diverse cuisines with Michelle on their famous date nights.

The Emilio's Ballato Regular

Obama has become one of the most famous regulars at Emilio's Ballato, the no-frills Italian spot in SoHo that's been around since 1956. His visits have included:

  • Post-presidency dinners - Multiple documented visits since 2017
  • Celebrity companions - Spotted with various friends and family
  • Date nights with Michelle - The couple's NYC dining tradition

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Barack Obama eat in New York City?
Obama is a famous regular at Emilio's Ballato in SoHo, a classic Italian restaurant where he's been photographed multiple times. He also enjoys date nights with Michelle at various upscale NYC restaurants.
What is Barack Obama's favorite food?
Obama has diverse tastes reflecting his background - Hawaiian shave ice from his childhood, Chicago deep dish pizza from his years there, and his homemade chili recipe. He's also known as an adventurous eater who explores global cuisines.
Where does Obama get shave ice in Hawaii?
Obama famously visits Island Snow in Kailua, Oahu every time he returns to Hawaii. His visits have made the shop legendary, often creating long lines when word spreads of his arrival.
Do Barack and Michelle Obama go on date nights?
Yes! The Obamas are known for their romantic date nights at restaurants in NYC, DC, and beyond. They've been spotted at various high-end establishments and casual favorites, always photographed looking happy together.
What did Barack Obama eat at Ben's Chili Bowl?
Obama visited Ben's Chili Bowl on DC's U Street multiple times and famously ordered the half-smoke, the restaurant's signature dish - a smoked sausage topped with chili, onions, and mustard.
