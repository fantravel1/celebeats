What does Joe Rogan eat?

Joe Rogan follows a carnivore diet focused primarily on meat, especially wild game like elk that he hunts himself. He regularly discusses his diet on his podcast, emphasizing grass-fed beef, elk, and avoiding processed foods and carbohydrates.

Tier A EvidenceHigh Confidence

The Carnivore Diet

Meat-Based Eating

Joe's carnivore diet staples include:

  • Elk Meat - From his own bow hunting trips
  • Grass-Fed Beef - Steaks and ground beef
  • Bison - Lean wild game alternative
  • Eggs - Pasture-raised eggs regularly
  • Bacon - High-quality, nitrate-free

What He Avoids

Joe famously eliminates or minimizes:

  • Bread & Pasta - No processed carbs
  • Sugar - Strict avoidance
  • Processed Foods - Nothing from packages
  • Seed Oils - Cooks with butter or tallow

Elk Hunting & Wild Game

Bow Hunting Passion

Joe's hunting trips supply much of his meat:

  • Annual Elk Hunts - Colorado, Montana expeditions
  • Freezer Full of Elk - Year-round supply from hunts
  • Compound Bow - Prefers bow over rifle hunting
  • Field Dressing - Processes his own game
  • Ethical Hunting - Advocates for wild game sustainability

Wild Game Benefits

Joe frequently discusses on his podcast why wild game is superior: no hormones, no antibiotics, lean protein, and the ethical satisfaction of hunting your own food. His freezer contains hundreds of pounds of elk meat.

Austin, Texas Life

Since relocating from Los Angeles to Austin in 2020, Joe has embraced Texas food culture:

  • Texas BBQ - Austin's legendary brisket scene
  • Steakhouses - High-end cuts at local spots
  • Comedy Club Dining - His own club, Comedy Mothership
  • Lake Austin Area - Private dining near his compound
  • Tex-Mex - Occasional Mexican food indulgence

Austin's meat-centric culture aligns perfectly with his carnivore lifestyle.

UFC & Performance Nutrition

Joe's diet supports his intense fitness regimen:

  • High Protein - Supports jiu-jitsu and kickboxing training
  • Ketosis - Fat-adapted for sustained energy
  • Intermittent Fasting - Often skips breakfast
  • Pre-UFC Events - Clean eating before commentary
  • Recovery Focus - Prioritizes nutrient-dense foods

His fitness routine includes martial arts, weightlifting, and sauna sessions.

Supplements & Wellness

Beyond food, Joe's wellness routine includes:

  • Athletic Greens - Daily greens supplement
  • Vitamin D - High-dose supplementation
  • Fish Oil - Omega-3 fatty acids
  • Sauna - Daily heat exposure
  • Cold Plunge - Ice bath recovery

He regularly discusses these protocols with health experts on his podcast.

Joe Rogan FAQs

Joe follows a carnivore diet heavy on elk meat from his hunting trips, grass-fed beef, and eggs. He avoids processed foods, sugar, and most carbohydrates.

Joe has done strict carnivore periods eating only meat, but typically follows an animal-based diet that includes eggs and occasionally some vegetables like jalapenos.

Joe Rogan moved from Los Angeles to Austin, Texas in 2020. He lives in a lakeside compound and owns the Comedy Mothership comedy club in downtown Austin.
