What does Priyanka Chopra eat?

Priyanka Chopra is a passionate lover of Indian cuisine, especially Punjabi dishes like butter chicken, biryani, and dal makhani. She opened her restaurant Sona in NYC to share Indian food with the world. She loves her mother's home cooking, Mumbai street food, and maintains her connection to Indian flavors no matter where she is globally.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Priyanka Chopra's favorite Indian food?
Priyanka Chopra loves traditional Indian cuisine, especially Punjabi food. Her favorites include butter chicken, biryani, dal makhani, and her mother's home cooking. She's spoken about her love for spicy food and authentic Indian flavors in numerous interviews.
What is Priyanka Chopra's restaurant Sona?
Sona is Priyanka Chopra's Indian restaurant in New York City, opened in 2021. Located in the Flatiron District, it serves contemporary Indian cuisine celebrating the regional flavors of India. The restaurant features dishes inspired by her heritage.
Where does Priyanka Chopra eat in NYC and Mumbai?
In NYC, Priyanka frequents her own restaurant Sona, along with upscale Manhattan restaurants and celebrity hotspots. In Mumbai, she enjoys street food, traditional restaurants, and the city's diverse food scene from Bandra to South Mumbai.
