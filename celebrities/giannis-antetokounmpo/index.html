Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Giannis obsessed with Oreos?
Giannis grew up in poverty in Athens, Greece, and didn't have access to American snacks as a child. When he came to the NBA, he discovered Oreos and became famously obsessed. His love for them represents his journey and appreciation for things he couldn't have growing up.
What is the Giannis 50 nuggets story?
After winning the 2021 NBA Championship, Giannis ordered 50 Chick-fil-A nuggets and ate them during his post-game press conference. The viral moment became one of the most iconic championship celebrations ever, with Giannis calling it his "championship meal."
Does Giannis really drink that many smoothies?
Yes! Giannis is known as the "Smoothie King" of the NBA. He reportedly drinks multiple smoothies daily as part of his nutrition regimen and has an official sponsorship with Smoothie King. His teammates frequently joke about his smoothie consumption.
Where does Giannis eat in Milwaukee?
Giannis frequents Chick-fil-A (famously) and Smoothie King locations around Milwaukee. He's also been spotted at upscale Milwaukee restaurants like Carnevor steakhouse for team dinners and special occasions.
What is Giannis Antetokounmpo's diet?
Despite his fun food moments, Giannis follows a strict nutrition plan to maintain his elite physique. He drinks multiple smoothies daily, focuses on high-protein meals, and works with team nutritionists. He balances this with his famous indulgences like Oreos and Chick-fil-A.
