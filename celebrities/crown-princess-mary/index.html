Where did Crown Princess Mary meet Prince Frederik?

Crown Princess Mary met Crown Prince Frederik at the Slip Inn pub in Sydney during the 2000 Olympic Games. Mary was working in marketing and didn't initially know he was a prince. The pub has become famous for this royal love story - an Australian commoner who became Danish royalty.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where did Crown Princess Mary meet Prince Frederik?
Crown Princess Mary met Crown Prince Frederik at the Slip Inn pub in Sydney during the 2000 Olympic Games. Mary was working in marketing and didn't initially know he was a prince. The pub has become famous for this royal love story.
Does Crown Princess Mary support New Nordic cuisine?
Yes, Crown Princess Mary has been a supporter of Denmark's New Nordic cuisine movement. She has attended events at Noma and supports Danish culinary innovation. The royal family promotes Danish food culture internationally.
What is Crown Princess Mary's food background?
Mary brings Australian roots to Danish royal life. Born in Tasmania and raised in Australia, she's embraced Danish food traditions including smorrebrod while supporting modern New Nordic cuisine.
Is Crown Princess Mary now Queen of Denmark?
Yes, as of January 2024 when Queen Margrethe II abdicated, Mary became Queen of Denmark alongside King Frederik X. She now hosts state dinners as Queen.
Does Mary return to Australia?
Yes, Crown Princess Mary returns to Australia regularly to visit family in Tasmania and maintains strong connections to her homeland, including enjoying Australian food and dining culture on her visits.
