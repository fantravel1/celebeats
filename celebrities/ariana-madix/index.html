Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Ariana Madix eat in LA?
Ariana frequents West Hollywood hotspots including Craig's, Catch LA, and The Nice Guy. She's also a regular at brunch spots like Granville and Gracias Madre, often dining with her close friend group.
Does Ariana Madix have a cocktail book?
Yes! Ariana co-authored "Fancy AF Cocktails" in 2019, featuring creative cocktail recipes from her bartending experience at SUR. The book includes party planning tips and unique drink combinations.
What happened with Ariana Madix and Scandoval?
In March 2023, it was revealed that Tom Sandoval had been having an affair with their castmate Raquel Leviss. Ariana's graceful handling of the scandal made her a fan favorite and led to numerous career opportunities.
Is Ariana Madix still on Vanderpump Rules?
Ariana remained on Vanderpump Rules after Scandoval and has expanded her career significantly. She now hosts Love Island USA, appeared on Broadway in Chicago, and competed on Dancing with the Stars.
Does Ariana Madix still work at SUR?
While Ariana is no longer a working bartender at SUR, she remains connected to the Vanderpump Rules universe. Her career has evolved into hosting, acting, and various entertainment ventures.
