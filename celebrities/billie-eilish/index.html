Is Billie Eilish vegan?

Yes, Billie Eilish has been vegan since 2014, when she was just 12 years old. She went vegan for ethical and environmental reasons, specifically citing animal welfare concerns. She frequents LA's best plant-based spots like Crossroads Kitchen, Gracias Madre, and Cafe Gratitude. She also loves cooking vegan food at home with her family.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Billie Eilish vegan?
Yes, Billie Eilish has been vegan since 2014, when she was 12 years old. She adopted a plant-based diet for ethical reasons, primarily concerning animal welfare. She has been outspoken about her veganism and encourages fans to consider plant-based eating.
Where does Billie Eilish eat in LA?
As an LA native and strict vegan, Billie frequents the city's best plant-based restaurants. Her favorites include Crossroads Kitchen in West Hollywood, Gracias Madre for vegan Mexican, Cafe Gratitude, and Monty's Good Burger. She grew up in Highland Park and knows the local vegan scene well.
What are Billie Eilish's favorite vegan foods?
Billie loves vegan comfort foods including plant-based burgers, vegan mac and cheese, smoothie bowls, and avocado toast. She also enjoys cooking vegan meals at home with her family and has shared some of her favorite recipes with fans.
Why did Billie Eilish go vegan?
Billie went vegan at age 12 primarily for ethical reasons related to animal welfare. She has said she "loves animals so much" and couldn't continue eating them. She's also motivated by environmental concerns and has used her platform to advocate for plant-based eating.
Does Billie Eilish cook?
Yes, Billie enjoys cooking vegan food at home with her family. She grew up in a creative household where cooking was valued, and she's shared some of her favorite vegan recipes and cooking experiences with fans on social media.
