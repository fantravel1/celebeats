Eddie Murphy

What does Eddie Murphy eat?

Eddie Murphy enjoys a blend of Brooklyn soul food favorites and Beverly Hills fine dining. He appreciates home-cooked meals with his large family and frequents upscale LA restaurants. His diet reflects both his New York roots and Hollywood success.

Brooklyn Soul Food Roots

New York Upbringing

Growing up in Brooklyn and later Long Island, Eddie developed a love for classic New York and soul food:

  • Soul Food - Fried chicken, collard greens, mac and cheese
  • New York Pizza - Brooklyn-style slices
  • Caribbean Influence - Jamaican and Caribbean flavors
  • Street Food - Hot dogs, pretzels, classic NYC fare
  • Sunday Dinners - Family tradition gatherings

Family Food Traditions

With 10 children, family meals are central to Eddie's life. He values traditional cooking passed down through generations and makes time for family dinners despite his busy schedule.

Beverly Hills Fine Dining

Hollywood Restaurant Circuit

Eddie has been a fixture at Beverly Hills' finest restaurants for decades:

  • Spago - Wolfgang Puck's legendary spot
  • Mr Chow - Chinese cuisine, celebrity favorite
  • Mastro's - Steakhouse for special occasions
  • Giorgio Baldi - Italian fine dining in Santa Monica
  • The Ivy - Classic Hollywood lunch spot

Private Chef & Home Cooking

Living in his Beverly Hills mansion, Eddie employs private chefs for daily meals while also valuing home-cooked comfort food. His kitchen serves the needs of his large extended family.

Coming to America Food Legacy

The iconic "Coming to America" films showcase Eddie's connection to food culture:

  • McDowell's - The fictional restaurant became a cultural phenomenon
  • Queens, NY Setting - Authentic neighborhood restaurants portrayed
  • Soul Food Scenes - Family dinner moments throughout the films
  • African Cuisine - Fictional Zamunda's royal dining

The films celebrated both African American food culture and immigrant restaurant entrepreneurship.

Health & Wellness

In recent years, Eddie has focused more on health-conscious eating:

  • Regular Exercise - Maintains fitness routine
  • Balanced Diet - Mix of indulgence and healthy choices
  • No Excessive Drinking - Known for moderate lifestyle
  • Quality Ingredients - Emphasis on fresh, high-quality food

His longevity in Hollywood reflects a balanced approach to health.
