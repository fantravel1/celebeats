Bronx Roots & Heritage

Cultural Heritage

Ice Spice (Isis Naija Gaston) is of Dominican and Nigerian descent, growing up in the Bronx with a rich multicultural food background:

  • Dominican Cuisine - Mangu, sancocho, and other traditional dishes
  • Bronx Bodegas - Classic NYC deli sandwiches and chopped cheese
  • Nigerian Influence - Jollof rice and West African flavors
  • Caribbean Food - The Bronx's diverse Caribbean restaurant scene

She's spoken about staying connected to her Bronx community and the food that shaped her upbringing.
