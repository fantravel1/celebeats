Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, Ralph Lauren owns The Polo Bar in New York City, located in the basement of the Ralph Lauren flagship store on 55th Street. He also operates Ralph's Coffee shops globally and Ralph's in Paris. Previously, he owned RL Restaurant in Chicago.

The Polo Bar is Ralph Lauren's upscale American restaurant in Midtown Manhattan. Opened in 2015, it features classic American cuisine in a sophisticated club-like atmosphere filled with Ralph Lauren's personal collection of equestrian art and memorabilia. It's one of NYC's most sought-after reservations.

Ralph Lauren is known for his love of classic American cuisine. The Polo Bar's menu reflects his preferences, featuring dishes like the famous Polo Bar Burger, prime steaks, and traditional American comfort food elevated to fine dining standards. The burger is reportedly his personal favorite.

The Polo Bar is notoriously difficult to book. Reservations open 30 days in advance and fill up quickly. You can try calling directly or booking through their website. The restaurant doesn't have a traditional walk-in policy, though you may have luck at the bar area. Many rely on concierges or connections in the fashion industry.
