Frequently Asked Questions

What is Justin Trudeau's favorite food?
Trudeau has championed Canadian foods including poutine and Montreal bagels. He's been photographed enjoying poutine on campaign stops and defends Montreal bagels in the classic debate with New York.
Does Trudeau prefer Montreal or New York bagels?
As a proud Montrealer, Trudeau favors Montreal-style bagels, which are sweeter, denser, and baked in wood-fired ovens. He's engaged in the playful rivalry between the two cities' bagel traditions.
What does Justin Trudeau serve at state dinners?
Trudeau's state dinners showcase Canadian cuisine with dishes featuring indigenous ingredients, Atlantic seafood, Alberta beef, BC salmon, Quebec cheeses, and Canadian wines. Maple syrup often appears in desserts.
Where does Justin Trudeau eat in Montreal?
Montreal institutions like St-Viateur Bagel, Fairmount Bagel, and Schwartz's Deli are part of the city's food culture that Trudeau celebrates. La Banquise is famous for poutine.
Does Justin Trudeau cook?
While specific cooking habits aren't well documented, Trudeau has spoken about family meals and Canadian food traditions. His father Pierre was also known for hosting informal dinners at 24 Sussex Drive.
