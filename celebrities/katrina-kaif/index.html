What is Katrina Kaif's diet?

Life with Vicky: Punjabi Food Adventures

Since marrying Vicky Kaushal in 2021, Katrina has embraced Punjabi food culture. While she maintains her clean eating most days, family gatherings with the Kaushals bring hearty Punjabi cuisine.

The Kaushal Family Kitchen

  • Sarson da Saag & Makki di Roti - Winter Punjabi specialty
  • Dal Makhani - Rich, creamy lentils the Punjabi way
  • Chole Bhature - Occasional indulgence with Vicky's favorites
  • Home-Cooked Meals - Mother-in-law's traditional recipes

Katrina has said she's learned to balance her clean eating with enjoying family food traditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Katrina Kaif's diet plan?
Katrina follows a clean eating approach focusing on whole foods, lean proteins (chicken, fish, eggs), lots of vegetables, and minimal processed foods. She avoids sugar, stays hydrated, and practices portion control. Combined with pilates, this keeps her in shape.
How does Katrina Kaif stay so fit?
Katrina maintains her physique through regular pilates sessions with trainer Yasmin Karachiwala, clean eating, and consistency. She's been following this routine for years, making it a lifestyle rather than a temporary diet.
Is Katrina Kaif vegetarian?
No, Katrina is not vegetarian. She includes lean proteins like chicken and fish in her diet. However, she focuses on clean, simple preparations rather than heavy or fried foods.
Who is Katrina Kaif's trainer?
Katrina works with celebrity pilates trainer Yasmin Karachiwala. Their workout videos and pilates sessions are famous on social media, inspiring many to take up pilates for fitness.
Does Katrina Kaif have cheat days?
Yes, especially since marrying Vicky Kaushal. She enjoys Punjabi food during family gatherings and has learned to balance her clean eating with enjoying her husband's family food traditions. Moderation is her key philosophy.
