Frequently Asked Questions

How does Aishwarya Rai prepare for Cannes?
Aishwarya doesn't follow crash diets before Cannes. She maintains consistent healthy habits year-round - clean eating, hydration, adequate sleep, and light exercise. Her approach is about wellness as a lifestyle, not last-minute changes.
Is Aishwarya Rai vegetarian?
Aishwarya is not strictly vegetarian. Her Mangalorean background includes fish and seafood. However, as part of the Bachchan household where Amitabh is vegetarian, she eats a balanced mix of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food.
What is Aishwarya Rai's diet?
Aishwarya follows a balanced diet with South Indian Mangalorean influences. She prefers home-cooked meals, includes fresh fruits and vegetables, stays hydrated, and doesn't follow extreme diets. Moderation is her philosophy.
What is Aishwarya Rai's beauty secret?
Aishwarya attributes her timeless beauty to hydration, fresh foods, adequate sleep, no extreme diets, and consistency. She believes beauty comes from within and there are no shortcuts - just healthy, balanced living.
Where does Aishwarya Rai live?
Aishwarya lives at Jalsa, the Bachchan family's iconic bungalow in Juhu, Mumbai, with husband Abhishek, daughter Aaradhya, and in-laws Amitabh and Jaya Bachchan.
