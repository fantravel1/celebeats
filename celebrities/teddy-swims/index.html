Where does Teddy Swims eat in Atlanta?

Teddy Swims (born Jaten Hodge) is a proud Atlanta native who loves the city's legendary soul food and BBQ scene. He frequents Fox Bros BBQ for smoked meats, Busy Bee Cafe for classic soul food, and various neighborhood joints across the city. He's also known for his love of cooking at home.
