Does Tom Sandoval own a restaurant in LA?

Yes! Tom Sandoval co-owns TomTom, a popular bar and restaurant in West Hollywood with Tom Schwartz and Lisa Vanderpump. Located on Santa Monica Boulevard, TomTom opened in 2018 and features craft cocktails, small plates, and a trendy atmosphere that's become a celebrity hotspot.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Tom Sandoval own a restaurant?
Yes, Tom Sandoval co-owns TomTom bar in West Hollywood with Tom Schwartz and Lisa Vanderpump. The bar opened in 2018 and has become a popular celebrity destination on Santa Monica Boulevard.
What restaurant is Vanderpump Rules filmed at?
Vanderpump Rules is primarily filmed at SUR (Sexy Unique Restaurant) in West Hollywood. Tom Sandoval worked there as a bartender for years. TomTom and PUMP also appear frequently on the show.
Where is TomTom located?
TomTom is located at 8932 Santa Monica Boulevard in West Hollywood, California. It's in the heart of WeHo's nightlife district, near other Vanderpump restaurants.
Is Tom Sandoval still involved with TomTom after Scandoval?
Yes, despite the 2023 Scandoval controversy, Tom Sandoval remains a co-owner of TomTom. The bar continues to operate and has seen increased attention following the scandal.
Where does Tom Sandoval hang out in LA?
Tom Sandoval frequents West Hollywood establishments including TomTom, SUR, PUMP, and various spots along Santa Monica Boulevard. He's also seen at celebrity hotspots like Craig's and Catch LA.
