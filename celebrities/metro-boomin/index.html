Where does Metro Boomin eat?

Metro Boomin splits his time between his St. Louis roots and Atlanta base. In Atlanta, he frequents upscale spots like Bone's Restaurant, STK Atlanta, and Nobu. He's often dining with collaborators like Future and 21 Savage at their favorite spots. In St. Louis, he maintains love for local BBQ institutions.

Tier B Evidence Moderate Confidence

St. Louis Food Roots

Born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri, Metro Boomin's food preferences were shaped by the Gateway City's unique culinary culture:

  • St. Louis BBQ - Sweet, tomato-based sauces and pork steaks
  • St. Louis-Style Pizza - Thin crust with Provel cheese
  • Toasted Ravioli - The city's signature appetizer
  • Ted Drewes Frozen Custard - St. Louis dessert institution

Though now based in Atlanta, Metro has spoken about his love for St. Louis and returns home regularly, enjoying the city's food scene.

Metro Boomin Food FAQs

Metro Boomin frequents upscale Atlanta restaurants including Bone's Restaurant, STK Atlanta, and various spots in Buckhead. As a successful producer, he dines at many of the same spots as the artists he works with like Future and 21 Savage.

Metro Boomin has roots in St. Louis BBQ culture and has adopted Atlanta's food scene. He enjoys steakhouses, Southern comfort food, and high-end dining experiences that come with his successful career.

Metro Boomin was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri but moved to Atlanta to pursue his music career. He's based in Atlanta now but maintains connections to his St. Louis roots.

As one of hip-hop's most connected producers, Metro is often seen dining with his frequent collaborators including Future, 21 Savage (they have multiple albums together), Travis Scott, and other Atlanta artists and industry figures.
