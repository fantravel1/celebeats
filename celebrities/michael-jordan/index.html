Where does Michael Jordan like to eat?

Michael Jordan is synonymous with premium steakhouses and upscale dining. The basketball legend owned Michael Jordan's Steakhouse in Chicago and has been a fixture at establishments like Gibsons Bar & Steakhouse and RPM Steak. Known for his competitive nature even at the dinner table, MJ enjoys premium cuts, fine wines, and the cigar lounge culture that complements his refined tastes.

MJ's Food Preferences

Playing Days Diet

During his Bulls dynasty years, Jordan was known for eating steak before games - a classic athlete's meal for protein and sustained energy. His competitive nature extended to eating, often challenging teammates.

Signature Orders

At steakhouses, MJ is known to order premium cuts - particularly ribeyes and porterhouses. He pairs his steaks with fine red wines and, of course, follows dinner with a cigar.

Business Dining

As a billionaire team owner and business mogul, Jordan frequently uses upscale restaurants for business meetings, preferring private dining rooms at establishments that can accommodate his security needs and desire for privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Michael Jordan's favorite restaurant?
Michael Jordan is particularly fond of upscale steakhouses. He owned his own restaurant 'Michael Jordan's Steakhouse' in Chicago for years. He's also been spotted at Gibsons Bar & Steakhouse, RPM Steak, and various high-end establishments across the country.
Does Michael Jordan own a restaurant?
Yes, Michael Jordan has had multiple restaurant ventures including Michael Jordan's Steak House which had locations in Chicago and New York. The restaurants were known for premium steaks and upscale ambiance reflecting Jordan's legendary status.
What does Michael Jordan eat?
During his playing days, Jordan was known for his steak-heavy diet, often eating steak before games. He enjoys premium cuts, fine wine, and is famously associated with cigar culture. His tastes run toward classic American fine dining with an emphasis on quality steakhouses.
Where does Michael Jordan go for cigars?
Michael Jordan is an avid cigar enthusiast and has been spotted at exclusive cigar lounges worldwide. He's known to frequent private clubs and high-end establishments that cater to cigar aficionados, often pairing cigars with his own Cincoro Tequila brand.
