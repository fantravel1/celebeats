Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Matt Rife eat in Los Angeles?
Matt Rife frequents spots near the LA comedy club circuit including areas around The Laugh Factory and Comedy Store on Sunset. Late-night spots like Canter's Deli are comedian staples for post-show meals.
Where is Matt Rife from?
Matt Rife was born in Columbus, Ohio on September 10, 1995. He moved to Los Angeles to pursue comedy and became the youngest cast member of Wild 'N Out at age 19.
What does Matt Rife eat on tour?
As one of the most touring comedians, Matt explores local restaurants in every city. He's known for trying regional favorites - from Texas BBQ to Chicago deep dish - and sharing food finds on social media.
Does Matt Rife follow a specific diet?
Matt maintains his fitness while balancing the demands of touring. He's been seen at LA health spots but also enjoys comfort food and late-night dining that comes with the comedy lifestyle.
How old was Matt Rife on Wild 'N Out?
Matt Rife joined Wild 'N Out at age 19, making him the youngest cast member in the show's history. He was discovered after performing at Laugh Factory in LA.
