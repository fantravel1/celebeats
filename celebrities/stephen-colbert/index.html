What does Stephen Colbert eat?

Stephen Colbert's food preferences blend his South Carolina Southern roots with NYC living. He grew up on Lowcountry cooking including shrimp and grits, she-crab soup, and traditional Southern comfort food, which he still cherishes alongside NYC dining.

South Carolina Roots

Lowcountry Cuisine

Stephen's Charleston upbringing included these classics:

  • Shrimp and Grits - The quintessential Lowcountry dish
  • She-Crab Soup - Charleston's signature creamy soup
  • Fried Oysters - Fresh from South Carolina waters
  • Hoppin' John - Black-eyed peas and rice
  • Lowcountry Boil - Shrimp, corn, potatoes, sausage

Family Food Traditions

The youngest of 11 children, Stephen grew up in a large Irish-Catholic family where meals were central to family life. His mother's cooking and the tradition of gathering around the table shaped his deep appreciation for shared meals.

NYC Late Night Life

Ed Sullivan Theater Area

Working in Midtown Manhattan offers dining options:

  • Theater District - Pre-show dining near work
  • Classic NYC Spots - Iconic Manhattan restaurants
  • Late Night Options - Post-taping meal spots
  • Guest Dinners - Entertaining celebrity guests
  • Quick Bites - Efficient meals during busy schedule

Montclair, New Jersey Home

Stephen and his wife Evelyn raise their three children in Montclair, NJ. The suburb offers a slower pace and family dining experiences away from the Manhattan spotlight.

Catholic Faith & Food

Stephen's devout Catholic faith connects to food traditions:

  • Sunday Dinners - Family gathering tradition
  • Lenten Fasting - Observes religious food practices
  • Irish-Catholic Heritage - Traditional feast days
  • Thanksgiving - Major family food celebration
  • Breaking Bread - Meals as spiritual connection

His faith shapes his appreciation for food as communion and gathering.

Late Show Food Segments

  • Celebrity Cooking - Guests cooking with Stephen
  • Food Tastings - Trying bizarre or trending foods

  • Thanksgiving Episodes - Holiday food celebrations
  • Band Dinners - Sharing meals with Jon Batiste/Stay Human

His genuine enthusiasm for food shines through these segments.

Comfort Food Favorites

Stephen's go-to comfort foods include:

  • Ice Cream - Famously loves Ben & Jerry's
  • Pizza - NYC pizza appreciation
  • Southern Comfort - Mac and cheese, fried chicken
  • Thanksgiving Classics - Turkey, stuffing, gravy
  • Late Night Snacks - Post-show wind-down eating

His humor often references food cravings and guilty pleasures.
