What is Jill Biden's heritage?

Jill Biden is Italian-American, born in Hammonton, New Jersey, a town with strong Italian heritage known as the "Blueberry Capital of the World." She was raised with Italian food traditions and has spoken about her family's cooking, including Sunday dinners with red sauce and traditional Italian recipes passed down through generations.
