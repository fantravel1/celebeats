Is Stella McCartney vegetarian?

Yes, Stella McCartney has been a lifelong vegetarian, raised by her mother Linda McCartney who was a famous animal rights activist and vegetarian cookbook author. Stella has never eaten meat and is a passionate advocate for plant-based living. Her fashion brand is entirely cruelty-free, using no leather or fur.

The McCartney Legacy

Family Heritage

Stella McCartney carries forward a powerful family legacy of ethical eating and animal rights:

  • Linda McCartney - Her mother pioneered vegetarian food products and wrote bestselling vegetarian cookbooks
  • Paul McCartney - Her father is a famous vegetarian advocate and supporter of "Meat Free Monday"
  • Linda McCartney Foods - The family brand continues to produce vegetarian products worldwide
  • Fashion Revolution - Stella brought these values to luxury fashion with her cruelty-free brand

For Stella, food choices and fashion choices stem from the same ethical foundation - respect for animals and the planet.
