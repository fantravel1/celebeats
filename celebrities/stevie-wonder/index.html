Is Stevie Wonder vegan?

Yes, Stevie Wonder adopted a vegan diet in 2016 for health reasons. He has spoken publicly about his plant-based lifestyle, crediting it with improving his energy and overall well-being. In LA, he frequents Crossroads Kitchen and other plant-based restaurants, while also relying on private chefs for meals at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stevie Wonder vegan?
Yes, Stevie Wonder adopted a vegan diet in 2016 primarily for health reasons. He has spoken publicly about his plant-based lifestyle and credited it with improving his energy levels and overall well-being.
Where does Stevie Wonder eat in Los Angeles?
Stevie frequents plant-based restaurants in LA, including Crossroads Kitchen in West Hollywood, Cafe Gratitude, and Gracias Madre. He also relies on private chefs for most of his meals at home.
Why did Stevie Wonder go vegan?
Stevie Wonder went vegan in 2016 primarily for health reasons. After health concerns, he adopted a fully plant-based diet and has spoken about the positive impact it has had on his energy and overall health.
What kind of food did Stevie Wonder grow up eating?
Stevie grew up in Saginaw, Michigan, eating classic soul food including greens, cornbread, and other African American culinary traditions. His upbringing in Michigan's soul food culture informs his appreciation for comfort food.
Does Stevie Wonder advocate for veganism?
Yes, Stevie has publicly spoken about his vegan lifestyle in interviews and has encouraged others to consider plant-based eating for health benefits. He is one of the highest-profile musicians to embrace a fully vegan diet.
