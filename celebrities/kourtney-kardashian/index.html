What is Kourtney Kardashian's diet?

Kourtney Kardashian follows a strict organic, gluten-free, and dairy-free diet. Through her lifestyle brand Poosh, she promotes clean eating, avocado smoothies, bone broth, and whole foods. She avoids processed foods, refined sugars, and has been a vocal advocate for reading ingredient labels.
