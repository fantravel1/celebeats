Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Olivier Rousteing eat in Paris?
Olivier Rousteing is based in Paris as creative director of Balmain. He frequents Paris's most exclusive restaurants and fashion industry hotspots, from Hotel Costes to classic French fine dining establishments.
Who does Olivier Rousteing dress?
Olivier Rousteing has dressed some of the world's biggest celebrities including the Kardashian-Jenner family, Beyonce, Rihanna, Jennifer Lopez, and many more. His bold, glamorous Balmain designs are red carpet favorites.
How old was Olivier Rousteing when he became Balmain creative director?
Olivier Rousteing became Balmain's creative director at just 25 years old in 2011, making him one of the youngest creative directors of a major fashion house. He has since transformed the brand completely.
Where is Olivier Rousteing from?
Olivier Rousteing was born in Bordeaux, France in 1985. He was adopted and his search for his birth parents was documented in the Netflix documentary "Wonder Boy." He is now based in Paris.
What is the Balmain Army?
The "Balmain Army" is Olivier Rousteing's loyal following of celebrity fans and social media followers. He pioneered using Instagram and social media to build a fashion brand community, featuring diverse celebrities and models.
