Frequently Asked Questions

What are Florence Pugh's favorite restaurants?
Florence enjoys a mix of fine dining and cozy local spots. In London, she frequents The Wolseley, Barrafina, and traditional pubs near Oxford. In LA, she's been spotted at Jon & Vinny's and Gjelina. However, she's most famous for cooking at home and sharing recipes online.
Does Florence Pugh cook her own food?
Yes! Florence Pugh is famous for her cooking videos on Instagram, branded "Cooking With Flo." She creates elaborate meals, comfort food classics, and experiments with international cuisines. Her videos are known for their fun commentary and genuine passion for cooking.
What is Florence Pugh's favorite food?
Florence loves Italian cuisine, particularly pasta dishes which she often makes on her cooking videos. She's also passionate about British comfort food like Sunday roasts and enjoys cheese and wine. Her cooking shows a love for hearty, flavorful dishes.
Is Florence Pugh vegetarian?
No, Florence Pugh is not vegetarian. Her cooking videos feature a variety of proteins including meat and seafood. However, she does prepare vegetable-forward dishes as well and appreciates a balanced approach to eating.
