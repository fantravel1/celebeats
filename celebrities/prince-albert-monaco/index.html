Frequently Asked Questions

Where does Prince Albert II dine in Monaco?
Prince Albert II dines at Monaco's finest establishments including Louis XV - Alain Ducasse at Hotel de Paris, palace events, and Monaco's grand dining venues. He hosts state dinners and supports Monaco's culinary scene.
Is Prince Albert II the son of Grace Kelly?
Yes, Prince Albert II is the son of Hollywood actress Grace Kelly and Prince Rainier III of Monaco. He inherited the throne in 2005 and continues Monaco's tradition of glamour and fine dining.
What is Louis XV - Alain Ducasse?
Louis XV - Alain Ducasse is a three Michelin star restaurant at Hotel de Paris in Monaco. It's one of the world's most prestigious dining establishments and represents the pinnacle of Monaco's culinary scene.
Was Prince Albert II an Olympic athlete?
Yes, Prince Albert II competed as a bobsledder in five Winter Olympics from 1988 to 2002. He's one of the few royals to compete at the Olympic level.
Is Prince Albert II married?
Yes, Prince Albert II married Charlene Wittstock in 2011. She is now Princess Charlene of Monaco, and they have twins: Prince Jacques and Princess Gabriella.
