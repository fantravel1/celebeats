What does Ed Sheeran eat?

Ed Sheeran is famous for his Heinz ketchup obsession - he puts it on nearly everything and even has a Heinz tattoo. His diet centers on British comfort food: Sunday roasts, fish and chips, Nando's peri-peri chicken, and classic pub fare. He's not into fancy dining, preferring hearty, simple meals that remind him of home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Ed Sheeran obsessed with Heinz ketchup?
Ed Sheeran's Heinz obsession stems from childhood comfort. He's stated he puts ketchup on almost everything and even has the Heinz logo tattooed on his arm. This led to an official partnership with the brand and limited edition bottles.
What is Ed Sheeran's favorite food?
Ed Sheeran loves British comfort food including Sunday roasts, fish and chips, Nando's peri-peri chicken, and curries. He prefers simple, hearty meals over fancy cuisine and always adds plenty of Heinz ketchup.
Does Ed Sheeran have a Nando's Black Card?
Yes, Ed Sheeran has confirmed he has a Nando's Black Card, which gives him free food at any Nando's restaurant. He's been spotted at various UK locations numerous times.
