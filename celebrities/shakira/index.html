What does Shakira eat?

Shakira's diet reflects her Colombian heritage with arepas, sancocho, and Caribbean ceviche, combined with Mediterranean cuisine from her years in Barcelona. Now in Miami, she enjoys the city's Latin food scene, fresh seafood, and maintains a balanced, healthy lifestyle. She loves traditional Colombian dishes that remind her of Barranquilla and family gatherings.

Colombian Food Heritage

Shakira's Barranquilla roots shine through her food preferences:

  • Arepas - Corn cakes, a Colombian breakfast staple
  • Sancocho - Hearty meat and vegetable soup, perfect comfort food
  • Bandeja Paisa - Traditional Colombian platter with beans, rice, meat, and plantains
  • Ceviche Costeno - Caribbean-style fresh seafood ceviche
  • Patacones - Fried green plantains, a coastal Colombian favorite

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shakira's favorite Colombian food?
Shakira loves traditional Colombian dishes from her hometown Barranquilla, including arepas, sancocho (hearty soup), bandeja paisa, and fresh seafood ceviche. She's mentioned that Colombian food reminds her of home and family gatherings.
What does Shakira eat in Miami?
In Miami, Shakira enjoys the city's Latin food scene, fresh seafood, and healthy options. She frequents Miami Beach restaurants, Cuban and Colombian eateries, and maintains a balanced diet with plenty of fresh fruits and vegetables.
Where did Shakira eat in Barcelona?
During her years living in Barcelona, Shakira embraced Spanish cuisine including tapas, paella, and Mediterranean seafood. She frequented restaurants in Eixample, the Gothic Quarter, and beachside eateries in Barceloneta.
