Where does Jamie Foxx like to eat?

Jamie Foxx frequents LA's hottest restaurants and nightlife spots. He's a regular at Craig's, Catch LA, and Nobu. His comedy club roots mean he knows every late-night spot in town, and his legendary house parties are famous for catered Southern comfort food.

LA Hotspots

Celebrity Favorites

Jamie Foxx is a fixture at LA's top celebrity dining destinations:

  • Craig's - West Hollywood's see-and-be-seen spot
  • Catch LA - Rooftop dining with the industry crowd
  • Nobu Malibu - Beachside Japanese luxury
  • Mr Chow - Beverly Hills Chinese fine dining
  • BOA Steakhouse - West Hollywood prime cuts

Comedy Club Scene

Jamie got his start in comedy, and he still loves the late-night comedy club lifestyle:

  • The Comedy Store - Sunset Strip legend where Jamie performed
  • The Laugh Factory - Hollywood comedy institution
  • Canter's Deli - Post-show late-night tradition

Post-comedy show meals are a tradition - the energy of a great set calls for good food and conversation.

The Entertainer's Lifestyle

Legendary House Parties

Jamie Foxx is famous for throwing some of Hollywood's most legendary house parties at his Thousand Oaks estate. These events feature:

  • Catered Southern food - BBQ, fried chicken, mac and cheese
  • Live music - Jamie often performs himself
  • A-list guests - Athletes, actors, musicians
  • Late-night hours - Parties go until sunrise

As both host and entertainer, Jamie ensures the food matches the energy of his legendary gatherings.

Texas Roots

Southern Comfort Food

Born Eric Marlon Bishop in Terrell, Texas, Jamie was raised by his grandmother, Estelle Marie Nelson. Her cooking had a profound impact on his palate:

  • Fried Chicken - Grandma's recipe remains the gold standard
  • Texas BBQ - Brisket, ribs, and all the fixings
  • Soul Food - Collard greens, black-eyed peas, cornbread
  • Mac and Cheese - The ultimate comfort dish
  • Peach Cobbler - Southern dessert perfection

Jamie has spoken emotionally about how his grandmother's cooking shaped his life and continues to influence his food preferences.

Late Night Dining

The entertainer lifestyle means late nights, and Jamie knows all the best spots for post-show and after-party dining in LA:

  • Canter's Deli - 24-hour Fairfax institution
  • Mel's Drive-In - Classic LA diner
  • Pink's Hot Dogs - LA late-night legend
  • In-N-Out Burger - California fast food royalty
