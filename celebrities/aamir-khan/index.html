How did Aamir Khan transform his body for Dangal?

For Dangal, Aamir Khan underwent one of Bollywood's most dramatic transformations. He first gained weight to 97 kg (consuming 5000+ calories daily including pizzas, ice cream, and sweets) to play the older Mahavir Phogat. He then lost 25 kg in 5 months through a strict diet of lean proteins, vegetables, and intense training with his team.

Aamir's Diet Philosophy

The Perfectionist's Approach

Aamir Khan views food as a tool for his craft, not just sustenance. His philosophy:

  • Food is part of character preparation - he commits fully to dietary changes
  • Works with nutritionists and trainers for every transformation
  • Believes in understanding what he eats and why
  • No crash diets - follows scientific, supervised plans
  • Between films, maintains moderate, healthy eating habits

"I don't believe in shortcuts. If a role needs me to look a certain way, I will do whatever it takes properly."

Frequently Asked Questions

How did Aamir Khan gain and lose weight for Dangal?
For Dangal, Aamir first gained weight to 97 kg by consuming 5000+ calories daily, including pizzas, sweets, and multiple heavy meals. He then lost 25 kg in 5 months through a strict diet of lean proteins, vegetables, no carbs after 6pm, and intense training with his fitness team.
Is Aamir Khan vegetarian?
Aamir Khan is not strictly vegetarian but has followed vegetarian diets during certain phases of his life. His diet varies based on his roles and health goals. He's flexible and has eaten both vegetarian and non-vegetarian based on what his body needs.
What is Aamir Khan's normal diet?
When not preparing for a role, Aamir prefers simple, home-cooked meals. He eats light dinners, enjoys chai throughout the day, and focuses on fresh, unprocessed foods. He believes in moderation and portion control.
Where does Aamir Khan eat in Mumbai?
Aamir Khan primarily eats at home and prefers private dining. He rarely frequents restaurants, preferring the privacy and control of home-cooked meals. When he does eat out, he chooses healthy, quiet spots in Bandra.
How does Aamir Khan prepare for body transformations?
Aamir works with professional nutritionists and trainers for every transformation. He follows scientific, supervised plans rather than crash diets. He commits fully to the process, treating it as part of his character preparation and method acting approach.
