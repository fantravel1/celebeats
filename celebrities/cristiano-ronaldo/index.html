What is Cristiano Ronaldo's diet?

Ronaldo follows one of the strictest diets in professional sports, eating six meals per day focused on lean proteins, complex carbohydrates, and fresh vegetables. He completely avoids alcohol, sugary drinks, and processed foods. His diet includes fish (swordfish, tuna, bacalhau), chicken breast, eggs, salads, and fresh fruits.
