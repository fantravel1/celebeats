Our Mission

About CelebEats

The world's premier platform for verified celebrity dining and cooking content.

What We Do

CelebEats documents real, verifiable celebrity food behavior across three core dimensions:

  • Restaurants visited by celebrities
  • Specific dishes they repeatedly order
  • At-home meals and recipes they publicly prepare

Our Philosophy

CelebEats is built as a travel + media directory, not a gossip site. We maintain strict editorial standards:

  • No rumors
  • No stalking or invasive coverage
  • No private locations
  • No unverified claims

Every published fact is evidence-backed, confidence-labeled, and human-reviewed.

Evidence Tiers

We use a rigorous verification system:

Tier A Primary source (interview, official post, TV show)
Tier B Reputable media coverage
Tier C Verified user-generated content

Our Coverage

Phase 1 launch cities include Los Angeles, New York City, Miami, and Toronto. We're expanding to London, Paris, Tokyo, Seoul, and more international destinations.
