Gigi Hadid's Spicy Vodka Pasta

The viral TikTok recipe that broke the internet. Creamy, spicy, and ready in 30 minutes with pantry staples.

30
Minutes
4
Servings
Easy

Tier A Evidence

What is Gigi Hadid's viral pasta?

Gigi Hadid shared this spicy vodka pasta recipe on Instagram in 2020, and it quickly went viral on TikTok. It's a simplified version of classic penne alla vodka, made with tomato paste, cream, vodka, and lots of red pepper flakes. The recipe became so popular it crashed grocery store tomato paste supplies.

Tier A EvidenceHigh Confidence

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (shells or rigatoni work best)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup vodka (can substitute pasta water)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water
  • Red pepper flakes (to taste - Gigi uses a lot!)
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • Fresh basil for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Start the sauce: In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato paste: Add the tomato paste and stir constantly for 2-3 minutes. This caramelizes the paste and builds flavor.
  4. Add vodka: Pour in the vodka and cook until reduced by half, about 2 minutes. The alcohol will cook off.
  5. Add cream and spice: Stir in heavy cream and red pepper flakes. Simmer for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Combine: Add drained pasta and some pasta water. Toss to coat evenly.
  7. Finish: Remove from heat, add parmesan and fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Tips from the Test Kitchen

Don't skip the vodka

The vodka releases flavors in the tomato that water can't. If avoiding alcohol, use pasta water but know it won't taste quite the same.

Use good tomato paste

This is the star of the sauce. Double-concentrated paste or tube paste works best. Mutti is a great brand.

Heat level is personal

Gigi uses a LOT of red pepper flakes. Start with 1/2 teaspoon and add more to taste.
