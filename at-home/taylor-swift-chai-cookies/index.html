Taylor Swift's Chai Sugar Cookies

The holiday baking recipe Taylor shared with Swifties. Warm chai spices, soft sugar cookies, and festive royal icing.

What are Taylor Swift's chai cookies?

Taylor Swift shared her chai sugar cookie recipe with fans, featuring warm spices like cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, and cloves in a classic sugar cookie base. She tops them with eggnog-flavored royal icing for the ultimate holiday treat. They've become a Swiftie tradition every holiday season.

Tier A EvidenceHigh Confidence

Cookie Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp ginger
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and all chai spices in a bowl.
  2. Cream butter and sugar: Beat softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add wet ingredients: Beat in egg and vanilla until combined.
  4. Combine: Gradually add dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Chill: Wrap dough in plastic and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  6. Roll and cut: Roll to 1/4 inch thick on floured surface. Cut into shapes.
  7. Bake: 350Â°F for 10-12 minutes until edges are just golden.
  8. Ice: Once cooled, decorate with royal icing (see below).

Eggnog Royal Icing

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tbsp meringue powder
  • 5-6 tbsp eggnog (or milk + nutmeg)
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Instructions: Beat all ingredients until stiff peaks form. Add more eggnog for thinner consistency. Pipe or spread onto cooled cookies.
