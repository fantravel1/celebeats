Top Celebrity Poke Spots

Sweetfin - Multiple LA Locations

Los Angeles | $15-20

The premium poke chain that celebrities actually eat at. Chef-driven combinations with sustainably sourced fish. The "OG" bowl with ahi tuna, avocado, and crispy onions is the celebrity go-to. Locations in Santa Monica, Beverly Hills, and WeHo are celebrity hotspots.

Tier A

Poke Bar - West Hollywood

Los Angeles | $14-18

Build-your-own poke with premium ingredients. The WeHo location draws celebrities who want to customize their bowls. Known for extra-fresh fish and generous portions. Great for celebrities with specific dietary requirements.

Tier B

Ono Seafood - Honolulu

Oahu, Hawaii | $12-16

The authentic Hawaiian poke experience. When celebrities vacation in Hawaii, this unassuming shop is the pilgrimage destination. Traditional shoyu ahi and spicy ahi are legendary. No mainland pretense, just perfect poke.

Tier A

Pokeworks - Multiple Locations

NYC & National | $14-18

The fast-casual poke chain with celebrity sightings across locations. Quality ingredients and efficient service appeal to busy celebrities. The signature Umami bowl with salmon and ahi is popular among the fitness crowd.

Tier B

North Shore Poke Co. - San Diego

San Diego | $13-17

The surf-culture poke spot beloved by athletes and celebrities visiting San Diego. Hawaiian-influenced preparations with California ingredients. The laid-back vibe makes it perfect for low-key celebrity visits.

Celebrity Poke Bowl FAQs

Celebrity favorite poke spots include Sweetfin in LA (loved by Jennifer Aniston and the Kardashians), Pokeworks in NYC, and Ono Seafood in Honolulu for authentic Hawaiian poke. Many celebrities prefer poke for its healthy, protein-rich profile.

Celebrities typically order protein-heavy poke bowls. Jennifer Aniston is known for ordering spicy ahi tuna with brown rice. Many health-conscious celebrities opt for greens base instead of rice. Popular additions include avocado, edamame, and cucumber.

Poke is one of the healthiest celebrity food choices. A typical bowl has 450-650 calories, 35g protein, and omega-3 fatty acids. Many trainers recommend poke for celebrities needing lean protein for film roles. It's low-carb friendly when ordered with greens base.

Celebrity poke spots typically charge $14-22 per bowl. Sweetfin bowls run $15-20, while authentic Hawaiian spots like Ono Seafood are slightly cheaper at $12-16. Premium additions like extra ahi or truffle can push prices higher.

The healthiest celebrity poke order is salmon or ahi on a greens base (not rice), with avocado, cucumber, edamame, and a light ponzu dressing. Skip crispy toppings and spicy mayo. This configuration is high protein, low carb, and rich in omega-3s.
