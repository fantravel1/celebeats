What is Nobu's Black Cod with Miso?

Nobu's Black Cod with Miso is a signature dish of sablefish marinated in sweet white miso for 2-3 days, then broiled until caramelized. Created by Chef Nobu Matsuhisa, it's been on the menu since 1994 and is ordered by virtually every celebrity who visits.

The Dish

What makes it special?

  • Black cod (sablefish) - incredibly buttery and rich
  • Saikyo miso marinade (sweet white miso from Kyoto)
  • 2-3 days of marination for deep flavor
  • Broiled until edges caramelize
  • Served with pickled ginger

The Origin Story

Chef Nobu Matsuhisa created this dish in the 1980s at his first restaurant in Beverly Hills. When Robert De Niro convinced him to open Nobu in New York in 1994, the black cod became an instant classic. It's now the most ordered dish across all Nobu locations worldwide.
