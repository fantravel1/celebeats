Top Celebrity Wing Spots

Wingstop - Nationwide

National Chain | $15-20/dozen

The wing chain with serious celebrity investment. Rick Ross owns 30+ locations. The lemon pepper and atomic flavors are celebrity favorites. Fast, consistent, and beloved by athletes and entertainers alike.

Anchor Bar - Buffalo, NY

Buffalo | $18-22/dozen

The birthplace of Buffalo wings. When celebrities visit Buffalo, this is the pilgrimage. The original medium sauce recipe from 1964 remains unchanged. A-listers from sports and entertainment make the trip.

Tier A

Bonchon - Multiple Cities

National | $16-20/dozen

Korean fried chicken that celebrities are obsessed with. Double-fried for extra crunch with soy garlic or spicy gochujang glaze. The K-pop influence has made this a celebrity favorite on both coasts.

J.R. Crickets - Atlanta

Atlanta | $14-18/dozen

The Atlanta institution famous for "lemon pepper wet" wings that hip-hop made legendary. When rappers and athletes are in ATL, this is the move. No frills, just perfect wings.

Celebrity Wing FAQs

Rick Ross is a major Wingstop franchise owner with over 30 locations. DJ Khaled, Lil Wayne, and other hip-hop artists have invested in wing chains. Samuel L. Jackson and Michael Jordan have been linked to wing restaurant investments.

Celebrity wing spots include Wingstop (celebrity investors and partners), Anchor Bar in Buffalo (original buffalo wings), Bonchon for Korean fried chicken, and local favorites in Atlanta like J.R. Crickets. Many athletes order massive wing platters for game day.

Celebrity wing preferences vary. Athletes often go for classic Buffalo hot wings. Rick Ross prefers lemon pepper wet (Atlanta style). Korean fried chicken wings with gochujang glaze have gained celebrity popularity. Garlic parmesan is the unexpected celebrity favorite for milder palates.

Lemon pepper wet is an Atlanta wing style where wings are tossed in lemon pepper seasoning AND wing sauce, making them "wet" instead of dry. Rick Ross, 2 Chainz, and Atlanta hip-hop culture made this style famous. It's now available at Wingstop and wing joints nationwide.

Athletes and entertainers are known for ordering massive quantities. Game day orders can exceed 100 wings. Shaq has been documented ordering 50+ wings at a time. For parties and events, celebrity wing orders often run into the hundreds.
