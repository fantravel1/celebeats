What is Dubai chocolate?

Dubai chocolate refers to the viral pistachio kunafa chocolate bars that originated at Fix It bakery (Can Melt brand) in Dubai. These bars feature thick pistachio cream, crispy kunafa (shredded phyllo) strands, all encased in premium chocolate. The combination of Middle Eastern flavors with chocolate created the #1 viral food sensation of 2025, with billions of views on TikTok and Instagram.

The Fix It / Can Melt Origin

The original Dubai chocolate bars were created by Fix It bakery, sold under the Can Melt brand in Dubai. What started as a local specialty exploded globally when TikTok videos of the satisfying crunch and pistachio reveal went viral, creating waitlists months long and inspiring bakeries worldwide to create their own versions.

Dubai Chocolate FAQs

Dubai chocolate refers to the viral pistachio kunafa chocolate bars from Fix It bakery (Can Melt brand) in Dubai. They feature pistachio cream, crispy kunafa strands, and premium chocolate - creating the #1 viral food of 2025.

The original is from Fix It bakery in Dubai (Can Melt brand). Due to viral demand, versions are now available at bakeries in NYC, LA, London, and major cities worldwide. Some retailers ship internationally, but expect waitlists.

The ASMR-worthy crunch, stunning visual of the green pistachio reveal, unique Middle Eastern flavors, scarcity driving hype, and celebrity endorsements all contributed to its viral success on TikTok and Instagram.

Prices vary widely - the original bars in Dubai cost around $15-20, but imported or shipped versions can cost $30-50+. Knockoff versions at local bakeries typically range from $10-25 depending on quality and location.
