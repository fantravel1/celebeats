Top Celebrity Donut Spots

Sidecar Doughnuts - Orange County & LA

California | $4-6/donut

The artisan donut shop that changed the game. Made fresh daily with premium ingredients. The Huckleberry, Butter & Salt, and seasonal flavors draw celebrities from across LA. The Santa Monica location is a weekend destination.

Tier A

Trejo's Coffee & Donuts - LA

Hollywood | $4-5/donut

Danny Trejo's coffee and donut shop draws fellow celebrities. Creative flavors with the Trejo brand attitude. The Hollywood location is perfect for celebrity sightings and quality donuts.

Tier A

Doughnut Plant - NYC

New York City | $4-6/donut

The NYC artisan donut institution since 1994. Square-shaped jelly donuts and creative glazes. The Lower East Side original and Chelsea Market locations draw foodie celebrities.

Tier A

Krispy Kreme - Nationwide

National | $1.50-3/donut

The classic chain that celebrities love for nostalgia. When the "Hot Now" light is on, even A-listers can't resist. The original glazed hot off the line is a universal pleasure.

Tier A

Blue Star Donuts - Portland

Portland & LA | $3-5/donut

The Portland pioneer of brioche-based donuts. Blueberry Bourbon Basil and Cointreau Creme Brulee are creative celebrity favorites. The LA expansion brought Pacific Northwest quality to Hollywood.

Celebrity Donut FAQs

Celebrity donut spots include Sidecar Doughnuts in Orange County and LA, Doughnut Plant in NYC, Blue Star Donuts in Portland, and classic Krispy Kreme. Trejo's Donuts (owned by Danny Trejo) in LA draws celebrity traffic. Many on-set craft services feature local donut shop deliveries.

Celebrity donut preferences range from classic glazed (Krispy Kreme hot off the line) to artisan creations. Sidecar's Huckleberry and Butter & Salt are celebrity favorites. Filled donuts with custard or jam are popular. Some health-conscious celebrities opt for baked rather than fried options.

Danny Trejo owns Trejo's Coffee & Donuts in LA, serving creative flavors to fellow celebrities. Mark Wahlberg had Wahlburgers expand into breakfast including donuts. Several celebrity chefs have donut operations. Ariana Grande famously licked donuts at a shop, creating controversy.

Sidecar Doughnuts is widely considered the best artisan donut shop in LA, with frequent celebrity sightings. In NYC, Doughnut Plant is the celebrity favorite. For nostalgia and convenience, Krispy Kreme remains the classic choice that celebrities grew up loving.

Artisan donuts at celebrity-favorite shops run $3-7 each. Sidecar Doughnuts charges $4-6, Doughnut Plant $4-6, and Blue Star $3-5. Classic Krispy Kreme donuts are $1.50-3 each. A dozen artisan donuts for on-set craft services can run $50-70.
