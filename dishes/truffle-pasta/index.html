Frequently Asked Questions

Where do celebrities eat truffle pasta?
Celebrities order truffle pasta at Funke in Beverly Hills, Carbone in NYC, Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica, and Cipriani locations worldwide. White truffle season (October-December) is peak time.
What is the most expensive truffle pasta?
Fresh white Alba truffle pastas can cost $200-500+ per serving during peak season (October-December). The price varies based on truffle market rates, which can exceed $4,000 per pound.
When is truffle season?
White truffle season runs October-December. Black winter truffle is December-March. Black summer truffle is May-August. Each has distinct flavor profiles and price points.
