Where do celebrities get ice cream?

Salt & Straw in LA is the celebrity ice cream headquarters, with locations in WeHo and Venice drawing constant A-list traffic. Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams has a cult celebrity following. In NYC, Van Leeuwen draws the fashion and entertainment crowd. Celebrities also love local artisan spots and nostalgic chains like Baskin-Robbins.

Celebrity Ice Cream FAQs

Celebrity ice cream spots include Salt & Straw in LA and Portland, Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams, Van Leeuwen in NYC, and classic spots like Baskin-Robbins for nostalgia. Many celebrities post about artisan ice cream shops on Instagram.

Celebrity ice cream preferences vary widely. Health-conscious celebrities often order dairy-free oat milk or coconut options. Classic vanilla and chocolate remain popular. Adventurous celebrities love Salt & Straw's seasonal creative flavors. Many celebrities go for salted caramel or cookie dough.

Several celebrities have invested in or partnered with ice cream brands. Oprah invested in True Food Kitchen which serves desserts. Many celebrities collaborate on limited-edition flavors. Some celebrities have created their own ice cream lines for grocery stores.

Salt & Straw is widely considered the top celebrity ice cream destination, especially the LA locations. Jeni's Splendid Ice Creams has the most passionate celebrity following. For classic vibes, celebrities love local shops and nostalgic chains.

Many celebrities prefer dairy-free options. Van Leeuwen's oat milk and vegan lines are celebrity favorites. Salt & Straw offers coconut-based vegan flavors. Health-conscious celebrities often choose dairy-free for digestion and inflammation reasons.
