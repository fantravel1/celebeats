What is hot honey?

Hot honey is honey infused with chili peppers, creating the perfect sweet-heat condiment. Mike's Hot Honey is the most famous brand, created in Brooklyn in 2010 by Mike Kurtz. It's become a menu staple at pizzerias, fried chicken spots, and upscale restaurants alike, offering the perfect balance of sweetness and spice that elevates everything it touches.

The Mike's Hot Honey Story

Mike Kurtz started drizzling chili-infused honey on pizza at Paulie Gee's in Brooklyn in 2010. What started as a side project became a sensation when diners couldn't stop asking for it. Now Mike's Hot Honey is in thousands of restaurants and grocery stores nationwide, the definitive hot honey brand that sparked a movement.

Hot Honey FAQs

Hot honey is honey infused with chili peppers, creating a sweet-heat condiment. Mike's Hot Honey is the most famous brand, created in Brooklyn in 2010. It's become a menu staple at pizzerias and fried chicken spots.

Hot honey is incredibly versatile - it's most popular on pizza (especially pepperoni), fried chicken, biscuits, charcuterie boards, cheese, cornbread, and even cocktails. Celebrity chefs have featured it on everything from burgers to ice cream.

Hot honey is now ubiquitous - from NYC pizzerias like Paulie Gee's and Prince Street Pizza to celebrity restaurants like Jon & Vinny's in LA. It's a standard offering at most quality pizzerias and fried chicken spots nationwide.

Mike's Hot Honey is the original and most popular brand, known for quality and the perfect sweet-heat balance. While many brands now exist, Mike's remains the gold standard that most restaurants feature.
