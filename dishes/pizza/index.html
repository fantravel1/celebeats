Celebrity Pizza Favorites

From wood-fired Neapolitan to classic New York slices. Discover where the A-list satisfies their pizza cravings.

Where Do Celebrities Eat Pizza?

Celebrities love pizza just like everyone else, but they have their favorite spots. Jon & Vinny's in LA is the undisputed celebrity pizza capital, frequented by Chrissy Teigen, John Legend, and the Kardashians. Lucali in Brooklyn drew Jay-Z and Beyonce before they got famous. Pizzana in Brentwood attracts the Westside celebrity crowd with Neo-Neapolitan pies. Mother Wolf in Hollywood serves Roman-style pizza to entertainment industry power players. The appeal is universal: even stars crave the simple pleasure of great pizza.

Best Celebrity Pizza Spots

Jon & Vinny's

Fairfax / Brentwood $$ Tier A - Verified

LA's most celebrity-dense pizzeria. The casual neighborhood spot where A-listers eat pasta and pizza without pretense. Reservations are nearly impossible.

The Order

Spicy Vodka Pizza - vodka sauce, mozzarella, Calabrian chiles, basil ($24) or Margherita ($20)

Celebrity Fans

Chrissy Teigen John Legend Kim Kardashian

Lucali

Brooklyn / Miami $$ Tier A - Verified

The legendary Carroll Gardens pizzeria that Jay-Z helped make famous. Mark Iacono's perfect pies are worth the 2+ hour wait (or celebrity connections).

The Order

Classic Cheese Pie - thin crust, fresh mozzarella, basil, olive oil ($28) or Calzone ($30)

Celebrity Fans

Jay-Z Beyonce David Beckham

Pizzana

Brentwood / WeHo $$ Tier A - Verified

Daniele Uditi's Neo-Neapolitan pizzeria with California ingredients. The Brentwood location is a celebrity magnet with its intimate, design-forward space.

The Order

Cacio e Pepe Pizza - Pecorino, black pepper, olive oil ($22) or Burrata Pizza ($26)

Celebrity Fans

Gwyneth Paltrow Jennifer Garner Reese Witherspoon

Mother Wolf

Hollywood $$$ Tier B - Reported

Evan Funke's Roman trattoria bringing pizza al taglio and pasta to Hollywood. The stunning space and VIP tables attract entertainment royalty.

The Order

Pizza al Taglio selection - Roman-style rectangular slices with seasonal toppings ($8-14 per slice)

Celebrity Fans

Chris Pratt Jennifer Lawrence Bradley Cooper

Roberta's

Brooklyn $$ Tier B - Reported

The Bushwick pizza institution that helped define Brooklyn cool. Musicians and artists flock to the industrial-chic space for creative pies.

The Order

Famous Original - tomato, mozzarella, caciocavallo, oregano, chili ($19) or Bee Sting ($21)

Celebrity Fans

Pete Davidson Lorde The Weeknd

Joe's Pizza

Greenwich Village $ Tier A - Verified

The iconic NYC slice shop. Even A-listers line up at the counter for Joe's classic New York slice. Spider-Man and countless other films have featured it.

The Order

Classic Cheese Slice - thin crust, tangy tomato sauce, perfectly melted mozzarella ($3.50)

Celebrity Fans

Leonardo DiCaprio Lady Gaga Post Malone

Why Celebrities Love Pizza

1

Low-Key Vibes

Pizza joints offer a casual atmosphere where celebrities can relax without the formality of fine dining. No dress code, no pretense, just good food.

2

Universal Comfort Food

Even with personal chefs and access to any cuisine, celebrities crave pizza's simple, satisfying comfort. It's the great equalizer of food.

3

Family-Friendly

Pizza is perfect for celebrity families. Kids love it, sharing is easy, and the casual environment lets parents relax with their children.

4

Cheat Day Champion

For celebrities with strict diets, pizza is the ultimate cheat day indulgence. Many share their pizza moments on social media, connecting with fans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Many A-list celebrities are known pizza lovers. Jennifer Lawrence famously ate pizza backstage at the Oscars. Chrissy Teigen and John Legend are Jon & Vinny's regulars in LA. Jay-Z and Beyonce put Lucali on the map. Lady Gaga, Post Malone, Pete Davidson, and Leonardo DiCaprio all frequent NYC pizza spots. Even health-conscious celebrities like Gwyneth Paltrow enjoy pizza at places like Pizzana.

The top celebrity pizza spots in LA include Jon & Vinny's (Fairfax and Brentwood) for Italian-American comfort, Pizzana (Brentwood) for Neo-Neapolitan pies, Mother Wolf (Hollywood) for Roman-style pizza al taglio, and Mozza (Hancock Park) for Nancy Silverton's legendary pies. Felix in Venice is another celebrity favorite with its casual Venice vibe and quality ingredients.

Pizza spots offer celebrities an escape from the formality of high-end restaurants. The casual, unpretentious atmosphere lets them feel normal and enjoy comfort food without being on display. Many celebrity-favorite pizzerias have modest decor and a neighborhood feel. Pizza is also quick, which suits busy celebrity schedules, and works well for families with children.

Celebrity pizza orders vary from classic to creative. At Jon & Vinny's, the Spicy Vodka pizza is most popular. At Lucali, celebrities stick with the classic cheese pie. Pizzana's Cacio e Pepe and Burrata pizzas are celebrity favorites. At Joe's in NYC, everyone gets the classic slice. Many celebrities prefer simple Margherita or cheese pizzas, though some like Jennifer Lawrence have been spotted enjoying more loaded options.

Celebrity pizza spots range widely in price. A slice at Joe's Pizza is just $3.50, while a full pie at Jon & Vinny's runs $20-26. Lucali's cheese pie is $28, and Pizzana's specialty pizzas are $22-26. Mother Wolf's pizza al taglio is $8-14 per slice. Unlike many celebrity dining destinations, these pizza spots are surprisingly accessible price-wise, contributing to their appeal.
