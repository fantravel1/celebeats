The Dish

What Makes It Special?

  • Chicken breast pounded paper-thin (the secret)
  • Light, crispy breadcrumb coating
  • Same vodka sauce used in the famous rigatoni
  • Fresh mozzarella, bubbly and perfectly melted
  • Pecorino Romano finish
  • Massive portion - easily shareable

The Carbone Experience

Carbone isn't just about the food - it's dinner theater. Waiters in red jackets, a 1950s Italian-American supper club vibe, and impeccable service. The Chicken Parm arrives sizzling, presented tableside with ceremonial flair. For celebrities, it's comfort food elevated to an art form.

The Perfect Order

Carbone regulars know the move: start with the Caesar salad (mixed tableside), share the Chicken Parm with the Spicy Rigatoni Vodka, and finish with the Coconut Layer Cake. Total damage for two: around $250 before drinks. Worth every penny according to A-listers.
