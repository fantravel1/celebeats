What is the best celebrity burger in America?

The Celebrity Burger Hall of Fame

Au Cheval Double Cheeseburger

Chicago | $18.50

The burger that sparked a national obsession. Two perfectly seasoned patties, dijonnaise, pickles, and American cheese on a pillowy bun. Add a fried egg for the full experience. Be prepared to wait 2+ hours - there are no reservations and no celebrity exceptions.

Tier A

Minetta Tavern Black Label Burger

NYC | $36

The most expensive burger in NYC that's actually worth it. Made with dry-aged prime beef from Pat LaFrieda, caramelized onions, and served on a brioche bun. A favorite of Jay-Z, Anna Wintour, and the fashion crowd. No ketchup required - or allowed.

Tier A

In-N-Out Double-Double

California | $5.45

The celebrity comfort food of choice on the West Coast. Gordon Ramsay, Kylie Jenner, and Julia Roberts have all been spotted. Order it "Animal Style" for the secret menu upgrade. Proof that celebrities sometimes just want a simple, perfect burger.

Tier A

Father's Office Burger

LA | $19

The OG gourmet burger that launched the movement. Dry-aged beef, gruyere, Maytag blue cheese, arugula, and caramelized onions. No substitutions, no ketchup, no exceptions. A favorite of chefs and food-obsessed celebrities.

Tier B

The Apple Pan Hickory Burger

LA | $10.35

Old-school LA since 1947. Counter service, no-frills, just perfect burgers with hickory sauce. Beloved by Quentin Tarantino and old Hollywood. Cash only, and that's part of the charm.

Tier B
