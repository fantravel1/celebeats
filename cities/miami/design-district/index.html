Swan: Pharrell's Miami Hotspot

Swan, co-owned by Pharrell Williams and David Grutman, has become the Design District's signature celebrity destination since 2018:

  • Garden Setting - Lush tropical courtyard under twinkling lights
  • Mediterranean Menu - Wood-fired meats, fresh seafood, housemade pastas
  • Bar Bevy - Adjacent cocktail lounge for pre/post dinner drinks
  • Fashion Crowd - Models, designers, Art Basel collectors
  • Celebrity Partners - Pharrell brings his A-list network

Regular visitors include Kim Kardashian, Dwyane Wade, DJ Khaled, and the entire fashion week circuit during Art Basel.

Art Basel & Design District

During Art Basel Miami (December), the Design District transforms into the epicenter of celebrity activity:

  • Private Dinners - Brand-hosted celebrity events at every restaurant
  • Gallery Openings - Art collectors and celebrities mingle
  • Fashion Shows - Louis Vuitton, Dior pop-ups with celebrity front rows
  • Impossible Reservations - Book 2-3 months ahead for Basel week
