Wynwood

Where Art Meets Appetite

Miami's vibrant arts district serves up celebrity dining among world-famous murals. From Asian BBQ to late-night tacos, Wynwood's food scene is as colorful as its walls.

South Beach Brickell Design District Coconut Grove

Where do celebrities eat in Wynwood?

Celebrities in Wynwood dine at KYU for wood-fired Asian BBQ, Coyo Taco for late-night tacos with a hidden speakeasy, Alter for chef-driven fine dining, and 1-800-Lucky food hall. During Art Basel, every Wynwood restaurant sees major celebrity traffic.

Art Basel Week

Every December, Art Basel transforms Wynwood into celebrity central. During this week, expect to see international art collectors, A-list celebrities, and fashion icons dining at every restaurant in the neighborhood. Reservations become nearly impossible‚Äîplan months ahead.
