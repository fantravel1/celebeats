Prime 112: The Original Celebrity Steakhouse

Prime 112 has been Miami's celebrity dining headquarters since 2004. Located in a historic Art Deco building on Ocean Drive, it's known for:

  • Truffle Mac & Cheese - The most famous side dish in Miami
  • A-List Regulars - LeBron, Drake, DJ Khaled, Bad Bunny
  • Prime One Twelve - Sister restaurant next door
  • Art Basel Hotspot - Packed during December art week
  • Late Night - Open until midnight, post-club crowd

Reservations are essential, especially on weekends and during major Miami events like Art Basel, Ultra, and F1 weekend.
