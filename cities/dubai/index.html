Dubai FAQs

The most popular celebrity restaurants in Dubai include Nusr-Et Steakhouse by Salt Bae, Nobu Dubai at Atlantis, Zuma in DIFC, La Petite Maison, and Cipriani. Hotel restaurants at Burj Al Arab, Atlantis The Palm, and Four Seasons are also celebrity favorites.

Palm Jumeirah and Downtown Dubai offer the most celebrity sightings. Palm Jumeirah has Atlantis The Palm, Nobu, and luxury beach clubs. Downtown Dubai near Burj Khalifa features At.mosphere, Armani restaurants, and Nusr-Et. DIFC is popular with international visitors for Zuma and La Petite Maison.

Dubai regularly hosts the Kardashian-Jenner family, David Beckham and family, DJ Khaled, Will Smith, Drake, Floyd Mayweather, and many Bollywood stars. Salt Bae's Nusr-Et is a celebrity magnet, and Atlantis The Palm attracts international A-listers.

At.mosphere on the 122nd floor of Burj Khalifa is Dubai's most elevated restaurant. Burj Al Arab restaurants like Al Muntaha and Nathan Outlaw at Al Mahara offer ultra-luxury dining. Nusr-Et's gold-wrapped steaks are famous for their extravagant prices.
