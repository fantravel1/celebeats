Palm Jumeirah FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Palm Jumeirah include Nobu Dubai at Atlantis The Palm, Hakkasan, Ossiano underwater restaurant, Gordon Ramsay's Bread Street Kitchen, and Seafire Steakhouse. The Waldorf Astoria Palm Jumeirah and One&Only The Palm also feature celebrity-frequented dining.

Atlantis The Palm regularly hosts celebrities including the Kardashian family, Kim Kardashian, Kylie Jenner, DJ Khaled, David Beckham, and numerous sports stars and musicians. The resort's Nobu restaurant and underwater suites are particular celebrity favorites.

Nobu Dubai at Atlantis The Palm is one of the most acclaimed Nobu locations worldwide. Set in a stunning space overlooking the Arabian Gulf, it serves signature dishes like Black Cod Miso and Yellowtail Jalapeno. It's consistently rated among Dubai's top restaurants and is a major celebrity hotspot.

Ossiano is the underwater restaurant at Atlantis The Palm, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows into the Ambassador Lagoon aquarium. Diners enjoy fine dining while watching sharks, rays, and thousands of fish. It's one of Dubai's most unique celebrity dining experiences.
