Downtown Dubai FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Downtown Dubai include At.mosphere on the 122nd floor of Burj Khalifa, Armani Ristorante at Armani Hotel, Nusr-Et Steakhouse by Salt Bae, Social by Heinz Beck, and Thiptara at Palace Downtown. The Dubai Mall also houses celebrity-frequented restaurants.

At.mosphere is the signature restaurant in Burj Khalifa, located on the 122nd floor. It offers fine dining with panoramic views of Dubai and is known as the world's highest restaurant. Armani Hotel Dubai at the base of Burj Khalifa also features multiple celebrity-favorite restaurants.

Yes, Nusr-Et Steakhouse by Salt Bae has a location at Four Seasons DIFC near Downtown Dubai. The restaurant is famous for its gold-wrapped steaks and Salt Bae's signature theatrical meat preparation, attracting numerous celebrities and social media attention.

Celebrities spotted dining at Burj Khalifa and Downtown Dubai restaurants include the Kardashian family, David Beckham, DJ Khaled, Will Smith, Floyd Mayweather, and numerous international sports stars and musicians. At.mosphere and Armani are particular favorites.
