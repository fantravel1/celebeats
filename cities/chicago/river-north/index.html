River North FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in River North Chicago include RPM Italian, Bavette's Bar & Boeuf, GT Fish & Oyster, The Dearborn, Siena Tavern, and Chicago Cut Steakhouse. These upscale restaurants are known for attracting celebrities, athletes, and Chicago power players.

RPM Italian is known for its modern Italian cuisine, celebrity ownership (Bill and Giuliana Rancic), power-lunch crowd, and frequent celebrity sightings. The restaurant is famous for its thin-crust pizzas, handmade pastas, and chic atmosphere that attracts Chicago's elite.

Yes, River North is one of the best neighborhoods in Chicago for celebrity sightings. The area's upscale restaurants, proximity to downtown hotels, and vibrant nightlife make it a magnet for visiting celebrities, professional athletes, and media personalities.

Most River North restaurants require smart casual to upscale attire. RPM Italian and Bavette's lean dressier, especially for dinner. Chicago Cut is business-appropriate. GT Fish & Oyster and Siena Tavern are more casual but still polished.
