Chicago's hottest food neighborhood. From former meatpacking district to Restaurant Row, West Loop is where celebrity chefs and food-loving stars converge.

Where do celebrities eat in West Loop Chicago?

Top celebrity restaurants in West Loop include Girl & The Goat (Top Chef winner Stephanie Izard's flagship), Au Cheval (home of "the best burger in America"), Roister (from the Alinea Group), Avec, and The Publican. This is where Chicago natives like Chance the Rapper, Kanye West, and Common come when they want chef-driven, trendy dining.

Restaurant Row Guide

Best for Group Dining

Worth The Wait

The Au Cheval Phenomenon

Is Au Cheval really worth the wait?

Au Cheval's double cheeseburger has been called "the best burger in America" by multiple publications. Expect 1-3 hour waits on weekends. Celebrities and locals alike brave the lines. Pro tip: Go on weeknights or try the bar seating for shorter waits. The burger, with its dijonnaise, thick-cut bacon, and perfectly cooked patties, lives up to the hype.
