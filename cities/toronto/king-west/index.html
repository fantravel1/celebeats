King West

Toronto's Hottest Dining & Nightlife District

Where Drake dines, TIFF celebrities party, and Toronto's fashion crowd gathers. King West is the city's premier destination for trendy restaurants and late-night energy.

Yorkville Entertainment District Distillery Queen West

Where do celebrities eat in King West Toronto?

King West's celebrity dining scene centers on Byblos for Eastern Mediterranean, KOST at the Bisha Hotel for rooftop glamour, Lee Restaurant for Susur Lee's Asian fusion, and Patria for Spanish tapas. During TIFF, the neighborhood transforms into a celebrity hotspot with film stars at every corner.

TIFF Season

Every September, the Toronto International Film Festival transforms King West into Hollywood North. Celebrities flood the neighborhood's restaurants for after-parties and industry dinners. Byblos, Patria, and KOST become virtual red carpets, with A-list sightings at every table.
