Where do celebrities eat in Yorkville Toronto?

The luxury shopping district's top celebrity restaurants include Baia for coastal Italian, Sotto Sotto (Drake's longtime favorite), Cafe Boulud at the Four Seasons, ONE Restaurant at the Hazelton Hotel, and STK Toronto for upscale steakhouse dining. The neighborhood's designer boutiques and five-star hotels make it a natural celebrity magnet.

Luxury Shopping Meets Fine Dining

Yorkville's "Mink Mile" on Bloor Street creates the perfect backdrop for celebrity dining:

  • Designer Flagships - Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Prada, Hermes
  • Five-Star Hotels - Four Seasons, Hazelton Hotel, Park Hyatt
  • Art Galleries - World-class contemporary art scene
  • Private Shopping - After-hours appointments for VIPs
  • Valet Parking - Discrete arrivals at every restaurant

Celebrities shop, stay, and dine all within steps of each other in this exclusive enclave.

TIFF Season in Yorkville

Every September, the Toronto International Film Festival transforms Yorkville into a celebrity paradise:

  • 10 days of non-stop celebrity activity
  • Roy Thomson Hall premieres bring A-listers to nearby restaurants
  • Hazelton Hotel is the unofficial celebrity headquarters
  • Private parties at Soho House Toronto and exclusive venues
  • Paparazzi spots along Yorkville Avenue and Cumberland Street

Past TIFF celebrities spotted in Yorkville include Brad Pitt, Angelina Jolie, George Clooney, Lady Gaga, and countless others.

Yorkville FAQs

Top spots include Baia, Sotto Sotto (Drake's favorite), Cafe Boulud at Four Seasons, ONE Restaurant at Hazelton Hotel, and STK Toronto. These restaurants combine upscale dining with the luxury shopping district atmosphere.

The "Mink Mile" on Bloor Street features flagship stores from Chanel, Louis Vuitton, Gucci, Prada, and Hermes. Combined with the Four Seasons and Hazelton hotels, it's Canada's premier luxury destination.

TIFF in September brings the highest celebrity concentration. Year-round, weekend brunch and dinner hours (7-9pm) at top restaurants offer the best chances for sightings.
