TIFF Season in Yorkville

Every September, the Toronto International Film Festival transforms Yorkville into a celebrity paradise:

  • 10 days of non-stop celebrity activity
  • Roy Thomson Hall premieres bring A-listers to nearby restaurants
  • Hazelton Hotel is the unofficial celebrity headquarters
  • Private parties at Soho House Toronto and exclusive venues
  • Paparazzi spots along Yorkville Avenue and Cumberland Street

Past TIFF celebrities spotted in Yorkville include Brad Pitt, Angelina Jolie, George Clooney, Lady Gaga, and countless others.
