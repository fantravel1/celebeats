Dining Guide

Essential Aspen Dining Experiences

The must-try experiences that bring celebrities to Aspen.

Nobu at Matsuhisa

Before there was Nobu, there was Matsuhisa in Aspen. This is where Chef Nobu Matsuhisa built his celebrity following. The black cod miso and yellowtail sashimi remain pilgrimage-worthy dishes.

Must Try

Cloud Nine Champagne Lunch

Ski into Cloud Nine Alpine Bistro for the legendary champagne-spraying party. Fondue, dancing on tables, and celebrity sightings make this the world's most famous mountain lunch.

Legendary

Apres-Ski at Ajax Tavern

At the base of Aspen Mountain, Ajax Tavern is ground zero for apres-ski. Celebrities mingle with skiers over truffle fries and champagne as the sun sets over the slopes.

Iconic

Casa Tua Private Dining

With its members-only vibe, Casa Tua offers the exclusivity that Aspen's elite demand. Italian cuisine in an intimate setting where billionaires and celebrities feel at home.

Aspen FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Aspen include Matsuhisa Aspen (Nobu's original mountain outpost), Casa Tua (Italian fine dining), element 47 (The Little Nell's flagship), and Cache Cache. On the mountain, Cloud Nine Alpine Bistro is legendary for celebrity lunches.

Ajax Tavern at the base of Aspen Mountain is the quintessential apres-ski spot. The Little Nell's terrace also offers prime people-watching. For on-mountain celebrations, Cloud Nine Alpine Bistro is unmatched.

Cloud Nine Alpine Bistro is famous for its champagne-spraying celebrations, ski-in lunch experience, and celebrity clientele. Located on Aspen Highlands, this Austrian-style restaurant features dancing on tables and a legendary party atmosphere.

Peak celebrity season runs from Christmas through New Year's, with another surge during the Aspen Food & Wine Classic in June. Winter weekends throughout ski season also bring steady celebrity traffic to Matsuhisa and other top restaurants.

Yes, absolutely. During peak season, reservations at Matsuhisa, Casa Tua, and Cloud Nine should be made weeks in advance. Hotel concierges at The Little Nell and St. Regis can assist with hard-to-get bookings.
