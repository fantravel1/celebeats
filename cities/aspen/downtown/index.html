Downtown Aspen FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Downtown Aspen include Matsuhisa, Casa Tua, Cache Cache, element 47 at The Little Nell, and Ajax Tavern. These restaurants form the core of Aspen's billionaire dining row.

Matsuhisa Aspen is Chef Nobu Matsuhisa's original restaurant, opened before the Nobu empire. Located on Main Street, it remains one of America's most exclusive Japanese restaurants and a celebrity pilgrimage destination.

Casa Tua has a private club atmosphere but accepts reservations from non-members. Reservations are essential and often require connections or hotel concierge assistance, especially during peak season.

Billionaire row refers to Downtown Aspen's concentration of ultra-luxury restaurants, hotels, and boutiques along Main Street. Matsuhisa, Casa Tua, The Little Nell, and St. Regis anchor this exclusive district.
