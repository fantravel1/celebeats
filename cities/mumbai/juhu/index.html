Culinary Guide

Juhu Food Experiences

Pav Bhaji

Juhu Beach is synonymous with Mumbai's beloved pav bhaji - spiced vegetable mash served with buttered bread. Evening stalls create the most authentic experience. Many celebrities have shared their love for this dish.

Must Try

Pani Puri / Golgappa

Crispy hollow puris filled with spiced water and tamarind - an essential Mumbai street food experience. Juhu Beach stalls serve some of the city's best. Even Bollywood stars queue up for this treat.

Iconic

Five-Star Hotel Dining

JW Marriott offers world-class dining from Italian to Pan-Asian. Celebrity events, wedding receptions, and film parties frequently take place here. Private dining rooms offer discreet celebrity experiences.

Luxury

Beach Sunset Dining

Juhu Beach at sunset offers a magical dining atmosphere. From humble chaat stalls to beachfront restaurants, the evening food scene attracts celebrities for casual outings away from paparazzi.

Juhu FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Juhu include the JW Marriott hotel restaurants (Romano's, Lotus Cafe), Mezzo Mezzo, and Soho House Mumbai. Celebrities also love Juhu Beach street food stalls for authentic pav bhaji and pani puri. The beachfront attracts paparazzi documenting celebrity beach walks.

Amitabh Bachchan's famous bungalow Jalsa is located in Juhu. It's one of Mumbai's most iconic celebrity homes where fans gather on Sundays. Big B often waves to crowds from outside his gates. He also owns Pratiksha and Janak in the same area.

Juhu Beach is famous for Mumbai street food including pav bhaji, pani puri (golgappa), bhel puri, and sev puri. Many celebrities have expressed their love for Juhu Beach street food. The evening stalls create a vibrant atmosphere where locals and tourists enjoy authentic flavors.

Juhu is home to Bollywood royalty including Amitabh Bachchan (Jalsa), the Bachchan family, Hrithik Roshan, and many other stars. The area has been a preferred celebrity residential area for decades due to its beachfront location and relative privacy.

Best spots for celebrity sightings in Juhu include outside Jalsa (especially Sunday mornings), JW Marriott hotel restaurants, celebrity beach walks in early mornings, and high-end restaurants in the JVPD Scheme area. Paparazzi regularly document celebrity movements in this neighborhood.
