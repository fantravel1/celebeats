Iconic Bandra Spots

Bandstand Promenade

The scenic seaside walkway where celebrities jog and fans gather outside Mannat. Several upscale restaurants and cafes line this iconic stretch with premium celebrity sighting potential.

Chapel Road

Trendy cafe street with Salt Water Cafe and other hip eateries. Popular with younger Bollywood stars, TV actors, and social media influencers for casual dining and coffee.

Pali Hill

Exclusive residential area home to many Bollywood celebrities. Pali Village Cafe and nearby restaurants offer intimate dining in this quiet, tree-lined neighborhood.

Linking Road

Shopping and dining hub where Bastian is located. The street food stalls and upscale restaurants create a vibrant mix where celebrities and locals mingle.

Bandra FAQs

The top celebrity restaurant in Bandra is Bastian, frequented by Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, and virtually every Bollywood star. Other celebrity favorites include Pali Village Cafe, Izumi, The Table, and various cafes along Chapel Road. Bandra offers the most documented celebrity dining in India.

Shah Rukh Khan's famous mansion Mannat is located on Bandstand Promenade in Bandra West. It's one of Mumbai's most iconic landmarks, with fans gathering daily outside. Many restaurants near Bandstand, including Bastian, are frequented by SRK and visiting celebrities.

Bastian is Mumbai's premier celebrity restaurant, known for its seafood and Asian cuisine. Located in Bandra, it's owned by celebrities and attracts A-list Bollywood stars regularly. The restaurant is famous for butter garlic crab, sushi, and its upscale yet trendy atmosphere that celebrities love.

Salman Khan lives at Galaxy Apartments in Bandra West. He has lived there since childhood and is famous for greeting fans from his balcony on Eid. The area around Galaxy Apartments has several restaurants where Salman and the Khan family have been spotted dining.

Best cafes in Bandra for celebrity sightings include Pali Village Cafe (casual celebrity favorite), Candies, Salt Water Cafe, and various spots along Chapel Road and Pali Hill. Younger Bollywood stars and social media influencers frequent these trendy establishments regularly.
