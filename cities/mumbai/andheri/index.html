Andheri

Mumbai's film studios district. Near Film City and home to Lokhandwala's celebrity apartments, this is where Bollywood's working professionals eat. A more casual, industry insider vibe with authentic food and trendy cafes.

Where do celebrities eat in Andheri?

Popular celebrity restaurants in Andheri include Versova Social for rooftop vibes, The Bagel Shop for celebrity breakfast, D:OH! All Day Dining in Lokhandwala, and various Versova cafes. Industry Life

Bollywood's Working Hub

Film City Nearby

Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari in Goregaon is India's largest film studio complex. Most Bollywood films and TV shows shoot here. Andheri restaurants cater to crews with quick, hearty meals.

Production Hub

Casting Director Spots

Many cafes double as informal audition spots where casting directors meet aspiring actors. Industry networking happens over coffee and chai in these casual settings.

Industry Tip

Late Night Culture

Film shoots run late, so Andheri restaurants stay open longer. Post-shoot dinners and wrap parties happen at Lokhandwala eateries that understand industry hours.

Night Life

Budget-Friendly

Unlike pricey Bandra, Andheri offers affordable dining. Struggling actors and established character actors alike appreciate the value without sacrificing quality.

Andheri FAQs

Popular celebrity restaurants in Andheri include various Versova cafes, Lokhandwala eateries like D:OH! All Day Dining, and restaurants near Film City. The area has a more casual vibe where industry insiders and film crews dine during shoots. Spots like The Bagel Shop and Versova Social attract younger stars.

Lokhandwala is a residential complex in Andheri West famous for housing many Bollywood celebrities and TV stars. It's more affordable than Bandra or Juhu, making it popular with younger actors and industry professionals. The area has numerous restaurants and cafes that cater to its celebrity residents.

Film City (officially Dadasaheb Phalke Chitranagari) is located in Goregaon East, just north of Andheri. It's one of India's largest film studio complexes where most Bollywood films and TV shows are shot. Restaurants in nearby Andheri cater to film crews during shoots.

Popular Versova cafes include Versova Social (rooftop vibes), The Bagel Shop (celebrity breakfast spot), Deli by The Blue, and various beachside cafes. Versova has a bohemian, artistic vibe that attracts younger Bollywood stars, musicians, and creative industry professionals.

Andheri, particularly Lokhandwala and Versova, houses many TV actors, character actors, and younger Bollywood stars. While A-listers typically live in Bandra or Juhu, many successful actors start their careers living in Andheri due to its proximity to Film City and relative affordability.
