Lower Parel FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Lower Parel include Masala Library (molecular gastronomy Indian by Jiggs Kalra), Hakkasan Mumbai (upscale Chinese), Farzi Cafe (modern Indian), Pa Pa Ya (Asian), and Tresind. The Phoenix Mills area is particularly popular for celebrity dining and nightlife events.

Masala Library by Jiggs Kalra is known for molecular gastronomy Indian cuisine - reimagining traditional dishes with modern techniques. Celebrity favorites include the deconstructed dishes and artistic presentations. It's considered one of India's best modern Indian restaurants and regularly hosts Bollywood celebrations.

Hakkasan Mumbai in Lower Parel is extremely popular with Bollywood celebrities. The upscale Chinese restaurant is known for its dim sum, Peking duck, and sophisticated atmosphere. Celebrity birthday parties and film celebrations frequently take place here. It's considered one of Mumbai's most glamorous dining destinations.

Phoenix Mills is a converted textile mill that's now one of Mumbai's premier shopping and dining destinations. High Street Phoenix and Palladium malls house numerous celebrity restaurants including Pa Pa Ya, and various lounges. It's a popular spot for film promotions and celebrity appearances.

Lower Parel has Mumbai's most vibrant nightlife scene. Clubs and lounges at Kamala Mills and Phoenix Mills attract Bollywood celebrities for parties and celebrations. Restaurants like Hakkasan transition into late-night hotspots. Film wrap parties and celebrity birthdays frequently happen in this high-energy area.
