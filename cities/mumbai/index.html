Culinary Guide

Essential Mumbai Food Experiences

The must-try dishes that bring Bollywood celebrities to Mumbai's tables.

Seafood at Bastian

Mumbai's coastal location means incredible seafood. Bastian has become the celebrity destination for fresh catches, butter garlic crab, and Asian-fusion preparations that draw every A-lister.

Must Try

Street Food Culture

Vada pav, pav bhaji, and pani puri define Mumbai's soul. Juhu Beach stalls and Mohammad Ali Road during Ramadan attract even Bollywood stars seeking authentic flavors.

Iconic

Modern Indian Fine Dining

Mumbai leads India's culinary revolution. Masala Library's molecular gastronomy and Tresind's contemporary interpretations have redefined Indian cuisine for celebrity diners.

Fine Dining

Parsi Cuisine

Mumbai's unique Parsi heritage offers dishes like dhansak and berry pulao. Britannia & Co. in Ballard Estate is a legendary institution that celebrities have frequented for decades.

Mumbai FAQs

Top Bollywood celebrity restaurants in Mumbai include Bastian in Bandra (celebrity hotspot), Hakkasan Mumbai, Wasabi by Morimoto at Taj Mahal Palace, Tresind in BKC, and Masala Library in Lower Parel. Stars like Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Ranveer Singh, and Kareena Kapoor are frequently spotted at these venues.

Bandra is Mumbai's premier celebrity neighborhood, known as the 'Queen of the Suburbs.' Shah Rukh Khan's mansion Mannat and Salman Khan's Galaxy Apartments are located here. Bastian restaurant is the most famous celebrity hotspot. Juhu is also excellent for sightings near Amitabh Bachchan's Jalsa.

Shah Rukh Khan has been spotted at various Mumbai restaurants including Bastian in Bandra (near his home Mannat), Hakkasan Mumbai, and Yauatcha. He also frequents fine dining venues at five-star hotels like Taj Mahal Palace and The Oberoi.

Mumbai's most expensive celebrity restaurants include Wasabi by Morimoto at Taj Mahal Palace (Japanese fine dining), Tresind (modern Indian), Masala Library (molecular gastronomy Indian), and Ziya at The Oberoi. These venues regularly host A-list Bollywood stars and business tycoons.

Juhu Beach is famous for pav bhaji and pani puri that even celebrities enjoy. Mohammad Ali Road during Ramadan attracts food lovers including Bollywood stars. Bademiya near Colaba is legendary for late-night kebabs. Many celebrities have shared their love for Mumbai's iconic street food culture.
