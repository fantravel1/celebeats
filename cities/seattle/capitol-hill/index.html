Capitol Hill Dining Guide

Best for Tasting Menus

Altura offers the most refined multi-course experience. Book 2-3 weeks ahead for weekend reservations.

Best for Steaks

Bateau's whole-animal program delivers exceptional dry-aged cuts in a butcher-shop atmosphere.

Best for Late Night

Canon stays open late with world-class whiskey. Perfect for post-dinner drinks and celebrity spotting.

Best for Groups

Stateside accommodates larger parties with shareable Vietnamese dishes in a lively setting.
