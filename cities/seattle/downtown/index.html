Downtown Seattle Dining Guide

Best for Business Dinners

The Metropolitan Grill and The Capital Grille offer the professional atmosphere and private dining options executives expect.

Best for Special Occasions

El Gaucho's tableside preparations and live music create memorable celebration experiences.

Best for Pre-Theater

Wild Ginger's location and efficient service make it ideal before shows at nearby theaters.

Reservation Tips

Book steakhouses 1-2 weeks ahead for dinner. Lunch is easier to secure and offers celebrity power-lunch sightings.
