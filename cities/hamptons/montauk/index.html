The Vibe

Essential Montauk Experiences

What makes Montauk the celebrity beach destination.

Sunset at Surf Lodge

The Surf Lodge sunset is a summer institution. Celebrities and influencers gather on the waterfront deck as DJs spin and the sun drops over Fort Pond. It's the defining image of Montauk summer.

Iconic

Lobster Roll at Duryea's

The warm, buttered lobster roll at Duryea's is essential Montauk eating. Watch fishing boats while devouring fresh seafood on the waterfront deck. Celebrities queue alongside locals for the experience.

Must Try

Beach Dining at Navy Beach

Sand between your toes dining at its finest. Navy Beach serves fresh seafood directly on Montauk's shores. The casual-luxury atmosphere perfectly captures the town's appeal.

Beach Vibes

Surf Culture

Montauk's legitimate surf culture sets it apart from the rest of The Hamptons. Celebrities who surf appreciate the authentic beach town vibe and early morning waves at Ditch Plains.
