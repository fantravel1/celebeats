The Legend

Nick & Toni's: The Celebrity Dining Room

Why this farm-to-table Italian restaurant defines Hamptons summer.

The Wood-Fired Oven

The legendary wood-fired oven at Nick & Toni's produces perfectly charred pizzas and roasted dishes that have been feeding celebrities since 1988. The technique and quality remain unchanged.

Signature

Farm-to-Table Pioneer

Before farm-to-table was trendy, Nick & Toni's was sourcing from local Long Island farms. The seasonal menu celebrates Hamptons agriculture with Italian preparation.

Original

The Celebrity Scene

On any summer weekend, you might spot Steven Spielberg, Jerry Seinfeld, or Sarah Jessica Parker. The restaurant treats celebrities like locals, which is exactly why they return.

A-List

35+ Years Strong

Since opening in 1988, Nick & Toni's has remained the standard-bearer for Hamptons dining. New restaurants come and go, but this institution endures as the definitive celebrity destination.

East Hampton FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Nick & Toni's (legendary Italian), The 1770 House (elegant inn dining), Dopo La Spiaggia (Italian seafood), and Rowdy Hall (casual American). Nick & Toni's remains the definitive celebrity destination.

Nick & Toni's is famous for its wood-fired oven, farm-to-table Italian cuisine, and extraordinary celebrity clientele. Since 1988, it has served as the unofficial dining room for Hollywood A-listers who summer in East Hampton.

Book well in advance during summer season, especially for weekends. Call directly or use Resy. Weeknight reservations are easier to secure. Regulars and hotel concierges may have better access during peak times.

East Hampton dining is elegant but relaxed. Think "Hamptons chic" - summer dresses, linen pants, tasteful casual wear. Nick & Toni's doesn't have a formal dress code, but guests typically dress well. No beach attire at dinner.
