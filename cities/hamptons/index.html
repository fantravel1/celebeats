Dining Guide

Essential Hamptons Dining Experiences

The must-try experiences that define Hamptons summer.

Wood-Fired Pizza at Nick & Toni's

The legendary wood-oven at Nick & Toni's has been turning out perfect pizzas for celebrity diners since 1988. The farm-to-table Italian menu changes seasonally, but the star-spotting never stops.

Must Try

Sunset at Surf Lodge

Surf Lodge's waterfront sunsets are legendary. Celebrities gather on the deck for cocktails as DJs spin and the Montauk sun dips below the harbor. It's the defining image of Hamptons summer.

Iconic

Lobster Roll at Duryea's

The Connecticut-style lobster roll at Duryea's - warm, buttered, and piled high - is essential Hamptons eating. Celebrities queue alongside locals at this beloved Montauk waterfront institution.

Essential

Farm-to-Table Dining

The Hamptons' farm-to-table movement draws celebrity foodies to restaurants sourcing from local farms. From Nick & Toni's to Tutto il Giorno, seasonal menus celebrate Long Island's agricultural bounty.

Hamptons FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Nick & Toni's (East Hampton), Surf Lodge (Montauk), Duryea's Lobster Deck (Montauk), and Sant Ambroeus (Southampton). East Hampton offers refined dining while Montauk provides beach vibes.

Nick & Toni's in East Hampton is the most famous celebrity restaurant. This farm-to-table Italian institution has been the summer dining room for Hollywood A-listers, media moguls, and fashion icons for over 30 years.

Celebrity season runs from Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day, with peak sightings in July and August. Weekends are especially busy as celebrities escape NYC. The Hamptons Film Festival in October also brings celebrity visitors.

East Hampton offers refined dining at Nick & Toni's and similar establishments. Montauk attracts a younger, casual celebrity crowd at Surf Lodge and beach venues. Many celebrities visit both - East Hampton for dinners, Montauk for daytime beach scene.

Yes, reservations are essential during summer season, especially for Nick & Toni's and popular restaurants on weekends. Book weeks in advance for prime times. Surf Lodge and beach spots may have walk-up availability on weekdays.
