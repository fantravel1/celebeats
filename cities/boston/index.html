Boston FAQs

The most popular celebrity restaurants in Boston include No. 9 Park (Beacon Hill), Menton (Seaport), Abe & Louie's (Back Bay), Neptune Oyster (North End), and O Ya. These restaurants are frequented by local celebrities like Mark Wahlberg, Matt Damon, Chris Evans, and John Krasinski.

Back Bay and the North End are the top neighborhoods for celebrity sightings in Boston. Back Bay offers upscale steakhouses and fine dining along Newbury Street, while the North End is Boston's Little Italy with authentic Italian restaurants that attract food-loving celebrities.

Mark Wahlberg frequents several Boston restaurants including Abe & Louie's steakhouse in Back Bay, Wahlburgers (his family's restaurant), Post 390, and various North End Italian spots. He's often spotted at casual hometown favorites when filming in Boston.

Matt Damon and Ben Affleck are known to frequent No. 9 Park, Eastern Standard, Toro, and Regina Pizzeria in the North End. They've been spotted at these hometown favorites during visits to Boston for film projects and family events.

Yes, most celebrity-frequented restaurants in Boston require reservations. No. 9 Park and Menton often book up weeks in advance. Neptune Oyster is walk-in only but expect long waits, especially on weekends. Abe & Louie's accepts reservations and is recommended for dinner.
