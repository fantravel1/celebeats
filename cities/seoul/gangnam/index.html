Gangnam FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Gangnam include Jungsik (2 Michelin stars), Mingles (2 Michelin stars), Maple Tree House (Korean BBQ favorite), Born & Bred, and numerous upscale dining spots near COEX. K-pop idols from nearby entertainment companies frequently dine in the district.

Maple Tree House and Born & Bred are the top celebrity Korean BBQ spots in Gangnam. Both feature premium hanwoo beef, private dining rooms for VIP guests, and have hosted countless K-pop idols, Korean actors, and international celebrities visiting Seoul.

Gangnam is the heart of K-pop culture. BTS members, BLACKPINK, EXO, TWICE, aespa, and virtually every major K-pop group has been spotted dining in Gangnam, often near the headquarters of SM, JYP, and other entertainment companies.

Gangnam is Seoul's most upscale dining district. While Michelin-starred restaurants and premium Korean BBQ spots can be expensive, there are also reasonably priced options. Expect to pay more than other Seoul neighborhoods for the celebrity-adjacent experience.
