Itaewon FAQs

Celebrities in Itaewon frequent international restaurants, upscale bars, and diverse global cuisines. Popular spots include Mexican, Middle Eastern, Indian, and Western restaurants. K-pop idols with international backgrounds often visit Itaewon for familiar comfort food.

Itaewon offers Seoul's most diverse dining options including Mexican, Turkish, Indian, Thai, Italian, American, Middle Eastern, and African cuisines. The neighborhood is famous for its authentic international restaurants run by expats and immigrants.

Itaewon is one of Seoul's premier nightlife destinations with rooftop bars, cocktail lounges, and clubs. Celebrities frequent the area for its cosmopolitan atmosphere and late-night dining options. The nearby Gyeongnidan-gil area offers a more refined dining experience.

Gyeongnidan-gil is a trendy street near Itaewon known for hip cafes, wine bars, and upscale restaurants. It emerged as a foodie destination with a mix of Korean and international cuisine, attracting celebrities seeking a more refined dining experience than main Itaewon.
