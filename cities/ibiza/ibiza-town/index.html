Ibiza Town FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Ibiza Town include Cipriani (Marina Ibiza), Lio Ibiza (dinner cabaret at Marina Botafoch), La Gaia by Oscar Molina (Japanese), and Es Boldado (clifftop seafood). The port area and Marina Botafoch are prime celebrity dining locations.

Marina Botafoch is Ibiza Town's most glamorous area, featuring superyacht berths, luxury restaurants, and high-end nightclubs. Lio Ibiza, Pacha, and multiple celebrity-favored restaurants line the marina, making it the epicenter of Ibiza's evening scene.

Dalt Vila, the UNESCO-listed old town, offers atmospheric restaurants with historic settings. While more intimate than the marina scene, restaurants within the ancient walls attract celebrities seeking romantic, cultural dining experiences away from the beach club crowds.

Lio Ibiza is a unique dinner-cabaret venue at Marina Botafoch, combining fine dining with world-class performances. Celebrities enjoy Mediterranean cuisine while aerial artists, dancers, and musicians perform throughout the evening. It's one of Ibiza's most exclusive dining experiences.
