The Sunset Strip Experience

Sunset Ritual

Every evening, thousands gather along the Sunset Strip for Ibiza's most iconic ritual. As the sun descends, DJs score the moment while celebrities and visitors share the communal experience of Mediterranean magic.

Legendary

DJ Culture

San Antonio birthed Ibiza's electronic music scene. Cafe del Mar's chill-out compilations and Cafe Mambo's pre-party sets defined generations. Today's superstar DJs still pay homage with sunset performances.

Iconic

Pre-Party Culture

Cafe Mambo hosts legendary pre-party sessions before club nights. Celebrities gather for dinner and drinks, enjoying sets from headlining DJs before heading to Pacha, Amnesia, or other superclubs.

Essential

Bay Dining

San Antonio Bay offers more relaxed dining away from the Strip's intensity. Beach restaurants and waterfront venues provide sunset views with lower-key atmospheres for celebrities seeking respite.

San Antonio FAQs

Top celebrity spots in San Antonio include Cafe del Mar (the original sunset bar), Cafe Mambo (pre-party institution), Savannah Beach Club, and various restaurants along the Sunset Strip. World-famous DJs regularly perform sunset sessions while celebrities dine.

Cafe del Mar is the legendary sunset bar that defined Ibiza's chill-out culture. Since 1980, celebrities and music lovers have gathered to watch the sunset while DJs play ambient and electronic music. The venue also spawned the famous Cafe del Mar compilation albums that spread worldwide.

The Sunset Strip is San Antonio's famous waterfront promenade featuring Cafe del Mar, Cafe Mambo, Savannah, and other bars. Every evening, thousands gather to watch the sunset over the Mediterranean while top DJs perform. It's one of Ibiza's most iconic and unmissable experiences.

Sunset times vary by season: around 9:15-9:30pm in peak summer (June-July), earlier in May and September. Arrive 1-2 hours before sunset for good positioning. The DJ sets typically build toward the sunset moment, creating an unforgettable crescendo.
