Palisades Village: The New Celebrity Hub

Rick Caruso's Palisades Village has transformed Pacific Palisades into a luxury destination for celebrity families. This upscale outdoor shopping center features:

  • Hank's Restaurant - American classics in a stylish setting
  • SunLife Organics - Celebrity juice and smoothie spot
  • Sweet Laurel Bakery - Paleo-friendly celebrity treats
  • No-Paparazzi Policy - Security ensures celebrity privacy
  • Family Atmosphere - Playground and kid-friendly design

Regular visitors include Matt Damon, Ben Affleck, Jennifer Garner, Kate Hudson, and Steven Spielberg shopping with their families.
