The Pasadena Dining Experience

Pasadena offers old Hollywood glamour and Caltech sophistication in a setting far from the Westside paparazzi:

  • The Langham - Historic hotel (formerly Ritz-Carlton) where celebrities retreat for afternoon tea and fine dining
  • Old Pasadena - Colorado Boulevard's restored downtown with upscale restaurants
  • Rose Bowl Events - Celebrity sightings spike around major games and concerts
  • Private Dining - Many restaurants offer discrete private rooms
  • Awards Season - Nearby studios mean awards show afterparties

Pasadena's old money atmosphere means celebrities can dine without the scene. Think refined service, understated elegance, and genuine privacy.
