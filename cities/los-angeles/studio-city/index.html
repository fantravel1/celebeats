The Studio City Dining Experience

Studio City is where the TV industry lives and eats. Unlike flashy Hollywood or exclusive Beverly Hills, it's refreshingly casual:

  • Art's Deli - The industry lunch spot since 1957. Sitcom stars, writers, and agents at every booth
  • Ventura Boulevard - Restaurant row stretches for miles with endless options
  • CBS Radford - The studio brings TV celebrities for quick lunches between takes
  • Neighborhood Feel - Stars live here. You'll spot them at casual breakfast spots
  • Omakase Excellence - Asanebo rivals any Westside sushi spot

Studio City celebrities dress down. Expect to see TV stars in casual wear, genuinely relaxed in their neighborhood spots.
