Gjelina: Venice's Celebrity Headquarters

Gjelina has been the defining restaurant of Venice's food scene since 2008. This Abbot Kinney institution draws celebrities for:

  • Wood-Fired Pizzas - Crispy, creative toppings, celebrity favorite
  • Seasonal Vegetables - Farm-to-table California produce
  • Late Night Scene - Open until midnight, post-beach crowd
  • Gjusta Spinoff - Same team's bakery/deli for breakfast
  • No Reservations Policy - Everyone waits, even celebrities

Regular celebrity visitors include Leonardo DiCaprio, Jessica Alba, Chris Hemsworth, and the entire Silicon Beach tech crowd.
