The Brentwood Country Mart Experience

The Brentwood Country Mart has been a celebrity sanctuary since the 1940s. This charming open-air shopping center offers:

  • Farmshop Restaurant - Jeff Cerciello's farm-to-table California cuisine
  • Diesel Bookstore - Celebrity browsing and coffee
  • James Perse - Celebrity shopping destination
  • Paparazzi-Free Zone - Strict no-photography policy respected
  • Family Atmosphere - Celebrity kids and nannies daily

Regular visitors include Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Garner, Ben Affleck, and Gwyneth Paltrow shopping with their children.
