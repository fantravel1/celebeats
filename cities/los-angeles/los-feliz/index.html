Little Dom's: The Celebrity Living Room

Little Dom's has become Los Feliz's unofficial celebrity gathering place, beloved for its Italian-American comfort food and neighborhood charm. Regular visitors include:

  • Kristen Bell & Dax Shepard - Los Feliz residents and regulars
  • Jon Hamm - Mad Men star, frequent diner
  • Ryan Gosling - Spotted at weekend brunch
  • Anna Faris - Family dinners with kids
  • Musicians & Artists - The creative community hub

The restaurant is famous for its meatballs, Sunday gravy, and the adjacent Big Dom's Deli for casual takeaway.
