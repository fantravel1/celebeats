Giorgio Baldi: The Celebrity Pasta Destination

Giorgio Baldi is arguably the most celebrity-frequented restaurant in all of Los Angeles. This intimate Italian restaurant has hosted:

  • Adele - Regularly spotted, known for loving their pasta
  • Beyonce & Jay-Z - Frequent date night destination
  • Kim Kardashian - Family birthday celebrations
  • Jennifer Lawrence - Post-awards dinner favorite
  • Taylor Swift - Squad dinners

The restaurant is known for its handmade pasta, especially the spaghetti with sea urchin, and its strict no-photo policy that keeps celebrities coming back.
