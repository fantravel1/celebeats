Sqirl: The Hipster Brunch Institution

Sqirl has become synonymous with LA brunch culture and Silver Lake's creative scene. This tiny restaurant regularly attracts:

  • Natalie Portman - Longtime neighborhood resident and regular
  • Kristen Stewart - Spotted frequently at weekend brunch
  • Indie Musicians - Members of Haim, Bon Iver regulars
  • Fashion Designers - Clare Waight Keller, Opening Ceremony crew
  • Directors & Writers - Greta Gerwig, Jordan Peele sightings

The restaurant is famous for its ricotta toast with seasonal jam, sorrel pesto rice bowl, and the long weekend lines that snake down Virgil Avenue.
