Where do celebrities eat in the Hollywood Hills?

The Hollywood Hills is home to legendary celebrity dining destinations including Chateau Marmont, the iconic hotel restaurant that has hosted Hollywood royalty for decades, Yamashiro with its stunning city views, and The Tower Bar at Sunset Tower Hotel. These exclusive venues, perched above Sunset Boulevard, continue to attract A-list stars seeking privacy and old Hollywood glamour.

The Chateau Marmont Legend

Since 1929, Chateau Marmont has been Hollywood's most storied hideaway. With its policy of discretion ("We don't comment on our guests"), it's where John Belushi spent his final hours, where Lindsay Lohan famously ran up her tab, and where countless industry deals have been sealed over martinis on the terrace.

Dining Tips for the Hollywood Hills

Best for Celebrity Sightings

The Tower Bar's lunch service and Chateau Marmont's garden on weekend evenings offer the highest likelihood of celebrity encounters. Yamashiro's view makes it popular for date nights and special occasions.

The winding roads of the Hollywood Hills require careful navigation. All top restaurants offer valet parking, which is strongly recommended. Rideshare is advisable if you plan to enjoy cocktails.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best celebrity restaurants in the Hollywood Hills?
The most iconic celebrity dining spots in the Hollywood Hills include Chateau Marmont, Yamashiro, The Tower Bar at Sunset Tower Hotel, and various exclusive members-only clubs. These venues have hosted everyone from old Hollywood legends to today's A-list stars.
Which celebrities live in the Hollywood Hills?
The Hollywood Hills is home to numerous celebrities including Leonardo DiCaprio, Keanu Reeves, Katy Perry, and many others. The neighborhood's privacy, views, and proximity to studios make it a perennial favorite among entertainment industry professionals.
What is the best restaurant with a view in the Hollywood Hills?
Yamashiro is widely considered the best restaurant with a view in the Hollywood Hills. The historic Japanese palace-style building offers panoramic views of Los Angeles, making it popular for special occasions and celebrity events.
