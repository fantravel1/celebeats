The Yacht Club Celebrity Scene

Marina del Rey hosts the world's largest man-made small boat harbor, attracting yacht-owning celebrities and entertainment executives. The marina lifestyle includes:

  • California Yacht Club - Exclusive membership, celebrity sailors
  • Del Rey Yacht Club - Historic club with entertainment industry members
  • Dock-and-Dine Options - Boat directly to restaurant docks
  • Sunset Cruises - Private chef experiences on yachts
  • Marina Views - Celebrity-owned waterfront condos

The marina attracts entertainment executives, tech billionaires, and sports personalities who prefer the relaxed waterfront lifestyle to traditional Hollywood scenes.
