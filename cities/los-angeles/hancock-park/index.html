Republique: Hancock Park's Culinary Crown

Housed in a stunning 1929 Charlie Chaplin building, Republique has become Hancock Park's most celebrated celebrity destination. This all-day dining institution attracts:

  • Entertainment Executives - Power breakfasts and industry meetings
  • Hollywood Stars - Weekend brunch favorites
  • Chefs & Foodies - Nancy Silverton's pastry legacy
  • Fashion Industry - Pre-shopping Larchmont brunches
  • Local Residents - Neighborhood regulars like Kylie Jenner

The restaurant is famous for its pastry program, house-made bread, and the stunning historic architecture that transports diners to old Hollywood.
