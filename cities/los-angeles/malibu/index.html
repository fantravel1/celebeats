Malibu FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Nobu Malibu, Geoffrey's Malibu, Soho House Malibu, Malibu Farm Pier Cafe, Carbon Beach Club, and Taverna Tony. These offer stunning ocean views and laid-back luxury.

Nobu Malibu is the most famous restaurant in Malibu. Located on Carbon Beach, it's a favorite of celebrities like the Kardashians, Cindy Crawford, and Rihanna, combining world-class Japanese cuisine with beachfront dining.

Nobu Malibu accepts reservations through their website and Resy. Book 2-4 weeks in advance for weekend dinners. Weekday lunches are easier to secure. Sunset tables are the most coveted and should be requested specifically.
