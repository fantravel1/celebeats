Beverly Hills FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Spago, Matsuhisa, Cut by Wolfgang Puck, The Polo Lounge, Mastro's Steakhouse, and Mr. Chow. These offer luxury dining with discreet celebrity service.

Spago by Wolfgang Puck is the most iconic restaurant in Beverly Hills. Since 1982, it has been a gathering place for Hollywood's elite and is credited with pioneering California cuisine.

Most Beverly Hills fine dining restaurants require smart casual to formal attire. Spago, Cut, and hotel restaurants typically require jackets for men at dinner. Avoid athletic wear and flip-flops at upscale establishments.
