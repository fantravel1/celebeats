San Francisco FAQs

Popular tech celebrity restaurants include Benu in SoMa, State Bird Provisions, Lazy Bear in the Mission, Quince in Jackson Square, and Saison. These establishments attract Silicon Valley executives, venture capitalists, and tech billionaires.

Mark Zuckerberg has been spotted at various Bay Area restaurants including Fuki Sushi in Palo Alto, The Slanted Door, and Benu. He's known to prefer low-key spots and has hosted Meta employee events at venues throughout the city.

Jack Dorsey is known for his wellness-focused lifestyle including intermittent fasting. When dining out, he's been spotted at health-conscious restaurants and upscale spots in SoMa near the former Twitter headquarters.

Elon Musk was frequently spotted at San Francisco restaurants before and during his Twitter acquisition. He's known for late-night dining sessions at various SoMa establishments near the Twitter (now X) headquarters.
