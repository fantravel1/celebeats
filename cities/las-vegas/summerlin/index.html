Summerlin FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Summerlin include T-Bones Chophouse at Red Rock Casino, Hearthstone Kitchen at Red Rock, Honey Salt, Grape Street Cafe, and various restaurants at Downtown Summerlin. Many Vegas-based celebrities live in Summerlin and dine locally.

Yes, Summerlin is one of the most popular residential areas for celebrities in Las Vegas. Many professional athletes (particularly Raiders and Golden Knights players), entertainers with Vegas residencies, and entertainment executives live in Summerlin's gated communities like The Ridges, Tournament Hills, and Canyon Gate.

T-Bones Chophouse & Lounge at Red Rock Casino is widely considered the best restaurant in Summerlin. This upscale steakhouse offers prime cuts, elegant atmosphere, and attracts local celebrities and high-profile residents. Hearthstone Kitchen & Cellar at the same property is also highly rated.

Red Rock Casino Resort is the best spot for celebrity sightings in Summerlin. Many celebrities who live in the area frequent its restaurants, spa, and pool. Unlike Strip casinos, sightings here tend to be more casual as celebrities treat it as their neighborhood resort.

Summerlin is approximately 20-25 minutes west of the Las Vegas Strip, depending on traffic. Red Rock Casino is about 10 miles from the Strip. Many celebrities appreciate the separation from the tourist areas, allowing them to live and dine in relative privacy.
