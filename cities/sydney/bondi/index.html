Bondi FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Bondi include Icebergs Dining Room (iconic ocean views), Bills Bondi (legendary brunch), Porch and Parlour (health-conscious), and Sean's at Bondi Beach (seasonal Australian). Celebrities appreciate Bondi's mix of fine dining and casual beach cafes.

Icebergs Dining Room is Bondi's most iconic restaurant, perched above the famous Bondi Icebergs swimming pool. The Italian-Australian fine dining restaurant offers spectacular ocean views and has hosted countless celebrities including Chris Hemsworth, Oprah Winfrey, and Leonardo DiCaprio.

Bills Bondi is the legendary brunch destination, home of the famous ricotta hotcakes. Porch and Parlour offers healthy, photogenic dishes popular with celebrities. Other favorites include The Depot Bondi, Speedos Cafe, and Harry's Bondi for its beachfront location.
