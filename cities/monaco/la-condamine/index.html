La Condamine Dining Areas

Port Hercule

Monaco's main harbor hosts some of the world's largest superyachts. Waterfront restaurants serve yacht owners and guests who step directly from their vessels to portside tables.

Yacht Scene

Yacht Club de Monaco

Designed by Norman Foster, the Yacht Club hosts sailing royalty and maritime celebrities. The terrace restaurant offers harbor views and exclusive membership atmosphere.

Exclusive

Place d'Armes

The daily market and surrounding cafes offer Monaco's most accessible dining. Local residents and off-duty celebrities gather for morning coffee and casual lunches.

F1 Circuit Section

La Rascasse corner and surrounding bars sit directly on the F1 track. During Grand Prix weekend, these venues offer prime viewing and legendary post-race celebrations.

La Condamine FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in La Condamine include Maya Bay (Asian fusion overlooking the port), La Maree (seafood), Stars'N'Bars (sports bar), and Yacht Club de Monaco restaurant. Port Hercule waterfront offers yacht-adjacent dining that's more accessible than Casino Square.

Port Hercule is Monaco's main harbor, accommodating superyachts up to 100+ meters. During F1 weekend and the Monaco Yacht Show, the port becomes a floating extension of Monaco's dining scene, with restaurants serving yacht owners and celebrity guests.

Yes, La Condamine offers more accessible dining than Casino Square. While still Monaco prices, the market area and port restaurants are generally less expensive than Hotel de Paris establishments. Stars'N'Bars provides a casual American-style option popular with celebrities.
