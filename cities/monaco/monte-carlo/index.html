Monte Carlo Dining Areas

Casino Square

The legendary Place du Casino anchors Monaco's glamour. Cafe de Paris terrace offers prime people-watching as Ferraris, Lamborghinis, and celebrities parade past the casino entrance.

Iconic

Hotel de Paris

The legendary hotel houses multiple celebrity restaurants including Le Louis XV and Le Grill. The lobby bar and terraces are gathering spots for Monaco's elite.

Legendary

Monte-Carlo Bay

The resort complex features Blue Bay restaurant, beach clubs, and poolside dining. A more relaxed alternative to Casino Square's intensity while maintaining luxury standards.

Resort

Fairmont Hairpin

The famous F1 hairpin turn sits below Fairmont Monte Carlo. Cipriani's terrace offers views of the race circuit, making it F1 weekend's most coveted dining spot.

Celebrity Sightings in Monte Carlo

F1 Weekend

The Monaco Grand Prix transforms Casino Square into F1's most glamorous paddock. Every terrace, hotel lobby, and restaurant fills with drivers, team principals, and celebrity guests.

Monte Carlo FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Monte Carlo include Le Louis XV - Alain Ducasse (3 Michelin stars at Hotel de Paris), Cafe de Paris (Casino Square terrace), Blue Bay (2 Michelin stars), and Le Grill (Hotel de Paris rooftop). Casino Square is the epicenter of celebrity sightings.

Le Louis XV - Alain Ducasse at Hotel de Paris is Monaco's most celebrated restaurant, holding 3 Michelin stars continuously since 1990. The ornate Belle Epoque dining room offers Mediterranean cuisine using the finest Riviera ingredients, attracting royalty and celebrities worldwide.

Yes, Monte Carlo restaurants typically require elegant attire. Le Louis XV and fine dining establishments require formal dress (jacket for men). Even Cafe de Paris terrace expects smart casual. Shorts, sandals, and beachwear are generally not accepted in Casino Square establishments.

Book Le Louis XV 4-8 weeks in advance for regular dates, and 3-4 months ahead for F1 weekend, the Rose Ball, or other major Monaco events. The restaurant closes for several weeks in winter. Concierge services at major hotels may assist with difficult reservations.
