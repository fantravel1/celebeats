Denver FAQs

The most popular celebrity restaurants in Denver include Frasca Food and Wine (Boulder), Guard and Grace (Downtown), Acorn (RiNo), Elway's Steakhouse (Cherry Creek), and The Wolf's Tailor. These restaurants are frequented by Broncos players, visiting celebrities, and Colorado's sports and entertainment elite.

LoDo (Lower Downtown), RiNo (River North Art District), and Cherry Creek are the top neighborhoods for celebrity sightings in Denver. LoDo offers proximity to sports venues, RiNo features trendy chef-driven spots, and Cherry Creek attracts luxury shoppers and upscale diners.

Denver Broncos players frequently dine at Elway's Steakhouse (owned by John Elway), Guard and Grace, Ocean Prime, and various LoDo restaurants near the stadium. Elway's has been a Broncos institution since 1997, attracting current and former players.

Top fine dining restaurants for celebrity sightings in Denver include Frasca Food and Wine in Boulder, Guard and Grace, Beckon, The Wolf's Tailor, and Fruition. These award-winning restaurants attract food-focused celebrities and discerning diners.

Yes, most celebrity-frequented restaurants in Denver require reservations. Frasca often books weeks ahead. Guard and Grace and Elway's are easier to book but still recommended for weekends. Game days significantly increase demand at LoDo and Downtown restaurants.
