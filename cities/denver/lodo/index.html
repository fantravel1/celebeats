Insider Tips

LoDo Dining Guide

Best for Game Days

Book early at Osteria Marco or TAG for pre-game dining. Expect crowds and celebrity sightings on Rockies home games.

Best for Business

Guard and Grace and The Capital Grille offer the professional atmosphere and private dining options executives expect.

Best for Date Night

Rioja's Mediterranean elegance and Larimer Square charm make it perfect for romantic evenings and special occasions.

Reservation Tips

Book 1-2 weeks ahead for weekends. Game days require earlier booking. Larimer Square restaurants are walkable for bar-hopping.
