Downtown Districts

Where to Find Celebrities

Downtown Austin has distinct dining districts, each with its own celebrity appeal.

Rainey Street

Converted bungalows turned into bars and restaurants. More refined than 6th Street. Emmer & Rye and Banger's lead the scene. Great for evening sightings.

Tier A Evidence

2nd Street District

Upscale shopping and dining near City Hall. Lamberts anchors the food scene. Tech executives and visiting celebrities frequent this area.

Tier A Evidence

Congress Avenue

Austin's main street with historic hotels and fine dining. Juniper, Second Bar + Kitchen, and power lunch spots. Business celebrity territory.

Tier B Evidence

6th Street

Famous party street. Less dining-focused but musicians and young celebrities hit the bars. SXSW headquarters. Casual late-night scene.

Festival Season

SXSW & Downtown Dining

Is Downtown Austin good for SXSW celebrity sightings?

Yes, Downtown Austin is ground zero for SXSW celebrity sightings. During the festival (March), every restaurant from 6th Street to Rainey Street is packed with musicians, actors, and tech celebrities. Book reservations months ahead or be prepared for 2+ hour waits. Lamberts, Emmer & Rye, and hotel restaurants like The Driskill are prime spots.

Downtown Austin FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Downtown Austin include Lamberts Downtown Barbecue, Perry's Steakhouse, Juniper, Second Bar + Kitchen, and Rainey Street spots like Emmer & Rye. Tech moguls like Elon Musk and visiting celebrities frequent these upscale establishments.

Rainey Street is Austin's trendy entertainment district, featuring converted bungalow bars and restaurants. Celebrities love spots like Emmer & Rye (upscale), Banger's Sausage House, and the laid-back backyard bar scene. It's less rowdy than 6th Street and attracts a more refined crowd.

Lamberts Downtown Barbecue is Austin's premier upscale barbecue destination, known for its "fancy BBQ" concept that combines Texas traditions with fine dining. Located on 2nd Street, it's a favorite for power lunches, celebrations, and celebrity sightings.

Tech celebrities and executives frequent Downtown's power dining spots like Perry's Steakhouse, Lamberts, and Juniper for business meals. Elon Musk has been spotted at various Downtown and South Lamar restaurants since relocating to Austin.

Most upscale Downtown restaurants recommend reservations, especially on weekends and during events. Lamberts, Perry's, and Juniper should be booked in advance. Rainey Street spots like Banger's are more casual. During SXSW, F1, and ACL, book weeks or months ahead.
