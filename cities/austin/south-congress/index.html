Top Celebrity Restaurants on SoCo

Perla's Seafood and Oyster Bar

S Congress Ave | Seafood | $$$ | 180+ Celebrity Visits

SoCo's premier celebrity destination. Coastal seafood, Gulf oysters, and an expansive patio perfect for Austin's weather. Sandra Bullock's favorite spot.

Tier A

Home Slice Pizza

S Congress Ave | Pizza | $$ | 150+ Celebrity Visits

Authentic New York-style pizza that draws celebrities and locals alike. The late-night slices are legendary. Worth the wait.

Tier A

Hopdoddy Burger Bar

S Congress Ave | Burgers | $$ | 120+ Celebrity Visits

The original location of Austin's famous craft burger chain. Celebrity-approved burgers, local beer, and spiked shakes.

Tier B

Guero's Taco Bar

S Congress Ave | Tex-Mex | $$ | 200+ Celebrity Visits

Austin institution since 1986. Presidents, musicians, and movie stars have filled this converted feed store. Margaritas and tacos in a quintessentially Austin setting.

Tier A

Elizabeth Street Cafe

S Congress Ave | Vietnamese-French | $$ | 90+ Celebrity Visits

Beautiful Vietnamese-French bakery and cafe. Instagram-worthy interiors, excellent banh mi, and French pastries loved by celebrities.

Tier B

June's All Day

S Congress Ave | Californian | $$$ | 70+ Celebrity Visits

Chic all-day restaurant in the new SoCo hotel scene. Light California-inspired fare and natural wines draw the fashion-conscious celebrity set.

Dining Guide

SoCo Dining Tips

Make the most of your South Congress dining experience.

Best Patios

Perla's has the best upscale patio. Guero's offers classic Austin outdoor dining with live music on weekends.

Best Late Night

Home Slice serves slices until late. More Home Slice next door is counter-service for quicker late-night cravings.

Best for Brunch

Elizabeth Street Cafe for beautiful Vietnamese-French. June's All Day for California vibes. Perla's for seafood brunch.

Walk the Strip

Park once and walk. SoCo is best experienced on foot - restaurants, boutiques, and the famous murals are all walkable.

South Congress combines Austin's 'Keep It Weird' spirit with excellent dining. The walkable strip features everything from upscale seafood (Perla's) to legendary pizza (Home Slice) to food trucks. Yes, South Congress is one of Austin's best neighborhoods for celebrity sightings. The walkable nature means celebrities can stroll, shop, and dine. Matthew McConaughey, Sandra Bullock, and visiting musicians are regularly spotted. Weekend afternoons and evenings offer the best chances.
