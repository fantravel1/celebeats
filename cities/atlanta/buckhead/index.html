Buckhead

Atlanta's Beverly Hills. Where old money meets new fame at legendary steakhouses and power dining destinations that have defined Southern luxury since the 1970s.

Midtown West Midtown All Atlanta

Where do celebrities eat in Buckhead?

Celebrities in Buckhead dine at Bones Restaurant for power steaks, Chops Lobster Bar for seafood, Kyma for upscale Greek, and Hal's The Steakhouse. Usher, Ludacris, and Tyler Perry have all been spotted at these establishments that have defined Atlanta luxury dining for decades.

Buckhead Dining Tips

  • Dress Code: Buckhead restaurants enforce dress codes. Jackets suggested at Bones; smart casual elsewhere.
  • Reservations: Book 2-3 weeks ahead for weekend dinner at top steakhouses. Same-week is difficult.
  • Valet: Use valet parking. Street parking is limited and Buckhead's sprawl makes walking difficult.
  • Power Lunch: Bones and Chops see significant business lunch traffic. Celebrities often spotted midday.
  • Late Night: Hal's stays lively until 1am with live jazz. Best for post-dinner drinks and celebrity sightings.
