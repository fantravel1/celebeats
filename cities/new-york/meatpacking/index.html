Meatpacking District

NYC's Most Glamorous Dining Scene

From the High Line to cobblestone streets, the Meatpacking District is where celebrity dining meets downtown cool. Home to Catch, STK, and TAO Downtown.

Greenwich Village SoHo TriBeCa Upper East Side Midtown

Where do celebrities eat in the Meatpacking District?

Celebrities dine at Catch (EMM Group's seafood hotspot), Catch Steak (their steakhouse sibling), STK (DJ-fueled steakhouse), Buddakan (dramatic Asian), and TAO Downtown (massive Asian-fusion temple). The neighborhood's nightlife-meets-dining scene makes it peak celebrity territory.

The High Line Effect

The opening of the High Line transformed the Meatpacking District from industrial relic to celebrity playground. What was once meatpacking warehouses is now rooftop bars, designer boutiques, and some of NYC's most photographed restaurants.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best celebrity restaurants in Meatpacking?
The best celebrity restaurants include Catch, Catch Steak, STK, TAO Downtown, and Buddakan. The neighborhood is known for its see-and-be-seen dining scene.
Is Meatpacking good for nightlife?
Yes! Meatpacking is one of NYC's top nightlife destinations. Many restaurants transition to a club-like atmosphere late night, and there are numerous bars and clubs in the area.
