Jean-Michel Basquiat's NoHo Legacy

NoHo was home to legendary artist Jean-Michel Basquiat, who lived and created at 57 Great Jones Street until his death in 1988. The building, now landmarked, sits above what was once a local restaurant. Today's NoHo continues to attract artists, musicians, and celebrities drawn to its creative history and authentic downtown character.

NoHo FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Bond Street, Il Buco, Great Jones Cafe, Acme, and Indochine. The intimate neighborhood creates a discreet dining atmosphere celebrities love.

NoHo is home to Cher, Keith Richards, Jonah Hill, and formerly Sophie Turner. The exclusive neighborhood with historic lofts attracts celebrities seeking privacy and downtown culture.

Bond Street is a sophisticated Japanese restaurant known for sleek design and high-quality sushi. Rihanna, Gigi Hadid, Drake, and Kanye West have been spotted at this NoHo institution.

NoHo (North of Houston) and SoHo (South of Houston) are separated by Houston Street. NoHo is smaller and more residential with landmarked buildings, while SoHo is larger with more shopping and tourism. Both attract celebrities but NoHo offers more privacy.
