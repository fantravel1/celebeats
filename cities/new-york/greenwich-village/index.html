The Carbone Phenomenon

Carbone has become the most talked-about celebrity restaurant in America. Here's why:

  • Viral Spicy Rigatoni Vodka - TikTok made this $32 pasta dish famous
  • Impossible Reservations - Books up months in advance, released at midnight
  • A-List Regulars - Drake, the Kardashians, Gigi Hadid, Rihanna
  • Retro Glamour - 1950s Italian-American supper club atmosphere
  • Major Food Group Empire - Same team behind ZZ's, Sadelle's, Dirty French

The restaurant has expanded to Miami, Las Vegas, Dallas, and Hong Kong, but the Greenwich Village original remains the ultimate celebrity hotspot.
