Chelsea Market Favorites

Chelsea Market, housed in the former Nabisco factory where Oreos were invented, is a food hall destination for celebrities and tourists alike. Top celebrity-spotted vendors include:

  • Los Tacos No. 1 - Celebrity favorite for authentic tacos
  • The Lobster Place - Fresh seafood and sushi bar
  • Mokbar - Korean rice bowls and ramen
  • Amy's Bread - Artisan bakery and cafe
  • Sarabeth's - Famous brunch spot
