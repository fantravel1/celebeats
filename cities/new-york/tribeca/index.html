Robert De Niro's Tribeca Empire

Robert De Niro has been instrumental in transforming Tribeca from an industrial area to a celebrity hotspot. His restaurant and hospitality ventures include:

  • Nobu (Co-owner) - The legendary Japanese restaurant empire
  • Tribeca Grill - His flagship American restaurant since 1990
  • Locanda Verde - Italian restaurant at The Greenwich Hotel
  • The Greenwich Hotel - His boutique hotel property
  • Tribeca Film Festival - Co-founded to revitalize the neighborhood post-9/11
