Peter Luger: Brooklyn's Legendary Steakhouse

Peter Luger Steakhouse has served legendary porterhouse steaks since 1887. This cash-only Brooklyn institution attracts celebrities from across NYC:

  • Jerry Seinfeld - Long-time regular, featured the restaurant in his show
  • Jay-Z - Brooklyn native and Peter Luger devotee
  • Leonardo DiCaprio - Steak dinners with friends
  • Jon Bon Jovi - Classic rock star, classic steakhouse
  • Robert De Niro - New York icon at a New York institution

Andrew Tarlow's Williamsburg Empire

Restaurateur Andrew Tarlow pioneered Williamsburg's dining scene with a collection of influential restaurants that attracted both locals and celebrities:

  • Diner - The original 1927 dining car that started it all
  • Marlow & Sons - Farm-to-table, brick-oven focused
  • Reynard - Wythe Hotel restaurant (now Le Crocodile)
  • Achilles Heel - Greenpoint cocktail bar extension
  • Roman's - Italian in Fort Greene
