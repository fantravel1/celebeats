Popular celebrity restaurants in NYC include Carbone, The Polo Bar, Balthazar, Rao's, and Le Bernardin. These restaurants attract A-list celebrities, fashion icons, and industry executives.

Rao's in East Harlem is considered the most exclusive restaurant in NYC, operating on a system where tables are essentially 'owned' by regulars. Carbone is also extremely difficult to book.
