Via Carota: NYC's Most Beloved Italian

Via Carota has become the defining restaurant of West Village celebrity dining. Co-owned by Jody Williams and Rita Sodi, it's known for:

  • No Reservations Policy - Creates an egalitarian dining experience
  • Fritto Misto - The most famous appetizer in the Village
  • Cacio e Pepe - Perfectly executed Roman classic
  • Garden Seating - The most coveted tables in summer
  • Cookbook - Their 2023 cookbook became a bestseller

Regular celebrity visitors include Sarah Jessica Parker, Gigi Hadid, Katie Holmes, Seth Meyers, and countless fashion industry insiders.

The Waverly Inn: Graydon Carter's Celebrity Club

The Waverly Inn, owned by former Vanity Fair editor Graydon Carter, is the West Village's most exclusive celebrity dining room:

  • Unlisted Number - Reservations require knowing someone
  • Media Elite - Editors, publishers, and journalists
  • Hollywood A-List - Oscar-winners and directors
  • Power Dinners - Business deals happen nightly
  • No Photos - Strict privacy policy

Regular visitors include Anna Wintour, Martin Scorsese, and former presidents. The fireplace room is the most coveted table.

West Village FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants include Via Carota, The Waverly Inn, Buvette, I Sodi, Bar Pitti, and Morandi. Celebrities love the intimate neighborhood feel and European-style dining.

Via Carota is beloved for its authentic Italian cuisine, romantic atmosphere, and no-reservations policy. Sarah Jessica Parker, Gigi Hadid, and Katie Holmes are frequent visitors.

West Village is home to Sarah Jessica Parker, Julianne Moore, Liv Tyler, Nicole Kidman, Jessica Chastain, and Amy Schumer. It's one of NYC's most desirable celebrity neighborhoods.

Via Carota operates on a no-reservations policy. Arrive early (5:30 PM) or late (9:30 PM) for the best chance. Weeknights are easier. The garden seats are first-come, first-served in warm weather.

Must-orders include the Fritto Misto (fried vegetables), Cacio e Pepe, seasonal vegetable dishes, and any of the house-made pastas. Save room for dessert, the olive oil cake is legendary.
