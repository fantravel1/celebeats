Hauz Khas Village

Delhi's artistic bohemian quarter where 13th-century Sultanate ruins meet trendy cafes. Narrow lanes, art galleries, rooftop restaurants with heritage views, and a creative crowd that includes young celebrities and influencers.

Unique Setting

Heritage Meets Hipster

Sultanate Ruins

13th-century Tughlaq-era ruins including a royal reservoir (hauz) and Islamic seminary. Restaurants offer rooftop views of these atmospheric monuments, creating unforgettable dining backdrops.

Heritage

Deer Park & Lake

The historic Hauz Khas lake and adjoining deer park create green oasis views. Lakeside restaurants and cafes offer peaceful settings amid Delhi's urban landscape.

Scenic

Art Galleries

The village lanes house contemporary art galleries and boutiques. Gallery openings and art events attract celebrities and Delhi's creative community.

Culture

Live Music Scene

Venues like Summer House Cafe host indie concerts and live music. Delhi's music scene gravitates here, bringing musicians and their celebrity friends.

Hauz Khas Village FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Hauz Khas Village include Imperfecto (Mediterranean-Italian), Coast Cafe (breezy coastal vibes), Raasta (Caribbean-reggae), Hauz Khas Social (trendy gastropub), and Mia Bella (Italian). The artistic atmosphere attracts young celebrities, musicians, and influencers.

Hauz Khas Village combines 13th-century Sultanate ruins with contemporary cafes and art galleries. The historic Hauz Khas lake and deer park create a unique setting. Narrow lanes lead to rooftop restaurants with heritage views, making it a favorite for Instagram-worthy dining experiences.

Hauz Khas Social is a popular gastropub in the Social chain, known for its creative cocktails, all-day dining menu, and vibrant co-working atmosphere. It attracts young professionals, creatives, and celebrities looking for a casual yet energetic setting with lake views.

Yes, Hauz Khas Village is one of Delhi's top nightlife destinations. Restaurants transition into lounges after dark with DJ nights and live music. Places like Raasta, Summer House Cafe, and various rooftop bars attract party crowds including young celebrities and influencers.

Hauz Khas Village attracts younger celebrities - indie musicians, social media influencers, TV actors, and young Bollywood stars. The artistic, bohemian vibe appeals to creatives rather than traditional power diners. Many music videos and photoshoots happen here.
