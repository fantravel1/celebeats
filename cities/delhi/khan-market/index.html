Why Khan Market?

Diplomatic Proximity

Located near diplomatic enclaves, Khan Market is where ambassadors and foreign dignitaries shop and dine. Embassy staff, expat families, and international visitors create a cosmopolitan atmosphere.

Prime Location

Power Meetings

Politicians and bureaucrats favor Khan Market for informal meetings over meals. The sophisticated setting allows power brokers to dine discreetly while maintaining accessibility.

Political Hub

Literary Culture

Home to iconic bookstores like Full Circle and Oxford Bookstore, Khan Market attracts intellectuals and culturally inclined celebrities. Book launches and literary events draw notable crowds.

Cultural

Exclusive Retail

One of the most expensive retail locations globally, Khan Market's exclusivity extends to its restaurants. The prestige attracts Delhi's elite seeking quality over spectacle.
