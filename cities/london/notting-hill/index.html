Notting Hill FAQs

Top celebrity restaurants in Notting Hill include The Ledbury (two Michelin stars), E&O for Asian cuisine, Electric Diner at the Electric Cinema, and The Cow gastropub. Many celebrities live in the area, making neighborhood spots popular for casual dining.

The Ledbury is a two Michelin-starred restaurant by chef Brett Graham, known for its refined modern British cuisine, exceptional tasting menus, and sophisticated ambiance. It consistently ranks among London's top restaurants and attracts discerning celebrity diners.

Notting Hill is home to many celebrities including Stella McCartney, Robbie Williams, and Claudia Schiffer. The neighborhood's charming streets and village atmosphere make it a popular residential area for British and international stars.

Notting Hill restaurants have a more relaxed, neighborhood feel compared to Mayfair or the West End. From the refined elegance of The Ledbury to the casual cool of Electric Diner, the area offers a village-like dining experience where celebrities can dine more discreetly.
