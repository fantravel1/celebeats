Insider Tips

Rittenhouse Dining Guide

Best for Celebrations

Barclay Prime's intimate library setting is perfect for special occasions. Book the private dining room for groups.

Best for Brunch

Parc's sidewalk seating offers prime people-watching. Arrive early on weekends or expect a wait.

Best for Food Lovers

Vernick's sophisticated menu rewards adventurous eaters. Trust the kitchen with the tasting menu.

Reservation Tips

Book Vernick and Barclay Prime 2-3 weeks ahead. Parc accepts walk-ins but expect waits during peak brunch hours.

Yes, Rittenhouse Square is Philadelphia's best neighborhood for celebrity sightings. The area's luxury hotels like The Rittenhouse, upscale restaurants, and high-end shopping attract visiting celebrities, while local stars and Eagles players frequent the neighborhood's power dining spots.
