Roma Norte

Mexico City's hippest neighborhood. Roma Norte draws celebrities and the fashion crowd with legendary Contramar, innovative Rosetta, and a perfect blend of Art Deco charm and contemporary cool.

Where do celebrities eat in Roma Norte?

Top celebrity restaurants in Roma Norte include Contramar (legendary seafood, the famous red & green tuna tostada), Rosetta (Chef Elena Reygadas' Italian-Mexican in a stunning mansion), Lardo (Mediterranean brunch favorite), and Maximo Bistrot (farm-to-table excellence). The fashion crowd and creative celebrities love Roma Norte's trendy, artistic atmosphere.

Signature Dishes in Roma Norte

The iconic dishes that make Roma Norte a celebrity dining destination.

Tostada de Atun at Contramar

Half red chile, half green parsley - the most photographed dish in Mexico City. This tuna tostada has launched a thousand Instagram posts and celebrity visits.

Instagram Famous

Pasta at Rosetta

Chef Elena Reygadas' handmade pastas showcase Italian technique with Mexican soul. The ricotta-filled ravioli and fresh bread are legendary among food-loving celebrities.

Chef's Signature

Weekend Brunch at Lardo

Roma Norte's brunch scene peaks at Lardo. Shakshuka, fresh pastries, and Mediterranean plates fuel celebrity weekends in CDMX.

Ceviche at Contramar

Beyond the famous tostada, Contramar's ceviches are extraordinary. Fresh catches prepared simply let the quality of ingredients shine through.

Roma Norte FAQs
