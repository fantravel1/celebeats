Shibuya FAQs

Popular celebrity spots in Shibuya include Florilege (French-Japanese fine dining), L'Effervescence (innovative cuisine), trendy cafes around Shibuya Crossing, and hidden gems in the backstreets. K-pop idols and international musicians frequently explore Shibuya's diverse dining scene.

The area around Shibuya Stream and Shibuya Scramble Square attracts celebrities visiting upscale restaurants. Harajuku (adjacent to Shibuya) is popular with younger celebrities exploring street food and quirky cafes. Cat Street and Omotesando offer more discreet celebrity dining options.

International celebrities spotted in Shibuya include Ariana Grande (exploring Harajuku), Justin Bieber, members of BTS and BLACKPINK, and various Hollywood stars filming in Tokyo. The district is a must-visit for celebrities seeking authentic Tokyo youth culture experiences.

Evening hours (7-10 PM) offer the best atmosphere for Shibuya dining, when the neon lights illuminate the district. For celebrity sightings, weekday evenings tend to be more exclusive. Weekend afternoons in Harajuku attract younger celebrities exploring the area.
