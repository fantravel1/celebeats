Our Evidence Standards

How we verify and rate information

Tier A

Primary Source

Direct from celebrity (interview, social media post), official restaurant confirmation, verified photo/video evidence

Tier B

Reputable Media

Major publications (People, TMZ, etc.), professional paparazzi, credentialed journalists

Tier C

Verified UGC

Multiple corroborating eyewitness accounts, verified social media posts from other diners

Tier D

Unverified

Single source tips, rumors, unconfirmed reports. We note when information is unverified.

Community Guidelines

When contributing to CelebEats, please:

  • Be truthful - Only submit information you believe to be accurate
  • Be respectful - Don't harass celebrities or restaurant staff
  • Be specific - Include dates, locations, and details when possible
  • Be ethical - Don't share private moments or violate privacy
  • Cite sources - Let us know where you got your information

By submitting content, you agree to our Terms of Use and grant CelebEats permission to publish your contribution.
